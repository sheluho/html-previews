Common Questions

Understanding Our Process

How is your method different from standard steam cleaning?
Traditional steam cleaning relies on pumping large volumes of soapy water into your carpets, which can take days to dry and leave a sticky residue. Our process utilizes a highly effective, low-moisture extraction method that cleans deeply without saturating the base of the carpet, resulting in a much faster drying time and zero residue.
Do you offer specialized rug cleaning for delicate materials?
Yes. We understand that area rugs, especially antique or oriental varieties, require specific care. Our technicians carefully evaluate the dyes, fibers, and construction of each piece before determining the safest and most effective rug cleaning approach to preserve its integrity and color.
How long does carpet cleaning take to completely dry?
Because our proprietary extraction system uses roughly 80% less water than typical steam cleaning, your carpets will usually dry within 1 to 2 hours. This minimizes disruption to your daily routine and significantly reduces the risk of mold or mildew growth under the carpet backing.
Can you handle tough pet odors and advanced stain removal?
Absolutely. We offer a specialized pet urine removal treatment that breaks down odor-causing crystals at the molecular level rather than just masking them. Combined with our advanced stain removal techniques, we can tackle the most stubborn marks and leave your home smelling fresh.
Is your upholstery cleaning safe for children and pets?
Safety is our top priority. The primary solutions we use for both carpet and upholstery cleaning are non-toxic, green-certified, and completely safe for homes with children and pets. We clean your furniture thoroughly without relying on harsh soaps or dangerous chemicals.
