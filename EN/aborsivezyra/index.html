Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should we book our wedding photographer?

We recommend booking your wedding photography services 9 to 12 months in advance, especially for popular dates in the spring and fall. Securing your date early ensures we are available to capture your special day.

Do you include engagement photos in your packages?

Yes, many of our comprehensive collections include a complimentary engagement session. We believe taking engagement photos is a vital step in getting to know each other and helping you feel relaxed in front of the lens before the wedding.

What is your approach to bridal portraits?

Our bridal portraits are focused on capturing the elegance and intricate details of your chosen attire in a stress-free environment. These sessions are typically scheduled a few weeks prior to the wedding day, allowing for perfect lighting and creative freedom without time constraints.

Are you available for destination wedding photography?

Absolutely. We love traveling and offer specialized packages for destination wedding photography. We handle our own travel arrangements to keep the process seamless for you, ensuring you get our signature artistic quality no matter where you choose to say "I do."

How and when will we receive our photos?

We deliver a sneak peek gallery within a week of your event. Your complete, fully edited high-resolution digital gallery will be delivered via a secure online link within 6 to 8 weeks following the wedding date.
