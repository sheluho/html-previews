Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical windshield replacement take?

Most professional windshield replacement services can be completed in about one to two hours. However, it is highly recommended to wait an additional hour before driving to allow the urethane adhesive to properly cure and bond, ensuring maximum safety.

Can any crack be fixed with a simple chip repair?

No, not all damage qualifies for windshield repair. Generally, chips smaller than a quarter and cracks shorter than a few inches can be repaired successfully. If the damage is in the driver's direct line of sight or near the edge of the glass, a full replacement is usually required for safety reasons.

Do you handle car window replacement for side and rear glass?

Yes, comprehensive auto glass services include car window replacement for side doors, quarter glass, and rear windshields. Unlike front windshields which are laminated, side windows are typically tempered glass and shatter upon impact, requiring complete replacement and careful clean-up of the debris.

Why is timely auto glass repair important?

Your windshield provides up to 30% of your vehicle's structural strength in a rollover accident and supports the proper deployment of passenger airbags. Delaying auto glass repair can cause a small chip to spread across the entire windshield due to temperature changes or road vibrations, compromising your safety.

Does the new glass match my vehicle's original specifications?

Professional services utilize high-quality glass that meets or exceeds Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) standards. This ensures that features like built-in antennas, rain sensors, and advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) continue to function correctly after the windshield replacement.
