Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about commissioning bespoke pieces.

How long does it take to build a custom dining table?
Lead times vary based on the complexity of the design and current workshop schedule. Typically, a solid wood custom dining table takes between 8 to 12 weeks from the final design approval to completion.
Can I choose the fabric and dimensions for a custom sofa?
Absolutely. The definition of bespoke furniture means you have control over the length, depth, cushion fill, and upholstery. The showroom offers a curated selection of premium textiles and leathers for your custom sofa.
What makes handmade furniture better than mass-produced pieces?
Handmade furniture utilizes superior joinery techniques (like mortise and tenon or dovetails), solid wood rather than veneers over particleboard, and is built with an attention to detail that mass production simply cannot replicate. It is designed to be a lifelong heirloom.
Do you only make custom furniture, or do you have a showroom?
The provider operates both as a custom furniture maker and a showroom. You can visit to see floor models, inspect the quality of the craftsmanship firsthand, and view material samples before commissioning your own piece.
How do I start the design process?
The best way to begin is by filling out the inquiry form below. Provide a brief description of what you are looking for (e.g., a walnut custom dining table for 8), and the team will reach out to schedule an initial consultation to discuss aesthetics, dimensions, and materials.
