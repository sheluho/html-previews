Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries regarding our event catering promotions.

Do you offer both buffet catering and plated dinners? ▼

Yes, the culinary partners we promote, including Catering By Design, are highly versatile. They offer elegantly served plated dinners for formal occasions as well as expansive buffet catering setups designed to encourage guest interaction and provide diverse menu options.

How far in advance should we book wedding catering? ▼

Because wedding catering requires extensive planning and dates fill up quickly, it is highly recommended to secure your catering partner 9 to 12 months in advance. This ensures ample time for menu tastings, venue walk-throughs, and finalizing dietary accommodations.

Is corporate catering available for large-scale conferences? ▼

Absolutely. Corporate catering is a specialty, ranging from executive boardroom lunches to multi-day conference feeding. The teams are equipped to handle large volumes efficiently while maintaining exceptional food quality and professional presentation.

Can the catering partners accommodate specific dietary restrictions? ▼

Yes, accommodating dietary needs is a standard part of premium event catering. Whether your guests require gluten-free, vegan, vegetarian, or allergy-specific meals, the culinary teams will craft specialized dishes to ensure every attendee enjoys a safe and delicious dining experience.

What is typically included in an event catering package? ▼

Event catering packages generally include menu customization, food preparation, professional on-site staff (servers and chefs), and basic setup and breakdown of the food stations. Additional rentals such as premium linens, specific glassware, and custom decor can often be coordinated through the provider upon request.
