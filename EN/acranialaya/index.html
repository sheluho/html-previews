Frequently Asked Questions

What are the primary benefits of energy efficient windows?

Energy efficient windows are specifically designed to prevent heated or cooled air from escaping your home. By utilizing advanced glazing, inert gas fills, and superior weatherstripping, these windows significantly reduce the workload on your HVAC system, leading to lower monthly utility bills and a more comfortable, draft-free indoor environment year-round.

How long does a typical window installation take?

Most standard residential window installation projects can be completed within one to two days, depending on the number of windows being replaced. Our specialized crews work efficiently to minimize disruption to your daily life, ensuring each opening is weather-tight and secure before they leave the site.

Are vinyl windows durable enough for extreme weather?

Yes. Modern premium vinyl windows are engineered with specialized compounds that resist UV degradation, extreme temperature fluctuations, and heavy moisture. Unlike older wooden frames, they will not warp, rot, or require regular painting, making them an exceptionally durable and low-maintenance choice for almost any climate.

What is the difference between single and double hung windows?

In a single hung window, only the bottom sash moves up and down while the top remains fixed. In double hung windows, both the top and bottom sashes are operable. This allows for superior ventilation control (letting warm air out the top and cool air in the bottom) and makes cleaning the exterior glass incredibly easy from the inside of your home.

Will replacement windows actually increase my home's value?

Absolutely. Upgrading to new replacement windows is consistently ranked as one of the top home improvement projects for return on investment. Not only do they enhance curb appeal and modernize your home's exterior, but prospective buyers highly value the immediate reduction in energy costs and maintenance requirements that come with newly installed energy efficient windows.
