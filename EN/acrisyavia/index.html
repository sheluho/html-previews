Frequently Asked Questions

Answers about professional home cleaning services.

What is typically included in a standard house cleaning?

A standard maid service visit generally includes dusting surfaces, vacuuming and mopping floors, sanitizing bathroom fixtures, wiping down kitchen countertops, and emptying standard trash bins. It's designed to maintain the general cleanliness of your home.

How often should I schedule a recurring cleaning?

The frequency depends entirely on your household's lifestyle, size, and presence of pets. Most homeowners opt for bi-weekly visits to keep dirt and dust at bay, while busier or larger households often prefer weekly recurring cleaning sessions.

Do I need to be present during the maid service?

No, you do not need to be home. Many clients provide a spare key, garage code, or lockbox access so the professional house cleaning team can perform their duties while you are at work or running errands.

What exactly does move-out cleaning entail?

Move-out cleaning is an exhaustive service performed on an empty home. It typically includes cleaning inside cabinets and drawers, wiping down baseboards, cleaning the inside of major appliances, and deep cleaning all living areas to prepare the space for its next resident.

Are the deep cleaning methods safe for my pets?

Yes, the service providers we introduce prioritize the use of safe, environmentally responsible products. Be sure to mention you have pets when scheduling your deep cleaning so the team can tailor their approach and product selection accordingly.
