Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the premium car wash?

Our premium car wash includes a meticulous exterior hand wash, tire shine, deep wheel cleaning, interior vacuuming, and streak-free window cleaning to ensure a complete refresh of your vehicle.

How long does an express wash take?

The express wash is designed for maximum efficiency without sacrificing quality. It typically takes under 20 minutes from start to finish, getting you back on the road swiftly.

What are the benefits of the auto wash membership?

An auto wash membership offers unlimited routine washes, priority service scheduling, and exclusive discounts on our advanced hand car wash and detailing packages. It is the most cost-effective way to maintain your vehicle's appearance.

Do you perform a hand car wash or use automated machines?

We strongly advocate for and specialize in a meticulous hand car wash. This approach is safer for your clear coat and allows professionals to address specific areas of dirt that automated machines often miss.

Are your cleaning products safe for all vehicles?

Yes, all products used are pH-balanced, eco-friendly, and completely safe for all types of automotive paint, ceramic coatings, and interior materials.
