Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in your wedding catering packages?

Our standard wedding catering services usually include menu customization, professional service staff, culinary preparation on-site or off-site, and essential dining equipment. Whether you prefer an intimate plated dinner or a lavish family-style feast, we tailor the offerings to fit your vision perfectly.

Do you offer corporate catering for large events?

Yes, corporate catering is a core specialty. We can handle events of varying scales, providing everything from morning pastry spreads and boxed lunches to multi-course executive dinners. We emphasize punctuality and professional presentation to ensure your business event runs smoothly.

Can we choose a buffet catering setup instead of a plated dinner?

Absolutely. Buffet catering is an excellent choice for offering guests a wider variety of options and encouraging a more relaxed, social atmosphere. We design beautiful buffet stations that maintain the elegance of a formal event while providing abundant choices.

Do you accommodate specific dietary restrictions?

Yes, any premier event catering service must be versatile. The culinary teams we promote are highly experienced in designing menus that cater to vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, and allergy-specific requirements without compromising on flavor or presentation.

How far in advance should we book our event catering?

For peak seasons (summer and early fall), we recommend securing your wedding catering or large-scale corporate catering at least 6 to 9 months in advance. For smaller private events or off-peak dates, a shorter lead time is often possible, but earlier booking guarantees availability.
