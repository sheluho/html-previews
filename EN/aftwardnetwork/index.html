Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should we book a wedding photographer?

It is highly recommended to secure your wedding photographer at least 9 to 12 months in advance, especially if you are planning a celebration during peak wedding season. Dates fill up incredibly fast for top-tier professionals.

Are engagement photos included in standard packages?

Many comprehensive collections do include an engagement session, as it is a fantastic way for the photographer and the couple to get to know one another before the wedding day. Custom packages can also be built to include or exclude engagement photos based on your specific needs.

Do you accommodate destination wedding photography?

Absolutely. Destination wedding photography is a specialized service designed to cover your event wherever it may take place. Travel and accommodation logistics are discussed during the consultation to ensure seamless coverage anywhere in the world.

How long does it take to receive our final gallery?

Post-production is an intensive process where each image is carefully edited for color, lighting, and consistency. Typically, couples receive their fully edited, high-resolution digital gallery within 6 to 8 weeks after their wedding date.

Can we request specific bridal portraits on the day?

Yes, creating a custom shot list is part of the pre-wedding planning process. While the primary focus is on candid, photojournalistic coverage, setting aside dedicated time for classic bridal portraits and essential family groupings ensures a well-rounded final collection.
