Frequently Asked Questions

Are cleaning supplies and equipment included in the house cleaning service?

Yes, the professional teams arrive fully equipped with all necessary supplies, tools, and vacuums required to perform a thorough clean of your home. You do not need to provide anything.

How long does a typical deep cleaning take?

The duration of a deep cleaning depends on the size of your home and its current condition. On average, it can take anywhere from 3 to 6 hours for a standard-sized home to ensure every detail is addressed.

Do I need to be home during the maid service?

No, it is entirely up to you. Many clients prefer to provide access via a key, garage code, or lockbox so the cleaning can be completed while they are at work or running errands.

What is included in a move-out cleaning?

A move-out cleaning is an intensive service designed to prepare a home for new occupants. It typically includes cleaning inside cabinets, drawers, ovens, and refrigerators, as well as detailed wiping of baseboards and fixtures.

Can I adjust the frequency of my recurring cleaning?

Absolutely. Schedules are flexible, allowing you to transition between weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly visits depending on your changing household needs and busy lifestyle.
