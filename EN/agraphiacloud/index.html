Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

What is included in your standard wedding videography package?

Our standard packages typically include full-day coverage by a primary wedding videographer, a fully edited cinematic wedding video (highlight film), and raw footage of key moments like the ceremony and toasts. Exact deliverables are customized based on your specific timeline and needs.

Do you travel for destination wedding films?

Yes, absolutely. Our partnered videography teams regularly travel for destination weddings. We handle all our own travel logistics to ensure a seamless experience, allowing us to capture your cinematic wedding video anywhere in the world.

How long does it take to receive our final wedding film?

Because editing a true cinematic wedding video requires meticulous attention to detail, color grading, and audio mixing, the delivery timeline is typically between 8 to 12 weeks following your event. We prioritize quality over speed to ensure your film is perfect.

What is your overall event videography style?

Our style is highly editorial, cinematic, and unobtrusive. We focus on capturing candid, authentic moments rather than staging awkward poses. The result is an event videography portfolio that feels natural, elegant, and deeply emotional.

Can we choose the music for our wedding video?

To ensure your wedding film can be shared online without copyright strikes, we strictly use fully licensed, premium music. While we select the tracks to perfectly match the emotional arc of your footage, we always take your musical tastes and preferences into account during the planning phase.
