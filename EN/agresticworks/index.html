Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our modern carpet and upholstery care.

What makes your carpet cleaning method different from steam cleaning?

Unlike traditional steam cleaning which saturates your floors with gallons of water, the oxygen-powered method uses an encapsulating foam that breaks down dirt at a molecular level. This uses 95% less water, meaning your carpets dry in about one hour instead of 12-24 hours, significantly reducing the risk of mold or mildew.

How long does upholstery cleaning typically take?

The active cleaning process for a standard sofa usually takes about 1 to 2 hours, depending on the condition and level of soil. Because our specialized upholstery cleaning machines control moisture output tightly, your furniture will typically be dry and ready for use within just a few hours after the cleaning is completed.

Are your stain removal products safe for children and pets?

Yes! The core cleaning solutions and stain removal treatments promoted by our partners are eco-friendly and designed with safety in mind. They avoid the harsh chemicals often found in DIY spot cleaners, ensuring that once the floor is dry, it is completely safe for your kids and pets to play on.

How often should I schedule professional rug cleaning?

For standard area rugs in average-traffic households, we recommend professional rug cleaning once every 12 to 18 months. However, if the rug is situated in a high-traffic area like an entryway, or if you have multiple pets, scheduling a cleaning every 6 to 9 months will help maintain the fiber integrity and vibrant colors.

Can you remove pet urine odors and stains?

Absolutely. Pet accidents require specialized attention because urine can seep beneath the carpet fibers into the backing. Our advanced stain removal process utilizes enzymatic treatments that specifically target and break down the odor-causing proteins, effectively removing both the smell and the visible stain from your carpets.
