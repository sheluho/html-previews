Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries regarding our landscaping and lawn care promotions.

How often should I schedule lawn mowing?

For optimal turf health during the growing season, weekly lawn mowing is generally recommended. This prevents the grass from becoming overgrown and reduces stress on the root system. Bi-weekly schedules can be arranged during slower growth periods in the cooler months.

What does landscape installation typically include?

Landscape installation covers a broad range of physical property enhancements. This often includes soil preparation, planting new trees and shrubs, laying sod, installing mulch or decorative rock, and constructing hardscapes like retaining walls or stone pathways to complete your garden design.

Do you offer seasonal garden design updates?

Yes, seasonal updates are a crucial part of maintaining a vibrant exterior. The professionals we promote can provide spring cleanups, fall leaf removal, and seasonal flower rotations to ensure your garden design remains colorful and appropriate for the time of year.

Why is professional tree service important?

Professional tree service is vital for safety and property preservation. Trained arborists have the specialized equipment and knowledge to remove dead or hazardous limbs safely, diagnose diseases, and perform structural pruning that encourages healthy, long-term growth without risking damage to your home.

How can proper lawn care improve my property value?

Curb appeal is a major factor in real estate valuation. A well-maintained landscape with lush grass, clean edges, and intentional garden design creates a strong first impression. Consistent lawn care demonstrates that the property as a whole has been respected and properly maintained over time.
