Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about our entry and interior door solutions.

How long does a standard front door replacement take?

A professional front door replacement typically takes a single day to complete. The process involves carefully removing the old frame, preparing the opening, and securing the new entry doors with precise weatherstripping and hardware adjustments to ensure a perfect fit.

What are the distinct benefits of choosing fiberglass doors?

Fiberglass doors offer incredible durability and require virtually no maintenance compared to traditional wood. They do not warp, rot, or expand with temperature changes, and they provide excellent insulation, making them highly energy-efficient while still offering the rich look of real wood grain.

Do the services cover interior doors as well as entry doors?

Yes, the professionals featured on Airmark Base specialize in comprehensive door installation. This includes everything from heavy-duty exterior entry doors to stylish, sound-dampening interior doors for bedrooms, bathrooms, and closets.

Are new entry doors energy efficient?

Absolutely. Modern entry doors, particularly fiberglass doors, are engineered with insulated cores and advanced weatherstripping. This significantly reduces thermal transfer, helping to keep your home comfortable year-round and potentially lowering your heating and cooling costs.

What should I prepare before a door installation?

Before the installation team arrives, simply ensure there is clear access to the doorway both inside and outside. Move any furniture, rugs, or decorative items away from the work area to prevent accidental damage and allow the installers ample space to work efficiently.
