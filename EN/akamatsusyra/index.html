Frequently Asked Questions

Are custom window treatments worth the investment?

Absolutely. Custom window treatments are tailored to the exact dimensions of your windows, ensuring a perfect fit that off-the-shelf products cannot match. They provide better insulation, superior light control, and often increase the overall value of your home.

What is the difference between blinds and shades?

Blinds are "hard" window treatments made of horizontal or vertical slats (like wood, faux wood, or metal) that can be tilted to control light. Shades are "soft" window treatments made of continuous rolls of fabric that you pull up or down, such as roller shades or Roman shades.

Are plantation shutters energy efficient?

Yes, plantation shutters are among the most energy-efficient window treatments available. Because they are installed tightly against the window frame, they create an excellent barrier against heat transfer, keeping your home cooler in the summer and warmer in the winter.

Can custom drapes be paired with other treatments?

Yes, layering is a highly popular design choice. Custom drapes look beautiful when paired with roller shades, blinds, or even plantation shutters. This approach allows for maximum versatility in light control and adds rich textural depth to your room.
