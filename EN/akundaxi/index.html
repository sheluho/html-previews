Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our tire storage and installation process.

How does your seasonal tire storage work?
When you bring your vehicle in for a seasonal tire installation, we take your off-season tires, clean them, inspect them for wear, and store them in our climate-controlled facility. When the next season arrives, simply book an appointment, and we'll swap them back on.
Do I need a wheel alignment with every tire replacement?
While not strictly mandatory every time, we strongly recommend a wheel alignment when installing a new set of tires. Proper alignment ensures your new tires wear evenly, maximizing their lifespan and keeping your vehicle driving straight and safely.
How often should I get a tire rotation?
We generally advise a tire rotation every 5,000 to 8,000 miles, or in accordance with your vehicle manufacturer's guidelines. Regular rotation helps distribute tread wear evenly across all four tires, extending their usable life.
Are my tires insured while in storage?
Yes, our secure tire storage facility is fully insured. Your tires are protected against fire, theft, and environmental damage while they are stored with us during the off-season.
Can you install tires that I purchased elsewhere?
Absolutely. We provide professional tire installation for tires purchased from other retailers. Just bring them in, and our team will mount and balance them with the same care as tires purchased directly from our shop.
