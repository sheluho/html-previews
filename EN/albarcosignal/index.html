Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about our wedding planning services.

What is the difference between a wedding planner and a day of coordinator?

A full-service wedding planner handles the entire scope of your event from start to finish, including booking vendors, designing the aesthetic, and managing the budget. A day of coordinator typically steps in a few weeks before the wedding to manage logistics, finalize timelines, and ensure everything runs smoothly on the actual day.

Do you plan destination weddings outside the country?

Yes, we specialize in destination wedding planning. We assist with scouting international venues, coordinating travel logistics for guests, and communicating with local vendors to ensure your faraway celebration is as seamless as a local one.

When should we hire a wedding coordinator?

Ideally, you should secure your wedding planning team 12 to 18 months before your desired wedding date. This allows ample time to secure top-tier venues and vendors. However, if you are looking for a day of coordinator, we recommend booking at least 6 months in advance.

Can you work within our specific budget?

Absolutely. Part of our role as your wedding planner is to maximize your budget. We provide transparent cost projections and recommend vendors that align with your financial parameters while still delivering a premium experience.

Do you provide design and styling services?

Yes, comprehensive event design is a core part of our full-service planning. We work closely with you to develop mood boards, color palettes, and stylistic concepts, ensuring your wedding visual identity is cohesive across floral arrangements, lighting, and table settings.
