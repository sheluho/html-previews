Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is a turnkey kitchen remodel?

A turnkey kitchen project means the entire process is managed for you from start to finish. This includes the initial design consultation, demolition, structural changes, plumbing, electrical work, installing custom cabinets, placing countertops, and final cleanup. You simply "turn the key" to a finished space.

How long does a custom kitchen project usually take?

Timelines can vary significantly based on the scope of work and material availability. Generally, once all materials like cabinets and countertops have arrived, the active construction phase of a full kitchen remodeling project takes between 4 to 8 weeks.

Can I just replace my countertops and cabinets without a full remodel?

Yes, partial kitchen remodeling is entirely possible. If your layout functions well, upgrading to new custom cabinets and fresh countertops can dramatically transform the look and feel of your space without the need for extensive demolition.

What are the most durable materials for countertops?

For high-traffic kitchens, quartz and granite are highly recommended. Both offer incredible durability, resistance to scratches and heat, and come in a wide variety of finishes to match any custom kitchen aesthetic.

Do you provide design assistance?

Absolutely. Every successful project begins with a comprehensive design phase. The process ensures that your specific needs are met, integrating your chosen cabinets, countertops, and appliances into a cohesive and functional layout.
