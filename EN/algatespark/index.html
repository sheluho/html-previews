Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our custom window treatments.

What is the difference between blinds and shades?

Blinds are "hard" window treatments composed of individual slats (made of wood, faux wood, or metal) that can be tilted to control light. Shades are "soft" window treatments made of a continuous roll of fabric that can be raised or lowered, such as roller shades or cellular shades. Both offer unique aesthetic and functional benefits depending on your room's needs.

Do you offer custom drapes as well?

Yes, we offer an extensive selection of custom drapes. Custom drapery allows you to perfectly match your interior decor with hundreds of fabrics, patterns, and lining options ranging from light-filtering to complete blackout.

How do I know what size to order?

Because all our window treatments are custom-made, precise measurements are critical. We recommend scheduling a professional design consultation where an expert will measure your windows exactly, ensuring your plantation shutters, blinds, or shades fit flawlessly.

Are plantation shutters energy efficient?

Absolutely. Plantation shutters are known for being one of the most energy-efficient window treatments available. They provide excellent insulation against heat in the summer and cold in the winter, helping to regulate your home's temperature and potentially lowering your energy bills.

How do I clean and maintain my new blinds?

Maintenance depends on the material. Faux wood and aluminum blinds can easily be wiped down with a damp cloth. Real wood blinds should be dusted regularly and cleaned with a specialized wood cleaner to avoid warping. Roller shades and custom drapes typically require light vacuuming with an upholstery attachment.
