Frequently Asked Questions

What are the benefits of upgrading to fiberglass doors?
Fiberglass doors are highly valued for their exceptional durability and energy efficiency. Unlike wood, they do not warp, rot, or expand due to moisture. They offer excellent insulation properties, which can help regulate indoor temperatures, and they can be manufactured to mimic the authentic look of natural wood grain.
How long does a typical front door replacement take?
In most standard cases, a professional front door replacement can be completed within a single day. The process involves removing the old unit, preparing the opening, ensuring the frame is structurally sound, installing the new pre-hung door, and applying necessary weatherproofing and trim.
Does interior doors installation require framing modifications?
It depends on the scope of the project. If you are simply replacing door slabs within existing, perfectly square frames, structural changes are minimal. However, if the existing frames are damaged, out of plumb, or if you are installing pre-hung units, some adjustments to the rough opening may be necessary to ensure a perfect fit.
Do you handle the disposal of the old entry doors?
Yes, standard door installation services generally include the clean-up and responsible disposal of your old doors and related construction debris. We ensure the work area is left pristine once the installation is complete.
Can a new door installation improve my home's energy efficiency?
Absolutely. Older entry doors often suffer from worn weatherstripping and poor core insulation, leading to drafts and energy loss. A modern front door replacement, especially with insulated fiberglass doors or high-quality weather seals, significantly reduces thermal transfer, helping to lower heating and cooling costs.
