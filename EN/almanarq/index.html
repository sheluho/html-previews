Common Questions

Insights into professional house cleaning expectations.

What is typically included in a recurring house cleaning?
A standard recurring cleaning generally covers dusting surfaces, vacuuming and mopping floors, emptying trash, and thoroughly cleaning bathrooms and kitchens. The goal is to maintain a high baseline of cleanliness throughout the home on a regular schedule.
How is deep cleaning different from a standard maid service?
Deep cleaning involves a more intensive focus on areas that accumulate dirt over time but aren't part of a daily routine. This includes hand-wiping baseboards, cleaning the exterior of high cabinets, detailing ceiling fans, and scrubbing grout. It is often recommended as an initial service before beginning a recurring schedule.
Do professionals bring their own cleaning supplies?
Yes, professional teams typically arrive fully equipped with all necessary vacuums, mops, cloths, and cleaning solutions. Many services highlighted by Almanarq utilize environmentally responsible products that are effective yet safe for families and pets.
Do I need to be home while the cleaning takes place?
It is entirely up to your preference. Many clients provide a spare key or garage code so the team can clean while they are at work or running errands, allowing them to return to a freshly cleaned home. Security and trust are paramount in professional maid services.
What is a move-out cleaning and why is it important?
A move-out cleaning is designed for empty properties to prepare them for the next resident or to secure a security deposit return. It involves thorough cleaning of empty cabinets, closets, and appliances, ensuring the home is in top condition after all furniture has been removed.
