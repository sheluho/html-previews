Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between an express wash and a hand car wash? ▼
An express wash utilizes automated, soft-touch technology to clean the exterior of your vehicle rapidly, making it ideal for regular maintenance when you are short on time. A hand car wash is a more intensive, manual process where technicians clean both the exterior and interior by hand, paying close attention to intricate details and delicate finishes.
How does the auto wash membership work? ▼
The auto wash membership is a monthly subscription that allows you to wash your vehicle as often as you like for a single flat fee. A small RFID tag is placed on your windshield, granting you seamless, automated access to the wash tunnel without needing to pay per visit.
Is a premium car wash safe for custom paint jobs? ▼
Yes, premium car wash facilities use pH-balanced soaps and non-abrasive materials designed to protect factory clear coats and custom finishes alike. For highly specialized paint jobs, we recommend requesting the hand car wash service to ensure the gentlest possible treatment.
Do you offer interior detailing services? ▼
Yes, comprehensive interior detailing is available alongside standard wash packages. This includes deep vacuuming, upholstery conditioning, dashboard cleaning, and window treatment to restore the inside of your vehicle to an impeccable standard.
How long does a full service wash typically take? ▼
An express exterior wash takes approximately 3 to 5 minutes. A full service or hand car wash, which includes interior vacuuming and hand drying, typically takes between 15 to 30 minutes depending on the vehicle's size and condition.
