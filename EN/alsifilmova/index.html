Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our door installation and replacement services.

What are the benefits of choosing fiberglass doors for my entryway?

Fiberglass doors are an excellent choice for front door replacement because they offer the aesthetic appeal of real wood without the associated maintenance. They are highly resistant to warping, rotting, and denting, making them incredibly durable. Additionally, they provide superior insulation, helping to improve your home's energy efficiency.

Do you handle both entry doors and interior doors?

Yes, the network of professionals we promote operates as a comprehensive entry and interior doors installation company. They can handle a single front door replacement or a whole-house interior door upgrade, ensuring consistent quality and style throughout your property.

How long does a typical door installation take?

For a standard front door replacement, the door installation typically takes about half a day to a full day, depending on the complexity of the frame and weatherproofing required. Interior doors usually take less time per door, though a whole-house project will be scheduled over a few days to ensure precision.

Will I need to paint or finish my new interior doors?

It depends on your selection. Many interior doors come pre-finished or pre-primed from the factory, ready for a final coat of paint to match your decor. During your consultation, you can choose the finish level that best suits your project timeline and budget.

Do you replace the frame during a front door replacement?

In many cases, yes. A full pre-hung door installation (which includes a new frame) is often recommended to ensure optimal security, a perfect weather seal, and smooth operation. However, if your existing frame is in excellent structural condition, a slab replacement might be possible. The installer will assess this during the initial estimate.
