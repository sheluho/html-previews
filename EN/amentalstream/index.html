Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about the photography process and coverage.

Do you travel for destination wedding photography?

Yes, the studios we promote are highly experienced in destination wedding photography. We provide information on packages that accommodate travel worldwide, ensuring your international celebration is captured with the same high editorial standards as a local event.

Are engagement photos included in your collections?

Most comprehensive collections feature an engagement session. Taking engagement photos is highly recommended as it allows you to become comfortable with the wedding photographer and the camera, resulting in more natural and relaxed images on your actual wedding day.

How would you describe your editing style?

The aesthetic leans towards timeless, editorial, and authentic. The goal is to produce true-to-color imagery with warm, rich tones that emulate the classic feel of film, ensuring your bridal portraits and candid moments never look dated.

Do we get the digital files?

Yes. Following the editing process, clients receive a curated gallery of high-resolution digital files. These are delivered via a secure online gallery where you can download, share with family, and order professional prints directly.

How far in advance should we book?

Due to high demand during peak wedding seasons, it is recommended to secure your wedding photographer 9 to 12 months in advance. This ensures availability for your date and allows ample time to schedule and shoot your engagement photos.
