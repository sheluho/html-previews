Common Questions

How often should I schedule lawn mowing?

For optimal lawn health, we generally recommend weekly lawn mowing during the peak growing seasons of spring and early summer. During hotter, drier periods, we may shift to bi-weekly mowing to prevent stressing the grass.

Do you provide comprehensive landscape installation?

Yes. Our landscaping services cover everything from initial garden design consultations to full-scale landscape installation, including planting beds, shrubs, seasonal flowers, and laying fresh sod or mulch.

Are your lawn care treatments safe for pets?

We prioritize safety in all our lawn care programs. After applying specific treatments, we typically recommend keeping pets and children off the treated areas for a short period until the application has fully dried. We will always provide clear instructions based on the specific services rendered.

What is included in your tree service?

Our professional tree service includes structural pruning, deadwood removal, disease and pest inspections, and deep root fertilization to ensure your trees remain healthy, beautiful, and safe year-round.

Can you help with a complete garden design overhaul?

Absolutely. If your yard needs a fresh start, our garden design specialists will work with you to conceptualize a new layout, select appropriate flora for your local climate, and execute the complete landscape installation to bring the vision to life.
