Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our tire maintenance and storage services.

How often should I schedule a tire rotation?

It is generally recommended to get a tire rotation every 5,000 to 8,000 miles, or every time you get an oil change. Regular rotation ensures even tread wear, which significantly extends the life of your tires.

What are the benefits of professional tire storage?

Professional tire storage protects your off-season tires from extreme temperature fluctuations, UV light, and moisture, which can degrade the rubber. It also frees up valuable space in your own garage or basement.

Do I need a wheel alignment when getting new tires?

Yes, a wheel alignment is highly recommended during any tire replacement. Proper alignment ensures that your new tires meet the road at the correct angle, preventing premature and uneven tread wear right from the start.

How do I know if I need a tire replacement?

You should consider tire replacement if your tread depth falls below 2/32 of an inch, if you notice visible cracks, bulges in the sidewall, or if the tires are more than six years old, regardless of the remaining tread.

Are your tire installation services fast?

Yes, standard tire installation is typically completed within an hour when you schedule an appointment. We prioritize efficiency without compromising the safety and accuracy of the mounting and balancing process.
