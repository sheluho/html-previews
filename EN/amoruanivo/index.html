Frequently Asked Questions

What is a cinematic wedding video?

A cinematic wedding video goes beyond traditional recording. It uses high-quality cameras, artistic angles, thoughtful pacing, and emotional audio mixing to tell the story of your wedding day like a short movie, rather than a simple home video.

Do you offer both wedding and event videography?

Yes, while we specialize as a wedding videographer, our team is highly experienced in comprehensive event videography for corporate events, galas, milestone birthdays, and other significant celebrations.

How long does it take to receive our wedding film?

Post-production is an intricate process where we carefully edit, color grade, and sound design your film. Standard delivery times for a full cinematic wedding video are generally between 8 to 12 weeks, depending on the season and coverage length.

Will the videographer be obtrusive during the event?

Not at all. A professional wedding videographer knows how to blend into the background. We capture candid, authentic moments as they happen naturally without interrupting the flow of your day.

Do you travel for destination weddings?

Absolutely. We love crafting destination wedding films and elopement videos. Travel accommodations can be discussed during our initial consultation to ensure seamless coverage wherever your love takes you.
