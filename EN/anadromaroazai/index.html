Frequently Asked Questions

Details on commissioning your bespoke pieces.

What exactly qualifies as bespoke furniture?

Bespoke furniture is crafted entirely from scratch based on your specific requirements. Unlike mass-produced items, every dimension, material, and finish is selected by you, ensuring the final piece perfectly fits your home's layout and aesthetic style.

How long does it take to create a custom dining table?

The timeline for a custom dining table generally ranges from 8 to 12 weeks. This period covers everything from sourcing the perfect wood slabs to the meticulous joinery, finishing, and final curing process required for high-end handmade furniture.

Can I provide my own fabric for a custom sofa?

Yes. While the showroom offers a vast library of premium textiles and leathers, you are welcome to provide COM (Customer's Own Material). The artisans will assess the fabric to ensure it meets the durability and stretch requirements for upholstery.

Do I need an appointment to visit the custom furniture maker and showroom?

While walk-ins are welcome to browse the floor models, we highly recommend scheduling a consultation. This ensures a dedicated design specialist is available to sit down with you, review your floor plans, and discuss your specific custom furniture needs in detail.

Do you handle delivery and installation?

Yes. The original provider manages all logistics, utilizing specialized white-glove delivery services. Your handmade furniture will be carefully transported, unpacked, and placed exactly where you want it in your home by trained professionals.
