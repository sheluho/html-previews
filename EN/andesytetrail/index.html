Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical bathroom renovation take?

The timeline varies based on the scope of work. A straightforward bathtub replacement or walk-in shower conversion can often be completed in just a few days. A full-scale bathroom remodel involving extensive plumbing adjustments and custom tile installation will take longer. During your initial consultation, the remodeling team will provide a precise schedule.

What are the main benefits of installing a walk-in shower?

Walk-in showers are highly sought after for their accessibility, eliminating the need to step over a high bathtub wall. This vastly improves safety for homeowners of all ages. Additionally, they create a sleek, modern aesthetic that visually opens up the space and makes the bathroom easier to clean and maintain.

Can my existing tub space be converted into a shower?

Yes, tub-to-shower conversions are one of the most popular services offered. The existing footprint of your standard bathtub is generally the perfect size for a spacious new walk-in shower. The installation team removes the old tub and retrofits the space with custom bases, waterproof wall surrounds, and modern fixtures.

Do you feature complete custom tile installation?

Absolutely. A premium bathroom remodel often hinges on excellent tile work. The featured contractors are skilled in installing a wide variety of tile materials for shower walls, bathroom floors, and backsplashes, ensuring proper waterproofing and beautifully aligned grout lines.

What should I do to prepare my home before the remodel begins?

Prior to the start date, it is recommended that you remove all personal items, toiletries, and fragile decor from the bathroom. You should also clear a pathway from the entrance of your home to the bathroom to allow the installation crew to safely transport materials and tools. The team will handle all the heavy lifting and demolition work.
