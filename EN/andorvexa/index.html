Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about our promotional wedding photography services.

What is included in the typical wedding photography packages?

Standard packages introduced through our platform usually include full-day coverage by a professional wedding photographer, a curated digital gallery of high-resolution images, and basic retouching. Specific inclusions vary based on your final agreement with the studio.

Do you offer engagement photos before the wedding?

Yes, engagement photos are highly encouraged and are often bundled with full photography packages. It gives couples a chance to work with their photographer beforehand and create beautiful images for invitations or displays.

Can we book services for destination wedding photography?

Absolutely. Our partners are experienced in destination wedding photography and are available to travel to your chosen location to capture your special day with the same level of artistic excellence as a local event.

How long does it take to receive our final bridal portraits?

Turnaround times for final, fully edited bridal portraits and wedding galleries generally range from 4 to 8 weeks, depending on the season and the specific details outlined in your service contract with the provider.

Who is the actual wedding photographer for our event?

Andorvexa is a promotional operator connecting you with Fox & Ivory. Your actual wedding photographer will be a highly trained and experienced professional assigned by the original provider, ensuring top-tier quality for your event.
