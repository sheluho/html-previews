About Our Landscaping Mission

Andyeyo is a premier landscaping and lawn care company focused on commercial property management. We understand that your grounds are the vital first impression for your clients, tenants, and visitors.

From routine lawn mowing to complex landscape installation and sophisticated garden design, our dedicated team delivers consistent, high-quality results. We also provide essential tree service to maintain the long-term health and structural integrity of your property's natural assets.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of properties do you service?

We specialize in commercial properties, including corporate campuses, retail centers, industrial parks, and Homeowner Associations (HOAs). Our teams are equipped to handle large-scale grounds management.

Does your lawn care include fertilization?

Yes, our comprehensive lawn care programs go beyond basic lawn mowing. We provide scheduled fertilization, weed control, and soil testing to ensure your turf remains robust and healthy throughout the seasons.

Can you handle large-scale landscape installation?

Absolutely. From initial garden design concepts to full-scale landscape installation, we manage the entire process to upgrade your property's exterior features and seasonal color displays.

Do you offer professional tree service?

Yes, tree service is a critical component of our offering. We provide pruning, structural maintenance, disease management, and emergency response to protect the valuable trees on your commercial landscape.

How often is lawn mowing scheduled?

Lawn mowing schedules are customized based on the season, grass type, and the specific needs of your property. Typically, we operate on weekly or bi-weekly rotations during peak growing seasons to maintain a manicured appearance.
