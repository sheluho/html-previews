Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our custom window treatments.

What types of window treatments do you offer?

We promote a comprehensive range of premium options including authentic plantation shutters, modern roller shades, horizontal and vertical custom blinds, and elegant custom drapes tailored to fit any window size or shape.

Do you provide custom measurements and fitting?

Yes. The original providers we partner with emphasize precise, professional measuring to ensure that every blind, shade, or shutter fits your windows flawlessly, accounting for unique architectural details.

How do I choose between blinds and shades?

The choice often depends on your functional needs and aesthetic preferences. Blinds offer flexible light tilting and a structured look, while shades provide a softer appearance and are excellent for total room darkening or subtle light filtering.

Are plantation shutters energy efficient?

Absolutely. Plantation shutters are known as one of the most energy-efficient window treatments available. They provide an excellent barrier against heat in the summer and cold in the winter, helping regulate your home's internal temperature.

How long does the custom process typically take?

Because every product is custom-built to your exact specifications, timelines vary based on the materials selected. Generally, from initial consultation and measuring to final installation, the process is clearly communicated by the provider to ensure a smooth experience.
