Frequently Asked Questions

Are your custom cabinets really manufactured in America? ▼

Yes, all products highlighted through our platform are proudly built by the American Made Cabinet Company, ensuring high-quality domestic craftsmanship and durable materials.

Do you offer traditional shaker cabinets? ▼

Absolutely. Shaker cabinets remain one of our most popular styles due to their timeless appeal and versatility in both modern and classic kitchen design setups.

Can I visit the kitchen showroom in person? ▼

Yes, visiting the kitchen showroom is highly recommended. It allows you to experience the build quality, test the hardware, and view accurate color swatches before making a final decision.

How long does the kitchen design process take? ▼

The initial design consultation and planning phase typically takes 1 to 2 weeks, depending on the complexity of your custom cabinets and layout requirements.

Do you handle the final installation? ▼

While Anguish Digital focuses on the promotional and design connection phase, the primary manufacturer or their certified local contractors handle the professional installation to ensure warranty compliance.
