Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about professional house cleaning.

What is included in a standard house cleaning?

Standard house cleaning typically encompasses dusting, vacuuming, mopping floors, cleaning bathrooms (sinks, mirrors, toilets, showers), and wiping down kitchen surfaces and appliances. It is designed to maintain a high level of hygiene on a recurring basis.

How does deep cleaning differ from standard service?

Deep cleaning is a much more intensive process. It involves detailed work such as scrubbing grout, cleaning inside appliances, washing baseboards, and reaching high or difficult-to-access areas. It is highly recommended for first-time visits or seasonal spring cleaning.

Do I need to provide the cleaning supplies?

Professional house cleaning providers generally arrive fully equipped with all necessary commercial-grade supplies and equipment. However, if you prefer they use specific products for certain surfaces, you can usually request that in advance.

Can I schedule recurring cleaning services?

Absolutely. Recurring cleaning is one of the most popular options. You can arrange for a maid service to visit weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly depending on your household's activity level and maintenance needs.

What is a move-out cleaning?

Move-out cleaning is designed to return an empty property to a pristine state. It focuses heavily on interior cabinets, appliances, deep carpet vacuuming, and ensuring the entire space meets the strict cleanliness standards often required by landlords or new buyers.
