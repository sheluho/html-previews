Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a turnkey kitchen renovation?

A turnkey kitchen renovation includes absolutely everything required to complete your project. This covers the initial design phase, demolition, plumbing and electrical updates, installation of custom cabinets and countertops, flooring, painting, and the final finishing touches. You simply turn the key and start cooking.

Do you design custom kitchen layouts?

Yes, our design partners specialize in creating completely custom kitchen layouts tailored to your home's unique footprint and your personal cooking style. We optimize for workflow, storage efficiency, and aesthetic impact.

What materials are used for your custom cabinets?

We connect you with providers who utilize premium materials, including solid hardwoods, high-grade plywood boxes, and durable, easy-to-clean veneers or painted finishes. The exact materials are chosen during your design consultation to match your budget and vision.

How long does kitchen remodeling usually take?

Timelines vary based on the scope of work. A full-scale custom kitchen remodeling project typically takes between 6 to 12 weeks from demolition to completion. We focus on strict project management to minimize disruption to your home life.

Can I just upgrade my countertops and doors without a full remodel?

Absolutely. While we specialize in turnkey kitchen renovations, we can also facilitate targeted upgrades such as cabinet refacing, door replacements, and installing new premium countertops to refresh your space efficiently.
