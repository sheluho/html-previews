Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical kitchen remodeling project take? ▼

Timelines vary widely based on the scope of the project. A basic update might take a few weeks, while a full-scale custom kitchen renovation involving structural changes can take several months. The contractors will provide a detailed schedule during your initial consultation.

Can I keep my existing layout and just replace the cabinets? ▼

Absolutely. If your current footprint works well for your lifestyle, a "remove and replace" approach focusing on new cabinets and countertops is a highly efficient way to dramatically update the look and feel of the room without the expense of moving plumbing or walls.

What are the best materials for durable countertops? ▼

Engineered quartz is highly recommended for its non-porous nature, scratch resistance, and low maintenance. Natural granite remains a popular choice for its unique patterns and heat resistance. The design experts can help you select the material that best matches your cooking habits and aesthetic goals.

Do you handle the design phase of a custom kitchen? ▼

As a promotional partner, we connect you with full-service firms that employ experienced designers. These professionals will work closely with you to draft 3D renderings, select materials, and plan a layout that maximizes both form and function before any construction begins.

What is included in a turnkey kitchen renovation? ▼

A turnkey kitchen service means the contractor manages the entire project from start to finish. This typically includes demolition, debris removal, plumbing and electrical rough-ins, drywall, painting, flooring installation, cabinetry fitting, countertop placement, and final appliance hookups.
