Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about starting your custom kitchen remodeling project.

What does a "turnkey kitchen" renovation include?

A turnkey kitchen remodel means the entire process is managed for you from start to finish. This includes the initial 3D design, material selection, obtaining permits, demolition, plumbing, electrical, installing custom cabinets and countertops, and the final cleanup.

How long does a full custom kitchen remodel take?

Timelines vary based on the project's scope, but a complete down-to-the-studs kitchen remodeling project typically takes between 6 to 10 weeks once construction begins. Custom materials, such as specific hardwood cabinets or imported countertops, may require additional lead time.

Can I reface my cabinets instead of replacing them?

Yes. If your existing cabinet boxes are structurally sound and you are happy with your current kitchen layout, cabinet refacing is a great option. It involves replacing just the doors and drawer fronts while applying a matching veneer to the existing boxes, saving both time and budget.

What are the best materials for kitchen countertops?

Quartz and granite remain the top choices for premium countertops. Quartz offers incredible durability, is non-porous (so it resists stains), and requires zero maintenance. Granite provides a unique, natural stone look that handles heat exceptionally well. Both are excellent investments for a custom kitchen.

Do you provide the architectural design services?

The renovation professionals we promote operate as full-service design-build firms. This means they utilize in-house designers to create 3D renderings and floor plans, ensuring the aesthetic vision perfectly aligns with the structural and plumbing realities of your home before any demolition begins.
