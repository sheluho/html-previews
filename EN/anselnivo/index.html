Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about door installation and replacement.

What are the benefits of upgrading to fiberglass doors?

Fiberglass doors are highly regarded for their exceptional durability, energy efficiency, and low maintenance requirements. Unlike wood, they won't warp, rot, or expand due to moisture. They can also seamlessly mimic the rich texture of natural wood while providing superior insulation for your entryway.

How long does a typical front door replacement take?

In most cases, a professional door installation for a single entry door can be completed in just a few hours. The exact timeline depends on whether the existing frame needs to be rebuilt or modified, but the installation crew will ensure your home is secure by the end of the day.

Do you offer services for interior doors installation?

Yes, our promotional partners handle comprehensive interior doors installation. Whether you are replacing a single bedroom door for better soundproofing or upgrading all the doors in your home to a modern style, the professionals we recommend can manage projects of any scale.

Can I keep my existing frame when installing a new door?

If your current door frame is square, plumb, and free of rot or damage, it is often possible to install a new door slab directly into it. However, if the frame is compromised, a pre-hung door replacement is recommended to guarantee an airtight seal and optimal security.

What makes an entry and interior doors installation company reliable?

A reliable company operates with full transparency, provides accurate upfront measurements, and employs experienced installers. We focus on promoting partners who specialize strictly in high-quality entry doors and interior doors, ensuring they possess the specialized skills necessary for a flawless fit and finish.
