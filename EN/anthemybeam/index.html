Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer both buffet catering and plated dinner options?
Yes, we specialize in both. Depending on the formality and flow of your event, you can choose an elegant buffet catering setup for a more relaxed atmosphere, or a sophisticated plated dinner for formal galas and traditional wedding receptions.
How far in advance should we book your event catering service?
For wedding catering, we recommend booking at least 9 to 12 months in advance to secure your date. For corporate catering and private events, a minimum of 4 to 6 weeks is highly advised, though we always try to accommodate tighter timelines when our schedule permits.
Can your corporate catering handle complex dietary restrictions?
Absolutely. Our culinary team is highly experienced in designing menus that accommodate gluten-free, vegan, vegetarian, and allergy-specific requirements without ever compromising on flavor or presentation.
Do you provide staff for the event catering?
Yes, full-service event catering includes our professional, trained staff. From executive chefs and sous chefs to servers and bartenders, we provide the necessary personnel to ensure your event runs flawlessly from setup to teardown.
What is included in your wedding catering packages?
Our wedding catering packages are comprehensive and typically include menu consultation, a private tasting, food preparation, professional service staff, and basic culinary equipment. We can also assist with coordinating rentals for linens, china, and glassware upon request.
