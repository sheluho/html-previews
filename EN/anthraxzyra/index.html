Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if I need a windshield repair or a full replacement? ▼

Generally, if the damage is smaller than a quarter and not directly in the driver's line of sight, a quick chip repair is sufficient. If the crack is larger, spreading quickly, or located at the edge of the glass, a complete windshield replacement is necessary for your safety.

How long does a car window replacement take? ▼

Most car window replacement services take about one to two hours to complete. The technician will remove the broken glass, clean the area, install the new window, and ensure the mechanical components are functioning properly.

Is it safe to drive immediately after an auto glass installation? ▼

For a full windshield replacement, you typically need to wait at least one hour before driving. This allows the high-quality urethane adhesive to cure properly. The technician will advise you on the exact safe drive-away time.

Do you handle auto glass services for all vehicle makes and models? ▼

Yes, the professionals in our network are equipped to handle auto glass and windshield replacement for almost all domestic and foreign vehicles, including cars, trucks, SUVs, and commercial vans.

Will a chip repair make the damage completely disappear? ▼

A windshield repair focuses on restoring the structural integrity of the glass and preventing the crack from spreading. While the visibility of the chip will significantly improve, a slight blemish or outline may still remain visible.
