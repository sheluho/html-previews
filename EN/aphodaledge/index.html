Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our event and wedding videography promotions.

What makes a cinematic wedding video different?

A cinematic wedding video goes beyond traditional point-and-shoot recording. It utilizes creative angles, high-end audio mixing, intentional pacing, and professional color grading to tell the emotional story of your day, much like a feature film.

How far in advance should we book our wedding videographer?

We strongly recommend booking your wedding videographer at least 6 to 12 months in advance, especially if you are planning your wedding during peak seasons (spring and fall). Top-tier videographers fill their schedules quickly.

Do you offer raw footage alongside the edited wedding film?

The availability of raw footage depends on the specific package you select with the original service provider. However, most professional studios do offer the option to purchase unedited footage alongside your polished cinematic wedding video.

Can you handle other types of event videography?

Absolutely. While weddings are a primary focus, the teams we promote are highly skilled in all forms of event videography. This includes capturing rehearsal dinners, engagement parties, corporate galas, and milestone anniversary celebrations.

How long does it take to receive our final wedding video?

Crafting a masterful cinematic wedding video requires meticulous post-production work. Typically, the final editing, audio mixing, and color grading process takes between 8 to 12 weeks following your event date.
