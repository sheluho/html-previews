Frequently Asked Questions

How is this process different from traditional steam cleaning?

Unlike traditional steam cleaning that pumps large volumes of water into your carpets, the carbonated extraction method promoted here uses millions of microscopic bubbles to lift dirt to the surface. This means a deeper clean, much faster drying times (usually 1-2 hours), and a significantly reduced risk of mold and mildew.

Is the upholstery cleaning safe for delicate fabrics?

Yes. Expert technicians are trained to identify specific fabric types and apply the appropriate, gentle, yet effective cleaning solutions. The upholstery cleaning process is designed to be tough on dirt but safe for your valuable furniture pieces.

Can you handle tough pet odors and stain removal?

Absolutely. Specialized pet urine removal treatments and advanced stain removal techniques target the source of the problem. Rather than just masking odors, these treatments break down the compounds causing the smell and discoloration at a molecular level.

Do you offer area rug cleaning?

Yes, comprehensive rug cleaning services are available. Whether you have an everyday synthetic rug or a fine Oriental or Persian heirloom, trained specialists evaluate the dyes and fibers to determine the safest, most effective cleaning method.
