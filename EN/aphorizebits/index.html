Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our cabinetry and design services.

What types of kitchen cabinets do you promote?
We feature a wide range of options through our partners, specializing in fully custom cabinets built to exact specifications, as well as highly sought-after shaker cabinets that offer timeless elegance and robust durability for any home.
Can I view the materials in a kitchen showroom?
Yes. We highly recommend visiting a dedicated kitchen showroom to touch and feel the materials, view the hardware options, and see full-scale displays before making your final decision. Showroom access depends on the specific manufacturing partner's locations.
Do you provide comprehensive kitchen design services?
Absolutely. Great cabinetry requires great planning. Our featured partners provide professional kitchen design consultations to help you optimize your layout, maximize storage efficiency, and select the perfect finishes for your space.
Are shaker cabinets a good choice for a modern home?
Shaker cabinets are incredibly versatile. Their clean, unadorned lines make them a perfect fit for modern, transitional, and traditional interiors alike. By selecting contemporary hardware and modern paint finishes, shaker cabinets easily adapt to the most current design trends.
How long does it take for a manufacturer to complete an order?
Lead times vary significantly based on the level of customization. Semi-custom options may be ready in a few weeks, while fully custom cabinets designed from scratch by a specialized manufacturer can take 6 to 12 weeks to complete and prepare for installation.
