Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical bathroom remodel take?

The timeline depends on the scope of the project. A simple bathtub replacement might take just a few days, while a full bathroom renovation involving custom tile installation and plumbing updates can take several weeks. We provide a clear schedule before any work begins.

Do you handle all the plumbing and electrical work?

Yes. Our turnkey approach means we coordinate all necessary trades, including plumbing and electrical work, to ensure your bathroom remodel is completed safely and up to code without you having to hire multiple contractors.

What materials are best for a walk-in shower?

We recommend durable, non-porous materials like porcelain or ceramic for tile installation, paired with high-quality tempered glass enclosures. These materials are stylish, easy to clean, and highly resistant to moisture.

Can I keep my existing layout during a bathroom renovation?

Absolutely. Keeping the existing layout is often more cost-effective because it minimizes major plumbing moves. We can completely refresh the look and feel of the space with new fixtures, a walk-in shower, and fresh tile while maintaining the original footprint.

How do I prepare my home for a bathtub replacement?

We handle the heavy lifting. All we ask is that you clear personal items from the bathroom and ensure a clear pathway from your entrance to the workspace. We take care of protecting your floors and managing the debris removal.
