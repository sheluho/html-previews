Frequently Asked Questions

Information regarding the window treatments we promote.

What types of window treatments do you feature?

We promote a comprehensive range of solutions including custom blinds (wood, faux wood, aluminum), various shades (roller shades, cellular, Roman), plantation shutters, and custom drapes. The focus is always on tailored, high-quality window coverings.

Are plantation shutters a good investment for my home?

Yes, plantation shutters are often considered a permanent fixture that can add architectural value to your property. They offer excellent light control, insulation, and durability, making them a highly sought-after window treatment.

What is the difference between blinds and shades?

Blinds have hard slats or vanes (like wood or metal) that can be tilted to control light, whereas shades are made of a continuous roll of soft material (like fabric) that is raised or lowered. Both offer unique aesthetic and functional benefits depending on your room's needs.

Do you provide information on motorized options?

Absolutely. Motorized roller shades and automated custom blinds are increasingly popular for convenience, safety (no cords), and smart home integration. We highlight these modern solutions for those looking to upgrade their home technology.
