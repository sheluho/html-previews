Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about planning your next residential move.

What defines a reputable moving company?

A reputable moving company is fully licensed, insured, and has a proven track record of positive customer reviews. They offer transparent pricing without hidden fees and employ trained professionals to handle your belongings with care.

Do you recommend professional packing services?

Absolutely. Professional packing services save you immense time and significantly reduce the risk of damage during transit. Expert movers use high-quality materials and specific techniques to protect fragile and valuable items.

How early should I contact local movers?

It is best to contact local movers at least four to six weeks before your desired move date. During peak seasons, such as summer months or month-end weekends, booking even earlier ensures you secure your preferred schedule.

What is the difference between local and long distance movers?

Local movers typically charge by the hour and operate within a specific metropolitan area or state radius. Long distance movers charge based on the weight of your inventory and the distance traveled, requiring different federal licensing for interstate transport.

How can I prepare for the movers' arrival?

Ensure all pathways are clear of obstacles. If you are not using packing services, have all boxes sealed and labeled. Keep essential documents, valuables, and daily necessities separate so they are not accidentally loaded onto the moving truck.
