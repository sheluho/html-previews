Frequently Asked Questions

Details about working with your wedding photographer.

How far in advance should we book our wedding photographer?

We recommend securing your wedding photography dates at least 9 to 12 months in advance, especially for popular seasons. This ensures availability and provides ample time to schedule your engagement photos.

Are engagement photos included in your packages?

Yes, many of our comprehensive collections include an engagement photo session. We find this is a wonderful way for couples to become comfortable in front of the camera before their big day.

Do you travel for destination wedding photography?

Absolutely. We frequently travel for destination wedding photography. We handle all our own travel arrangements and provide custom quotes based on the location and duration of the festivities.

How long does it take to receive our final gallery?

You can expect a sneak peek of your photos within a few days of the wedding. The fully edited, high-resolution final gallery, including your stunning bridal portraits, is typically delivered within 6 to 8 weeks.

Can we request specific shots, like classic bridal portraits?

Of course. Prior to your wedding, we collaborate with you to build a shot list. This ensures we capture all family combinations, specific details, and the dedicated bridal portraits you envision.
