Frequently Asked Questions

What are the main benefits of energy efficient windows? ▼
Energy efficient windows are designed to prevent the transfer of heat. By utilizing advanced glazing, inert gas fills, and specialized coatings, they keep your heated air inside during winter and block solar heat during summer. This significantly reduces the workload on your HVAC system, leading to lower monthly energy bills and a more consistently comfortable home.
Why should I choose double hung windows? ▼
Double hung windows feature two operable sashes that move vertically. This design allows you to open both the top and bottom portions simultaneously, creating excellent natural ventilation. Hot air can escape through the top while cool air enters through the bottom. Additionally, many models tilt inward, making exterior glass cleaning exceptionally easy from inside the home.
Are vinyl windows better than traditional wood frames? ▼
For many homeowners, vinyl windows offer superior value and convenience. Unlike wood, vinyl does not rot, warp, peel, or require periodic repainting. It offers excellent thermal insulation properties and stands up exceptionally well to harsh weather conditions. While wood offers a specific traditional aesthetic, high-quality vinyl provides a virtually maintenance-free solution that lasts for decades.
How long does a typical window installation take? ▼
The duration of a window installation depends on the number of units being replaced and the condition of the existing frames. However, a professional installation crew can typically replace an entire house of standard windows in one to two days. The installers work methodically to ensure each window is sealed, insulated, and finished before moving to the next, minimizing any disruption to your routine.
When is the right time to invest in replacement windows? ▼
You should consider replacement windows if your current windows are drafty, difficult to open or close, have condensation trapped between the glass panes, or if your energy bills have been steadily increasing. Additionally, if the frames are noticeably rotting or damaged, it is critical to replace them quickly to protect your home's structural integrity from moisture damage.
