Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in your standard wedding videography package? +

Our core packages generally include a dedicated lead wedding videographer, multi-camera coverage of the main events (ceremony, speeches, first dances), professional audio recording, and a fully edited cinematic wedding video. We also offer add-ons like a short highlight reel and raw footage delivery depending on your specific needs.

Do you travel for destination event videography? +

Yes, our team frequently travels to provide event videography for destination weddings and corporate retreats. We love capturing new environments and incorporating the unique landscape of your chosen destination into the final wedding film. Travel fees are calculated based on location and logistics.

How long does it take to receive our cinematic wedding video? +

Editing a true cinematic wedding video requires meticulous attention to detail, color grading, and sound design. Typically, you can expect to receive your final films within 10 to 14 weeks following your event date. We prioritize quality over speed to ensure your film is a timeless heirloom.

Do you use drones for capturing the wedding film? +

Yes, aerial footage is often a stunning addition to establish the venue and scale of your event. Drone operation is heavily dependent on local aviation laws, venue restrictions, and weather conditions on the day of the event. If conditions permit, we love incorporating aerials into your wedding film.

What is your approach on the day of the event? +

As your wedding videographer, our goal is to be as unobtrusive as possible while capturing genuine emotion. We lean heavily into a documentary style for the majority of the day, allowing moments to unfold naturally without heavy direction. During portrait sessions, we may offer light, artistic prompts to capture the best light and movement.
