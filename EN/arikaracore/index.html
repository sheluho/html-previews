Frequently Asked Questions

What does a turnkey kitchen remodeling project include?

Our turnkey kitchen remodeling covers every phase of your renovation. This includes the initial 3D design and layout planning, demolition, plumbing, electrical work, installation of custom kitchen cabinets and premium countertops, and all final finishing touches.

Can I choose the materials for my custom kitchen cabinets?

Absolutely. We offer a wide variety of high-quality materials, finishes, and hardware for your custom cabinets. During the design consultation, we will help you select options that align with your aesthetic goals and budget.

How long does a standard kitchen remodeling take?

Timelines vary based on the scope of the project, but a full-service renovation typically takes between 4 to 8 weeks from demolition to final walk-through. We provide a detailed project schedule before any work begins.

Do you offer different types of countertops?

Yes, our premium countertops selection includes top-tier materials such as quartz, granite, marble, and durable solid surfaces. We source only the best materials to ensure longevity and beauty in your new kitchen.

Will I be able to see a design before the kitchen renovation begins?

Yes! As part of our comprehensive service, we create a detailed 3D rendering of your new kitchen layout. This allows you to visualize the custom cabinets, countertops, and overall flow before we start the physical renovation.
