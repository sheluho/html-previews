Common Inquiries

Do the providers offer tasting sessions for wedding catering?
Yes, the original catering providers typically offer private tasting sessions for booked couples. This allows you to sample menu options, refine the flavor profiles, and finalize the presentation for your wedding catering before the big day.
Can dietary restrictions be accommodated for a plated dinner?
Absolutely. The culinary teams are highly experienced in designing custom menus that respect vegan, gluten-free, dairy-free, and other specific dietary needs without compromising on taste or elegance during a plated dinner service.
What is generally included in corporate catering packages?
Corporate catering packages are designed for seamless execution. Depending on the provider, they usually include menu planning, food preparation, delivery, professional buffet catering setup, necessary serving equipment, and breakdown after the event concludes.
Is service staff provided for large-scale event catering?
Yes, professional service is a hallmark of premium event catering. You can request trained servers, skilled bartenders, and an on-site event captain to ensure food and beverages flow smoothly throughout your celebration.
How far in advance should we book our event catering?
For popular dates, especially during the summer and early fall, it is highly recommended to secure your wedding or event catering 9 to 12 months in advance. Corporate catering for smaller meetings can often be arranged with a few weeks' notice.
