Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our car detailing solutions.

What exactly is a ceramic coating?

A ceramic coating is a liquid polymer that chemically bonds with your vehicle's factory paint, creating a layer of durable paint protection. It prevents dirt, grime, and stain marks from bonding to the clear coat, while offering a deep, hydrophobic gloss that makes maintenance washing incredibly easy.

How often should I get my car detailed?

For optimal appearance and protection, we recommend a full auto detailing service 2-3 times a year. However, if your vehicle has a ceramic coating applied, simple maintenance washes every few weeks are usually sufficient to keep it looking pristine year-round.

Does interior detailing remove deep stains and odors?

Yes. Our comprehensive interior detailing process includes deep hot-water extraction and specialized enzyme treatments to lift heavy stains from upholstery and neutralize lingering odors, restoring a fresh environment to your cabin.

What is paint correction?

Paint correction is the mechanical process of removing surface imperfections like swirl marks, fine scratches, and oxidation from your clear coat using machine polishers. It is a vital step before applying ceramic coating to ensure the surface is flawless before it gets sealed.

How long does a full detail take?

The duration depends on the specific services requested. A standard interior and exterior detail usually takes 3 to 5 hours. Advanced services involving multi-stage paint correction and ceramic coating application can take anywhere from a full day to two days to allow for proper curing.
