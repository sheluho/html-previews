Frequently Asked Questions

Information about our custom furniture process.

What is the typical lead time for a custom dining table?

Lead times vary depending on the complexity of the piece and current workshop schedule. Generally, a bespoke dining table takes between 8 to 12 weeks from the finalization of the design and material selection to completion.

Can I choose the specific wood for my handmade furniture?

Absolutely. Material selection is a crucial part of the bespoke furniture process. We offer a variety of premium hardwoods, including walnut, oak, maple, and cherry, and can source specialty woods upon request.

Do you build custom sofas to specific dimensions?

Yes, building to exact dimensions is the primary benefit of a custom sofa. We can adjust the length, depth, back height, and arm style to perfectly fit your room's layout and your ergonomic preferences.

How does the design process work?

The process begins with an initial consultation to discuss your needs, style, and space. We then provide sketches or 3D renderings and material samples. Once the design and quote are approved, fabrication begins in the workshop.

Do you deliver and install the furniture?

Delivery and installation services are typically arranged through the original provider, Studio 4 RI. They ensure that your custom pieces are transported safely and set up correctly in your home or showroom.
