Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about our recommended kitchen remodeling services.

What is a turnkey kitchen renovation?

A turnkey kitchen renovation means that every aspect of the project is managed by a single team. This includes demolition, electrical and plumbing updates, installing custom cabinets, laying countertops, and final finishing touches, allowing you to simply "turn the key" and enjoy your new space without managing multiple contractors.

Do they build and install custom cabinets?

Yes, the remodeling experts we promote specialize in designing and installing custom cabinets. These are tailored to fit your kitchen's unique dimensions and your specific storage requirements, utilizing high-quality materials.

What types of countertops are available?

A wide variety of premium countertops are offered, including highly durable quartz, classic granite, and other solid surface materials. The design team assists in selecting the best option to match your custom kitchen aesthetic and lifestyle needs.

How long does a kitchen remodeling project typically take?

Timelines vary based on the scope of work. A minor cosmetic update might take a few weeks, whereas a full turnkey kitchen overhaul involving structural changes, new flooring, and custom cabinetry can take several weeks to a few months. A detailed schedule is provided during the consultation phase.

Will I have a dedicated point of contact?

Absolutely. Full-service remodeling relies on clear communication. You will typically work with a dedicated project manager who oversees the entire renovation process, ensuring that your custom kitchen is completed to your satisfaction and keeping you informed at every step.
