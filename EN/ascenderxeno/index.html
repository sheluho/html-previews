Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in your standard lawn care program?

Our standard program typically includes regular lawn mowing, precise edging, seasonal fertilization, and weed control treatments to ensure a consistently healthy and vibrant yard year-round.

Do you offer custom garden design?

Yes, custom garden design is a specialty. Professionals evaluate your property's specific soil conditions, sunlight patterns, and your personal aesthetic preferences to create a tailored outdoor environment.

Is your tree service safe for large properties?

Absolutely. The tree service teams utilize professional-grade equipment and adhere to strict safety protocols for both routine trimming and complex removals, ensuring zero damage to your surrounding property.

How long does a typical landscape installation take?

Timelines vary widely based on the project's scale and complexity. However, most standard residential landscape installations are efficiently completed within one to two weeks from the start date.

When is the best time to schedule major landscaping work?

Spring and early fall are generally considered the ideal seasons for planting and major landscaping projects due to moderate temperatures, while standard lawn care and mowing run consistently throughout the active growing season.
