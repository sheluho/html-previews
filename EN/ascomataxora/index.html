Frequently Asked Questions

What types of events do you cater?

We promote comprehensive event catering services that cover a wide range of gatherings. This includes luxurious wedding catering, professional corporate catering for meetings and galas, as well as private parties and milestone celebrations.

Do you offer both plated dinner and buffet catering options?

Yes, the providers we feature specialize in diverse service styles. You can choose an elegant, multi-course plated dinner for a formal atmosphere or opt for a bountiful buffet catering setup that encourages guest interaction and variety.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions and custom menus?

Absolutely. Exceptional wedding catering relies on ensuring every guest is satisfied. Custom menus can be crafted to accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other specific dietary requirements without compromising on taste or presentation.

When should we book our event catering service?

For large-scale events, particularly wedding catering, it is highly recommended to secure your date at least 6 to 12 months in advance. For smaller corporate catering or private parties, a few months' notice is typically sufficient, subject to availability.

Do the catering packages include professional staff?

Yes. A premium event catering service includes highly trained chefs, professional servers, and attentive waitstaff to ensure your plated dinner or buffet catering runs smoothly, allowing you to relax and enjoy your event.
