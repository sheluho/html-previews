Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I schedule professional carpet cleaning? +

For most households, professional carpet cleaning is recommended every 12 to 18 months. However, homes with indoor pets, children, or high foot traffic may benefit from steam cleaning every 6 to 9 months to maintain a healthy environment.

Is your upholstery cleaning safe for all fabric types? +

Yes, the upholstery cleaning techniques utilized by our partner professionals are tailored to the specific fabric of your furniture. They assess the material first to ensure that the cleaning agents and methods used will not cause shrinkage, color bleeding, or damage.

Does steam cleaning guarantee complete stain removal? +

While steam cleaning is highly effective for deep soil extraction and general stain removal, certain permanent dyes or chemical stains may alter the carpet fibers permanently. The technicians will evaluate the spots and use the most advanced targeted solutions available to minimize or completely remove them.

Can you perform rug cleaning in my home, or does it need to be taken away? +

Basic rug cleaning can often be performed on-site using specialized protective barriers to keep your underlying floors safe. For delicate oriental or heavily soiled area rugs, off-site cleaning might be recommended to allow for full immersion and controlled drying.

How long does it take for carpets to dry after steam cleaning? +

Typically, carpets take between 4 to 8 hours to dry completely after a professional steam cleaning session. Variables such as indoor humidity, airflow, and the thickness of the carpet fibers can affect drying times. Keeping windows open or running fans can accelerate the process.
