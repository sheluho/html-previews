Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries regarding premium car care.

What is the difference between an express wash and a hand car wash?

An express wash is designed for speed and efficiency, perfect for keeping your car clean on a tight schedule. A hand car wash is a meticulous, manual process that safely removes dirt using premium soaps and plush microfiber towels, reducing the risk of micro-scratches on your clear coat.

How does the auto wash membership work?

The auto wash membership is a convenient subscription plan provided by the facility. For a flat monthly fee, members gain access to regular express washes, ensuring their vehicle remains in pristine condition all month long without the hassle of individual payments.

What does full interior auto detailing include?

Full interior auto detailing goes far beyond a simple vacuum. It includes deep cleaning of upholstery, leather conditioning, stain extraction from carpets, careful sanitization of all hard surfaces, and window polishing to restore the cabin to a factory-fresh feel.

Is a premium car wash safe for ceramic-coated vehicles?

Yes, a premium hand car wash is highly recommended for ceramic-coated cars. The specialized pH-neutral soaps and gentle washing techniques preserve the integrity of the coating, preventing degradation while maximizing its hydrophobic properties.

Do I need to book an appointment for auto detailing?

While standard washes can often be accommodated quickly, comprehensive auto detailing requires dedicated time and specific equipment. We strongly recommend scheduling an appointment in advance to ensure the detailing team can allocate the proper time required for perfection.
