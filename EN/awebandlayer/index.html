Frequently Asked Questions

Answers about our professional house cleaning connections.

What is included in a standard house cleaning?
A standard house cleaning typically covers essential upkeep tasks. This includes dusting surfaces, vacuuming carpets, mopping hard floors, and thoroughly cleaning the bathrooms and kitchen surfaces. It is designed to maintain a high level of cleanliness on a regular basis.
How does deep cleaning differ from a standard cleaning?
Deep cleaning is a much more intensive process. It addresses areas that are not usually covered in a standard session, such as wiping down baseboards, cleaning inside window sills, scrubbing grout, and removing heavy buildup from kitchen appliances. It is recommended for homes that haven't been professionally cleaned in several months.
Do I need to provide cleaning supplies for the maid service?
In most cases, no. Professional house cleaning teams arrive fully equipped with their own commercial-grade vacuums, mops, and specialized cleaning solutions. If you have specific, preferred products you'd like them to use, you can usually arrange that ahead of time.
Can I customize a recurring cleaning schedule?
Absolutely. Recurring cleaning schedules are highly flexible. You can choose weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly visits depending on your household's activity level and budget. The maid service will work with you to establish a cadence that keeps your home consistently beautiful.
Is move-out cleaning guaranteed to satisfy my landlord?
While we act as a promotional operator and do not directly provide guarantees, the professional services we introduce specialize in thorough move-out cleanings designed to meet stringent property management standards. They focus heavily on the details landlords look for, such as immaculate appliances and scrubbed bathrooms.
