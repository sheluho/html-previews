Frequently Asked Questions

What does commercial lawn care typically include? +

Standard commercial lawn care programs usually encompass regular lawn mowing, edging, weed control, turf fertilization, and debris removal to ensure your property maintains a neat, professional appearance throughout the growing season.

Do the providers offer custom garden design for corporate parks? +

Yes, many of the landscaping professionals we feature offer comprehensive garden design services. They can assess your campus environment and recommend plant varieties, hardscaping, and layouts that thrive in your specific climate and soil conditions.

How often should we schedule lawn mowing services? +

For most commercial properties, weekly lawn mowing is recommended during the peak growing season to maintain an optimal turf height and a crisp look. The frequency may be adjusted during slower growth periods or droughts.

Is landscape installation disruptive to daily business operations? +

Professional teams are accustomed to working around active business environments. They typically phase landscape installation projects and maintain clean work sites to ensure minimal disruption to your employees, tenants, and customers.

When is the best time to schedule professional tree service? +

While emergency tree service can be needed at any time, routine pruning and maintenance are often best performed during the dormant season (late winter or early spring). This promotes robust spring growth and allows arborists to better see the tree's structure.
