Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should we book our wedding photographer?

It is highly recommended to secure your date at least 9 to 12 months in advance, especially for popular seasons. Booking early ensures that the preferred team from Said Yes Studios is available to dedicate their time entirely to your celebration.

Do you offer destination wedding photography?

Yes, destination wedding photography is a core specialty. The team is equipped to travel worldwide to document your day in any spectacular setting. Custom travel collections are developed based on the location and multi-day coverage requirements.

Can we include engagement photos in our package?

Absolutely. In fact, adding an engagement photos session is strongly encouraged. It builds a comfortable rapport between you and the photographer, resulting in more relaxed, authentic images on the actual wedding day.

What is the process for scheduling bridal portraits?

Bridal portraits are typically scheduled 1 to 2 months prior to the wedding day. This allows ample time for hair and makeup trials and ensures that your dress fits perfectly, resulting in stunning, editorial-quality portraits without the time constraints of the wedding day.

How long does it take to receive our final gallery?

The meticulous editing process is crucial to delivering the high standard of wedding photography you expect. Typically, a curated sneak peek is provided within a few days, while the comprehensive, fully edited digital gallery is delivered within 6 to 8 weeks following the event.
