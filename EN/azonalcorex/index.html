Frequently Asked Questions

What does a full bathroom remodel entail?

A full bathroom renovation includes the complete transformation of your space. This often involves demolition, plumbing updates, electrical work, drywall, painting, and the installation of new fixtures, vanities, and flooring.

How long does a walk-in shower conversion take?

Most walk-in shower conversions can be completed in just a few days, depending on the extent of the plumbing adjustments required and the specific materials chosen for the enclosure and base.

Do you handle bathtub replacement and disposal?

Yes, when performing a bathtub replacement, the service includes the safe removal and responsible disposal of your old unit, ensuring a clean slate for your new installation.

What materials are best for tile installation?

For bathroom environments, we highly recommend porcelain or ceramic for tile installation due to their durability and water resistance. Natural stone is also a premium option when properly sealed.

Will a bathroom renovation increase my home's value?

Absolutely. Upgrading your bathroom is widely considered one of the top return-on-investment home improvements, making your property more attractive and functional.
