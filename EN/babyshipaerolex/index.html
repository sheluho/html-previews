Frequently Asked Questions

Details about our custom furniture process and capabilities.

How long does it take to build a custom dining table?
The timeline for a bespoke custom dining table typically ranges from 8 to 12 weeks, depending on the complexity of the design and the availability of specific rare woods. We prioritize quality over speed, ensuring the wood is properly acclimated and finishes are fully cured before delivery.
Can I customize the dimensions of a custom sofa?
Absolutely. Because we are a true custom furniture maker, every piece is built to order. We can adjust the length, depth, back height, and even the firmness of the cushions to perfectly suit your body and the exact dimensions of your room.
What materials do you use for your handmade furniture?
We specialize in domestic hardwoods such as Walnut, White Oak, Cherry, and Maple for our wooden pieces. For seating and upholstery, we offer a curated selection of premium performance fabrics, top-grain leathers, and natural linens sourced from respected mills.
Do you have a showroom where I can see the bespoke furniture?
Yes, our showroom is open for consultations. We highly recommend scheduling an appointment so we can dedicate time to discuss your vision, review wood samples, and allow you to test the comfort of our floor models.
Do you offer delivery for custom pieces?
Yes, we arrange specialized "white glove" delivery services for all our custom furniture. This ensures your investment is transported safely, brought into your home, and fully assembled in the room of your choice without you having to lift a finger.
