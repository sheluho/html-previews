Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our recommended lawn care services.

What is included in a standard lawn mowing service?
Standard lawn care typically includes complete mowing of the property, precision string trimming around obstacles and fences, edging along sidewalks and driveways, and blowing clippings off all hard surfaces.
Do you offer custom garden design and landscape installation?
Yes, the professionals at Augusta Lawn Care provide comprehensive garden design and landscape installation. They can help you select the right plants, install new garden beds, and create a beautiful, cohesive outdoor aesthetic.
How often should mulch be replaced in my garden beds?
It is generally recommended to have fresh mulch installed once a year, typically in the spring. This helps retain soil moisture, suppress weeds, and gives your landscaping a clean, vibrant look for the growing season.
What does tree service and pruning entail?
Tree service involves trimming overgrown branches, shaping shrubs for optimal growth, and removing dead or hazardous limbs. Proper pruning ensures the long-term health of your plants and keeps your property safe.
Are property cleanups available year-round?
While spring and fall are the most common times for major property cleanups to remove leaves and seasonal debris, cleanup services can be scheduled whenever your landscape requires a thorough clearing to maintain its appearance.
