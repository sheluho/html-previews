Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about preparing for your session.

Do you offer destination wedding photography?

Yes, we specialize in destination wedding photography and are available to travel worldwide. We work closely with you to manage travel logistics and ensure your international celebration is captured flawlessly.

What is included in your standard wedding photography package?

Our standard packages typically include full-day coverage by a lead wedding photographer, a curated online gallery of high-resolution images, and full printing rights. Custom additions like bespoke albums are also available.

How do we schedule our engagement photos?

We recommend scheduling your engagement photos 6 to 9 months before your wedding date. This allows ample time for you to use the images for save-the-dates and helps us build a comfortable rapport behind the lens.

What style do you use for bridal portraits?

We employ an editorial, fine-art approach for bridal portraits. This means we focus on dramatic lighting, elegant posing, and capturing the intricate details of your attire in a way that feels natural yet visually striking.

How long after the wedding will we receive our photos?

You will receive a sneak peek gallery within the first week of your event. The complete, carefully edited digital collection will be delivered within 6 to 8 weeks following your wedding day.
