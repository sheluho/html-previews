Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our wedding planning services.

What does a full-service wedding planner do?

A full-service wedding planner handles everything from conceptual design and budget management to venue sourcing, vendor negotiations, and timeline creation. We act as your primary point of contact throughout the entire planning process, ensuring every detail aligns with your vision.

Do I really need a day of coordinator if my venue provides one?

Yes. A venue coordinator works for the venue to ensure food is served and facilities are managed. An independent day of coordinator works exclusively for you. We manage your specific timeline, coordinate external vendors (like florists and photographers), and handle personal details to protect your interests.

Can you help plan a destination wedding?

Absolutely. Our destination wedding services are tailored to manage the unique logistical challenges of planning an event afar. We handle remote venue scouting, local vendor coordination, travel logistics advice, and multi-day event scheduling for your guests.

When should we hire a wedding coordinator?

For full-service wedding planning, we recommend reaching out 12 to 18 months in advance. If you are only looking for a day of coordinator, contacting us 6 to 8 months prior is ideal to secure your date and allow us time to review your established plans.

Are your services customizable?

Yes. While we offer structured packages for full-service planning and day-of coordination, we understand every couple is unique. We gladly customize our proposals based on your specific needs, guest count, location, and the level of support you desire.
