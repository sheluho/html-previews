Frequently Asked Questions

What are the main benefits of vinyl windows?

Vinyl windows are incredibly popular because they offer excellent insulation, are highly durable, and require virtually no maintenance. Unlike wood, they won't warp, peel, or rot, making them a cost-effective and long-lasting choice for replacement windows.

How long does professional window installation typically take?

The timeframe for window installation depends on the number of windows and the specific structural requirements of your home. However, an experienced professional crew can typically complete a standard whole-house installation within one to two days, ensuring minimal disruption to your daily life.

Are double hung windows hard to maintain?

Not at all. Modern double hung windows are designed specifically for easy maintenance. Both the top and bottom sashes can be tilted inward, allowing you to clean the exterior glass safely and easily from inside your home.

Do energy efficient windows really save money?

Yes, upgrading to energy efficient windows can noticeably reduce your monthly utility bills. By minimizing heat transfer—keeping warm air inside during winter and hot air outside during summer—your HVAC system doesn't have to work as hard, leading to long-term financial savings.

When is the best time to invest in replacement windows?

If you notice drafts, condensation between glass panes, difficulty opening or closing sashes, or rising energy costs, it is time to consider replacement windows. While spring and fall are popular for installation, modern weatherproofing techniques allow professionals to install windows year-round.
