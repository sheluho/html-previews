Frequently Asked Questions

Find out more about upgrading your home's windows.

What are the main benefits of energy efficient windows? ▼

Energy efficient windows are designed to prevent heated or cooled air from escaping your home. By utilizing advanced glazing, inert gas fills, and superior framing materials, they dramatically reduce the workload on your HVAC system, leading to a more comfortable indoor climate and noticeably lower monthly energy bills.

How do I know if I need replacement windows? ▼

Common signs that you need replacement windows include feeling drafts near closed windows, noticing condensation trapped between glass panes, experiencing difficulty opening or closing the sashes, or seeing visible decay on the frames. Upgrading not only solves these issues but enhances the overall value and security of your property.

Are vinyl windows a durable choice? ▼

Yes, modern vinyl windows are highly durable. They resist moisture, do not require painting, and are immune to rotting or peeling. Because they are practically maintenance-free and offer excellent thermal insulation, they are one of the most popular materials chosen for window replacement projects today.

What makes double hung windows so popular? ▼

Double hung windows feature two operating sashes that move up and down, allowing for excellent airflow control. Because both sashes can tilt inward, cleaning the exterior glass from inside your home is incredibly easy. Their traditional aesthetic seamlessly fits almost any architectural style.

How long does the window installation process take? ▼

The duration of the window installation process depends on the number of windows being replaced and the condition of the existing frames. However, professional installation teams are highly efficient and can often complete the replacement of an average-sized home's windows within one to two days, minimizing disruption to your daily routine.
