Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should we book a wedding photographer?

It is highly recommended to secure your wedding photographer at least 9 to 12 months in advance, especially if you are planning a celebration during peak wedding season. Popular dates book quickly, and securing your date early ensures you have the artist of your choice for your special day.

Do you feature artists available for destination wedding photography?

Yes, JPP Studios is available for destination wedding photography worldwide. Whether you are exchanging vows on a tropical beach or in a historic European estate, they are equipped to travel. Custom travel quotes are provided based on the specific location and logistical requirements of your celebration.

Are engagement photos included in standard collections?

Many curated collections do include a session for engagement photos. This is because these sessions are incredibly valuable; they serve as an icebreaker between you and the camera. It helps build a relationship with the photographer, ensuring everyone is comfortable and natural when the wedding day arrives.

What is the benefit of scheduling bridal portraits?

Bridal portraits offer a stress-free environment to capture the full elegance of your bridal look. On the actual wedding day, timelines can be tight. A dedicated session beforehand allows for creative freedom, perfect location choices, and acts as a fantastic trial run for your hair and makeup styling.

When will we receive our final image gallery?

The meticulous editing process is vital to fine art wedding photography. You can typically expect your fully edited, high-resolution digital gallery to be delivered within 6 to 8 weeks following your wedding date. A small preview gallery is often provided shortly after the event to share with family and friends.
