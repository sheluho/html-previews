Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about preparing for your wedding photography.

What is included in your wedding photography packages?

Most standard packages promoted through our platform include full-day coverage by a primary wedding photographer, a digital gallery of high-resolution edited images, and printing rights. Custom additions like albums and second shooters are often available upon request.

Do you offer engagement photos as part of the service?

Yes! Engagement photos are highly recommended and are often bundled into comprehensive wedding packages. It is the perfect opportunity to get comfortable in front of the camera and establish a rapport with your photographer before the big day.

Can we book through you for destination wedding photography?

Absolutely. Destination wedding photography is a specialty. When you inquire, please provide your venue location and dates so custom travel arrangements and specialized coverage packages can be outlined by the provider.

When is the best time to schedule bridal portraits?

We recommend scheduling your bridal portraits 1 to 2 months before the wedding. This allows ample time to coordinate with your hair and makeup trials, ensures your dress fits perfectly, and provides enough buffer to have portraits printed for the reception.

How do we secure our wedding photographer?

To secure your date, fill out the inquiry form below. You will be connected with the studio to discuss availability and details. A signed contract and a retainer fee processed directly by the photography studio will officially reserve your date.
