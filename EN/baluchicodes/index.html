Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between a hand car wash and an automated wash?

A hand car wash involves trained professionals manually cleaning your vehicle using soft mitts and specialized products. This prevents the micro-scratches and swirl marks often caused by the stiff brushes and harsh chemicals used in automated tunnel washes, resulting in a safer, superior finish.

How does the auto wash membership work?

The membership provides you with unlimited access to standard or premium car wash services for a flat monthly fee. Simply drive up, present your membership credentials, and enjoy a spotless car whenever you need it, without worrying about per-wash costs.

How long does an express wash take?

An express wash is designed for speed and efficiency, typically taking only 15 to 20 minutes from start to finish. It is the perfect solution for maintaining your vehicle's appearance when you are on a tight schedule.

Do I need to book an appointment for a premium car wash?

While drive-ups are always welcome for standard and express services, we highly recommend scheduling an appointment for full premium detailing. This ensures dedicated time is blocked off to give your vehicle the meticulous attention it requires.

Are the cleaning products safe for custom paint jobs?

Absolutely. The team uses only high-end, pH-balanced soaps and waxes that are completely safe for all clear coats, custom paint jobs, and vinyl wraps. The hand-wash method ensures these products are applied gently and effectively.
