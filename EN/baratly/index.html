Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between custom blinds and shades?

Custom blinds are typically hard window treatments made from materials like wood, faux wood, or metal, featuring slats or louvers that can be tilted to control light. Shades, such as roller shades or Roman shades, are made from continuous rolls of soft fabric that are raised or lowered to adjust light and privacy.

Are plantation shutters a good investment for Los Angeles homes?

Yes. Plantation shutters are considered a permanent fixture and can actually increase the value of your home. They are highly durable, provide excellent insulation against heat, and offer superior light control, making them ideal for the sunny Southern California climate.

Can I combine custom drapes with other window treatments?

Absolutely. Layering window treatments is a popular design choice. You can easily pair custom drapes over custom blinds or roller shades. This approach not only adds depth and texture to your room's decor but also gives you maximum flexibility over light filtering and privacy throughout the day.

How do I know which window treatments are right for my space?

Choosing the right product depends on your primary goals: light control, privacy, insulation, or aesthetics. Our platform connects you with experts who provide professional consultations to assess your specific windows, discuss your design preferences, and recommend the ideal solutions for your home.

Do the providers offer professional measurement and installation?

Yes. Precise measurement is crucial for custom window treatments to function correctly and look their best. The providers we feature across Los Angeles handle everything from exact initial measurements to professional, flawless installation of your new blinds, shades, or shutters.
