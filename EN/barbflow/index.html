Frequently Asked Questions

Details about our wedding and event videography process.

What is your approach as a wedding videographer?

Our approach is fundamentally cinematic and narrative-driven. We act as quiet observers for the majority of the day, capturing authentic moments as they unfold naturally. During portrait sessions, we provide gentle direction to ensure you look your best while still feeling like yourselves. The final product is a cinematic wedding video that feels like a high-end movie of your life.

How long does it take to receive our wedding film?

Crafting a masterpiece takes time. Our editing process is meticulous, involving careful color grading, audio mastering, and narrative structuring. Typically, you can expect to receive your final cinematic wedding films within 12 to 16 weeks following your event. We prioritize quality over speed to ensure your legacy is perfect.

Do you travel for destination weddings?

Yes, absolutely. While we are based locally, our event videography team frequently travels worldwide to document love stories. Travel fees are calculated based on the destination and are transparently included in a custom proposal during the inquiry process.

Do you offer raw footage alongside the edited film?

We believe our artistry lies in the edit—curating the best moments to tell a cohesive story. Therefore, we do not typically provide completely unedited raw files. However, we do offer "Documentary Edits" of full ceremonies and speeches as add-ons, ensuring you have complete records of the most important spoken moments.

How do we book you for our event videography?

The first step is to fill out the inquiry form below to check our availability for your date. Once we confirm availability, we will schedule a consultation to discuss your vision, explain our collections, and determine if we are the perfect fit to be your wedding videographer. A signed contract and retainer secure your date.
