Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about preparing for your wedding photography sessions.

What is generally included in a wedding photography package?
Most wedding photography packages offered by the studios we promote include full-day coverage, a secondary photographer, a digital gallery of high-resolution edited images, and personal printing rights. You can inquire directly about adding special features like physical albums or prints.
Do you provide dedicated engagement photos sessions?
Yes, engagement photos are highly recommended and are often bundled with main event services. They give you a chance to build rapport with your wedding photographer and result in beautiful, relaxed images for your save-the-dates or wedding website.
Are bridal portraits done on the wedding day or beforehand?
Bridal portraits can be captured during the designated portrait hour on your actual wedding day, or they can be scheduled as a separate, relaxed session a few weeks beforehand depending on your preference and schedule.
How does destination wedding photography work?
For destination events, the provider typically creates a custom quote that incorporates travel, accommodation, and multi-day coverage (like welcome dinners or day-after brunches). The focus remains on delivering a cohesive visual story of your destination celebration.
When should we book our wedding photography studio?
It is heavily advised to book your wedding photographer at least 9 to 12 months in advance. Popular dates during peak wedding seasons book up rapidly, and securing your preferred studio early ensures peace of mind.
