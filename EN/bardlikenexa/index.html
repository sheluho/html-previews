Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is bespoke furniture?

Bespoke furniture is created from scratch to meet your exact specifications. Unlike mass-produced items, every element—from the wood species and dimensions to the finish and upholstery—is selected by you and handcrafted by our artisans.

How long does it take to build a custom dining table?

Lead times vary depending on the complexity of the design and the availability of specific materials. Typically, a custom dining table takes between 8 to 12 weeks from the final design approval to delivery.

Can I provide my own fabric for a custom sofa?

Yes. We welcome COM (Customer's Own Material) for upholstery projects. Our team will verify that your chosen fabric is suitable for the structural demands of the custom sofa you have designed.

Do you have a showroom where I can see the handmade furniture?

Yes, the original custom furniture maker operates a physical showroom in Los Angeles, CA. There, you can examine the craftsmanship, feel the finishes, and discuss your project in person with a lead designer.

What kind of wood do you use for your custom furniture?

We source a variety of premium, sustainable hardwoods including walnut, white oak, cherry, and maple. The choice of wood is entirely up to you and the specific aesthetic and durability requirements of your piece.
