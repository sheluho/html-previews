Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries regarding the wedding photography services featured by Bargir Vector.

What is included in a standard wedding photography package?

Most standard packages offered by the studio include comprehensive day-of coverage, a dedicated lead wedding photographer, high-resolution digital files, and a private online gallery for viewing and sharing your images with family and friends.

Do you accommodate destination wedding photography?

Yes, the featured professionals frequently travel for destination wedding photography. Custom packages are available to cover travel logistics, multi-day events, and specialized location scouting to ensure your destination celebration is captured flawlessly.

How do we schedule engagement photos?

Once you connect with the studio through our inquiry form, you can discuss adding engagement photos to your package or booking them separately. Sessions are typically scheduled several months before the wedding to allow time for processing and printing save-the-dates.

When will we receive our final wedding gallery?

Post-production is handled with meticulous care. While timelines can vary based on the season, most couples receive their fully edited, high-resolution digital galleries within 6 to 8 weeks following their wedding day.

Can we book bridal portraits separately from wedding coverage?

Absolutely. Standalone bridal portraits are highly recommended for brides who want a dedicated session without the time constraints of the actual wedding day. These can be booked individually or bundled into a comprehensive photography package.
