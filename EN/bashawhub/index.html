Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our premium car wash services.

What is included in a premium hand car wash?
Our premium hand car wash includes a meticulous exterior hand wash using scratch-free mitts and pH-neutral foam. We also clean the wheel faces and barrels, dress the tires, clean exterior glass, and perform a gentle hand dry to prevent water spots.
Do you offer auto wash memberships?
Yes, we feature comprehensive auto wash memberships designed for regular maintenance. Members enjoy priority booking, discounted rates on detailing upgrades, and unlimited or structured monthly wash plans to keep their vehicle looking perfect all year round.
Is hand washing safer for my car's paint?
Absolutely. Automated drive-through washes often use harsh abrasive brushes and recycled water that can leave micro-scratches and swirl marks on your clear coat. A professional hand car wash uses fresh materials and precise techniques to safely lift dirt away from the paint.
What interior services do you provide?
Our interior detailing goes far beyond a basic vacuum. We offer deep upholstery extraction, leather cleaning and conditioning, dashboard and console detailing, streak-free interior glass cleaning, and specialized stain and odor removal.
How long does an express wash take?
An express wash typically takes between 15 to 30 minutes, depending on the vehicle size and specific package chosen. It is designed to quickly refresh your car's exterior while still providing a much higher standard of care than automated options.
