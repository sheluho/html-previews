Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I schedule lawn mowing services?

For optimal health and appearance, weekly lawn mowing is generally recommended during the active growing season. This ensures that no more than one-third of the grass blade is cut at a time, which prevents stress and promotes a thicker, greener turf.

What does landscape installation entail?

Landscape installation covers the physical process of bringing a garden design to life. This includes soil preparation, planting trees, shrubs, and flowers, laying fresh sod, and installing mulch or decorative rock. It is a comprehensive service that completely transforms your outdoor space.

Do you offer specialized tree service?

Yes, Custom Lawn provides professional tree service. This encompasses structural pruning, disease management, and the safe removal of dead or hazardous trees. Proper tree care is vital for the safety of your property and the overall health of your landscaping.

When is the best time for garden design planning?

The ideal time to begin garden design planning is during the late winter or early spring. This allows ample time to finalize layouts, select the appropriate plant species, and schedule the landscape installation right as the optimal planting season begins.

Are your lawn care programs customized?

Absolutely. Every property has unique soil conditions, sun exposure, and grass types. The lawn care programs provided are tailored specifically to the needs of your yard, ensuring the right balance of nutrients, weed control, and aeration for maximum vitality.
