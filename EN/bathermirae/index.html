Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a bathroom renovation usually take?

Most walk-in shower installations and standard bathtub replacement projects can be completed in just a few days. The exact timeline depends on the complexity of the project, including custom tile installation and plumbing adjustments.

Can I convert my existing tub into a walk-in shower?

Yes, bathtub replacement is one of the most popular requests. The remodeling partners we promote specialize in seamlessly removing your old tub and installing a beautiful, accessible walk-in shower in its exact footprint.

Do you offer custom tile installation?

Absolutely. The full-service bathroom remodeling contractors we feature provide extensive tile options. They handle complex patterns, natural stone, and modern ceramics for floors, walls, and shower enclosures.

Are modern walk-in showers easy to maintain?

Yes. Today's walk-in showers often feature minimal-grout wall panels, large-format tiles, and specially treated glass doors that resist water spots and soap scum, making regular cleaning fast and effortless.

What is included in a full-service bathroom remodeling project?

A full-service approach covers everything from the initial design consultation and safe demolition to plumbing updates, electrical work, custom tile installation, and the final placement of modern fixtures.
