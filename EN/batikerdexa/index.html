Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about booking your maid service.

What is included in a standard house cleaning?

Our standard house cleaning covers the essential upkeep of your home. This includes dusting all surfaces, vacuuming and mopping floors, sanitizing bathroom fixtures, wiping down kitchen counters, and emptying trash bins. It is designed to keep your home fresh and tidy on a regular basis.

How is deep cleaning different from regular service?

Deep cleaning is a much more thorough, intensive process. While regular service focuses on visible surfaces, deep cleaning targets hidden dirt. It involves hand-washing baseboards, cleaning the interior of windows, scrubbing grout, and removing heavy buildup in kitchens and bathrooms.

Do I need to be home during the maid service?

No, you do not need to be present. Many of our clients prefer to provide entry instructions or a spare key so the professionals can perform the recurring cleaning while they are at work or running errands. Your home is always treated with the utmost respect and security.

Can I customize my recurring cleaning schedule?

Absolutely. Batiker Dexa promotes digital ease, meaning you can set up weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly visits depending on your household's traffic and needs. The frequency can be adjusted at any time to match your changing lifestyle.

Are cleaning supplies and equipment provided?

Yes, the professional house cleaning teams arrive fully equipped with high-quality, professional-grade cleaning supplies and vacuums. If you have specific products you prefer them to use on certain surfaces, you can simply leave them out with instructions.
