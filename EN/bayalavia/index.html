Frequently Asked Questions

Details regarding the photography process and what to expect.

How far in advance should we book our wedding photographer?
It is highly recommended to secure your date 9 to 12 months in advance, especially for popular wedding seasons. Early booking ensures availability and provides ample time to schedule and plan your engagement photos.
Do you offer bridal portraits prior to the wedding day?
Yes, formal bridal portraits are a wonderful addition to your coverage. These sessions are typically scheduled a month or two before the wedding, allowing for a relaxed environment to capture elegant, timeless images in your attire.
What is included in destination wedding photography packages?
Destination packages are custom-tailored to your specific location and itinerary. They generally include multi-day coverage (such as a welcome dinner, the wedding day, and a day-after session), travel logistics coordination, and a comprehensive digital gallery.
How long does it take to receive our final image gallery?
A curated selection of sneak peek images is usually provided within a few days of the event. The complete, fully edited digital gallery is typically delivered within 6 to 8 weeks, ensuring each photograph receives meticulous professional attention.
Do you help with timeline planning for photography?
Absolutely. Creating a smooth timeline is crucial for stress-free photography. We collaborate closely with you and your planner to map out ideal times for getting ready, first looks, family portraits, and sunset sessions to maximize beautiful natural light.
