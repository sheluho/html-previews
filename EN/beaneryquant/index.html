Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our door installation services.

What are the benefits of fiberglass doors for front door replacement?

Fiberglass doors are an excellent choice for front door replacement because they offer the aesthetic appeal of real wood without the maintenance. They are highly durable, energy-efficient, and resistant to warping, rotting, and denting, making them perfect for handling various weather conditions.

How long does a typical door installation take?

A standard entry door installation usually takes between 3 to 5 hours, while replacing interior doors can take 1 to 2 hours per door. Our professional team works efficiently to ensure minimal disruption while maintaining the highest quality standards.

Do you customize interior doors to fit non-standard frames?

Yes, we specialize in custom fits for all interior doors. Older homes often have non-standard framing, and our technicians take precise measurements to ensure your new doors fit perfectly, operating smoothly without any binding or gaps.

Can I replace my old closet doors with sliding or mirrored options?

Absolutely. We offer a wide variety of closet door systems, including sliding, bifold, and mirrored options. These upgrades not only modernize the look of your bedroom but can also help maximize your usable floor space.

Do you remove and dispose of the old doors?

Yes, our comprehensive door installation service includes the careful removal and environmentally responsible disposal of your old entry doors and interior doors. We leave your home clean and fully updated.
