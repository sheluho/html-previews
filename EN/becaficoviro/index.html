Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is a turnkey kitchen remodel?

A turnkey kitchen remodel means we handle every single aspect of the project from start to finish. You won't need to hire separate plumbers, electricians, or painters. We manage the design, demolition, installation of custom cabinets and countertops, and all finishing work so you can simply turn the key and enjoy your new space.

Do you manufacture your own custom cabinets?

Yes, we specialize in high-quality custom cabinets designed to fit your unique space and style preferences perfectly. This allows us to maximize storage in awkward corners, match specific wood stains, and build to precise architectural dimensions rather than relying on standard box sizes.

What types of countertops do you install?

We offer a wide selection of premium countertops, including granite, marble, quartz, and quartzite. During the design phase, we help you weigh the pros and cons of each material—balancing aesthetics, maintenance requirements, and durability for your specific cooking habits.

Can you change the footprint or layout of my current kitchen?

Absolutely. Changing the layout is often the most impactful part of kitchen remodeling. We can remove non-load-bearing walls to create an open concept, relocate plumbing for a new island sink, or completely rethink the appliance triangle to improve the workflow of the room.

How long does a full kitchen remodeling project take?

The timeline varies based on the scope of the project. A straightforward update with new countertops and refaced cabinets might take a few weeks, while a full structural turnkey kitchen remodel requiring custom fabrication and layout changes can take several months. We provide a detailed schedule before any demolition begins.
