Frequently Asked Questions

What types of window treatments do you feature?

We showcase a comprehensive range of custom solutions including wood and faux wood blinds, energy-efficient honeycomb shades, modern motorized roller shades, classic plantation shutters, and luxurious custom drapes.

Are custom blinds better than store-bought options?

Yes. Custom blinds are precision-measured to fit your exact window dimensions, eliminating light gaps and awkward overhangs. They also offer superior materials, robust operating mechanisms, and endless personalization options compared to off-the-shelf alternatives.

Do plantation shutters increase home value?

Absolutely. High-quality plantation shutters are often considered permanent fixtures or hard window treatments. Because they are custom-built and securely mounted to the window frame, they typically remain with the house and can increase its appraisal value.

How do I maintain and clean my new roller shades?

Roller shades are generally very low-maintenance. For regular cleaning, light dusting with a feather duster or gentle vacuuming with a brush attachment is sufficient. Spot cleaning with a damp cloth and mild soap works well for most fabric options.

Can custom drapes help with home insulation?

Yes, custom drapes are excellent for insulation. When lined with thermal or blackout fabrics, they create a significant barrier against drafts in the winter and heat gain in the summer, helping to regulate your home's temperature and reduce energy costs.
