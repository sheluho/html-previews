Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about our custom window treatments.

What types of window treatments do you offer?

We provide a comprehensive range of custom window coverings. This includes premium plantation shutters, versatile window blinds (wood and faux-wood), modern roller shades, cellular shades, and elegant custom drapes.

Are your products custom-measured for my specific windows?

Yes, absolutely. We do not sell off-the-shelf sizes. Every project begins with a precise measurement of your window openings to ensure your blinds, shades, or shutters fit flawlessly and operate smoothly.

How do plantation shutters differ from traditional blinds?

Plantation shutters are built-in window fixtures with a solid frame and operable louvers, rather than free-hanging slats on cords. They are highly durable, offer exceptional insulation, and are often considered a permanent architectural upgrade to a home.

Do roller shades provide good insulation?

The insulation value of roller shades depends heavily on the fabric chosen. We offer specialized thermal and blackout fabrics that significantly reduce heat transfer, keeping your home cooler in the summer and warmer in the winter.

Can you help me choose the right style for my home's decor?

Certainly. Part of our service includes an in-home design consultation. We bring samples of our blinds, shades, and custom drapes directly to you, so you can see how different materials and colors interact with your existing lighting and furnishings.
