Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to provide my own cleaning supplies?

No, the professional house cleaning teams arrive fully equipped with their own high-quality cleaning supplies and equipment. If you have specialized products you prefer them to use, simply let them know beforehand.

What is included in a deep cleaning service?

A deep cleaning goes beyond standard maintenance. It includes detailed dusting of baseboards, cleaning inside window sills, scrubbing grout lines, wiping down doors and frames, and targeting buildup in kitchens and bathrooms.

How do I set up recurring cleaning?

Setting up recurring cleaning is simple. You can choose a weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly schedule that fits your routine. Use our contact form to request your preferred frequency, and the provider will lock in a consistent time slot for you.

Are the maid service professionals vetted?

Yes. We only promote established, professional cleaning companies that rigorously vet their staff. This includes thorough background checks, reference verifications, and professional training to ensure reliability and trust.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

It is entirely up to you. Many clients provide entry instructions (such as a lockbox code or hidden key) so the cleaners can work while they are at the office or running errands. If you prefer to be home, that is perfectly fine as well.
