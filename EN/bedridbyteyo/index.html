Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer both plated dinner and buffet catering options?

Yes. The culinary experts we promote offer flexible dining styles, from highly formal plated dinners to expansive, interactive buffet catering setups to match the tone of your event perfectly.

What types of events do you cover with your event catering?

We cater to a wide array of occasions. While wedding catering is a major specialty, we also accommodate corporate catering events, private birthday parties, holiday galas, and intimate family gatherings.

How far in advance should we book wedding catering?

For peak wedding seasons, we recommend securing your wedding catering partner at least 9 to 12 months in advance. This ensures availability and allows ample time for menu tastings and customization.

Can you accommodate specific dietary restrictions?

Absolutely. The providers we feature are well-versed in creating delicious menus that accommodate vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, and other specific dietary requirements without compromising on taste or presentation.

Are corporate catering packages customizable?

Yes. Every corporate catering package can be tailored to suit the specific needs of your business, from boxed lunches for working meetings to multi-course plated dinners for executive retreats.
