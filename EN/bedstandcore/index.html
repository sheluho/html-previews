Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I consider a walk-in shower over a traditional tub?
A walk-in shower offers unmatched accessibility and safety by removing the high barrier of a standard tub. This makes it ideal for aging in place, individuals with mobility issues, or anyone who prefers a sleek, modern bathroom design that is remarkably easy to clean and maintain.
How long does a typical bathroom renovation take?
Timelines vary greatly depending on the scope of the project. While a simple bathtub replacement or acrylic shower insert might be completed in just a few days, a full-scale custom bathroom remodel involving plumbing changes and intricate tile installation can take several weeks.
What is the best material for shower walls?
The best material depends on your budget and aesthetic preferences. High-quality acrylic is excellent for durability, affordability, and ease of cleaning since it resists mold and mildew. For a more luxurious and customized look, ceramic or porcelain tile installation remains the premium standard.
Can I convert my existing tub into a walk-in shower?
Yes, tub-to-shower conversions are highly popular. The footprint of your existing bathtub is usually the perfect size for a spacious new walk-in shower. This process involves removing the old tub and installing a custom shower pan, surround, and fixtures in its place.
Do I need to worry about water damage during a remodel?
Professional contractors prioritize proper waterproofing above all else. During a bathroom remodel, advanced waterproofing membranes and backer boards are installed beneath the tile or wall surrounds to ensure water never reaches your home's structural framing, preventing mold and rot.
