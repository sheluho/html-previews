Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I schedule lawn mowing?

For optimal health and a pristine appearance, we recommend weekly or bi-weekly lawn mowing during the peak growing seasons (spring and early summer). The exact frequency depends on your specific grass type, local climate conditions, and how much rainfall your area is receiving.

Do you provide full landscape installation?

Yes, we handle the entire process from initial concept to completion. Our landscape installation services encompass everything from detailed garden design and soil preparation to the planting of trees, shrubs, and the integration of functional hardscaping elements to complete your vision.

What does your standard lawn care program include?

Our comprehensive lawn care program is tailored to your yard's specific needs. It typically includes scheduled, nutrient-dense fertilization, pre-emergent and post-emergent weed control, core aeration to relieve soil compaction, and targeted treatments to manage common surface insects and lawn diseases.

Is professional tree service really necessary?

Absolutely. Regular tree service is crucial for both safety and aesthetics. Proper structural pruning prevents heavy branches from damaging property during storms, while routine health assessments can catch diseases early. Healthy trees also provide essential shade and character to your overall landscaping.

When is the best time to start a new garden design project?

Early spring or early fall are generally the optimal times for executing a new garden design and proceeding with landscape installation. The milder temperatures and natural rainfall during these seasons allow new plants, shrubs, and turf to establish strong root systems before facing the stress of extreme summer heat or freezing winter temperatures.
