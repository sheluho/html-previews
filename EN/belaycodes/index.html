Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries regarding our kitchen design and manufacturing promotions.

What makes custom cabinets superior to stock options?

Custom cabinets are built to your exact specifications, maximizing your available space without the need for unsightly fillers. They offer unlimited choices in materials, finishes, and hardware, ensuring a kitchen design that is truly unique to your home.

Are shaker cabinets a good choice for modern homes?

Absolutely. Shaker cabinets are renowned for their clean, unadorned lines, which makes them incredibly versatile. They can easily bridge the gap between traditional and contemporary kitchen design, depending on the hardware and finishes selected.

How can I view samples before committing to a design?

We highly recommend visiting the kitchen showroom of the original manufacturer. There, you can interact with various wood species, stain options, and hardware selections to confidently finalize your kitchen design.

Does Belay Codes handle the manufacturing directly?

No, Belay Codes is an independent promotional operator. We highlight the exceptional work of Kountry Kraft, the actual manufacturer and service provider. All fabrication and installation services are handled directly by them.

What is the typical lead time for custom cabinetry?

Lead times can vary significantly based on the complexity of your kitchen design, current manufacturing schedules, and material availability. It is best to consult directly with the manufacturer during your initial design consultation for an accurate timeline.
