Frequently Asked Questions

Do you manufacture your own kitchen cabinets?

Yes, all of our custom cabinets are manufactured directly by our skilled craftsmen to ensure the highest quality control and a perfect fit for your home's exact dimensions.

Can I visit your kitchen showroom?

Absolutely. Our kitchen showroom is open to the public, allowing you to explore different styles, including our popular shaker cabinets, and consult directly with our design team.

How long does the kitchen design process take?

The initial kitchen design phase typically takes one to two weeks, depending on the complexity of your project and the specific custom cabinets required to fulfill your vision.

What materials do you use for your custom cabinets?

We use premium hardwoods, high-grade plywood for the boxes, and durable, soft-close hardware. We never use cheap particle board, ensuring your kitchen cabinets provide lasting value.

Do you handle the installation of the cabinets?

Yes, our experienced installation team ensures that your custom cabinets are perfectly leveled and securely mounted in your home for a flawless, professional finish.
