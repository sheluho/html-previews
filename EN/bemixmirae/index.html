Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our premium car wash service.

What is the difference between an express wash and a hand car wash?
An express wash uses automated, soft-touch technology to quickly clean your vehicle's exterior in just a few minutes. A premium hand car wash is a manual, detail-oriented process performed by professionals, ensuring every inch of your vehicle is carefully cleaned without the use of heavy machinery.
How does the auto wash membership work?
The auto wash membership is a monthly subscription that provides you with unlimited washes for your registered vehicle. You simply pay a flat fee and can bring your car in as often as you like to maintain a consistently clean exterior.
Is a premium car wash safe for luxury and classic cars?
Absolutely. For delicate paint jobs, luxury vehicles, or classic cars, we highly recommend the premium hand car wash. The professionals use pH-balanced soaps and ultra-soft microfiber towels to ensure a safe, scratch-free finish.
Do I need to make an appointment?
While drive-ups are always welcome for an express wash, we recommend reaching out via our contact form to schedule comprehensive detailing or a full premium hand car wash to ensure prompt service upon arrival.
What products are used during the wash?
Our partners use industry-leading, eco-friendly cleaning agents, premium waxes, and ceramic sealants designed to lift dirt safely while leaving a protective, high-gloss shine on your vehicle's exterior.
