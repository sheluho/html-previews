Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about upgrading your home's windows.

How long does a typical window installation take?

Most standard residential window installation projects can be completed within one to two days. The exact timeframe depends on the number of windows being replaced, the complexity of the custom sizing, and weather conditions. Professional crews prioritize minimizing disruption to your daily life.

What are the benefits of vinyl windows?

Vinyl windows are incredibly popular due to their exceptional durability, energy efficiency, and low maintenance requirements. Unlike wood, they will not rot, peel, or require repainting. They also feature excellent insulating properties, making them a cost-effective choice for long-term home improvement.

Do you recommend double hung windows?

Yes, double hung windows are one of the most versatile and requested styles. They feature two operable sashes that move vertically, allowing for excellent ventilation control. Furthermore, modern designs often allow both sashes to tilt inward, making exterior glass cleaning safe and easy from inside your home.

What makes a window "energy efficient"?

Energy efficient windows typically feature multiple panes of glass separated by insulating gas fills (like argon). They also utilize Low-E (low-emissivity) coatings that reflect infrared light, keeping heat inside during winter and outside during summer. High-quality frame materials further prevent thermal transfer.

When is the best time of year for replacement windows?

While replacement windows can be installed year-round by experienced professionals, spring and fall are traditionally the most popular seasons due to milder weather. However, modern installation techniques allow crews to replace windows efficiently even in winter, minimizing heat loss during the process.
