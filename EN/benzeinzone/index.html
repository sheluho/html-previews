Frequently Asked Questions

Do local movers charge by the hour or by weight?

Typically, local movers charge an hourly rate based on the number of crew members required for the job. Long distance movers, however, usually base their estimates on the total weight of your belongings and the distance traveled.

Are packing materials included in packing services?

When you opt for professional packing services, the moving company usually supplies all necessary materials, including heavy-duty boxes, bubble wrap, packing paper, and tape. These materials may be billed separately or included in a comprehensive package quote.

How far in advance should I book a moving company?

It is highly recommended to book your residential moving services at least 4 to 6 weeks in advance, especially during the peak summer months. Booking early ensures you secure your preferred date and gives you ample time to prepare.

What items will movers refuse to transport?

For safety and legal reasons, movers cannot transport hazardous materials, including explosives, flammable liquids, corrosive chemicals, and combustible items. Perishable foods and live plants are also typically excluded from long distance moves.

Who handles customer service and claims?

As Benzein Zone is a promotional partner, all actual moving services, customer support, guarantees, and damage claims are managed directly by the original service provider, USA Moving Co. We introduce you to the service, and they handle the logistics.
