Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a windshield replacement take?

Most standard windshield replacement services can be completed in one to two hours. However, the specialized urethane adhesive requires additional time to cure to ensure your vehicle is safe to drive. Your technician will provide a precise safe-to-drive time upon completion.

Can you fix a cracked windshield without replacing it?

Yes, through professional chip repair. If the crack or chip is smaller than a quarter and not in the driver's direct line of sight, it can often be repaired by injecting a clear resin. This restores the structural integrity of the auto glass and prevents the damage from spreading.

Do you handle car window replacement for all makes and models?

Absolutely. Our network of professionals has access to auto glass inventory for nearly all domestic and foreign vehicles, ensuring a perfect fit for your specific car window replacement needs.

What is the difference between OEM and aftermarket auto glass?

OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) glass is made to the exact specifications of your vehicle's maker, ensuring precise fit and functionality. Aftermarket glass is produced by non-OEM manufacturers. We strongly advocate for high-quality OEM or OEM-equivalent glass for all windshield replacement services to maintain safety standards.

Is auto glass repair covered by my insurance?

Many comprehensive insurance policies cover windshield repair and replacement, often waiving the deductible for chip repair. The service providers we connect you with can frequently assist in verifying your coverage and processing the claim directly.
