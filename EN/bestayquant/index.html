Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical bathroom renovation take?

The timeline for a bathroom renovation varies depending on the scope of work. A straightforward bathtub replacement might take a few days, while a complete, down-to-the-studs bathroom remodel can take several weeks. An accurate timeline is provided after a detailed consultation.

Can you convert my old tub into a walk-in shower?

Yes, tub-to-shower conversions are one of the most requested services. Replacing an unused tub with a spacious, modern walk-in shower is an excellent way to update the space, improve accessibility, and increase the overall appeal of the room.

What is included in a full bathroom remodel?

As a full-service bathroom remodeling company, a complete overhaul typically includes demolition, updated plumbing and electrical work, drywall repair, waterproofing, custom tile installation, new cabinetry, countertops, fixtures, and final painting.

Do you handle the tile installation yourselves?

Absolutely. Professional tile installation is critical for both the waterproofing integrity and the visual finish of your bathroom. Experienced craftsmen handle all tile work, from simple ceramic floors to complex glass mosaic accents in the shower.

Is a bathtub replacement a messy process?

Any construction involves some level of dust, but strict measures are taken to minimize the mess. Drop cloths, dust barriers, and daily cleanups ensure that the rest of your home remains protected while the bathtub replacement or renovation is underway.
