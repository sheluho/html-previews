Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I schedule professional carpet cleaning?

For most households, it is recommended to have your carpets professionally cleaned every 12 to 18 months. However, homes with indoor pets, young children, or high foot traffic may benefit from professional steam cleaning every 6 to 9 months to maintain optimal hygiene and appearance.

Is steam cleaning safe for all rug types?

Steam cleaning is highly effective and safe for most synthetic and standard wool rugs. However, delicate materials like silk or certain antique weaves require specialized rug cleaning methods. The professionals we promote will assess your rug's material before selecting the safest cleaning technique.

Do you offer specific upholstery cleaning for delicate fabrics?

Yes, comprehensive upholstery cleaning services include assessing the fabric type of your furniture. Whether it's durable microfiber or delicate linen, tailored cleaning solutions are used to lift dirt and stains without compromising the integrity or color of the fabric.

Can you guarantee complete stain removal?

While industry-leading techniques are employed for stain removal, no professional can guarantee 100% removal of every spot, as permanent discoloration can occur depending on the substance, fabric dye, and how long the stain has set. However, significant improvement is almost always achieved.

How long does the carpet take to dry after steam cleaning?

Drying times vary based on humidity, airflow, and the thickness of the carpet pile. Typically, carpets take between 4 to 8 hours to dry completely after a professional steam cleaning session. Keeping windows open or running fans can help accelerate the drying process.
