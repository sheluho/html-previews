Questions & Answers

Planning Inquiries

What is included in your full-service wedding planning package?

Our full-service wedding planning package is comprehensive. It includes budget development, venue scouting, vendor curation and contract negotiation, personalized event design, timeline creation, and complete on-site management from setup to teardown on your wedding day. We act as your primary point of contact for all logistics.

Do I need a day of coordinator if my venue provides a manager?

Yes, highly recommended. A venue manager is responsible for the venue's operations (facilities, in-house catering, staff). A dedicated day of coordinator works exclusively for you, managing your specific timeline, coordinating external vendors, setting up personal decor, and ensuring the entire event flows smoothly according to your vision.

How far in advance should we hire a wedding coordinator?

For full-service planning, we recommend booking 12 to 18 months in advance to ensure availability and secure the best vendors. For a day of coordinator (often referred to as month-of coordination), booking 6 to 9 months prior to your date is ideal, as we typically begin taking over logistics 4 to 6 weeks before the event.

Do you specialize in destination wedding planning?

Absolutely. We have extensive experience designing and executing destination weddings. Our team manages the unique challenges of remote events, including navigating local regulations, sourcing reliable international vendors, organizing multi-day itineraries, and assisting with guest accommodations to ensure a seamless experience abroad.

How do you handle budget management during the planning process?

Financial transparency is a cornerstone of our service. During our initial consultations, we establish a realistic budget based on your priorities. We then utilize detailed spreadsheets to track all expenses, upcoming payment deadlines, and vendor quotes, ensuring you remain informed and in control of your investment throughout the entire process.
