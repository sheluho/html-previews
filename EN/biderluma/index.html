Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our wedding videography process.

What makes a cinematic wedding video different from regular coverage?

A cinematic wedding video goes beyond merely recording the events of the day. It uses creative camera angles, professional audio, emotive music, and advanced editing techniques to weave a compelling narrative. The result is a wedding film that feels like a beautifully crafted movie about your love story, rather than just a home video.

Will the wedding videographer be obtrusive during the ceremony?

Absolutely not. We pride ourselves on our documentary-style approach to event videography. Our wedding videographer team uses zoom lenses and discreet positioning to capture intimate moments without interrupting the flow of your day or blocking the view of your guests.

Do you provide raw footage alongside the edited wedding film?

We focus on delivering a highly polished, fully edited cinematic wedding video that tells your story perfectly. While we typically do not include raw, unedited files—as they are uncolored and lack professional audio sync—we do offer longer documentary edits that include full ceremonies and speeches as part of our comprehensive event videography packages.

How long does it take to receive our final cinematic wedding video?

Quality editing takes time, as we meticulously color grade every clip and mix the audio for the best experience. Typically, you can expect to receive your final wedding film within 8 to 12 weeks after your event date.

Do you travel for destination event videography?

Yes, we love capturing love stories in new and exciting locations. Our wedding videography services are available worldwide. Travel accommodations and fees are discussed during our initial consultation to ensure everything is planned smoothly for your destination wedding film.
