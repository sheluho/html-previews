Common Questions

Details about our window solutions and process.

What makes your replacement windows energy efficient?

Our energy efficient windows utilize advanced glass packages, including low-E coatings and argon gas fills between the panes. This technology reflects heat back into your home during winter and blocks solar heat during summer, significantly reducing the workload on your HVAC system.

Why should I choose vinyl windows over wood?

Vinyl windows offer exceptional durability with virtually no maintenance. Unlike wood, vinyl will not rot, warp, or require repainting. They provide excellent insulation properties and are custom-manufactured to fit the exact dimensions of your existing window openings.

What are the benefits of double hung windows?

Double hung windows feature two operable sashes that slide vertically. This allows for superior ventilation control—you can open the top, the bottom, or both. Additionally, both sashes typically tilt inward, making it incredibly easy to clean the exterior glass from safely inside your home.

How long does the window installation process take?

The timeline depends on the number of replacement windows being installed, but our expert crews typically complete an average-sized home in one to two days. We prioritize minimizing disruption to your daily life while ensuring a perfect, weather-tight seal on every unit.

Are the windows custom made for my house?

Yes. Every replacement window we provide is custom-measured and manufactured to the precise specifications of your home's existing openings. This custom approach guarantees a better fit, superior energy efficiency, and a cleaner aesthetic compared to off-the-shelf builder-grade windows.
