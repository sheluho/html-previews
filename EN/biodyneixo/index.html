Frequently Asked Questions

Insights into our bespoke process.

What is the process for ordering custom furniture?
The journey begins with a consultation to discuss your vision, space, and functional needs. We then develop conceptual designs and material samples. Once approved, our artisans begin crafting your bespoke furniture piece, keeping you informed throughout the build phase.
How long does a bespoke furniture piece take to build?
Lead times vary based on the complexity of the design and material availability. Generally, handmade furniture requires between 8 to 14 weeks from final design approval to completion. We pride ourselves on meticulous craftsmanship, which requires unhurried dedication.
Can I customize the dimensions of a custom dining table?
Absolutely. The primary advantage of custom furniture is exact scaling. We engineer custom dining tables to fit your specific dining room dimensions perfectly, ensuring optimal seating capacity and proportional balance within the space.
Do you use sustainable materials for your handmade furniture?
Yes, material sourcing is a critical part of our ethos. We prioritize sustainably harvested woods, reclaimed timber when appropriate for the design, and environmentally conscious finishing processes to ensure your piece is as responsible as it is beautiful.
Can I choose the fabric for a custom sofa?
Certainly. When designing a custom sofa, you have access to an extensive library of premium textiles, performance fabrics, and fine leathers. We can also accommodate COM (Customer's Own Material) if you have a specific fabric already selected by your interior designer.
