Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about our carpet and upholstery care services.

How often should I schedule professional carpet cleaning?

For most households, we recommend professional carpet cleaning at least once every 12 to 18 months. However, homes with indoor pets, children, or high foot traffic may benefit from steam cleaning every 6 to 9 months to maintain optimal air quality and appearance.

Are your upholstery cleaning methods safe for delicate fabrics?

Yes. Before any upholstery cleaning begins, the fabric is carefully assessed to determine the most appropriate cleaning method. We utilize specialized, gentle techniques designed to lift dirt and stains without compromising the integrity or color of delicate textiles.

How long does it take for carpets to dry after steam cleaning?

Drying times vary depending on humidity, temperature, and airflow in the home, but typically carpets take between 4 to 8 hours to dry completely after a professional steam cleaning session. We recommend keeping foot traffic to a minimum during this time.

Can you remove all types of stains from my rugs?

Our advanced stain removal processes are highly effective against a wide variety of blemishes, including pet accidents, wine, and food spills. While we have a high success rate, certain permanent dyes or long-set chemical stains may alter the fiber permanently. We will evaluate the stain and provide an honest assessment before cleaning.

Do you clean area rugs in the home or take them off-site?

This depends on the type and condition of the rug. Standard synthetic area rugs can often be cleaned on-site alongside your wall-to-wall carpet. For delicate, antique, or heavily soiled natural fiber rugs (like wool or silk), specialized off-site rug cleaning is usually recommended to ensure a thorough and safe wash.
