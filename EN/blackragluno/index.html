Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take for carpets to dry after steam cleaning?

Drying times can vary based on humidity, airflow, and the specific carpet cleaning method used. Typically, carpets take between 4 to 8 hours to dry completely. Proper ventilation and air conditioning can significantly speed up the process.

Can you guarantee complete stain removal?

While advanced stain removal techniques are highly effective on a wide variety of spots, some stains permanently alter the fabric dye. The professionals we connect you with will assess the stain and apply the best possible treatment, though absolute removal cannot be guaranteed for every mark.

Is upholstery cleaning safe for all types of furniture fabrics?

Yes, professional upholstery cleaning starts with a thorough fabric inspection. The cleaning method is then customized—whether it requires low-moisture cleaning, dry cleaning, or gentle steam extraction—to ensure it is perfectly safe for your specific material.

How often should I schedule professional rug cleaning?

For normal household traffic, it is generally recommended to have a professional rug cleaning every 12 to 18 months. Homes with pets, children, or high foot traffic may benefit from more frequent cleanings to maintain hygiene and appearance.

Do I need to vacuum before the technicians arrive?

While not strictly mandatory, a light vacuuming before the service helps remove loose surface debris, allowing the deep carpet cleaning equipment to focus entirely on extracting embedded dirt and tough soils.
