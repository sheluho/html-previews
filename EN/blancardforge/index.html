Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our tire installation and storage services.

How often should I schedule a tire rotation?

We generally recommend a tire rotation every 5,000 to 7,000 miles, or whenever you get an oil change. Regular rotation ensures even tread wear, which maximizes the life of your tires and improves overall vehicle handling.

How does your seasonal tire storage work?

When you bring your vehicle in for a seasonal tire change, we remove your current set and safely store them in our climate-controlled facility. When the next season arrives, simply book an appointment, and we'll swap them back. It saves you space and heavy lifting.

What is included in a standard tire installation?

Our tire installation service includes removing the old tires, mounting the new tires onto your wheels, professional computerized balancing to prevent vibrations, and installing new valve stems if necessary. We also check your wheel alignment as part of our comprehensive safety review.

How do I know if I need a wheel alignment or a tire replacement?

If your car pulls to one side, or if your steering wheel vibrates, you likely need a wheel alignment. If your tire tread is visibly low, uneven, or fails the "penny test," it’s time for a tire replacement. Our technicians can perform a full inspection to advise you on the best course of action.

Can you install tires that I purchased somewhere else?

Absolutely. We provide professional tire installation services regardless of where you purchased your tires. Bring them in, and our certified mechanics will mount and balance them with the same high standard of care.
