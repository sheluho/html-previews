Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about our custom furniture process.

How long does it take to build a custom dining table?
Lead times vary based on the complexity of the design and our current workshop schedule. Typically, a bespoke furniture piece like a custom dining table takes between 8 to 12 weeks from final design approval to delivery.
Can I choose my own fabric for a custom sofa?
Absolutely. We have a curated library of premium fabrics and leathers in our showroom. Alternatively, we accept Customer's Own Material (COM) if you or your designer have already sourced a specific fabric for your custom sofa.
Do you offer design consultations?
Yes, every project begins with a comprehensive consultation. We discuss your spatial requirements, aesthetic preferences, and functional needs to ensure the handmade furniture we create is a perfect fit for your lifestyle.
What kind of wood do you use?
We primarily work with high-quality, responsibly sourced domestic hardwoods such as Walnut, White Oak, Cherry, and Maple. The choice of wood is entirely up to you and will be discussed during the design phase of your bespoke furniture project.
Do you deliver and install?
Yes, we provide white-glove delivery and installation services for all local clients. For larger built-in pieces, our workshop team will handle the installation to ensure flawless integration into your space.
