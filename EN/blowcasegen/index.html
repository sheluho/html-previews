Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about entry doors, interior doors, and installation.

What are the benefits of fiberglass doors for a front door replacement?

Fiberglass doors are highly durable, energy-efficient, and resistant to warping, rotting, and denting. They offer the beautiful appearance of real wood grain but require significantly less maintenance, making them an ideal choice for front door replacement.

How long does a typical door installation take?

A standard single entry or interior door installation usually takes a few hours. However, timelines can vary depending on the complexity of the project, whether structural modifications are needed to the frame, and the specific materials chosen.

Do you offer custom sizing for interior doors?

Yes, professional installers can accommodate custom dimensions for interior doors to fit unique archways, older homes with non-standard frames, or specific design requirements you may have for your living space.

Will a new entry door improve my home's energy efficiency?

Absolutely. Modern entry doors, particularly high-quality fiberglass doors, feature advanced core insulation and superior weatherstripping. When paired with professional door installation, they eliminate drafts and help lower your heating and cooling costs.

How do I prepare my home for door installation day?

To ensure a smooth process, please clear the area around the door frame inside and outside. Remove fragile items, furniture, and wall decorations nearby. The installation team will handle the rest, including site cleanup and disposal of your old door.
