Frequently Asked Questions

What does a full-service bathroom remodel include?

A full-service bathroom remodel encompasses everything required to transform your space. This includes initial design consultations, safe demolition, necessary plumbing and electrical updates, precise tile installation, and the final placement of all fixtures. The partnered professionals manage the entire project from start to finish.

How long does a walk-in shower installation typically take?

Depending on the complexity of the design and any custom glass requirements, a walk-in shower installation usually takes between a few days to a full week. The contractors strive to work efficiently to minimize any disruption to your daily routine while ensuring top-tier quality.

Do you offer bathtub replacement without a full room remodel?

Yes, targeted bathtub replacement services are available. If the rest of your bathroom is in good shape, professionals can efficiently remove your old tub and install a new, modern fixture, seamlessly updating the immediate surrounding area as needed without tearing apart the whole room.

Can I choose my own tile for the bathroom renovation?

Absolutely. Clients are encouraged to select tiles that perfectly match their personal vision. The remodeling experts will also offer professional guidance on the best materials for wet environments, ensuring your chosen tile installation remains beautiful and durable for years.

Are the bathroom renovations guaranteed?

All physical products and labor warranties are provided directly by the service partners at American Conservation & Plumbing. Boaster Works acts as a promotional operator connecting you with these reputable professionals who stand behind the quality of their work.
