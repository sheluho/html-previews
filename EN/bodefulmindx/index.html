Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our wedding and event videography services.

How far in advance should we book our wedding videographer?
We recommend booking your wedding videography services at least 6 to 12 months in advance, especially for popular spring and fall dates, to ensure we are available for your special day.
What is included in your cinematic wedding video packages?
Our packages typically include full-day coverage, a stunning cinematic highlight reel, a full-length documentary edit of the ceremony and key speeches, and professional audio capture.
Do you provide event videography for non-wedding occasions?
Yes, our comprehensive event videography services cover a wide range of gatherings including corporate functions, milestone anniversaries, and large private parties.
How long does it take to receive our final wedding film?
Delivering a high-quality cinematic wedding video requires meticulous editing. You can generally expect to receive your final wedding film within 8 to 12 weeks after your event date.
Can we choose the music for our wedding video?
We license high-quality, cinematic music that perfectly matches the mood and pacing of your wedding film to ensure a cohesive, legal, and emotionally impactful final product. We welcome your input on musical styles and genres!
