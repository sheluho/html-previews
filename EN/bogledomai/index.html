Frequently Asked Questions

Understanding Paint Protection

What exactly is a ceramic coating?
A ceramic coating is a liquid polymer applied by hand to the exterior of a vehicle. It chemically bonds with the factory paint, creating a protective, hydrophobic layer that prevents dirt, grime, and stain marks from bonding to the paint.
Why do I need professional paint protection?
Professional paint protection acts as a sacrificial barrier between your car’s clear coat and the environment. It guards against UV damage, oxidation, minor scratches, and chemical etching from bird droppings and bug splatter, preserving your vehicle's resale value.
How long does a thorough auto detailing session take?
Depending on the condition of the vehicle and the specific services requested, a premium auto detailing session can take anywhere from 4 to 8 hours. Advanced paint correction and ceramic coating applications may require your vehicle to be left at the facility overnight to cure properly.
Does your interior detailing remove all stains?
The meticulous interior detailing methods promoted here utilize deep extraction and professional-grade specialized cleaners. While the vast majority of common stains, odors, and blemishes are permanently removed, certain chemical stains or severe physical wear may require specialized restoration beyond standard detailing.
How often should I schedule car detailing?
For standard maintenance, a full car detailing is recommended twice a year to maintain the interior fabrics and exterior clear coat. If you opt for a professional ceramic coating, your routine maintenance drops significantly, requiring only basic, safe washes to maintain the pristine look.
