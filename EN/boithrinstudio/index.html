Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about commissioning your piece.

How long does a custom dining table take to build?

Lead times vary depending on the complexity of the design and material availability. Typically, a custom dining table requires 8 to 12 weeks from the finalization of design sketches to delivery.

Can I choose the fabric for a custom sofa?

Yes. When you commission a custom sofa, you have complete control over the upholstery. The makers provide a curated selection of high-end fabrics and leathers, or you can provide your own material (COM).

What materials are used in your handmade furniture?

Our featured artisans prioritize sustainably sourced, premium hardwoods such as walnut, oak, and maple. Every piece of handmade furniture is crafted using robust joinery techniques and finished with durable, protective sealants.

Is bespoke furniture more expensive than retail?

Bespoke furniture is an investment in quality, longevity, and individualized design. While initial costs may be higher than mass-produced retail items, the value lies in receiving a highly durable, one-of-a-kind piece built exactly to your specifications.

How do I start the design process for my custom furniture?

To begin the journey toward your custom furniture, simply reach out via our contact form. Provide brief details about the piece you are envisioning (e.g., dimensions, style, function), and we will connect you with the appropriate craftsmen for an initial consultation.
