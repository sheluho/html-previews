Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our tire management process.

How does the seasonal tire storage work?

When you come in for your seasonal tire installation, we take your off-season tires, clean them, tag them with your information, and store them securely in our facility. When the next season arrives, simply book an appointment, and we'll have your tires ready to go.

Is a wheel alignment necessary after a tire replacement?

Yes, we highly recommend a wheel alignment whenever you get a tire replacement. Proper alignment ensures your new tires wear evenly, giving you the best return on your investment while maintaining safe handling and optimal fuel efficiency.

How often should I schedule a tire rotation?

As a general rule, tire rotation should be performed every 5,000 to 8,000 miles. However, if you utilize our seasonal changeover service, we effectively rotate your tires each time we reinstall them, ensuring balanced wear year-round.

Are my tires insured while in storage?

Yes, our tire storage facility is secure and closely monitored. We treat your property with the utmost care and maintain coverage to ensure your tires are protected against unexpected incidents while they are off your vehicle.

Do you mount tires that are already on rims?

Absolutely. We can perform tire installation whether your off-season tires are mounted on their own dedicated set of wheels or if they need to be swapped onto your current rims. We handle both scenarios quickly and safely.
