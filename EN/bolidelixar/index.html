Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our wedding planning services.

What exactly does a full-service wedding planner do?

A full-service wedding planner handles everything from budget creation and venue selection to vendor negotiations, design concepts, and day-of execution. We manage the entire process so you can enjoy being engaged without the stress of logistics.

Do I need a day of coordinator if my venue has a manager?

Yes. A venue manager represents the venue's interests (food, facilities), while a day of coordinator represents you. We manage your personal timeline, personal items, external vendors (florist, photographer), and handle family dynamics to ensure your specific vision is realized.

How far in advance should we start wedding planning?

For full-service planning, we recommend reaching out 12 to 18 months before your desired date. This allows ample time to secure top-tier venues and vendors. However, we are equipped to handle shorter timelines when necessary.

Do you handle destination wedding logistics?

Absolutely. We specialize in destination wedding coordination, managing travel logistics, multi-day event itineraries, and sourcing reliable local vendors in your chosen location to guarantee a seamless experience abroad.

How does a wedding coordinator save us money?

An experienced wedding coordinator helps you avoid costly mistakes. We review contracts for hidden fees, recommend vendors that fit your specific budget, and allocate your funds efficiently to maximize the impact of your design without overspending.
