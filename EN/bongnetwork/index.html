Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about our house cleaning service.

What is included in a standard house cleaning?

A standard house cleaning typically includes dusting all surfaces, vacuuming carpets, sweeping and mopping hard floors, wiping down kitchen counters and appliance exteriors, and fully sanitizing bathrooms. It is designed to maintain a baseline level of cleanliness for your home.

How is deep cleaning different from recurring cleaning?

While recurring cleaning handles weekly maintenance, deep cleaning is a more intensive service. It targets hard-to-reach areas, tackles built-up grime, includes detailed baseboard washing, interior window sill cleaning, and thorough scrubbing of bathroom tile grout.

Do I need to provide supplies for the maid service?

Generally, the professional maid service arrives fully equipped with all necessary commercial-grade cleaning solutions and equipment, including vacuums and mops. If you have specific products you prefer them to use on certain surfaces, you can usually provide them upon request.

What does move-out cleaning involve?

Move-out cleaning is designed to return an empty property to a pristine state. It includes everything in a deep clean, plus wiping down the insides of empty cabinets and drawers, cleaning inside the refrigerator and oven, and ensuring all fixtures are spotless for the next resident.

Can I schedule a one-time cleaning without a contract?

Absolutely. While many clients prefer the convenience of recurring cleaning, you can easily book a one-time service for special occasions, post-renovation cleanup, or just when you need an extra hand getting your home back in order.
