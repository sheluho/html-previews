Protect your investment from harsh environmental elements. Professional ceramic coating forms a durable, hydrophobic shield over your vehicle's clear coat. Frequently Asked Questions

What is ceramic coating and why do I need it?

Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer applied to your vehicle's exterior that chemically bonds with the factory paint. It creates a semi-permanent layer of protection that resists chemical etching, UV damage, and minor abrasions. It makes your car vastly easier to clean and provides a stunning, high-gloss finish.

How is auto detailing different from a standard car wash?

A standard car wash typically removes surface dirt and can sometimes introduce micro-scratches to your paint. Auto detailing is a meticulous, step-by-step process that involves deep cleaning, decontamination, paint correction (polishing), and applying protective layers. It focuses on restoring the vehicle to a like-new condition.

What does interior detailing include?

Interior detailing involves a comprehensive cleaning of the inside cabin. This includes thorough vacuuming, steam cleaning carpets and upholstery, leather conditioning, cleaning all glass surfaces, and sanitizing touchpoints like the steering wheel and dashboard. It removes deep-set stains and lingering odors.

How long does a full car detailing session take?

The duration depends heavily on the condition of the vehicle and the specific services requested. A standard interior and exterior detail might take 3 to 5 hours, while advanced paint correction and ceramic coating application can take an entire day or more to ensure curing times are met.

Is paint protection really necessary for new cars?

Absolutely. New cars often arrive with minor paint imperfections from transport and lack substantial factory protection. Applying paint protection, such as a ceramic coating, immediately upon purchase seals the pristine clear coat, preventing early degradation from bird droppings, tree sap, and road debris.
