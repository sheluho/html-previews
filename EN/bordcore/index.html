Frequently Asked Questions

What does a full-service wedding planner actually do?

A full-service wedding planner manages every facet of your event. We oversee budget allocation, source and contract top-tier vendors, design the overarching aesthetic, coordinate logistics, and manage the entire wedding weekend. Our goal is to take on the heavy lifting of the planning process so you can engage only in the fun, creative decisions.

Do we really need a day of coordinator if our venue provides a manager?

Yes. A venue manager represents the venue's interests—ensuring the building is secure, the kitchen is running, and the facilities are operational. A day of coordinator works exclusively for you. We manage your personal timeline, direct your specific vendors (photographers, florists, bands), handle your personal items, and ensure your specific vision is executed without relying on the venue staff.

Can you help us plan a destination wedding?

Absolutely. Destination weddings require specialized logistical coordination. We assist with sourcing reliable vendors in unfamiliar locales, managing guest travel communications, navigating different time zones, and ensuring that all legal and cultural requirements are met for a flawless celebration abroad.

When should we hire a wedding coordinator?

If you are seeking full-service wedding planning, we recommend reaching out 12 to 18 months before your desired date. If you only require a day of coordinator, securing our services 6 to 8 months in advance is ideal, as popular dates fill up quickly.

How involved will we be in the wedding planning process?

You are in complete control of the final decisions. Our role is to curate the best options, present them to you clearly, and execute the choices you make. We filter out the noise and handle the administrative burden, allowing you to be as hands-on or hands-off as you prefer regarding the granular details.
