Frequently Asked Questions

What makes a premium hand car wash different? ▼

A hand car wash utilizes meticulous manual techniques, premium pH-neutral soaps, and fresh microfiber towels to prevent the micro-scratches and swirl marks often caused by automated tunnel washes.

How long does an express wash take? ▼

The express wash is designed for efficiency without sacrificing quality. It typically takes between 15 to 25 minutes, providing a thorough exterior clean and shine for those on a tight schedule.

What is included in the auto wash membership? ▼

The auto wash membership generally includes unlimited or a set number of premium washes per month, priority scheduling, and exclusive discounts on deeper detailing services to keep your vehicle consistently clean.

Do you clean the interior of the vehicle? ▼

Yes, interior detailing services are available. This includes deep vacuuming, wiping down of all hard surfaces, leather conditioning, and interior glass cleaning to ensure the inside looks as good as the outside.

Are the cleaning products safe for custom paint jobs? ▼

Absolutely. The professionals we promote use high-grade, safe chemicals and specialized techniques designed to protect custom paint, ceramic coatings, and delicate clear coats from any damage.
