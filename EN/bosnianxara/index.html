Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is included in a deep cleaning service?

A deep cleaning is a comprehensive service that addresses areas often missed during regular upkeep. This typically includes detailed dusting of baseboards, cleaning inside window sills, intensive scrubbing of bathroom grout and fixtures, and a thorough wipe-down of kitchen cabinet exteriors. It is designed to reset the cleanliness baseline of your home.

Do I need to provide my own cleaning supplies?

Generally, professional house cleaning teams arrive fully equipped with high-quality, effective cleaning supplies and equipment. This ensures consistent results and saves you the hassle of maintaining a stocked supply closet. Specific preferences regarding products can usually be discussed with the provider.

How flexible is the recurring cleaning schedule?

Recurring maid service schedules are designed around your lifestyle. You can typically choose from weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly visits. The provider aims to offer consistent appointment times to establish a reliable routine that keeps your home effortlessly clean.

Is a move-out cleaning required to get my deposit back?

While requirements vary by lease, a professional move-out cleaning significantly increases the likelihood of a full deposit return. This specialized service addresses the specific standards expected by landlords and property managers, ensuring the property is left in exceptional condition.
