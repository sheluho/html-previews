Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about embarking on a bathroom renovation journey.

What is included in a full-service bathroom remodel?
A full-service bathroom remodel generally covers everything from the initial design consultation to the final touches. This includes demolition, plumbing and electrical updates, custom walk-in shower creation, bathtub replacement, premium tile installation, and fitting new vanities and fixtures.
How long does a typical bathroom renovation take?
The timeline varies based on the scope of the project. A straightforward bathtub replacement might take just a few days, while a comprehensive master bathroom remodel involving structural changes and custom tile installation can take several weeks.
Can I convert my old tub into a walk-in shower?
Absolutely. Tub-to-shower conversions are highly popular for improving accessibility and modernizing the space. The contractors we promote specialize in seamlessly removing your old unit and installing a beautiful, safe, and modern walk-in shower.
Do you help with material selection like tile and fixtures?
Yes, part of a full-service experience involves guiding you through material choices. Professionals will help you select the best materials for your tile installation, vanity, and hardware that fit both your aesthetic preferences and your budget.
Is my bathroom remodel fully insured and licensed?
The service providers connected through this platform operate as fully licensed and insured professionals in their respective service areas. They handle all necessary permits required to ensure your bathroom renovation is completed safely and up to local codes.
