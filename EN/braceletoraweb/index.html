Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our residential moving company and services.

How far in advance should I book my moving company?

We recommend booking your movers at least four to six weeks in advance, especially if you are planning to move during the busy summer months or at the end of the month. Early booking ensures you get your preferred date and time.

What is included in your packing services?

Our packing services can be customized to your needs. We offer full-service packing where we box up your entire home, partial packing for specific rooms (like kitchens), or just providing the professional-grade materials if you prefer to pack yourself.

Do your local movers handle large or specialty items?

Yes, our teams are trained to carefully disassemble, transport, and reassemble large furniture items, including beds, dining tables, and sectionals. Please let us know in advance if you have exceptionally heavy items like pianos or large safes.

What makes a move qualify as a "long distance" move?

Generally, any move that crosses state lines or is over a certain mileage threshold (often 100 miles or more) is considered long distance. Long distance movers require different logistics, routing, and regulations than local relocations.

Are my belongings protected during the move?

Yes, as a professional moving company, standard valuation coverage is included with every move. We also offer options for full-value protection that you can discuss with your relocation consultant prior to moving day.
