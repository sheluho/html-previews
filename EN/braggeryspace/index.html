Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer motorized roller shades?

Yes, motorized roller shades are among our most popular window treatments. They offer exceptional convenience and can be integrated into many smart home systems, allowing you to control light and privacy with a remote, smartphone, or voice commands.

How do I choose between custom blinds and custom drapes?

Custom blinds provide precise light control and a clean, architectural look, ideal for modern or transitional spaces. Custom drapes add texture, warmth, and sound insulation. Many of our clients actually layer both to achieve maximum versatility and a rich aesthetic.

Are plantation shutters energy efficient?

Absolutely. Plantation shutters are securely mounted to your window frame, creating a solid barrier that provides excellent insulation. They help keep rooms cooler in the summer by blocking direct sunlight and retain heat during colder months, making them a highly energy-efficient choice.

How long does custom window treatment installation take?

Because all our shades, blinds, and shutters are custom-manufactured to your exact window dimensions, fabrication typically takes a few weeks. The actual installation day is usually completed within a few hours, depending on the number of windows and the complexity of the treatments.

What types of shades are best for a bedroom?

For bedrooms, blackout roller shades or cellular shades are highly recommended to ensure optimal darkness for sleeping. Layering these functional shades with custom drapes can add a touch of elegance while hiding the shade hardware when raised.
