Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your custom closet project.

How long does it take to install a custom closet?

Most standard reach-in closet and walk-in closet installations are completed within a single day. The overall timeline from your initial design consultation to final installation typically spans a few weeks, allowing time for custom manufacturing of your specific materials and closet organizer elements.

Can you design a walk-in closet for unusual room shapes?

Absolutely. Our custom closets are entirely bespoke, meaning we expertly design around angled ceilings, awkward corners, radiators, or irregular floor plans. We measure everything meticulously to ensure your new space is maximized efficiently.

What materials are used in your custom wardrobes?

We utilize high-quality, durable materials designed to withstand daily use. Depending on your aesthetic preferences and budget, options range from premium thermally fused laminates to real wood veneers, all featuring sturdy hardware and smooth-glide drawer mechanisms.

Do I need to empty my current reach-in closet before the consultation?

No, it is actually helpful for us to see your closet exactly as it is during the initial design consultation. This allows our designers to assess your current wardrobe volume, see how you naturally organize your items, and recommend the best closet organizer solutions to solve your specific challenges.

Are your closet organizer systems adjustable over time?

Yes, many of our track-based and modular custom closet systems feature adjustable shelving and hanging rods. This means that as your wardrobe changes—such as needing more long-hang space for winter coats—you can easily reposition elements to adapt to your evolving needs.
