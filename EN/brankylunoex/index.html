Frequently Asked Questions

Details regarding our promoted catering services.

What types of events do you cater?

The services showcased encompass a full spectrum of gatherings. This includes luxury wedding catering, high-profile corporate catering, galas, fundraisers, and intimate private event catering. Each service is scaled and customized to the specific nature of the occasion.

Do you offer both plated dinner and buffet catering options?

Absolutely. The culinary teams are highly proficient in multiple service styles. You can select a formal, multi-course plated dinner for a traditional feel, or opt for elegantly designed buffet catering and interactive food stations to encourage mingling among your guests.

Can dietary restrictions and allergies be accommodated?

Yes, catering menus are fully customizable. The chefs routinely prepare exquisite alternatives for vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, and other specific dietary requirements, ensuring every guest enjoys a meal of the same high caliber as the main menu.

How far in advance should we book wedding catering?

For prime wedding dates, particularly in the spring and fall seasons, it is highly recommended to secure your wedding catering services 9 to 12 months in advance. This allows ample time for comprehensive menu design, tastings, and logistical planning.

Are staffing and event rentals included in the service?

Comprehensive event catering goes beyond just the food. Professional staffing—including chefs, servers, and bartenders—is typically coordinated as part of the proposal. Assistance with sourcing high-quality rentals such as linens, china, and glassware is also a standard part of the planning process.
