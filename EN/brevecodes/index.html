Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about custom woodwork and design.

What is the advantage of choosing custom cabinets over pre-fabricated options?
Custom cabinets are built specifically for your space, eliminating awkward gaps and maximizing storage. Unlike mass-produced units, they offer limitless choices in wood species, finishes, and hardware. This tailored approach ensures a perfect integration with your specific kitchen design and structural requirements.
Should I visit a kitchen showroom before finalizing my design?
Absolutely. Visiting a kitchen showroom allows you to experience the tactile quality of the materials, observe the smooth action of high-end drawer glides, and see how different finishes interact with light. It is a critical step in ensuring the final product aligns with your vision.
What are shaker cabinets, and why are they so popular?
Shaker cabinets feature a classic, simple design characterized by a five-piece door with a recessed center panel. Their popularity stems from their timeless versatility; they seamlessly blend into traditional, transitional, and contemporary kitchen designs without looking dated.
How long does the custom kitchen design and build process typically take?
The timeline varies based on the complexity of the project and current manufacturing schedules. Generally, the initial consultation and kitchen design phase takes a few weeks, followed by 8 to 12 weeks for the actual crafting of the custom cabinets before installation begins.
Do manufacturers provide installation services?
Policies vary by provider. Many premium manufacturers offer professional installation to ensure their custom cabinets are fitted perfectly, while others work closely with your chosen general contractor. This detail is usually finalized during the initial kitchen design consultations.
