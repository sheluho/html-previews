Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a standard house cleaning?

A standard house cleaning covers the essential upkeep of your home. This typically includes dusting surfaces, vacuuming carpets, mopping hard floors, thoroughly sanitizing bathrooms, and wiping down kitchen counters and exterior appliances.

Do I need to provide my own cleaning supplies?

No, you do not. The professional maid service arrives fully equipped with all necessary high-quality cleaning supplies and state-of-the-art equipment to ensure a flawless clean.

What makes deep cleaning different from a regular clean?

Deep cleaning is a much more intensive process. It involves detailed attention to areas often missed during regular upkeep, such as hand-wiping baseboards, cleaning inside appliances, scrubbing grout lines, and addressing hard-to-reach areas.

Can I easily schedule a recurring cleaning?

Absolutely! We make it simple to connect with providers for weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly recurring cleaning options to perfectly fit your lifestyle and keep your home consistently fresh.

Are the house cleaners fully vetted and insured?

Yes. Every professional introduced through our platform is thoroughly vetted, rigorously trained, and fully insured, ensuring total peace of mind while they service your home.
