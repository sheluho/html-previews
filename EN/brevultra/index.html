Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our auto detailing and ceramic coating solutions.

What exactly is a ceramic coating and how does it work?

A ceramic coating is a liquid polymer applied to the exterior of a vehicle. It chemically bonds with the factory paint, creating a rigid layer of protection. This semi-permanent bond does not wash away or break down quickly, offering superior hydrophobic properties, UV resistance, and a deep, glossy finish compared to traditional wax.

How long does interior detailing usually take?

Comprehensive interior detailing typically takes between 2 to 5 hours, depending on the condition and size of the vehicle. This process includes deep-cleaning the carpets, sanitizing surfaces, conditioning leather, and ensuring every crevice is meticulously addressed.

Does paint protection remove existing scratches?

Paint protection itself (like a coating or film) does not remove scratches; it seals the paint in its current condition. However, our services always include a preliminary paint correction stage where we machine polish the exterior to remove swirl marks and light scratches before applying any protective layer.

How often should I have my car professionally detailed?

For standard maintenance, a professional auto detailing service is recommended every 4 to 6 months. If you have a ceramic coating applied, simple maintenance washes are usually sufficient, with a professional inspection and top-up detail done once a year to maintain the coating's warranty and performance.

Can you detail classic or exotic vehicles?

Absolutely. We specialize in handling delicate, classic, and exotic vehicles. We use specialized, pH-neutral products and gentle techniques tailored specifically to older single-stage paints and sensitive exotic interior materials to ensure safe and stunning results.
