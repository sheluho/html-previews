Frequently Asked Questions

Insights into the kitchen remodeling process.

What does a turnkey kitchen remodel entail?

A turnkey kitchen remodel means that the service provider handles every aspect of the project from start to finish. This includes the initial layout design, sourcing custom cabinets and countertops, obtaining necessary permits, demolition, and full installation, allowing you to walk into a completely finished space without managing multiple contractors.

How long does a custom kitchen renovation typically take?

Timelines vary based on the scope of the project and material availability. A standard kitchen remodeling project can take anywhere from 4 to 8 weeks once construction begins. Custom cabinet fabrication often requires additional lead time prior to the start of demolition.

Can I choose specific materials for my countertops?

Absolutely. The contractors offer a wide array of premium materials including granite, quartz, marble, and solid surface options. During the design phase, you will be guided through the benefits of each material to ensure your new countertops match your aesthetic and functional needs.

Are the cabinets pre-fabricated or custom built?

The focus is on delivering true custom kitchen cabinets that are built to the exact specifications of your space. While semi-custom options may be available depending on the provider, the primary offering highlighted here is premium, fully customized cabinetry that maximizes storage and elevates design.

Do I need to hire my own designer?

No external designer is necessary. As part of a full-service kitchen renovation company offering, comprehensive design consultations are included. Experienced professionals will work closely with you to draft layouts, select finishes, and finalize the vision before any construction begins.
