Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I schedule lawn mowing? +

For optimal health and appearance, weekly lawn mowing is generally recommended during the active growing season. Adjustments may be made during periods of drought or slower growth to avoid stressing the turf.

What does a standard lawn care fertilization program include? +

A standard program includes strategically timed applications of essential nutrients, weed control, and targeted treatments for common local pests. The exact mix is tailored to your soil type and grass species.

Do you promote complete landscape installation services? +

Yes, Brickelaero connects you with providers who handle full landscape installation, including garden design, planting, hardscaping, and establishing new lawns from seed or sod.

When is the best time for tree service and pruning? +

Late winter or early spring, while trees are still dormant, is typically the best time for major structural pruning. However, dead, diseased, or hazardous branches should be addressed by a tree service professional immediately.

How do I get an estimate for my property? +

You can easily request a consultation by filling out the contact form on this page or calling the listed number. The service provider will typically conduct an on-site property evaluation to provide an accurate estimate.
