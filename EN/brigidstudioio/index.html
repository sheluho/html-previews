Frequently Asked Questions

What materials are used in your custom furniture?
The custom furniture featured is crafted from premium, sustainably sourced solid hardwoods, high-quality veneers, and durable hardware. Upholstered items like a custom sofa utilize top-tier fabrics and high-resilience foams to ensure long-lasting comfort and structural integrity.
Can I order a custom dining table in a specific size?
Absolutely. As a bespoke furniture maker, the dimensions, wood species, and finishes can be tailored to perfectly fit your dining room. You can specify exact lengths, widths, and seating capacities.
How long does it take to craft bespoke furniture?
Because every piece of handmade furniture is built to order, lead times typically range from 8 to 12 weeks. This ensures ample time for material selection, meticulous crafting, and rigorous quality checks before delivery.
Do you offer design assistance for a custom sofa?
Yes, design consultations are a core part of the process. Showroom experts will guide you through selecting the right silhouettes, cushion fill densities, and upholstery fabrics to ensure your custom sofa meets your aesthetic and functional needs.
Where can I view the furniture in person?
You can explore various designs, materials, and finished pieces directly at the custom furniture maker and showroom location. Visiting the showroom allows you to experience the texture, scale, and craftsmanship of the handmade furniture firsthand.
