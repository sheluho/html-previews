Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about planning your custom storage.

How much space is required for a walk-in closet?

Generally, a true walk-in closet requires a minimum space of about 4 feet by 4 feet, but larger dimensions allow for features like central islands and extensive shelving. Even with smaller footprints, custom designs can maximize the utility of the area.

Can a reach-in closet be upgraded without changing the walls?

Absolutely. A custom closet organizer is designed to fit precisely within your existing wall dimensions. By utilizing adjustable shelving, double-hang rods, and built-in drawers, we can drastically increase your storage capacity without any structural demolition.

What materials are used for the wardrobe installations?

Installations typically utilize premium, durable materials ranging from high-density engineered woods with thermal-fused laminate finishes to solid wood veneers. These materials ensure longevity, resist warping, and provide a beautiful aesthetic finish.

How long does a custom closet installation take?

While the design and manufacturing phase takes a few weeks to ensure precision, the actual installation of a custom closet or wardrobe is usually completed within one to two days, minimizing disruption to your home.

Are accessories included in the closet organizer systems?

Yes, closet designs can be fully customized with a wide array of accessories. Options include valet rods, pull-out hampers, jewelry trays, specialized shoe racks, and tie or belt organizers, all integrated seamlessly into the design.
