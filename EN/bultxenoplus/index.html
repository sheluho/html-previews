Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the wedding photography services we promote.

What is included in a typical wedding photography package?

Most packages promoted through our partners include full-day coverage, a secondary photographer, high-resolution edited digital images, and an online gallery for easy sharing. Specific details vary by the photographer you select.

Do you promote destination wedding photography?

Yes! We proudly feature photographers who specialize in destination wedding photography. Travel fees are typically customized based on the location and duration of your celebration.

Are engagement photos required before the wedding?

While not mandatory, engagement photos are highly recommended. It allows you to become comfortable with your wedding photographer and results in natural, relaxed portraits that can be used for save-the-dates.

How long does it take to receive our final gallery?

Delivery times depend on the specific studio's workflow and the season. Generally, couples can expect to receive their fully edited digital gallery within 6 to 8 weeks following the event.

What is the aesthetic of the bridal portraits you feature?

The partners we promote focus on a blend of authentic, photojournalistic moments and refined, fine-art portraiture. The goal is to capture the genuine atmosphere while ensuring you look your absolute best.
