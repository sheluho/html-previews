Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer tastings for wedding catering?

Yes, we arrange private tasting sessions for engaged couples to ensure every flavor profile matches your expectations for the big day.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely. Whether you need vegan, gluten-free, or allergy-conscious options, we can customize your plated dinner or buffet catering to keep all guests safe and satisfied.

How far in advance should we book for corporate catering?

For large corporate events, we recommend booking at least 4 to 6 weeks in advance. However, we can often accommodate smaller luncheon requests with shorter notice.

Do you provide staffing and equipment?

Yes, our full-service event catering includes professional servers, bartenders, and necessary dining equipment like chafing dishes and serving utensils.

Is there a minimum guest count for events?

Minimums vary depending on the style of service and the date. Please reach out to us directly to discuss the specifics of your event size and budget.
