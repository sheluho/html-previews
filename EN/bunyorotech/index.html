Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our remodeling process.

What exactly does a "turnkey kitchen" remodel include?
A turnkey kitchen means we handle absolutely everything required to deliver a finished space ready for use. This includes demolition, electrical and plumbing updates, drywall, flooring, installation of custom cabinets, fitting countertops, and final painting. You only deal with one project manager from start to finish.
Can you design a completely custom kitchen layout?
Yes. We specialize in altering existing floor plans to maximize flow and functionality. Whether you want to remove a wall to create an open concept, add a large central island, or optimize storage, our design team will map out a custom kitchen that fits your specific lifestyle.
What types of materials do you use for countertops?
We work with a wide variety of premium surfaces depending on your budget and aesthetic preferences. Our most popular installations include durable quartz, natural granite, and luxury marble. We guide you through the selection process to ensure you choose a material that matches your cooking habits and design goals.
Do you offer custom cabinets or prefabricated options?
We specialize in custom cabinets designed to fit the exact dimensions of your space, maximizing every inch of storage. However, for projects with strict budget constraints or tighter timelines, we can also source and expertly install high-quality semi-custom or prefabricated cabinetry.
How long does a typical kitchen remodeling project take?
The timeline varies based on the scope of the renovation. A straightforward update might take 3 to 5 weeks, while a full-scale custom kitchen tear-down involving structural changes can take 8 to 12 weeks. We provide a detailed project schedule before construction begins so you know exactly what to expect.
