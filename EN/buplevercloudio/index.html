Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical windshield replacement take?

Most standard windshield replacements are completed within one to two hours. However, you will need to wait an additional hour or more for the urethane adhesive to safely cure before driving.

Can you perform a chip repair instead of replacing the whole glass?

Yes, if the chip or crack is smaller than a quarter and is not located directly in the driver's line of sight, a simple chip repair is often possible, saving you time and money.

Do your partners handle car window replacement for all vehicle makes?

Absolutely. The auto glass professionals we promote are equipped to handle windshields, side windows, and rear glass for virtually all domestic and foreign makes and models.

Will my auto insurance cover the cost of auto glass repair?

Many comprehensive insurance policies cover chip repair with no out-of-pocket cost to you, and full replacements may simply require paying your deductible. The service providers can often help process the insurance claims directly.

Is mobile auto glass service available?

Yes, mobile service is highly common. Technicians can travel to your home or workplace to perform the windshield replacement or repair on-site, offering maximum convenience for your schedule.
