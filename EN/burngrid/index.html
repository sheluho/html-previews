Frequently Asked Questions

What are the benefits of spray foam insulation?

Spray foam provides an airtight seal that traditional materials often miss. It offers superior thermal resistance, helps prevent moisture buildup, and can significantly lower your monthly heating and cooling costs by keeping conditioned air inside.

How do I know if I need new attic insulation?

Key indicators that your attic needs attention include fluctuating indoor temperatures, unusually high energy bills, drafts in upper rooms, and ice dams forming on your roof during winter months.

What is the difference between vinyl siding and fiber cement siding?

Vinyl siding is highly cost-effective, lightweight, and requires very little maintenance. Fiber cement siding is a thicker, heavier material that offers extreme durability against harsh weather, fire resistance, and a premium appearance that closely mimics real wood.

Will new house siding improve my home's value?

Yes, updating your exterior is consistently ranked as one of the top ways to increase curb appeal and overall property value. It makes a strong first impression on potential buyers and signals that the home has been well-maintained.

How long does home insulation last?

The lifespan depends on the material used. High-quality materials like closed-cell spray foam can last the lifetime of your home if left undisturbed. Traditional fiberglass may settle over 15-20 years and require a top-up to maintain optimal efficiency.
