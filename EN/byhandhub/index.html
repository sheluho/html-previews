Frequently Asked Questions

What types of kitchen cabinets do you manufacture?

We manufacture a wide variety of custom cabinets, focusing heavily on premium wood finishes, contemporary flat-panel styles, and highly sought-after shaker cabinets. Every piece is crafted in-house to ensure optimal quality control.

Can I visit your kitchen showroom to see samples?

Absolutely. Our kitchen showroom is designed to help you visualize your future space. We display full kitchen vignettes, hardware selections, and various finish options for our custom cabinets.

Do you offer custom kitchen design services?

Yes. Expert kitchen design is a core part of what we do. We collaborate with you to create a blueprint that maximizes space, improves workflow, and seamlessly integrates your chosen cabinetry.

What is the difference between custom cabinets and pre-fabricated?

Pre-fabricated cabinets come in standard, unchangeable sizes and frequently use lower-grade materials. Our custom cabinets are built to the exact millimeter of your kitchen's dimensions using high-quality woods, ensuring no wasted space and superior longevity.

Are shaker cabinets a good choice for modern kitchens?

Shaker cabinets are incredibly versatile. While rooted in traditional craftsmanship, their clean, unadorned lines make them a perfect fit for transitional and modern kitchen design when paired with contemporary hardware and countertops.
