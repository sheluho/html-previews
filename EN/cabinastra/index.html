Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our professional house cleaning service.

What is included in a standard house cleaning?

A standard house cleaning covers the essential tasks to keep your home tidy. This generally includes dusting all surfaces, vacuuming carpets, mopping hard floors, emptying trash, and thoroughly cleaning the bathrooms and kitchen surfaces. It's the perfect baseline for a recurring maid service.

How does deep cleaning differ from standard cleaning?

Deep cleaning is a much more intensive process. While standard cleaning focuses on visible surfaces, deep cleaning tackles hidden dirt and buildup. It involves scrubbing baseboards, wiping down doors and frames, cleaning inside appliances (upon request), and detailed sanitization of high-touch areas.

Do I need to be home during the maid service?

No, you do not need to be home. Many clients prefer to provide access instructions (such as a spare key or garage code) so the team can perform the house cleaning while they are at work or running errands. All professionals are thoroughly vetted for your peace of mind.

What should I expect from move-out cleaning?

Move-out cleaning is designed to return the property to a pristine, move-in-ready state. It includes all deep cleaning tasks in empty rooms, ensuring cabinets are wiped inside and out, appliances are thoroughly cleaned, and every corner is scrubbed to help secure your deposit.

Can I customize my recurring cleaning schedule?

Absolutely. We understand every household has different needs. You can set up a recurring cleaning schedule that works best for you—whether that is weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly. The service can also be tailored to focus on specific rooms or tasks you prioritize.
