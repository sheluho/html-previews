Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our premium car wash service.

What is included in the premium hand car wash?

Our premium hand car wash includes a thorough exterior wash by our trained technicians, tire dressing, interior vacuuming, window cleaning, and a final hand-dry to ensure a completely streak-free finish. We treat every vehicle with the utmost care.

How does the auto wash membership work?

The auto wash membership is a monthly subscription that grants you unlimited washes for a single vehicle. Members simply drive up to our dedicated lanes, where their vehicle is quickly recognized, allowing them to enjoy expedited service and consistent care without the hassle of paying per visit.

How long does an express wash take?

Our express wash is designed for maximum efficiency and typically takes only 3 to 5 minutes to complete. It is the perfect solution for drivers who want a brilliant shine but are on a tight schedule.

Are your cleaning products safe for my car's paint?

Absolutely. We exclusively use high-quality, pH-balanced soaps, waxes, and conditioners that are specifically formulated to be tough on dirt but gentle on your vehicle's clear coat and custom paint finishes.

Do I need to make an appointment for a wash?

No appointment is necessary for our express wash or standard premium car wash services; you can simply drive in. However, if you are looking for specialized detailing services, we recommend contacting us in advance to reserve a dedicated time slot.
