Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about preparing for your wedding photographer.

How far in advance should we book a wedding photographer?

We recommend booking your wedding photography studio 9 to 12 months in advance, especially for popular seasons. Securing your date early ensures you get the team dedicated to capturing your specific vision.

Do you offer engagement photos as part of your packages?

Yes, engagement photos are a fantastic way for couples to get comfortable in front of the camera. The studio offers dedicated sessions that can be integrated into your overall photography coverage.

What is the approach to bridal portraits on the wedding day?

Bridal portraits are treated with a mix of editorial elegance and relaxed candor. The goal is to capture the details of your attire and your natural glow without making the process feel rigid or overly posed.

Can you handle destination wedding photography?

Absolutely. Destination wedding photography requires precise logistical planning and a keen eye for new environments. The Fox & Ivory team is experienced in traveling to capture your celebration wherever it takes place.

How many images can we expect to receive?

While the exact number varies based on the hours of coverage and the flow of the day, a full wedding day typically yields hundreds of fully edited, high-resolution images that tell the complete story of your event.
