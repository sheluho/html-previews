Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our landscaping and lawn care services.

How often should I schedule lawn mowing?

For most properties, weekly lawn mowing during the active growing season is ideal. This frequency prevents cutting off too much of the grass blade at once, which helps maintain a thick, healthy turf that naturally resists weeds.

Do you provide custom garden design?

Yes, our landscape installation services begin with a comprehensive garden design phase. We work with you to select plants, hardscaping materials, and layouts that suit your local climate, soil type, and aesthetic preferences.

What does your tree service include?

Our tree service covers structural pruning, disease and pest management, and deep root fertilization. We focus on preserving the health and safety of your large trees, ensuring they remain beautiful assets to your landscaping.

When is the best time for landscape installation?

Early spring and early fall are generally the best times for new landscape installation. The milder temperatures and natural rainfall help new plants and sod establish strong root systems before the extreme heat of summer or the deep freeze of winter.

Is your lawn care safe for pets and children?

We prioritize safety in all our treatments. When applying fertilizers or weed control, we provide clear instructions on how long to keep pets and children off the lawn—typically until the product is completely dry, ensuring a safe environment for your family.
