Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose the right residential moving company?

Selecting the right partner involves checking their licensing, reading independent reviews, and ensuring they offer the specific services you need, such as professional packing services or specialized long-distance transport. Cabrioleiqio simplifies this by promoting established, reputable providers.

Are packing services included in a standard quote?

Typically, packing services are offered as an add-on to the standard transportation quote. This allows you to customize your moving package based on your budget and how much physical work you wish to handle yourself.

Do your partner local movers handle fragile or antique items?

Yes. Professional movers are trained to handle delicate items, antiques, and fine art. They utilize specialized crating and wrapping techniques to ensure these valuables are protected during transit.

How far in advance should I book long distance movers?

For interstate or cross-country relocations, it is highly recommended to secure your long distance movers at least 4 to 8 weeks in advance, especially during the peak summer moving season.

What items cannot be loaded onto the moving truck?

For safety and legal reasons, movers cannot transport hazardous materials, flammable liquids, explosives, corrosive chemicals, or perishable foods. Always ask your moving provider for their specific restricted items list prior to packing day.
