Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about our remodeling process.

How long does a typical bathroom renovation take?

The timeline for a bathroom renovation varies based on the scope of the project. A simple bathtub replacement can often be completed in just a few days, while a full, custom bathroom remodel involving extensive tile installation and plumbing adjustments may take several weeks. We provide a clear schedule before any work begins.

Do you handle all the plumbing and electrical work?

Yes. As a comprehensive bathroom remodeling company, we manage every aspect of the project. This includes necessary plumbing, electrical work, drywall, and finishing touches, ensuring a cohesive and stress-free process.

Can you convert my existing tub into a walk-in shower?

Absolutely. Tub-to-shower conversions are one of our most popular services. We safely remove your old tub and install a custom walk-in shower that fits perfectly into the existing space, enhancing both accessibility and aesthetics.

What materials do you use for tile installation?

We offer a wide selection of premium materials for tile installation, including ceramic, porcelain, natural stone, and glass. Our design consultants will help you select the ideal materials that match your style preferences and durability requirements.

Do I need to purchase fixtures before the project starts?

No, you do not need to source your own fixtures. We provide a full turnkey service, supplying high-quality fixtures, vanities, and hardware as part of your bathroom remodel package.
