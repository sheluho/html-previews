Frequently Asked Questions

Insights into our window treatment solutions.

What is the difference between blinds and shades?

Blinds are "hard" window treatments composed of individual slats (wood, faux wood, metal) that can be tilted to adjust light. Shades are "soft" window treatments made of a continuous roll of fabric that are raised or lowered to cover the window.

Are plantation shutters energy efficient?

Yes, plantation shutters are among the most energy-efficient window treatments available. Because they are custom-built to fit your window frame tightly, they create a solid barrier that helps insulate against both summer heat and winter cold.

Can roller shades provide complete blackout?

Absolutely. Roller shades are available in various opacities, including specific blackout fabrics. When paired with side channels to block light gaps at the edges, they offer excellent room-darkening capabilities ideal for bedrooms and media rooms.

Do you offer motorized window treatments?

Motorization is a highly popular option for many of the products showcased, particularly roller shades and custom blinds. Automated systems provide convenience, enhance safety by eliminating cords, and can often be integrated into smart home setups.

How should I clean my custom drapes?

Regular maintenance involves gentle vacuuming with an upholstery attachment to remove dust. For deeper cleaning, it is highly recommended to consult the specific fabric care instructions, which often require professional dry cleaning to maintain the fabric's integrity and shape.
