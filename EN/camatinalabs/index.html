Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the premium car wash experience.

What is included in a premium car wash?

A premium car wash generally goes beyond a standard exterior rinse. It typically includes a thorough exterior wash, wheel cleaning, tire dressing, interior vacuuming, window wiping, and often a protective wax or sealant application to help maintain the clear coat.

Is the express wash safe for my paint?

Yes. Modern express wash systems utilize soft foam or specialized cloth materials combined with high-lubricity soaps. This minimizes friction and is designed to safely clean your vehicle without causing scratches or swirl marks on standard factory paint.

How does the auto wash membership work?

An auto wash membership allows you to pay a flat monthly fee for unlimited or a set number of washes during that period. It operates on a subscription basis, automatically renewing each month, providing convenience and significant savings for frequent washers.

How long does a hand car wash usually take?

Because a hand car wash involves careful, manual cleaning by technicians, it typically takes longer than an automated wash. Expect the process to take anywhere from 30 minutes to over an hour, depending on the size of the vehicle and the level of interior detailing requested.

Do I need an appointment for premium detailing?

While express services are usually drive-up, it is highly recommended to schedule an appointment for comprehensive hand washing and detailing services. This ensures that a dedicated technician is available to dedicate the necessary time to perfect your vehicle.
