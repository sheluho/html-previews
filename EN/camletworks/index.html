Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a deep cleaning?

A deep cleaning goes beyond standard surface work. It involves thorough scrubbing of baseboards, detailed wiping of doors and frames, dusting ceiling fans, cleaning the interior of windows, and removing built-up grime from kitchen appliances and bathroom tiles.

Do I need to provide the cleaning supplies?

No, professional maid service teams typically arrive fully equipped with all necessary cleaning supplies and tools, including vacuums and mops, to effectively clean your home. If you have specific products you prefer them to use, you can usually request this in advance.

Do I need to be home while you clean?

Not at all. Many clients prefer to provide access via a spare key, garage code, or lockbox. You are welcome to stay if you prefer, but the goal of recurring cleaning services is to free up your schedule so you can be out living your life while the house is cleaned.

What is the difference between standard and move-out cleaning?

Standard cleaning maintains a furnished home's cleanliness. A move-out cleaning is designed for an empty home and includes heavy-duty tasks like cleaning inside all empty cabinets, inside the refrigerator and oven, and thoroughly washing all baseboards to prepare the home for new occupants.

How do I schedule my house cleaning service?

Scheduling is simple. Use the contact form below to request a quote. Once you receive your estimate and select your preferred service (such as a one-time deep clean or weekly upkeep), you'll be connected directly with the professional team to finalize your date and time.
