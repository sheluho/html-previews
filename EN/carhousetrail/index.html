Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a standard house cleaning?
A standard maid service generally covers dusting all surfaces, vacuuming carpets and rugs, sweeping and mopping hard floors, emptying trash, and thoroughly cleaning the bathrooms and kitchen surfaces. It is designed to maintain a high baseline of cleanliness in your home.
Do I need to be home during the maid service?
No, it is entirely up to your preference. Many clients provide a spare key, garage code, or lockbox access so the professional house cleaning team can work while they are away at work or running errands.
How often should I schedule recurring cleaning?
Frequency depends on your household's activity level, presence of pets, and personal preference. Bi-weekly is the most popular choice for maintaining a consistent standard, while busy families with pets often opt for weekly visits.
What is the difference between regular and deep cleaning?
Regular cleaning focuses on surface-level maintenance and general tidiness. Deep cleaning is much more intensive, targeting baseboards, interior windows, inside appliances, and removing deeper buildup of grime that accumulates over time in hard-to-reach areas.
What is expected for a move-out cleaning?
Move-out cleaning is designed to return a property to a near-original state. It typically includes everything in a deep clean, plus cleaning inside empty cabinets and drawers, wiping down doors and frames, and ensuring the space is completely ready for a final walkthrough or new residents.
