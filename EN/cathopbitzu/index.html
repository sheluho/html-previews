Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions regarding home exteriors and efficiency.

What are the main benefits of spray foam insulation?

Spray foam expands to fill gaps and cracks that traditional batts might miss. This creates a superior air barrier that drastically reduces drafts, lowers energy costs, and minimizes the entry of outdoor allergens and moisture into your home.

Should I choose vinyl siding or fiber cement siding?

Vinyl siding is highly cost-effective, low-maintenance, and resistant to rot. Fiber cement siding offers a more premium, wood-like appearance and boasts extreme durability against fire, pests, and severe weather. The right choice depends on your budget and desired aesthetic.

How often should attic insulation be replaced?

While modern home insulation can last for decades, it may need supplementing or replacing if it becomes compressed, water-damaged, or infested by pests. If your home feels drafty or your energy bills are spiking, it is time for an inspection.

Can new house siding improve energy efficiency?

Yes, especially when paired with insulated backing or proper wall insulation during the installation process. High-quality house siding acts as a critical external shield, reducing thermal bridging and keeping your indoor climate stable.

Is winter a bad time for exterior renovations?

While certain materials like specific exterior paints require warmer temperatures, professional contractors can safely install home insulation, vinyl siding, and fiber cement siding year-round, provided extreme weather conditions are avoided.
