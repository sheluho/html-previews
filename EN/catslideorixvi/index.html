Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about our wedding photography studio.

How far in advance should we book a wedding photographer?

We recommend booking your wedding photographer at least 9 to 12 months in advance. Popular dates during the spring and fall seasons fill up quickly, and securing your date early ensures we have ample time to plan your engagement photos and wedding day timeline.

Do you offer engagement photos and bridal portraits?

Yes, absolutely. We believe engagement sessions are a fantastic way for us to get to know each other before the big day. We also offer dedicated bridal portraits, providing a relaxed environment to capture stunning, editorial-quality images of you in your wedding attire.

Are you available for destination wedding photography?

Yes, we frequently travel for destination wedding photography. Whether you are getting married on a tropical beach or in a historic European chateau, our team is equipped to travel worldwide to document your celebration with the same level of care and artistry.

How would you describe your wedding photography style?

Our style is an elegant blend of editorial fine art and documentary photojournalism. We aim to capture authentic, unscripted moments while also providing gentle guidance for stunning, cinematic portraits that look like they belong in a high-end magazine.

When will we receive our final wedding photos?

We know how excited you are to see your images! We typically provide a curated sneak peek within a few days of the wedding. The full, meticulously edited gallery is usually delivered within 6 to 8 weeks, ensuring each image meets our high standards of quality.
