Frequently Asked Questions

What makes a reliable residential moving company? ▼

A reliable residential moving company is fully licensed, insured, and has a proven track record of safe transport. We highlight partners who use trained personnel, operate well-maintained trucks, and provide transparent estimates without hidden fees.

Do you offer both local movers and long distance movers? ▼

Yes, our featured partners provide solutions for any distance. Whether you need local movers to quickly relocate your family across town or long distance movers equipped to handle state-to-state logistics, there are specialized teams ready to assist.

Are packing services included in a standard move? ▼

Packing services are typically offered as an add-on to a standard moving package. You can choose to pack your own belongings or hire professionals to handle the entire process, ensuring maximum protection using specialized boxes and wrapping materials.

How far in advance should I book my moving company? ▼

It is highly recommended to schedule your movers at least 4 to 6 weeks in advance, particularly during the busy summer months. Early booking guarantees that your preferred moving dates are secured and gives the company ample time to organize logistics.

Are my belongings insured during transit? ▼

Professional movers provide basic liability coverage by default. However, for complete peace of mind, most companies offer comprehensive valuation coverage options that you can purchase prior to moving day to fully protect high-value items.
