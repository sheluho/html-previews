Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I choose fiberglass doors for my front entryway?

Fiberglass doors are highly durable, energy-efficient, and resistant to warping, rotting, and rust. They mimic the beautiful look of natural wood without the heavy maintenance, making them an ideal choice for front door replacement.

Do you handle both entry doors and interior doors installation?

Yes, we specialize in comprehensive door solutions. Whether you need a secure, weather-tight entry door or want to upgrade all the interior doors in your home for better soundproofing and style, our installation team is fully equipped to help.

How long does a typical door installation take?

A standard single front door replacement generally takes half a day to complete, ensuring minimal disruption to your home. Interior doors can often be installed faster, depending on the framing and hardware requirements.

Will a new entry door improve my home's energy efficiency?

Absolutely. Modern entry doors, especially high-quality fiberglass doors, feature advanced insulation cores and superior weatherstripping. This tight seal prevents drafts, keeping your home warmer in winter and cooler in summer.

Do I need to replace the door frame when getting a new door?

It depends on the condition of your current frame. If the existing frame is rotted, damaged, or out of square, a full-frame door installation is necessary for security and proper operation. We will assess this during your initial consultation.
