Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my move?

For local movers, we recommend booking at least two to four weeks in advance, especially during the busy summer months. If you require long distance movers, it is best to secure your date four to eight weeks ahead of time to ensure truck availability and proper logistical planning.

Do you provide the boxes for packing services?

Yes, when you opt for our professional packing services, our teams arrive fully equipped with high-quality boxes, bubble wrap, packing paper, and specialized containers for wardrobes and fragile items. We bring everything needed to secure your belongings safely.

Are my belongings insured during the move?

As a reputable moving company, basic liability coverage is typically included with your move. However, for valuable items, we highly recommend discussing comprehensive valuation coverage options during your initial consultation to ensure you have the appropriate level of protection for your peace of mind.

Do I need to empty my dresser drawers?

Generally, lightweight clothing can remain in sturdy dresser drawers during transport. However, please remove all heavy items, books, fragile objects, jewelry, and important documents. If the furniture piece is exceptionally heavy or fragile itself, it is best to empty it completely before the movers arrive.

Can you move large specialty items like pianos or safes?

Yes, our network includes movers trained in handling heavy and oversized items. Please inform us about any specialty items—such as grand pianos, large safes, or bulky fitness equipment—when you request your estimate so we can assign the right crew and equipment to handle them safely.
