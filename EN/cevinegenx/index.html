Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a standard front door replacement take?

Typically, a standard front door replacement can be completed in a single day. Our professional door installation team will remove the old frame, prepare the opening, install the new door, and ensure all weatherstripping and hardware function perfectly before leaving.

Are fiberglass doors better than wood entry doors?

Fiberglass doors offer incredible durability and are highly resistant to warping, rotting, and extreme weather changes compared to traditional wood. They provide excellent energy efficiency and can be manufactured to mimic the beautiful texture of real wood, making them a top choice for modern entry doors.

Do you replace interior doors as well?

Yes, as a comprehensive entry and interior doors installation company, we provide full interior door upgrades. Whether you need solid core doors for better soundproofing or updated aesthetics for your bedrooms and closets, we have a wide selection to choose from.

Do I need to prepare my home before door installation?

We simply ask that you clear the area immediately surrounding the doorway on both the inside and outside of your home. This gives our installation crew the necessary space to work safely and efficiently. We handle the heavy lifting and the clean-up afterward.

Can you install doors that I have already purchased?

Our primary focus is providing a seamless, guaranteed experience by supplying and installing our own premium product lines. This ensures the highest quality control and allows the original provider to fully stand behind the product warranties. Please contact us to discuss the specifics of your project.
