Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if I need a windshield replacement or just a repair?

Generally, if a chip is smaller than a quarter and not in the driver's direct line of sight, a windshield repair is sufficient. If the crack is longer than a few inches, spreads to the edge of the glass, or significantly obstructs vision, a full windshield replacement is required to maintain safety.

How long does a car window replacement take?

A standard car window replacement usually takes about one to two hours to complete. However, you should factor in additional time for the adhesive to cure properly before driving the vehicle safely.

Is chip repair a permanent fix?

Yes, professional chip repair is designed to be a permanent solution. The specialized resin injected into the chip cures and bonds with the auto glass, restoring the structural integrity and preventing the damage from spreading further.

Do I need to replace my auto glass if the crack is very small?

Small cracks or chips should be addressed immediately, but they do not always require full replacement. A timely windshield repair can fix small blemishes before temperature changes or road vibrations cause them to expand into large, irreparable cracks.

Are the replacement windshields of high quality?

Yes, the providers in our network utilize high-quality auto glass that meets or exceeds Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) standards. This ensures that your new windshield fits perfectly and offers the same level of safety and durability as the original factory glass.
