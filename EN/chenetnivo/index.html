Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about our event catering service styles and capabilities.

What is the difference between a plated dinner and buffet catering?

A plated dinner offers a highly formal, restaurant-style experience where guests are served individual courses at their seats. Buffet catering is more relaxed, allowing guests to mingle, approach food stations, and select their preferred portions and dishes from a diverse spread.

Do you offer specialized corporate catering packages?

Yes. The corporate catering services highlighted on our platform cover everything from drop-off boxed lunches for board meetings to fully staffed holiday galas. Menus can be customized to accommodate the schedule and dietary needs of your team.

How far in advance should we book our wedding catering?

Wedding catering dates book up quickly, especially during peak summer and fall seasons. We recommend securing your date and beginning the menu consultation process at least 9 to 12 months in advance of your special day.

Can you accommodate specific dietary restrictions or allergies?

Absolutely. Event catering menus can be seamlessly adapted to include vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and allergy-safe options without ever compromising on flavor or presentation. Your guests' safety and satisfaction are paramount.

Do you provide bar services alongside food catering?

Yes, comprehensive event catering often includes professional bartending services. This can range from pouring beer and wine to crafting custom signature cocktails specifically designed to match the theme of your event.
