Frequently Asked Questions

What is the process for designing custom closets?

The process typically begins with an in-home consultation to measure your space and discuss your specific needs. From there, a 3D design is created so you can visualize the final product. Once approved, the installation team schedules a date to build out your new space.

Can you upgrade an existing reach-in closet?

Absolutely. A standard reach-in closet can be completely transformed by removing a single rod and shelf and replacing it with a comprehensive closet organizer system that utilizes vertical space efficiently.

Do you offer accessories for a walk-in closet?

Yes, custom designs often include a variety of specialized accessories. This can range from velvet-lined jewelry drawers and pull-out hampers to angled shoe racks and tie racks, ensuring everything has its proper place.

How long does a typical wardrobe installation take?

Most residential installations are completed within one to two days, depending on the complexity and size of the project. The teams work efficiently to minimize disruption to your home.

Are the closet organizer systems adjustable?

Many of the systems introduced are designed with adaptability in mind. Shelves and hanging rods can often be adjusted later on as your wardrobe or storage needs change over time.
