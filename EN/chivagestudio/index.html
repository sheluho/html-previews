Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a premium car wash service?

A premium car wash service goes beyond a standard rinse. It typically includes a thorough exterior wash, wheel and tire cleaning, protective wax application, and an intricate hand-drying process to prevent water spots and protect your vehicle's finish.

How does the auto wash membership work?

An auto wash membership allows you to pay a flat monthly fee for unrestricted access to specific express wash services. It is designed for drivers who want to maintain a consistently clean vehicle without paying individually for every visit.

Is a hand car wash better for my vehicle's paint?

Yes, a dedicated hand car wash is often considered the safest method for preserving a vehicle's clear coat and paint. Trained professionals use specialized microfiber mitts and premium soaps to gently lift dirt without introducing micro-scratches.

How long does an express wash take?

An express wash is engineered for efficiency and speed. Typically, the entire automated process from entering the wash tunnel to exiting takes between 3 to 5 minutes, making it highly convenient for busy schedules.
