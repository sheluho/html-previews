Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I schedule professional carpet cleaning? ▼

For most households, we recommend professional carpet cleaning every 12 to 18 months. However, if you have pets, children, or suffer from allergies, scheduling a deep steam cleaning every 6 to 12 months is advisable to maintain optimal air quality and prolong the life of your carpets.

Does steam cleaning effectively remove tough pet stains? ▼

Yes, advanced steam cleaning combined with targeted stain removal treatments is highly effective at breaking down and extracting stubborn pet stains and associated odors. The high temperatures and powerful extraction ensure a deep clean that surface scrubbing simply cannot achieve.

Is upholstery cleaning safe for all types of fabrics? ▼

Professional technicians assess your furniture's specific fabric type before beginning any upholstery cleaning. They utilize customized cleaning solutions and methods—ranging from hot water extraction to dry cleaning—to ensure delicate fibers are thoroughly cleaned without being damaged or discolored.

Do you provide specialized rug cleaning services? ▼

Absolutely. Area rugs require different care than standard wall-to-wall carpeting. The rug cleaning services promoted on our site include careful inspection, specialized dusting, and gentle washing processes designed to preserve the dyes and structural integrity of both synthetic and delicate natural fiber rugs.

How long does it take for carpets to dry after a deep clean? ▼

Drying times can vary based on humidity, temperature, and the specific cleaning method used. Typically, carpets take between 4 to 8 hours to dry completely after a professional steam cleaning. Technicians utilize powerful extraction equipment to remove as much moisture as possible to accelerate the drying process.
