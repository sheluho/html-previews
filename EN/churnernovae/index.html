Frequently Asked Questions

Insights on your upcoming remodel

How long does a typical bathtub replacement take?

Depending on the complexity of the plumbing and the specific materials chosen, a standard bathtub replacement converting to a walk-in shower can often be completed in just a few days. Our partnered professionals prioritize efficiency without compromising the quality of the bathroom renovation.

What is included in a full bathroom remodel?

A comprehensive bathroom remodel can include everything from structural layout changes, new plumbing fixtures, and electrical updates to custom tile installation, vanity replacement, and the installation of a modern walk-in shower. The scope is entirely tailored to your vision and requirements.

Are walk-in showers safer than traditional bathtubs?

Yes, walk-in showers are generally considered much safer, especially for individuals with mobility concerns. They eliminate the high step-over barrier of traditional tubs, reducing the risk of slips and falls. Features like built-in seating and grab bars can be seamlessly integrated into the design.

Can I customize the tile installation in my new shower?

Absolutely. Custom tile installation is a cornerstone of our bathroom renovation offerings. You can choose from a wide array of colors, patterns, and materials—such as ceramic, porcelain, or natural stone—to create a unique aesthetic that complements your home's interior.

Do I need to prepare my space before the contractors arrive?

We recommend removing personal items, toiletries, and any fragile decorations from the bathroom and the immediate hallway leading to it. The renovation team will handle the heavy lifting, demolition, and protective coverings to ensure the rest of your home remains pristine during the project.
