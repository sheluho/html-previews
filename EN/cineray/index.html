Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our door installation services.

How long does a typical front door replacement take?

A standard front door replacement usually takes between 4 to 8 hours to complete. This includes removing the old door and frame, preparing the opening, installing the new unit, insulating, and applying the final trim. Custom sizes or structural adjustments may require additional time.

Are fiberglass doors better than wood or steel?

Fiberglass doors are highly recommended for entryways because they offer an excellent balance of durability, energy efficiency, and aesthetics. Unlike wood, they do not warp, rot, or expand significantly with moisture changes. They also resist denting better than steel doors and can be manufactured to mimic the texture of real wood grain.

Do you handle both entry and interior doors installation?

Yes, Cineray is a comprehensive entry and interior doors installation company. We manage complete home upgrades, ensuring the design and quality of your interior bedroom, bathroom, and closet doors match the high standards of your exterior entrances.

Will I need to paint or stain the doors after installation?

Many of our premium fiberglass doors and interior doors come pre-finished from the manufacturer with high-quality, durable paints or stains. However, if you prefer custom colors or choose unfinished wood products, painting or staining will be required. We can discuss finishing options during your initial consultation.

How do I know if my door frame needs replacing?

If you notice soft wood around the hinges, visible rot, significant drafts, or if your current door sticks constantly despite hinge adjustments, the frame is likely compromised. During our assessment for door installation, we inspect the structural integrity of the rough opening and recommend a pre-hung unit (which includes a new frame) if the existing one is failing.
