Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose between custom blinds and shades?

Blinds feature adjustable slats that allow you to precisely angle the amount of light entering the room, making them great for living areas and home offices. Shades are made from solid panels of fabric that offer a softer look and roll or fold up, making them ideal for bedrooms where blackout capabilities are desired.

Are custom window treatments worth the investment?

Absolutely. Unlike off-the-shelf options, custom window treatments are measured to fit your specific window dimensions perfectly, eliminating light gaps and awkward sizing. They are crafted from superior materials designed for longevity, providing better insulation, aesthetics, and lasting value to your home.

Do you offer motorized roller shades?

Yes. We highly recommend motorized roller shades for hard-to-reach windows or for the ultimate in daily convenience. Motorized systems can be operated via remote control, smartphone apps, or integrated into your existing smart home ecosystem for automated light management.

What makes plantation shutters unique?

Plantation shutters are permanently affixed to your window frame, acting as a piece of architectural furniture rather than just a covering. They are incredibly durable, easy to clean, provide excellent thermal insulation, and are one of the few window treatments proven to increase a home's appraisal value.

Can custom drapes be paired with other treatments?

Yes, layering is a highly popular design choice. Pairing custom drapes with roller shades or wood blinds adds depth, texture, and versatility to a room. You can keep the shades drawn for privacy while leaving the drapes open to frame the window elegantly.
