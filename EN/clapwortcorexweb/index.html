Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about our moving company operations.

What areas do your local movers cover?

Our local movers service the greater metropolitan area and surrounding suburban districts. If your origin and destination are within a 50-mile radius, your relocation typically falls under our local operational guidelines.

How do you structure pricing for long distance movers?

Long distance moves are calculated based on the total weight of your inventory and the exact mileage between your current residence and your new home. We provide comprehensive estimates outlining these logistical costs upfront.

Are packing services included in the standard moving rate?

Packing services are offered as a customizable add-on to our standard moving packages. You can opt for full-home packing, partial packing for delicate items, or simply purchase professional-grade materials to pack yourself.

How far in advance should I schedule my moving company?

To ensure availability, especially during peak summer months or end-of-month dates, we recommend booking your move at least four to six weeks in advance. However, we do our best to accommodate short-notice logistics when our schedule permits.

Are there items your team cannot transport?

Yes, for safety and regulatory reasons, our movers cannot transport hazardous materials, flammable liquids, explosives, corrosive chemicals, or live animals. We will provide a complete list of restricted items during your initial consultation.
