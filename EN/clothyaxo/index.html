Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our catering services.

Do you offer customized menus for wedding catering?

Absolutely. We believe every couple is unique, and their menu should be too. Our culinary team will work closely with you to design a personalized menu, whether you prefer a formal plated dinner, family-style service, or an expansive buffet.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions and allergies?

Yes, we take dietary needs very seriously. Whether your guests require gluten-free, vegan, vegetarian, or allergy-specific options, our chefs are highly experienced in preparing delicious alternatives without compromising on flavor or presentation.

What is included in your corporate catering packages?

Our corporate catering services typically include menu planning, food preparation, delivery, setup, and high-quality disposable or rented service ware. We can provide anything from simple drop-off buffet catering to fully staffed executive luncheons.

How far in advance should we book your event catering services?

For weddings and large-scale corporate events, we recommend booking 6 to 12 months in advance to secure your date. For smaller social events or corporate drop-offs, we request at least a two-week notice, though we will always do our best to accommodate last-minute requests when our schedule permits.

Do you provide staffing and rentals?

Yes, we are a full-service catering company. We can provide professional servers, bartenders, and culinary staff for your event. We can also assist with coordinating rentals such as tables, chairs, linens, glassware, and china through our trusted partners.
