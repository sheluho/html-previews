Frequently Asked Questions

Expert insights on auto detailing and ceramic coating.

How long does a ceramic coating typically last?

Depending on the specific grade applied and your maintenance routine, a professional ceramic coating can last anywhere from 2 to 5 years. Regular hand washing and avoiding harsh automated car washes will help maximize its longevity and hydrophobic properties.

What exactly is included in an interior detailing service?

Our comprehensive interior detailing includes a thorough vacuuming of all carpets and seats, deep cleaning and conditioning of leather or fabric upholstery, sanitizing high-touch surfaces, cleaning the dashboard and center console, and streak-free interior glass cleaning.

Does paint protection fix existing scratches on my car?

Paint protection films and ceramic coatings themselves do not fix scratches; they are designed to prevent them. However, before applying any protection, we perform a paint correction process (machine polishing) to remove existing swirl marks, oxidation, and light scratches to ensure a flawless surface.

How often should I have my vehicle professionally detailed?

For optimal maintenance, we recommend a full auto detailing every 4 to 6 months. This schedule prevents the buildup of harmful contaminants on the exterior clear coat and maintains a hygienic, fresh-smelling interior environment.

Is your detailing process safe for premium leather interiors?

Absolutely. We use specialized, pH-balanced cleaners and high-grade conditioners specifically formulated for automotive leather. This ensures the material is cleaned gently without drying out, preserving its soft texture and preventing premature cracking.
