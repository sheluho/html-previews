Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a turnkey kitchen renovation?

A turnkey kitchen renovation means we handle absolutely everything required to complete your project. This includes design, demolition, plumbing, electrical work, drywall, flooring, and the installation of your custom cabinets and countertops. You simply turn the key to your beautiful new space.

How long does a custom kitchen remodel typically take?

The timeline varies depending on the scope and complexity of the project. A standard kitchen remodeling job usually takes between 4 to 8 weeks from demolition to final walkthrough. We provide a detailed schedule during your initial consultation so you know exactly what to expect.

Do you design and build custom cabinets?

Yes, providing high-quality custom cabinets is one of our specialties. We work with you to design cabinetry that maximizes your storage space, fits your specific floor plan flawlessly, and matches your desired aesthetic, whether modern, transitional, or traditional.

What types of countertops do you offer?

We offer a wide array of premium countertop materials. Our most popular options include durable quartz, natural granite, elegant marble, and solid surface materials. We will help you select the best countertops based on your cooking habits, budget, and design preferences.

Can I keep my existing layout during a kitchen remodeling project?

Absolutely. If your current footprint works well for you, we can perform a "pull-and-replace" remodel. We will update your space with brand new custom cabinets, fresh countertops, and modern fixtures without moving major plumbing or electrical lines, which can also be more budget-friendly.
