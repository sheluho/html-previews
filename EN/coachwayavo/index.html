Frequently Asked Questions

How often should lawn mowing be scheduled?

For optimal turf health, lawn mowing should typically occur once a week during the active growing season. This frequency ensures that no more than one-third of the grass blade is removed at a time, which prevents stress and promotes a denser, greener lawn.

What is included in landscape installation?

Landscape installation covers everything from preparing the soil and defining garden beds to planting shrubs, trees, and flowers based on a custom garden design. It may also include laying mulch, installing edging, and placing decorative stone to complete the outdoor aesthetic.

When is the best time for tree service and pruning?

While emergency tree service can happen anytime, standard pruning is best done during late winter or early spring before new growth begins. This dormant period minimizes stress on the tree and reduces the risk of disease transmission.

Do you help with comprehensive garden design?

Yes, professional garden design is a key component of the landscaping process. It involves assessing your property's soil, light conditions, and layout to select appropriate plant varieties and create a cohesive, sustainable outdoor living space.

Why is routine lawn care important?

Routine lawn care goes beyond just aesthetics; it prevents weed infestations, controls pests, and ensures the soil retains necessary nutrients. A well-maintained lawn also prevents soil erosion and provides a safe, comfortable area for outdoor activities.
