Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a windshield replacement take?
A standard windshield replacement typically takes between 60 to 90 minutes. However, we advise waiting an additional hour before driving the vehicle to ensure the urethane adhesive has properly cured and set for your safety.
Can a chip repair save my current windshield?
Yes, if the chip is smaller than a quarter and not in the driver's direct line of sight, a chip repair can effectively restore the glass. Acting quickly prevents the damage from spidering out into a larger crack that would mandate a full replacement.
Do you handle side and rear car window replacement?
Absolutely. We provide comprehensive auto glass services, including side door glass, vent glass, quarter glass, and rear back glass replacements. We also vacuum the interior to remove broken glass fragments.
Is the new auto glass structural?
Yes. The windshield is a crucial structural component of modern vehicles, supporting the roof and aiding in proper airbag deployment. We only use industry-approved auto glass and high-quality adhesives to ensure your vehicle remains structurally sound.
Should I wash my car immediately after installation?
We strongly recommend waiting at least 24 hours before taking your vehicle through a high-pressure car wash. This waiting period allows the seals and adhesives to fully cure, preventing any potential leaks or shifts in the newly installed glass.
