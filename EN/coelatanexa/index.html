Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our cabinet solutions.

What materials are used in your custom cabinets?
Our manufacturing partners utilize a variety of high-grade materials, including solid hardwoods like oak, maple, and cherry, as well as premium furniture-grade plywood for box construction. This ensures superior durability and a flawless finish for all kitchen cabinets.
Can I see the cabinets in person before deciding?
Absolutely. We strongly encourage visiting the dedicated kitchen showroom. There, you can explore full-scale kitchen displays, examine the build quality firsthand, and test the functionality of various drawer glides and hinges.
Do you offer custom kitchen design services?
Yes, comprehensive kitchen design is a core part of the service. Experienced designers will collaborate with you to create detailed layouts and 3D renderings, ensuring your new layout maximizes both space and aesthetic appeal.
Are shaker cabinets available in different finishes?
Yes, shaker cabinets are incredibly versatile and can be customized with a wide array of finishes. Whether you prefer a natural wood stain, a crisp white paint, or a bold custom color, the finish can be tailored to match your specific home decor.
How long does it take to manufacture custom cabinets?
Because each piece is built to order, manufacturing timelines vary based on the complexity and scale of the project. Typically, custom cabinet production takes several weeks from the finalization of the design. A precise timeline will be provided during your consultation.
