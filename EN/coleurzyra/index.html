Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to help you plan your upcoming move.

How do I prepare for the moving company to arrive?
Ensure that all pathways are clear of obstacles and secure any pets in a safe room. If you are handling your own packing, make sure all boxes are taped shut and clearly labeled by room. It is also wise to keep important documents and valuables with you personally.
Are packing services included in the standard quote?
Standard moving quotes typically cover the loading, transportation, and unloading of your items. Full packing services are usually offered as an add-on. We highly recommend requesting this service upfront if you want a fully hands-off moving experience.
What is the difference between local movers and long distance movers?
Local moves are generally defined as relocations within a 50 to 100-mile radius and are usually billed at an hourly rate. Long distance moves cross state lines or exceed standard local radiuses; these are typically billed based on the total weight of your belongings and the exact mileage.
How far in advance should I book my move?
For the best availability, especially during the peak summer season or at the end of the month, we recommend booking your residential moving company at least 4 to 6 weeks in advance.
What items will the moving company not transport?
By law, professional movers cannot transport hazardous materials, including propane tanks, paint, chemicals, and explosives. Perishable foods and live plants are also generally prohibited on long-distance hauls.
