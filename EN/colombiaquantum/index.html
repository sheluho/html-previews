Common Questions

Information to help you plan your kitchen transformation.

What does a turnkey kitchen remodel include? +

A turnkey kitchen project covers every phase of the renovation. It typically includes the initial design consultation, demolition, structural adjustments, installation of custom cabinets, plumbing and electrical updates, countertop fitting, and final finishing touches, meaning you don't have to hire separate contractors.

Can I keep my existing layout during a kitchen remodeling project? +

Yes, absolutely. If your current layout works well for you, the focus can shift entirely to upgrading materials—such as installing new custom kitchen cabinets, modern countertops, and updated fixtures—without moving plumbing or walls.

What are the best materials for durable countertops? +

Quartz and granite remain the top choices for durability in busy kitchens. Quartz is engineered to be non-porous and highly stain-resistant, while granite offers natural heat resistance and unique stone patterns. The professionals we connect you with can help you choose the best fit for your lifestyle.

How long does a full custom kitchen installation take? +

Timelines vary based on the scope of work. A straightforward replacement of cabinets and countertops might take a few weeks, while a major structural kitchen remodeling project can take several months. A precise timeline will be provided during your initial consultation.

Are custom cabinets worth the investment? +

Custom cabinets are built specifically to fit the exact dimensions and storage needs of your space, eliminating awkward gaps and maximizing utility. They are constructed with higher quality materials than pre-fabricated options, adding significant long-term value and functionality to your home.
