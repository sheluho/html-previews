Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about preparing for your perfect catered event.

What is the difference between buffet catering and a plated dinner?

Buffet catering offers your guests a wider variety of choices and encourages mingling, creating a relaxed atmosphere. A plated dinner is a more formal, sit-down experience where courses are served directly to guests at their tables, ideal for elegant wedding catering scenarios.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions and allergies?

Absolutely. The catering professionals we promote are highly skilled in accommodating vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, and allergy-specific requirements to ensure all your event catering guests enjoy a safe and delicious meal.

How far in advance should we book our wedding catering?

For wedding catering, it is highly recommended to secure your date at least 6 to 9 months in advance. Popular dates during peak wedding seasons book up very quickly.

Are tasting sessions available before finalizing the menu?

Yes, tasting sessions are a standard part of the planning process for weddings and large-scale event catering. This allows you to sample various dishes and refine your menu before the big day.

What is included in your corporate catering packages?

Corporate catering packages typically include menu planning, food preparation, delivery, professional setup, and breakdown. Options range from simple boxed lunches to elaborate executive buffets or formal plated dinners.
