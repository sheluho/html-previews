Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the carpet cleaning process take to dry?

Unlike outdated steam cleaning that can leave carpets wet for 12 to 24 hours, the advanced low-moisture system allows your carpets to dry in approximately one hour, minimizing disruption to your day.

Is the upholstery cleaning safe for delicate fabrics?

Yes. The upholstery cleaning process uses specialized solutions that are tough on trapped dirt and body oils but exceptionally gentle on fabrics, ensuring your furniture is safely revitalized.

Do you provide professional rug cleaning for area rugs?

Absolutely. Rug cleaning requires specific care depending on the fibers and dyes. The recommended service carefully extracts dust, allergens, and stains from area rugs without risking color bleed or fiber damage.

How effective is your stain removal process?

The stain removal techniques utilize oxygenated boosters that break the bond between the dirt and the carpet fibers. This makes it highly effective at removing stubborn spots, pet accidents, and high-traffic soil.

Why is this considered better than traditional steam cleaning?

Traditional steam cleaning relies on pumping gallons of water into your floors, which can lead to extended dry times and potential mold growth in the carpet pad. The modern low-moisture alternative cleans deeply while using significantly less water, making it safer and more convenient.
