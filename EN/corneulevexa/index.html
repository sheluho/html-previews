Common Inquiries

Details regarding our filming process and deliverables.

How far in advance should we book a wedding videographer?

We recommend securing your date 9 to 12 months in advance, especially for peak wedding seasons (spring and fall). Our calendar fills quickly, and booking early ensures our availability to dedicate the necessary time and resources to your event.

What is the turnaround time for a wedding film?

Your cinematic wedding video undergoes meticulous editing, color grading, and sound design. Typically, highlight films are delivered within 8 to 10 weeks following your event, ensuring every frame meets our high editorial standards.

Do you travel for event videography?

Absolutely. We regularly travel to capture destination weddings and upscale events. Travel arrangements and associated costs can be discussed and customized during our initial consultation to fit your specific itinerary.

Can we choose the music for our wedding video?

To maintain a cohesive cinematic experience and comply with copyright laws, we select fully licensed music from premium catalogs. We choose tracks that perfectly match the mood, emotion, and pacing of your footage, creating a truly bespoke film.

Are raw footage options available?

Our primary focus is on delivering polished, finalized wedding films that represent our artistic vision. However, we understand the desire to preserve every moment, and we can discuss options for unedited documentary edits or raw footage delivery depending on your chosen collection.
