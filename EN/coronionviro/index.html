Frequently Asked Questions

What types of entry doors do you install?

We install a wide variety of entry doors, focusing primarily on high-quality fiberglass doors, solid wood, and steel options. Our team can help you select a door that matches your home's architectural style while providing maximum security and energy efficiency.

How long does a front door replacement take?

A standard front door replacement typically takes about half a day (4 to 6 hours) from removal of the old door to the final installation and cleanup of the new one. Custom fittings or structural adjustments may require additional time.

Can you replace all the interior doors in my house?

Yes, we specialize in whole-house interior doors installation. Upgrading all your interior doors at once ensures a cohesive look throughout your home and can often be completed more efficiently than piecemeal replacements.

Why should I choose fiberglass doors over wood?

Fiberglass doors offer incredible durability and are highly resistant to the elements. Unlike wood, they won't warp, rot, or expand with humidity changes. Modern manufacturing techniques allow fiberglass to mimic the authentic grain and texture of natural wood, giving you beauty without the high maintenance.

Do you handle the disposal of my old doors?

Absolutely. As part of our comprehensive door installation service, our crew will safely remove and properly dispose of your old doors and any resulting construction debris, leaving your home clean and your new entryway ready to enjoy.
