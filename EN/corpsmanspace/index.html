Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I schedule lawn mowing?

For optimal health and appearance, we recommend scheduling lawn mowing on a weekly or bi-weekly basis during the growing season. Consistent care prevents overgrowth and pest issues while encouraging thick, resilient grass.

Do you provide custom garden design?

Yes, custom garden design is a core part of the landscaping services offered. Professionals will assess your property's soil, light conditions, and layout to recommend native plants, flowers, and hardscape features that thrive in your environment.

What does landscape installation include?

Landscape installation covers everything from planting new trees and shrubs to laying fresh sod, installing flower beds, applying mulch, and placing decorative stones. It transforms the approved garden design into reality.

When is the best time for property cleanups?

Major property cleanups are typically best performed in the early spring to prepare beds for new growth, and late fall to remove dropped leaves and prevent winter rot. However, post-storm cleanups can be scheduled year-round.

Do you offer tree service alongside regular landscaping?

Absolutely. Comprehensive tree service—including pruning, dead branch removal, and shaping—is available to keep your trees healthy and ensure they don't pose a safety risk to your property or home.
