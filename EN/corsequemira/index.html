Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer motorized window treatments?

Yes, we provide advanced motorization options for most of our products, including roller shades, blinds, and custom drapes. These can be integrated with your smart home systems for ultimate convenience and light control.

What is the difference between blinds and shades?

Blinds are "hard" window treatments made of individual slats (wood, faux wood, or metal) that can be tilted to adjust light. Shades are "soft" treatments made from continuous rolls of fabric that are raised or lowered to control light and privacy.

Are plantation shutters energy efficient?

Absolutely. Custom-fitted plantation shutters provide an excellent barrier against heat transfer. They help keep your home cooler in the summer and warmer in the winter, potentially lowering your energy costs.

How do I know which fabric to choose for custom drapes?

During your consultation, our design specialists will bring fabric samples to your home. We evaluate your room's lighting, existing decor, and your functional needs (like blackout lining for bedrooms) to help you select the perfect material.

Do you provide professional installation?

Yes, all of our custom window treatments are measured and installed by experienced professionals. This ensures a perfect fit, flawless operation, and a clean, finished look in your home.
