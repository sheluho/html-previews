Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a deep cleaning?
Deep cleaning goes far beyond a standard maid service. It involves intensive scrubbing of baseboards, wiping down interior doors, cleaning inside window sills, detailed dusting of blinds, and thoroughly sanitizing bathrooms and kitchens to remove deep-seated dirt.
Do I need to be home during the house cleaning?
No, it is entirely up to you. Many clients provide entry instructions (like a lockbox code or hidden key) so the professional team can complete the maid service while they are at work or running errands.
Are cleaning supplies and equipment provided?
Yes. The professionals arrive fully equipped with high-quality, effective cleaning supplies and vacuums. If you require specific products to be used on certain surfaces, you can request that the team uses the supplies you provide.
How does move-out cleaning differ from regular cleaning?
Move-out cleaning is performed in an empty home and includes tasks not typically covered in recurring cleaning, such as cleaning inside all empty cabinets and drawers, wiping down the inside of the refrigerator and oven, and intensive cleaning of floors and fixtures to prepare the home for the next occupant.
How do I set up recurring cleaning services?
Simply fill out the inquiry form on our website to request an introduction. You can choose a weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly schedule that best fits your lifestyle, ensuring your home remains consistently pristine.
