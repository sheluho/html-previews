Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about our wedding photography studio.

How far in advance should we book our wedding photographer?

We strongly recommend securing your date 9 to 12 months in advance, especially for peak wedding seasons and destination wedding photography services. This ensures ample time for planning, consultations, and scheduling your engagement photos.

Do you offer destination wedding photography packages?

Absolutely. We specialize in destination wedding photography and are available to travel worldwide to document your celebration. We handle all our own travel logistics so you can focus entirely on enjoying your spectacular event.

Are engagement photos included in your collections?

Yes, many of our comprehensive wedding photography collections include a complimentary session for engagement photos. We find that this session is crucial for building rapport and ensuring you feel comfortable in front of the lens before your wedding day.

How long does it take to receive our final gallery?

You can expect to receive your fully edited, high-resolution digital gallery within 6 to 8 weeks following your wedding day. We dedicate significant time to the post-production process to ensure every image meets our studio's high artistic standards.

Can we schedule a separate session for bridal portraits?

We highly encourage it. A dedicated session for bridal portraits allows for a relaxed pace and creative exploration that the wedding day timeline rarely permits. It is a wonderful opportunity to capture the elegance of your attire in perfect lighting.
