Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between full-service planning and a day of coordinator? ▼

Full-service wedding planning means we handle everything from the very first concept meeting to managing the budget and booking vendors. A day of coordinator (or month-of coordinator) is for couples who have already planned their wedding but need a professional to take over the final weeks, manage the timeline, and direct the actual wedding day.

Do you specialize in destination weddings? ▼

Yes, we are experienced destination wedding specialists. We manage the unique logistical challenges of planning an event from afar, including sourcing reliable local vendors, understanding regional marriage requirements, and coordinating travel schedules for the wedding party.

When should we hire a wedding planner? ▼

For full-service wedding planning, we recommend hiring us 12 to 18 months in advance to secure the best venues and vendors. If you are looking for a day of coordinator, reaching out 6 to 8 months prior to your date ensures we are available and can properly integrate into your plans.

Will you work with vendors we have already chosen? ▼

Absolutely. While we have a curated list of trusted professionals we highly recommend, our wedding coordinator team is more than happy to collaborate with and manage any vendors you have already booked for your special day.

How do you handle wedding day emergencies? ▼

Our team arrives equipped with comprehensive emergency kits and years of problem-solving experience. We anticipate potential issues before they arise and quietly handle any unexpected hurdles behind the scenes, ensuring your experience remains completely stress-free.
