Frequently Asked Questions

What are the benefits of double hung windows?
Double hung windows feature two operable sashes that move up and down, providing excellent ventilation options. They are classic in design, making them a perfect fit for historical and traditional homes. Furthermore, modern double hung windows often tilt inward, making them incredibly easy to clean from inside the house.
How do energy efficient windows save money?
Energy efficient windows reduce the amount of heat transfer between the interior of your home and the outdoors. By keeping heated air inside during the winter and hot air outside during the summer, your HVAC system does not have to work as hard. This reduction in energy use directly translates to lower monthly utility bills over time.
Why choose custom vinyl windows over wood?
Vinyl windows offer a remarkable balance of durability, insulation, and low maintenance. Unlike wood, vinyl will not rot, warp, or require regular painting to maintain its appearance. It provides excellent thermal insulation and is generally more cost-effective, making it the preferred choice for modern replacement windows.
What should I expect during window installation?
Professional window installation involves carefully removing your old windows without damaging the surrounding structure. The installation team will prepare the opening, insert and level the new frame, and thoroughly seal the exterior to prevent drafts and moisture. Finally, they will clean the work area, leaving you with beautiful, functional new windows.
Are replacement windows customized for my home?
Yes. High-quality replacement windows are manufactured to the exact measurements of your existing window openings. This custom approach ensures a precise fit, which is critical for maximizing energy efficiency and maintaining the structural integrity of the installation.
