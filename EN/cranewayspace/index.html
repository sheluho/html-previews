Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our catering connections.

What types of events do you cater?

We promote comprehensive event catering services suitable for a wide range of occasions. This includes elaborate wedding catering, professional corporate catering for meetings and galas, and private social events ranging from intimate dinners to large celebrations.

Do you offer both buffet and plated dinner options?

Yes, the caterers we highlight specialize in multiple service styles. You can choose a dynamic buffet catering setup which is great for varied tastes and mingling, or opt for a formal, sit-down plated dinner for a more traditional and elegant dining experience.

Can menus be customized for dietary restrictions?

Absolutely. Creating a tailored experience means accommodating all guests. The culinary teams can design specific menus or adapt existing ones to ensure vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other dietary requirements are met without compromising on flavor or presentation.

How far in advance should I book corporate catering?

For large-scale corporate catering events or multi-day conferences, it is recommended to secure your date at least several months in advance. For smaller executive lunches or routine meetings, a few weeks' notice is typically sufficient, though earlier is always better to ensure availability.

What is the process to get a quote for my wedding?

To begin planning your wedding catering, simply use the inquiry form on our site. Provide the estimated guest count, date, preferred style (such as plated dinner or buffet), and any specific venue details. You will then be connected with the catering team to discuss menu options and receive a customized proposal.
