Frequently Asked Questions

What is typically included in a standard house cleaning?
A standard professional house cleaning generally includes dusting surfaces, vacuuming carpets and rugs, mopping hard floors, and sanitizing bathrooms and kitchens. This ensures the main living areas are kept hygienic and presentable on a regular basis.
How often should I schedule recurring cleaning?
The ideal schedule depends entirely on your household's activity level and personal preferences. Many clients opt for bi-weekly maid service to maintain a consistent baseline of cleanliness, while larger or busier households may prefer weekly visits.
Do I need to provide my own cleaning supplies?
In most cases, the professionals arrive fully equipped with their own high-quality cleaning equipment and specialized products. However, if you have specific product preferences or allergies, you can usually discuss providing your own supplies.
What is the difference between standard and deep cleaning?
While standard cleaning focuses on surface-level maintenance, deep cleaning involves a much more rigorous process. It typically includes wiping down baseboards, cleaning inside window sills, scrubbing grout, and reaching behind appliances to remove built-up grime.
Is move-out cleaning guaranteed to get my security deposit back?
While a professional move-out cleaning significantly improves the condition of the property and fulfills most standard lease cleaning requirements, the return of a security deposit ultimately depends on your specific lease agreement and the overall physical condition of the property beyond cleanliness.
