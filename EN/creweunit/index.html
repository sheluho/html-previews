Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I schedule maintenance for my HVAC system?
It is generally recommended to have your heating and cooling systems inspected at least twice a year. Schedule an air conditioner check in the spring and a furnace inspection in the fall to ensure optimal performance.
Do you provide information on full AC installation?
Yes, we highlight comprehensive AC installation services. Upgrading to a newer, more efficient unit can significantly reduce your energy bills and improve indoor air quality during the summer.
What is the main difference between a furnace and a heat pump?
A furnace burns fuel or uses electrical resistance to generate heat, making it excellent for extremely cold climates. A heat pump, however, transfers heat from the outside air into your home, and can reverse the process to act as an air conditioner in the summer.
Why is my air conditioner making unusual noises?
Strange sounds like grinding, squealing, or rattling from your HVAC unit usually indicate a mechanical issue, such as a loose belt, failing motor, or debris in the fan. It is best to have a professional inspect it immediately to prevent further damage.
Is it important to change air filters regularly?
Absolutely. Clogged or dirty filters restrict airflow, forcing your heating and cooling system to work harder, which increases energy costs and can lead to premature system failure. We recommend checking them monthly.
