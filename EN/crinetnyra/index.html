Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about commissioning bespoke furniture.

What is the process for ordering custom furniture?

The process begins with a consultation to understand your spatial requirements and aesthetic preferences. From there, design sketches and material samples are provided before the master craftsmen begin building your bespoke furniture.

How long does a custom dining table take to build?

Because every piece of handmade furniture is built from scratch, lead times typically range from 8 to 12 weeks. This ensures ample time for material sourcing, precise joinery, and proper finishing.

Can I choose specific wood species for my piece?

Absolutely. The beauty of custom furniture is total control over materials. You can select from a variety of premium hardwoods including Walnut, White Oak, Maple, and reclaimed materials based on availability.

Do you design custom sofas as well?

Yes, we facilitate the design of fully custom sofas and seating arrangements. You dictate the dimensions, cushion density, frame style, and upholstery fabric to perfectly match your interior space.

Where is the handmade furniture crafted?

The bespoke furniture showcased on our platform is crafted by master builders in dedicated workshops, including premier fabricators located in Brooklyn, NY, ensuring rigorous quality control and local artistry.
