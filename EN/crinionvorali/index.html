Frequently Asked Questions

What are the benefits of fiberglass doors over wood? ▼

Fiberglass doors offer incredible durability and are highly resistant to warping, rotting, and expanding caused by weather changes. They provide excellent insulation, improving your home's energy efficiency, and can beautifully mimic the look of natural wood grain without the heavy maintenance.

How long does a typical front door replacement take? ▼

A standard front door replacement usually takes a few hours to a full day, depending on the complexity of the frame and whether custom adjustments are needed. The process ensures minimal disruption to your daily routine while achieving a weather-tight fit.

Do you handle interior doors installation as well? ▼

Yes, we cover comprehensive interior doors installation services. Whether you need solid core doors for better soundproofing between rooms, or stylish modern panels to update your interior design, the process covers precise measuring, hardware fitting, and hanging.

Can I choose custom hardware for my new entry doors? ▼

Absolutely. The aesthetic of entry doors is greatly enhanced by the right hardware. You can select from a wide variety of premium handles, smart locks, and hinges to perfectly match your home's exterior style while maximizing security.

Why is professional door installation important? ▼

A door is only as good as its installation. Professional door installation ensures the frame is perfectly plumb and square, preventing drafts, uneven wear, and security vulnerabilities. It guarantees that the door swings smoothly and seals properly against the elements.
