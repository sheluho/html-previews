Frequently Asked Questions

How early should I book a moving company? +
It is highly recommended to book your moving company at least 4 to 6 weeks in advance, especially if you plan to move during the peak summer season or at the end of the month. Early booking guarantees availability and gives you ample time to coordinate packing services.
Do you offer full packing services? +
Yes, our featured partners provide comprehensive packing services. This includes bringing high-quality boxes, bubble wrap, and specialized containers for fragile items. You can choose full-home packing or partial packing for specific rooms like the kitchen.
Are your local movers insured? +
Absolutely. We ensure that the local movers in our network are fully licensed and insured. Standard valuation coverage is typically included, and full-value protection options are available directly through the moving provider for added peace of mind.
What is the difference between local and long distance movers? +
Local movers generally handle relocations within a 50 to 100-mile radius, often charging by the hour. Long distance movers handle intrastate or interstate moves beyond that radius, and their pricing is usually based on the total weight of your belongings and the distance traveled.
Can I pack my own boxes? +
Of course. You are welcome to pack your own belongings to save on costs. However, please note that moving companies typically will not assume liability for damages to items packed by the customer unless there is visible damage to the exterior of the box caused during transit.
