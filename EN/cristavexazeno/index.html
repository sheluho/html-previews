Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should we book a wedding videographer?

We recommend booking your wedding videography package at least 9 to 12 months in advance. Popular dates during peak wedding season fill up quickly, and securing your date early guarantees our team is available to craft your cinematic wedding video.

Do you travel for destination weddings?

Yes, our event videography team is available for travel worldwide. We love capturing love stories in new and inspiring locations. Travel fees are customized based on the destination and scope of the event.

What is the difference between a traditional and a cinematic wedding video?

Traditional videography often simply documents events as they happen from a fixed perspective. A cinematic wedding video utilizes creative angles, specialized lighting, high-end camera movement, and meticulous narrative editing to tell an emotional, movie-like story of your day.

Do you provide raw footage along with the final wedding film?

Depending on the package selected, we can offer organized raw footage as an add-on. However, our primary focus is delivering a perfectly polished, beautifully color-graded wedding film that you will want to watch repeatedly.

Can you cover multiple days of events?

Absolutely. Our event videography services are flexible. We frequently film multi-day cultural weddings, rehearsal dinners, welcome parties, and post-wedding brunches to ensure the complete story of your celebration is documented.
