Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to design and install custom closets?

The timeline varies depending on the complexity and scale of your project. Typically, after the initial design consultation and approval, manufacturing and installation of your custom closets can take anywhere from a few weeks to a couple of months. We prioritize careful craftsmanship over rushed timelines.

Can you design a closet organizer for a very small reach-in closet?

Absolutely. Small spaces are where intelligent design truly shines. Our closet organizer systems are specifically tailored to maximize every inch of a reach-in closet. We use adjustable shelving, multi-level hanging rods, and smart drawer placements to ensure you get the most out of your available space.

What materials are used in your wardrobe constructions?

We use a variety of high-quality, durable materials designed to withstand daily use while looking elegant. Our wardrobes and shelving units typically feature premium laminates, durable wood veneers, and robust hardware. During your consultation, we will present material samples that match your home's interior.

Do I need to empty my walk-in closet before the installation?

Yes, for our team to properly install your new walk-in closet system, the space must be completely clear of clothing, boxes, and existing wire shelving or rods. We recommend preparing the space a day before your scheduled installation to ensure a smooth and efficient process.

Are the shelves in your custom closets adjustable?

Yes, adaptability is a core feature of our designs. Most of our closet organizer systems feature adjustable shelving. This allows you to reconfigure your storage layout as your wardrobe changes over the years, accommodating taller boots, larger storage boxes, or changing seasonal items with ease.
