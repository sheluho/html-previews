Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical bathroom renovation take?

The timeline varies depending on the scope of the project. A simple bathtub replacement might take just a few days, while a complete full-service bathroom remodel, including extensive tile installation and structural changes, can take several weeks. We provide a detailed schedule during your initial consultation.

Do you specialize in walk-in shower conversions?

Yes, converting old tubs into modern, accessible walk-in showers is one of our specialties. We handle the demolition, plumbing adjustments, waterproofing, and custom glass installation to create a safe and luxurious showering experience.

Do I need to purchase my own materials?

As a full-service bathroom remodeling company, we can supply all necessary materials, from fixtures to the final tile installation. However, if you have specific items you've already purchased, we are happy to incorporate them into the project design.

Are your remodeling services fully licensed and insured?

Absolutely. We operate with strict adherence to local building codes and industry standards. Our teams are fully trained, licensed, and insured to protect your home and ensure the highest quality craftsmanship on every bathroom remodel.

Can you help with bathroom design ideas?

Yes! Our experts work closely with you during the initial consultation to understand your vision, functional needs, and aesthetic preferences. We provide guidance on layouts, material selections, and fixtures to ensure your new bathroom is exactly what you envisioned.
