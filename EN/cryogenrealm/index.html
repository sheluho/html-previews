Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about partnering with our team.

What is the difference between a wedding planner and a day of coordinator?

A full-service wedding planner is with you from the very beginning, helping secure the venue, manage the budget, and design the event. A day of coordinator typically steps in 4-6 weeks before the wedding to finalize timelines, confirm details with vendors, and manage the logistics on the actual wedding day.

Do you handle destination wedding logistics?

Yes, we specialize in destination wedding planning. Our team is experienced in managing complex travel itineraries, coordinating with international or out-of-state vendors, and creating unforgettable multi-day experiences for you and your guests.

When should I hire a wedding coordinator?

For full-service planning, we recommend reaching out 12 to 18 months in advance. If you are only looking for a day of coordinator, contacting us 6 to 9 months prior to your date is ideal to ensure availability.

Is full-service wedding planning right for me?

If you have a busy lifestyle, feel overwhelmed by the countless decisions, or simply want a professional to guide the entire design and execution process, full-service planning is the perfect investment. We take the stress off your shoulders so you can enjoy being engaged.

How do you handle vendor relationships?

We have spent years building relationships with the best vendors in the industry. We match you with professionals who align with your aesthetic and budget, and we serve as the primary point of contact to ensure everyone works together flawlessly on your big day.
