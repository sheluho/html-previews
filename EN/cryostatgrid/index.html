Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about customizing your home's natural light.

What types of window treatments do your partners offer?
Our featured network provides a comprehensive range of custom window coverings. This includes premium plantation shutters, cellular shades, roller shades, horizontal wood and faux-wood blinds, as well as elegant custom drapes and top treatments designed to fit any architectural style.
How long does the custom installation process typically take?
Because every product is made to order for your exact window dimensions, timelines vary based on the materials selected. Generally, standard blinds and roller shades take about 3 to 4 weeks from the date of final measurement, while custom plantation shutters and specialized drapes may take 6 to 8 weeks to manufacture and install.
Are your window treatments energy efficient?
Yes, significantly. Products like cellular honeycomb shades trap air to provide excellent insulation against Texas heat. Plantation shutters also create a solid barrier against solar heat gain. We can guide you toward the best materials to help lower your cooling costs and protect your interiors from UV damage.
Can roller shades be motorized and linked to a smart home?
Absolutely. Motorization is one of the most popular upgrades for roller shades and blinds. The systems offered by our partners can be operated via remote control, smartphone apps, or integrated seamlessly with smart home hubs like Amazon Alexa, Google Assistant, or Apple HomeKit for automated scheduling.
How do I clean and maintain plantation shutters?
Plantation shutters are incredibly easy to maintain compared to traditional fabric curtains. For regular upkeep, simply wipe the louvers with a dry microfiber cloth or a feather duster. For deeper cleaning, a slightly damp cloth with mild soap works perfectly. Never use harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaners on the finish.
