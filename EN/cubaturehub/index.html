Common Inquiries

Insights into maintaining your ideal environment.

How often should an air conditioner be serviced? ▼

It is generally recommended to have your air conditioner inspected and serviced at least once a year, ideally in the spring before the heavy cooling season begins. Regular maintenance ensures optimal efficiency and can prevent costly breakdowns.

What is the expected lifespan of a modern furnace? ▼

With proper installation and consistent annual maintenance, a high-quality modern furnace can reliably heat your home for 15 to 20 years. Neglecting filter changes or routine check-ups can significantly reduce this lifespan.

Are heat pump systems effective in extreme cold? ▼

Yes, advancements in technology have made modern heat pumps highly effective even in freezing temperatures. However, in regions with prolonged, extreme cold, they are often paired with a supplemental heating source for maximum comfort and efficiency.

What does a typical AC installation involve? ▼

A professional AC installation involves calculating the correct unit size for your space, safely removing old equipment, installing the new compressor and indoor coils, checking ductwork integrity, and testing the entire system to ensure balanced airflow and proper refrigerant levels.

How can I improve my overall heating and cooling efficiency? ▼

Beyond regular HVAC maintenance, you can improve efficiency by upgrading to a smart thermostat, ensuring your home is properly insulated, sealing drafts around windows and doors, and changing your air filters every 1 to 3 months.
