Frequently Asked Questions

How does the seasonal tire storage process work? ▼

When you schedule your seasonal tire installation, the service provider removes your current tires and transports them to a secure, climate-controlled facility. They remain safely stored, cataloged, and insured until you schedule your next swap.

When should I schedule a tire rotation? ▼

Generally, a tire rotation should be performed every 5,000 to 8,000 miles to ensure even tread wear. If you swap seasonal tires twice a year, that is often the perfect time to rotate your sets.

Do you facilitate complete tire replacement? ▼

Yes. Through our promotional partners, you can book complete tire replacement services. This includes removing old tires, mounting and balancing the new ones, and eco-friendly disposal of the discarded rubber.

Why is wheel alignment important after installation? ▼

Proper wheel alignment ensures your vehicle drives straight and your new tires wear evenly. Skipping an alignment can lead to premature tread wear and compromised handling, reducing the overall lifespan of your investment.

Are my off-season tires protected from the elements? ▼

Absolutely. The tire storage facilities utilized by our partners are indoors, dry, and climate-controlled to prevent dry rot and rubber degradation caused by extreme temperature fluctuations and UV exposure.
