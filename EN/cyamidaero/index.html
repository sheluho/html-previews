Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about our HVAC heating and air conditioning services.

How often should I schedule HVAC maintenance?

We recommend having your HVAC system inspected and serviced at least twice a year. Ideally, you should schedule an air conditioner tune-up in the spring and a furnace inspection in the fall to ensure optimal performance year-round.

What are the common signs I need a new air conditioner?

Key indicators include frequent breakdowns, rising energy bills, poor airflow, strange noises or odors, and uneven cooling. If your AC unit is over 10-15 years old and requiring costly repairs, an AC installation for a newer, efficient model is usually the best financial choice.

Can a heat pump replace both my furnace and AC?

Yes, a heat pump is designed to handle both heating and cooling. During the summer, it pulls heat out of your home to cool it down, and in the winter, it extracts heat from the outside air and moves it indoors. They are highly efficient alternatives for moderate climates.

Do you offer emergency heating and cooling repairs?

Yes, we understand that HVAC failures don't stick to business hours. We prioritize urgent heating and cooling issues, especially during extreme weather conditions, to restore your home's comfort and safety as quickly as possible.

How long does a typical AC installation take?

Most standard residential AC installations can be completed within a single day. Our team works efficiently to minimize disruption to your home while ensuring the new air conditioner is installed flawlessly and tested for peak performance.
