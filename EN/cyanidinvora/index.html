Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly does a turnkey kitchen renovation include? +
A turnkey kitchen renovation means we handle the entire project from start to finish. This includes the initial design consultation, demolition, structural adjustments, plumbing and electrical updates, installation of custom cabinets and countertops, and the final finishing touches. You simply hand over the keys and return to a fully completed, ready-to-use space.
Can I fully customize the design of my cabinets and countertops? +
Absolutely. Customization is at the core of what we do. Our team works closely with you to select the exact wood species, door profiles, paint or stain finishes for your custom cabinets, as well as the perfect stone or composite material for your countertops to match your personal aesthetic.
How long does a full kitchen remodeling project usually take? +
The timeline for a full kitchen remodeling project varies based on the scope of work and the materials selected. On average, once the design is finalized and custom cabinets are manufactured, the on-site installation and renovation phase takes anywhere from 4 to 8 weeks. We provide a detailed schedule before any work begins.
Do you handle the architectural and layout design phase? +
Yes, our full-service approach includes comprehensive layout planning and design. We assess your current space, listen to your functional needs, and draft detailed blueprints that optimize workflow, storage, and visual appeal before moving into the construction phase.
Do I need to hire separate contractors for plumbing or electrical work? +
No, you do not. As a full-service renovation company, we manage and coordinate all necessary trades, including specialized plumbing and electrical work. This unified management ensures the project stays on schedule and meets our rigorous quality standards.
