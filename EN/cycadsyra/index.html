Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about maintaining a beautiful landscape.

How often should I schedule lawn mowing?

For optimal health and appearance, weekly lawn mowing is generally recommended during the peak growing seasons of spring and early summer. Depending on weather conditions and grass type, this may shift to bi-weekly in the hotter, drier months.

What is included in landscape installation?

Landscape installation covers the physical implementation of a garden design. This can include planting trees, shrubs, and flowers, laying fresh sod, installing mulch or decorative rock beds, and setting up hardscapes like retaining walls or walkways.

Do I need regular tree service?

Yes, routine tree service is vital for the safety and aesthetics of your property. Regular pruning removes dead or diseased branches, promotes healthy growth, and prevents potential damage to your home during severe weather events.

Can you help with custom garden design?

Absolutely. The professionals we highlight specialize in creating custom garden designs tailored to your specific climate, soil type, and aesthetic preferences, ensuring a unique and sustainable outdoor environment.

Is professional lawn care worth the investment?

Investing in professional lawn care saves you significant time and effort while yielding superior results. Experts have access to commercial-grade equipment and specialized knowledge regarding soil health, fertilization schedules, and pest control that most homeowners do not.
