Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about our event catering services.

Do you offer tastings for wedding catering?

Yes, tastings are highly recommended for all wedding catering clients. It allows you to experience the quality of the food, finalize your menu selections, and discuss presentation styles directly with the culinary team before your big day.

Can you accommodate severe dietary restrictions and allergies?

Absolutely. We understand the importance of guest safety and satisfaction. The catering teams we promote are well-versed in preparing distinct, delicious meals for vegan, gluten-free, nut-free, and other specific dietary requirements without compromising on flavor or presentation.

What is the difference between buffet catering and a plated dinner?

Buffet catering offers a more relaxed atmosphere where guests can choose from various stations and select their portion sizes, which is great for encouraging mingling. A plated dinner is a more formal, synchronized service where guests are served multiple courses simultaneously at their tables, ideal for elegant galas and formal weddings.

Do you provide staffing and tableware for corporate catering events?

Yes, full-service event catering includes not just the food, but also professional servers, bartenders, and all necessary equipment including high-quality plates, flatware, and glassware to ensure your corporate event looks professional and runs seamlessly.

How far in advance should I book my event catering?

For large events like weddings, it is recommended to secure your caterer 9 to 12 months in advance, especially during peak seasons. For corporate catering or smaller private parties, 4 to 8 weeks is usually sufficient, though earlier is always better to ensure availability.
