Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our door installation services.

How long does a typical front door replacement take?
In most cases, a standard front door replacement can be completed in a single day by experienced professionals. Custom frame adjustments or specialized entry doors might require slightly more time, but our partners strive to secure your home by nightfall.
What are the main benefits of fiberglass doors?
Fiberglass doors offer exceptional durability, energy efficiency, and low maintenance. Unlike traditional wood, they resist warping, rotting, and cracking under harsh weather conditions, while still mimicking the authentic look and feel of wood grain.
Do you install both entry doors and interior doors?
Yes, the professionals we connect you with handle comprehensive door installation for both exterior and interior spaces. Whether you need a secure entry door or a set of matching interior doors for a renovation, we have the solutions.
How should I prepare my home for door installation?
We simply ask that you clear the area around the doorway, both inside and outside, to provide the installers with ample workspace. Removing fragile items, rugs, and wall decorations near the entrance ensures a smooth and safe process.
Are the doors you recommend energy efficient?
Absolutely. Energy efficiency is a primary focus for modern front door replacement. The entry doors and fiberglass doors installed feature advanced core insulation and high-quality weatherstripping to help reduce drafts and lower your heating and cooling costs.
