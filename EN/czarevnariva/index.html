Common Inquiries

What types of window treatments do you feature?

We highlight a comprehensive range of custom solutions, including precision roller shades, classic plantation shutters, elegant custom drapes, and versatile blinds. Each category offers various materials and control options to suit different architectural styles.

Are custom blinds a better investment than off-the-shelf options?

Yes. Custom blinds and shades are manufactured to the exact millimeter of your window casing, ensuring a flawless fit that eliminates light gaps. Furthermore, custom options utilize superior materials and hardware, providing significantly longer lifespans and better aesthetic integration.

How do roller shades contribute to energy efficiency?

High-quality roller shades act as an additional layer of insulation at the window pane. Solar shades block harmful UV rays and reduce solar heat gain in the summer, while heavier blackout fabrics can help retain interior warmth during cooler months, ultimately reducing HVAC loads.

Can I integrate motorized shades into my smart home system?

Absolutely. Many modern window treatments, particularly roller shades and cellular shades, can be equipped with quiet, battery-operated or hardwired motors. These can be controlled via remote, smartphone applications, or integrated into broader smart home ecosystems for automated scheduling.

What is the fundamental difference between shades and blinds?

Blinds are constructed from hard materials (wood, faux wood, aluminum) featuring individual slats that can be tilted to adjust light direction. Shades are typically made from a continuous roll or panel of soft fabric, offering a softer aesthetic and operating by rolling up or folding away completely.
