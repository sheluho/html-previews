Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a standard house cleaning?

A standard house cleaning covers the essentials to keep your home tidy and fresh. This includes dusting surfaces, wiping down kitchen counters and bathroom fixtures, vacuuming carpets, and mopping hard floors. It is the perfect maintenance choice after an initial deep cleaning.

How does your move-out cleaning differ from regular services?

Move-out cleaning is significantly more intensive. The maid service professionals will clean inside empty cabinets and appliances, tackle accumulated grime on baseboards, and ensure the entire property is entirely sanitized and ready for the next occupant.

Can I schedule a recurring cleaning plan?

Absolutely. We highly recommend recurring cleaning for maintaining a spotless home year-round. You can arrange for weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly visits depending on your household's unique needs and schedule.

Why is a deep cleaning recommended for my first appointment?

A deep cleaning resets your home's baseline cleanliness. It allows the professional house cleaning staff to remove built-up dust and dirt from neglected areas, making subsequent regular or recurring cleaning appointments faster and much more effective.

Are the cleaning supplies provided?

Yes, all necessary professional-grade cleaning supplies and equipment are provided by the maid service team. If you have specific preferences or allergies, please let the team know prior to your appointment so accommodations can be made.
