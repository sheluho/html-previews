Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment to visit the kitchen showroom?
While walk-ins are always welcome, we highly recommend scheduling a dedicated kitchen design appointment. This ensures that one of our specialists is available to walk you through the custom cabinets and materials uninterrupted.
What makes shaker cabinets so popular?
Shaker cabinets are celebrated for their minimalist, clean lines and sturdy construction. Their understated elegance allows them to blend perfectly with modern, transitional, or traditional kitchen decors, making them a highly versatile and timeless investment.
How long does the manufacturing process take for custom cabinets?
Because custom cabinets are built to your precise specifications by our manufacturer partners, lead times generally range from 4 to 8 weeks depending on the complexity of the design and the specific materials chosen.
Can you help with the overall kitchen design layout?
Absolutely. Our kitchen design service includes comprehensive space planning. We work closely with you to optimize storage, workflow, and aesthetic balance before the cabinetry goes into production.
What materials are used in your custom cabinets?
Our manufacturer partners utilize a variety of premium hardwoods, high-density fiberboards (for painted finishes), and durable plywood boxes. We detail all material options during your initial consultation so you can make an informed decision for your home.
