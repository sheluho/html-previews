Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our premium car care services.

What is the difference between a hand car wash and an automated one?

A hand car wash is performed manually by trained professionals, eliminating the risk of swirl marks and scratches often caused by the harsh brushes in automated tunnels. It allows for meticulous attention to detail on every curve of your vehicle.

What does the premium car wash include?

Our premium car wash includes a gentle, thorough exterior hand wash, deep wheel and tire cleaning, tire dressing, and a careful wipe-down of the interior surfaces to leave your cabin feeling fresh and clean.

How fast is the express wash?

The express wash is designed for drivers on the go. While times vary based on vehicle size, it generally provides a rapid yet comprehensive clean in under 30 minutes, without compromising on quality.

Is an auto wash membership worth it?

Absolutely. If you wash your vehicle more than twice a month, an auto wash membership is incredibly cost-effective. It ensures your car remains pristine year-round while saving you money on per-wash fees.

Are your cleaning products safe for ceramic coatings?

Yes, the hand car wash techniques rely on pH-neutral soaps and high-quality microfiber towels that are perfectly safe for vehicles equipped with ceramic coatings, clear bras, or vinyl wraps.
