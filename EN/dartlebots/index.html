Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I schedule professional carpet cleaning?
For most residential properties, we recommend professional carpet cleaning every 12 to 18 months. However, if you have pets, young children, or high foot traffic, scheduling a deep steam cleaning every 6 to 9 months will help maintain hygiene and prolong the life of your carpets.
Is steam cleaning safe for delicate rug cleaning?
Yes, steam cleaning is highly effective and safe for most synthetic and wool rugs. For antique, silk, or highly delicate textiles, our service partners perform a thorough fiber assessment first and may utilize specialized low-moisture or dry cleaning techniques to prevent any structural or color damage.
Can you remove all types of stains?
Our advanced stain removal processes successfully treat the vast majority of common household spots, including pet urine, coffee, wine, and ink. While we utilize industry-leading formulas, the success of complete removal depends on the stain's age, the carpet fiber type, and any previous chemical treatments applied to the spot.
How long does it take for carpets and upholstery to dry?
With our professional-grade extraction equipment, most carpets dry within 4 to 6 hours after steam cleaning. Upholstery cleaning drying times can vary depending on the fabric density, usually taking between 2 to 4 hours. Adequate ventilation and air circulation in the room can significantly speed up the drying process.
Do I need to vacuum before the technicians arrive?
While our partners perform a thorough pre-cleaning inspection and treatment, we kindly ask that you perform a light vacuuming of the areas to be serviced to remove loose top-layer debris. It is also helpful to move small, breakable items and light furniture out of the cleaning zones before the team arrives.
