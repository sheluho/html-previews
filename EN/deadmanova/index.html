Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about upgrading your windows.

What are the main benefits of energy efficient windows?

Energy efficient windows significantly reduce heat transfer, keeping your home cooler in the summer and warmer in the winter. This leads to lower utility bills, increased indoor comfort, and a reduced carbon footprint. They also often feature special coatings that block harmful UV rays, protecting your interior furnishings from fading.

Why should I choose vinyl windows over wood?

Vinyl windows are highly durable, exceptionally energy-efficient, and require virtually no maintenance. Unlike wood, vinyl will not rot, warp, or require repainting over time. They offer a cost-effective solution without compromising on aesthetic appeal or structural integrity.

How long does a typical window installation take?

Most standard residential window replacement projects can be completed in just one to two days. A professional crew typically installs between 10 to 15 windows per day, ensuring minimal disruption to your daily routine while maintaining strict quality control standards.

Are double hung windows difficult to clean?

Not at all. Modern double hung windows are designed with a convenient tilt-in feature. This allows you to effortlessly tilt both the top and bottom sashes inward, enabling you to clean the exterior glass safely and easily from inside your home.

How do I know it's time for replacement windows?

Signs that you need replacement windows include feeling drafts near the window frames, noticing condensation or fog between glass panes, difficulty opening or closing the sashes, or seeing visible rot and water damage. Additionally, if your energy bills are unexpectedly rising, outdated windows might be the culprit.
