Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between full-service planning and a day of coordinator?

Full-service wedding planning involves us managing every single aspect of your wedding from the very beginning, including budget, design, and vendor selection. A day of coordinator steps in closer to the event date to manage the logistics, timeline, and flow of the day, assuming you have already booked your vendors and planned the details.

Do you handle destination wedding logistics?

Yes. We offer specialized services for destination weddings. Our team handles the complexities of international planning, including sourcing reliable local vendors, coordinating guest travel logistics, and arranging multi-day itineraries for your entire wedding weekend.

When should we book our wedding planner?

For a full-service wedding planner, we recommend booking 12 to 18 months in advance to ensure the best availability for top-tier venues and vendors. If you are seeking a day of coordinator, reaching out 6 months prior to your wedding date is ideal.

Will you be there on the actual wedding day?

Absolutely. Whether you hire us for comprehensive planning or strictly for coordination, our dedicated team will be on-site on your wedding day. We arrive early to oversee setup, manage the timeline, and handle any unforeseen issues so you can relax.

Can you help us stay within our budget?

Budget management is a core component of our full-service planning package. We work closely with you to establish a realistic budget, track expenses rigorously, and negotiate with vendors to maximize the value of your investment without compromising your vision.
