Frequently Asked Questions

What types of window treatments do you offer?

We specialize in a comprehensive range of high-quality window coverings. This includes custom blinds, elegant roller shades, beautifully tailored custom drapes, and durable plantation shutters. We offer options in various materials and styles to perfectly match your home's décor.

Do you provide custom measurements for odd-shaped windows?

Absolutely. Every window treatment we provide is entirely custom-made. During your initial consultation, we take precise measurements of your windows—including arches, angles, and oversized frames—to ensure a flawless, edge-to-edge fit for your new blinds or shades.

How long does the process take from consultation to installation?

The timeline generally depends on the specific products you choose. Standard roller shades and custom blinds typically take about 3 to 4 weeks to manufacture and install. Custom drapes and handcrafted plantation shutters may take a bit longer due to the intricate detailing required. We will provide a clear timeline during your consultation.

Are your roller shades and blinds available with motorization?

Yes, motorization is one of our most popular upgrades. We can equip your roller shades, custom drapes, and certain styles of blinds with smart-home compatible motors, allowing you to control the flow of light with a remote, an app on your smartphone, or voice commands.

How do I maintain and clean my new plantation shutters?

Plantation shutters are incredibly easy to maintain. For regular upkeep, simply wipe down the louvers with a dry microfiber cloth or a feather duster. If they need a deeper clean, a lightly damp cloth with mild soap is sufficient. Avoid using harsh chemicals or excessive water to preserve the finish of the wood or faux-wood material.
