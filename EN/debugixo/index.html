Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between an express wash and a hand car wash? ▼

An express wash utilizes advanced automated soft-touch equipment to provide a fast, comprehensive exterior clean in just minutes. A hand car wash involves our technicians manually washing, drying, and detailing your vehicle, providing a gentler touch and extreme attention to detail for delicate finishes.

How does the auto wash membership work? ▼

Our auto wash membership operates on a fixed monthly fee that grants you unlimited access to our specified wash packages. Your vehicle is recognized upon arrival via license plate or a discrete RFID tag, allowing you to breeze through the process without daily transactions.

Is your premium car wash safe for ceramic-coated vehicles? ▼

Absolutely. We use pH-neutral soaps and specialized cleaning agents that are entirely safe for vehicles with ceramic coatings, paint protection films (PPF), and custom wraps. We recommend our hand car wash option for the utmost care of specialized finishes.

Do I need to book an appointment for an express wash? ▼

No appointment is necessary for our express wash services; you can simply drive up during our operating hours. However, for extensive detailing or a full premium hand car wash, we recommend contacting us in advance to secure a dedicated time slot.

How often should I get a premium car wash? ▼

For optimal maintenance of your vehicle's clear coat and appearance, we recommend a wash every one to two weeks. Frequent washing removes corrosive contaminants like bird droppings, tree sap, and road salt before they can permanently damage your paint.
