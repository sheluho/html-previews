Frequently Asked Questions

What types of window treatments do you feature?
We feature a comprehensive range of solutions including classic plantation shutters, modern roller shades, custom blinds, and tailored custom drapes designed to suit various architectural styles and interior aesthetics.
How do plantation shutters improve energy efficiency?
Plantation shutters provide an excellent barrier against heat transfer. When closed, they create an insulating layer of air between the shutter and the window pane, helping to keep your home cooler in the summer and warmer in the winter.
Are roller shades available in blackout materials?
Yes, roller shades come in a variety of opacities, from sheer light-filtering fabrics perfect for living rooms to complete blackout materials that are ideal for bedrooms and media rooms.
Can I combine custom blinds with drapes?
Absolutely. Layering window treatments is a highly recommended design choice. Combining functional blinds or shades with decorative custom drapes adds texture, depth, and enhanced light control to your space.
How do I choose the right shades for my home?
Choosing the right shades involves considering your primary needs for each room: privacy levels, light management, and interior style. We recommend assessing whether you need UV protection, thermal insulation, or simply an aesthetic upgrade to guide your choice.
