Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about our promoted landscaping services.

How often should I schedule lawn mowing services?

For optimal turf health, weekly lawn mowing is generally recommended during the peak growing seasons in spring and early summer. As growth slows in the late summer and fall, a bi-weekly schedule may be sufficient to maintain a neat appearance without stressing the grass.

What is included in a typical landscape installation project?

Landscape installation encompasses a wide range of services designed to build your outdoor space from the ground up. This typically includes soil preparation, planting of trees, shrubs, and flowers, laying sod or seeding, and the construction of hardscape elements like patios, walkways, and retaining walls based on an approved garden design.

When is the best time of year for tree service and pruning?

The ideal time for major tree service and pruning is typically during the dormant season, which is late winter or early spring before new growth begins. Pruning during dormancy minimizes sap loss and stress on the tree, while also reducing the risk of pest infestation or disease transmission.

Do you offer custom garden design consultations?

Yes, the professionals we promote offer comprehensive garden design consultations. During these sessions, experts will assess your property's soil, lighting, and layout, listen to your aesthetic preferences, and develop a tailored plan that ensures your new landscape is both beautiful and sustainable.

Can commercial properties benefit from these landscaping services?

Absolutely. First impressions matter for businesses, and professional landscaping is crucial for commercial properties. The experts highlighted by DellaHub provide reliable, large-scale lawn care, seasonal cleanups, and robust landscape installation designed to create a welcoming and professional exterior for your clients and employees.
