Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I schedule HVAC maintenance?

It is generally recommended to service your heating and cooling systems twice a year. Schedule a check-up in the spring for your air conditioner to prepare for summer, and once in the fall for your furnace or heat pump to ensure reliable performance during winter.

What are the signs I need a new air conditioner?

Frequent breakdowns, steadily rising energy bills, inconsistent cooling across different rooms, and operating a system older than 10-15 years are strong indicators. In these cases, a modern, energy-efficient AC installation might be significantly more cost-effective than continued repairs.

Do you provide services for heat pumps?

Yes, heat pump solutions are a core part of our highlighted services. From new installations to regular maintenance and complex troubleshooting, experts can manage your heat pump to ensure optimal year-round heating and cooling performance.

How can I improve my home's indoor air quality?

Beyond standard temperature control, improving indoor air quality involves addressing filtration and humidity. Solutions such as upgraded high-efficiency filters, UV air purifiers, and whole-home humidifiers or dehumidifiers can be integrated directly into your existing HVAC setup.

What should I check before calling for furnace repair?

Before scheduling a repair, verify that your thermostat is set to 'heat' and that the batteries are fresh. Also, check your electrical panel for tripped breakers and ensure your air filters are clean, as a clogged filter can cause the system to shut down. If these are fine and it's still not working, it is time to call a professional.
