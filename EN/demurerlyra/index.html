Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about HVAC services and maintenance.

How often should I schedule HVAC heating and cooling maintenance?

It is highly recommended to have your HVAC system checked twice a year. Schedule a tune-up for your air conditioner in the spring and an inspection for your furnace in the early fall. Regular maintenance improves efficiency and prevents emergency breakdowns.

What are the signs I need a new AC installation?

If your air conditioner is over 10-15 years old, requires frequent repairs, struggles to cool your home evenly, or is causing a spike in your energy bills, it may be time to consider a modern, energy-efficient AC installation.

Can a heat pump replace both my AC and furnace?

Yes! A heat pump provides both heating and cooling by transferring heat rather than generating it. In moderate climates, it can entirely replace traditional air conditioner and furnace systems while significantly reducing energy consumption.

Why is my air conditioner freezing up?

An air conditioner usually freezes due to poor airflow (such as a dirty filter) or low refrigerant levels. If changing the filter does not resolve the issue, you should schedule a professional service call to check the refrigerant line and coils.

Do you promote emergency HVAC services?

Yes, we connect users with local professionals who understand that a broken furnace in winter or a failed AC in summer is an emergency. Use our contact form or phone link to quickly request immediate assistance.
