Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about our custom kitchen remodeling process.

What is included in a turnkey kitchen remodeling project?

A turnkey kitchen renovation means we handle everything from start to finish. This includes the initial design consultation, demolition, structural adjustments, electrical and plumbing updates, installing custom cabinets, laying countertops, and final finishing touches.

How long does a custom kitchen installation take?

The timeline varies depending on the scale of the project and the materials chosen. On average, a full-scale kitchen remodeling takes between 4 to 8 weeks after the design is finalized and materials are delivered. We provide a detailed schedule before work begins.

Can I choose the materials for my custom cabinets?

Absolutely. Our custom kitchen cabinets are tailored to your preferences. You can select from a wide range of solid woods, veneers, paint colors, stains, and hardware options to create a look that perfectly complements your home.

What types of countertops do you install?

We offer a diverse selection of premium countertops, including durable quartz, classic granite, elegant marble, and modern solid surface materials. Our experts will help you choose the best surface based on your lifestyle and design goals.

Do you handle both design and installation?

Yes, Depeople Beam promotes full-service providers who manage both the design and installation phases. This ensures a cohesive process, minimizing miscommunication and keeping your custom kitchen remodeling project on track.
