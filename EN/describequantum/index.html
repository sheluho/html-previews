Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our wedding videography process.

What is included in your cinematic wedding video packages?

Our standard packages include full-day coverage by a lead wedding videographer, a beautifully edited cinematic highlight film, professional audio capture for vows and speeches, and drone footage (weather and venue permitting). We also offer raw footage as an optional add-on.

How long does it take to receive our final wedding film?

We take immense pride in the editing process, meticulously color-grading and sound-designing your film. Typically, you can expect to receive your final cinematic wedding video within 8 to 12 weeks following your event date.

Do you offer both wedding videography and photography?

While our primary expertise and focus is as a dedicated wedding and event videography company, we regularly collaborate seamlessly with external photographers. If requested, we can also recommend highly trusted photography partners whose style complements our cinematic approach.

Can you travel for destination event videography?

Yes! We love capturing love stories around the world. For destination weddings, we create custom packages that account for travel and accommodation, ensuring you receive the same high-tier cinematic wedding video experience no matter where you celebrate.

How do you choose the music for our wedding film?

Music is the heartbeat of a cinematic wedding video. We license high-quality, emotive tracks from professional music platforms to ensure your video can be legally shared online. We carefully select the soundtrack to match the unique energy, pacing, and emotional tone of your specific day.
