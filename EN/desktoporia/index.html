Frequently Asked Questions

Get clear answers about your upcoming residential relocation.

What areas do your local movers cover?

Our partnered local movers operate extensively within major metropolitan areas and surrounding suburbs. When you request a quote, we match you with a moving company team stationed near your current residence for fast, efficient service.

How far in advance should I book a moving company?

It is highly recommended to schedule your residential moving company at least 4 to 6 weeks in advance, especially during peak summer months. Early booking ensures you secure your preferred dates and optimal pricing.

Do you offer packing services?

Yes, comprehensive packing services are available. Professional teams can handle everything from carefully wrapping delicate glassware to boxing up your entire household, saving you immense time and physical strain.

Are long distance movers different from local ones?

Yes. Long distance movers specialize in interstate logistics, adhering to federal transport regulations, and ensuring secure packing for extended transit. Local moves are typically billed by the hour, whereas long distance moves are usually calculated by distance and weight.

How is the cost of my move calculated?

Pricing depends on several factors: the total distance of the move, the volume/weight of your belongings, and any additional requests like packing services or heavy item handling. Requesting a custom quote provides the most accurate estimate.
