Frequently Asked Questions

What is your style as a wedding videographer?

We specialize in a documentary and cinematic style. Our goal as your dedicated wedding videographer is to capture authentic moments as they unfold naturally, while using high-end equipment to give the final product a beautiful, movie-like aesthetic.

Do you offer both wedding videography and event videography?

Yes, absolutely. While our primary focus is crafting the perfect cinematic wedding video, we also bring our expertise to corporate events, private galas, and special celebrations requiring top-tier event videography.

How long does it take to receive our cinematic wedding video?

Editing a comprehensive wedding film is an intricate process. Typically, you can expect to receive your final, polished video within 8 to 12 weeks after your event date, ensuring every frame is meticulously color-graded and sound-designed.

Can we choose the music for our wedding film?

Because of strict music licensing laws for online distribution, we curate a selection of high-quality, fully licensed music that perfectly matches the mood and pacing of your day. We welcome your input on the genre and overall vibe during our pre-wedding consultation.

Do you travel for destination weddings?

Yes! We love traveling to capture destination weddings and elopements. Whether your celebration is across the state or across the globe, we can build a custom travel package to ensure your destination wedding film is spectacular.
