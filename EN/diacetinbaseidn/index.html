Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a premium car wash?

A premium car wash typically includes a thorough exterior clean, deep wheel and tire scrubbing, a protective wax coating, and meticulous interior vacuuming and surface wiping. It goes beyond a standard wash to restore your vehicle's pristine condition.

How long does a hand car wash take?

Depending on the condition of your vehicle and the specific package selected, a dedicated hand car wash usually takes between 45 minutes to an hour and a half. The focus is on precision and care rather than speed.

Is the express wash safe for my paint?

Yes, the express wash utilizes state-of-the-art soft cloth technology and pH-balanced soaps designed specifically to lift dirt without scratching or dulling your vehicle's clear coat.

How does the auto wash membership work?

The auto wash membership is a convenient monthly subscription that allows you to wash your vehicle as often as you like for one flat rate. It is the most cost-effective way to maintain your car's appearance all year.

Do I need to book my detailing service in advance?

While walk-ins are often accommodated for standard and express washes, we highly recommend scheduling your premium detailing or hand car wash in advance to ensure dedicated time with the specialists.
