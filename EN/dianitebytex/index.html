Frequently Asked Questions

Details about our wedding videography process.

Do you travel outside of Los Angeles for event videography?

Yes, while we are a frequently booked wedding videographer in the Los Angeles area, we love to travel. We are available for destination weddings and events worldwide. Travel fees are calculated based on the location and will be included in your custom proposal.

How long does it take to receive our cinematic wedding video?

Editing a high-quality wedding film requires time and meticulous attention to detail. Typically, you can expect to receive your fully edited cinematic wedding video within 10 to 12 weeks following your event date.

How would you describe your wedding videography style?

Our style is best described as cinematic, emotional, and documentary-driven. We prioritize capturing authentic moments as they unfold naturally, paired with high-end production techniques to create a visually stunning and narrative-focused wedding film.

Do we get to choose the music for our wedding film?

Due to strict music licensing laws, we legally license all music used in our films from specific curated platforms. While you cannot choose mainstream radio songs, we work closely with you to understand your musical tastes and select an evocative, legally licensed soundtrack that perfectly matches the mood of your day.

What is the process to book you for our date?

To secure us as your event videography team, please fill out the inquiry form below. We will check our availability and schedule a consultation call. Once we confirm details, a signed contract and a retainer fee are required to officially reserve your date on our calendar.
