Frequently Asked Questions

What are the benefits of energy efficient windows?

Energy efficient windows use specialized glass, multiple panes, and insulative gas fills to prevent heat transfer. This keeps your home warmer in the winter and cooler in the summer, directly lowering your monthly energy bills and reducing strain on your HVAC system.

Why should I choose double hung windows?

Double hung windows feature two movable sashes that can open from the top or bottom, providing superior ventilation control. Additionally, they often tilt inward, making it incredibly easy to clean both sides of the glass from inside your home.

How long does professional window installation take?

While the timeline varies based on the size of the project and the type of replacement windows selected, a standard window installation typically takes one to two days for an average-sized home. The professionals ensure the site is cleaned and secure before leaving.

Are vinyl windows durable?

Yes, modern vinyl windows are highly durable. They are engineered to resist moisture, meaning they will not rot, peel, or warp over time. They are also virtually maintenance-free, requiring no painting or staining throughout their lifespan.

How do I know it's time for replacement windows?

Signs that you need replacement windows include feeling drafts near the frames, visible condensation between glass panes, difficulty opening or closing the sashes, and noticing a steady increase in your heating and cooling costs.
