Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about our house cleaning promotions.

What is typically included in a deep cleaning?

A deep cleaning goes beyond standard surface wiping. It generally includes hand-washing baseboards, thoroughly cleaning the exterior and interior of accessible appliances, scrubbing grout, dusting high ceiling fans, and meticulous attention to areas that accumulate long-term dust and grime.

Do I need to be home while the maid service is there?

It is entirely up to you. Many clients prefer to provide a key or garage code so the house cleaning can be completed while they are at work or running errands. If you choose to be home, the professionals will simply work around you to ensure a seamless experience.

How does recurring cleaning scheduling work?

Recurring cleaning can be scheduled based on your needs: weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly. Once you establish a frequency, you will typically be assigned a consistent day and time slot to ensure your home is regularly maintained without you having to repeatedly book appointments.

Do I need to provide my own cleaning supplies?

In most cases, professional maid service teams arrive fully equipped with high-quality cleaning products, vacuums, and tools. If you have specific, specialized products you prefer them to use on certain surfaces, you can usually request that they use your provided supplies.

What is a move-out cleaning?

A move-out cleaning is designed for empty homes. It involves a rigorous top-to-bottom clean to prepare the property for the next resident or to meet landlord requirements. This usually includes cleaning inside empty cabinets, deep cleaning bathrooms, and ensuring all floors and surfaces are spotless.
