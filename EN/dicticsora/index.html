Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical lead time for custom furniture?

Because every piece of handmade furniture is built from scratch, lead times typically range from 8 to 12 weeks. The exact timeline depends on the complexity of the design and material availability at the time of your order.

Can I choose the wood species for my custom dining table?

Absolutely. We highlight designs available in a variety of premium hardwoods, including walnut, white oak, cherry, and maple. The choice of wood allows you to perfectly match your home's aesthetic.

Do you offer shipping for bespoke furniture?

Yes. White-glove delivery services are coordinated by the original provider to ensure your bespoke pieces arrive safely and are installed correctly in your home, no matter where you are located.

Is it possible to customize the dimensions of a custom sofa?

Yes, sizing can be tailored to fit your specific room layout. From length and depth to seat height, your custom sofa is engineered for both your space and your personal comfort preferences.

Can I visit the custom furniture maker and showroom?

The showcased provider operates a physical space in Seattle, WA. Showroom visits are highly recommended to experience the craftsmanship, test the seating, and view wood samples in person.
