Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I schedule lawn mowing?

For a healthy, vibrant lawn, weekly mowing is generally recommended during the peak growing seasons (spring and early summer). During slower growth periods, bi-weekly service is often sufficient to maintain an optimal height.

Do you provide custom garden design?

Yes, the professionals we promote specialize in comprehensive garden design. They will assess your property's soil, sunlight exposure, and drainage to create a custom layout that incorporates native plants, hardscaping, and beautiful focal points.

What does landscape installation include?

Landscape installation covers the physical construction of your outdoor design. This includes planting trees and shrubs, laying sod, building retaining walls, installing patios or walkways, and setting up mulch or decorative stone beds.

Why is professional tree service important?

Proper tree care ensures the long-term health of your trees and prevents property damage. Professional arborists can safely remove dead or diseased branches, structural pruning, and manage heavy limbs that threaten roofs or power lines.

When is the best time for a landscape overhaul?

Early spring or early fall are typically the best times for major landscaping projects. The moderate temperatures put less stress on new plants, allowing roots to establish deeply before the harsh heat of summer or freezing winter arrives.
