Frequently Asked Questions

What does a wedding videographer do on the day?

A professional wedding videographer discreetly captures the key moments of your celebration, from getting ready to the final dance. They focus on audio, lighting, and cinematic composition to gather the raw material needed to create your final wedding film without interrupting the flow of your day.

How long does it take to receive our cinematic wedding video?

Post-production is a detailed process that involves color grading, audio mixing, and narrative editing. Typically, a high-quality cinematic wedding video takes between 8 to 12 weeks to be delivered, depending on the season and the complexity of the final edit.

Do you offer event videography for non-weddings?

Yes, the professionals featured on Dietary Trail offer comprehensive event videography services. This includes corporate galas, milestone anniversaries, engagement parties, and other significant gatherings that require high-end documentation.

What is the difference between a highlight reel and a full wedding film?

A highlight reel is typically a 3 to 7-minute heavily curated video set to music, designed to showcase the best emotional moments of the day. A full wedding film is a longer documentary-style edit (often 30-60 minutes) that includes full speeches, extended ceremony coverage, and more ambient moments.
