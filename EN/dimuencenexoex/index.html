Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about professional house cleaning services.

What is included in a standard house cleaning?
A standard service typically includes dusting surfaces, vacuuming carpets, mopping hard floors, and thoroughly cleaning bathrooms and kitchens to maintain a consistently tidy and hygienic environment.
Do I need to provide the cleaning supplies?
Professional maid services generally arrive fully equipped with their own high-quality, effective cleaning equipment and solutions, so you do not have to worry about stocking up on supplies.
How often should I schedule recurring cleaning?
Most clients opt for weekly or bi-weekly visits. The ideal frequency depends on the size of your household, the presence of pets, and your overall lifestyle demands to keep the space manageable.
What is the difference between standard and deep cleaning?
Deep cleaning involves more intensive, time-consuming tasks. This includes scrubbing baseboards, cleaning the interiors of appliances, and reaching neglected areas that are not typically covered in regular maintenance visits.
Can I book a move-out cleaning at the last minute?
It is highly recommended to schedule your move-out cleaning well in advance to ensure availability. These comprehensive cleans require significantly more time and resources to execute properly.
