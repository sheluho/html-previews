Common Questions

Everything you need to know about starting your cabinetry project.

What styles of kitchen cabinets do you feature?

We promote a wide variety of styles to suit any home decor. Our primary focus includes timeless shaker cabinets, sleek modern slab doors, and traditional raised panel designs. All styles can be customized with various woods, paints, and stains.

Is professional kitchen design assistance available?

Absolutely. Comprehensive kitchen design services are a core part of the offering. Experienced designers will work closely with you to optimize your layout, ensure proper workflow, and select the perfect cabinetry to bring your vision to life.

Are the custom cabinets manufactured locally?

Yes, a key value proposition of the partner we promote is that their products are proudly American-made. This ensures high-quality construction standards, reliable sourcing of materials, and support for domestic manufacturing.

Can I view the materials in a kitchen showroom?

Yes, visiting the physical kitchen showroom is highly recommended. It allows you to feel the build quality, view exact color swatches under proper lighting, and explore various hardware setups before making a final decision on your custom cabinets.

How long does the customization and ordering process take?

Timelines vary based on the complexity of your design and current manufacturing schedules. Generally, after the final kitchen design is approved and measurements are verified, custom cabinetry production takes several weeks. Your dedicated representative will provide a specific timeline for your project.
