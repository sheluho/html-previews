Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a standard house cleaning?

A standard professional house cleaning typically includes thorough dusting of all surfaces, vacuuming carpets and rugs, mopping hard floors, and complete sanitation of bathrooms and kitchens. This ensures your home maintains a baseline of hygiene and order.

Do I need to provide cleaning supplies?

No, you do not need to provide anything. A professional maid service arrives fully equipped with all necessary tools, vacuums, and high-quality, eco-friendly cleaning products required to tackle any job in your home.

What is the difference between regular and deep cleaning?

While regular cleaning focuses on maintenance (surfaces, floors, general tidiness), deep cleaning is an intensive process. Deep cleaning involves detailed work like hand-wiping baseboards, cleaning inside appliances, dusting ceiling fans, and reaching high-dust areas that are often missed during routine upkeep.

Can I customize my recurring cleaning schedule?

Absolutely. Recurring cleaning plans are designed to be flexible. You can choose from weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly intervals depending on your household's activity level, budget, and specific lifestyle requirements.

How do you handle move-out cleaning?

Move-out cleaning is an intensive, top-to-bottom service designed to prepare a vacant property for the next resident or a final inspection. It typically includes cleaning inside all empty cabinets and drawers, intensive scrubbing of bathroom fixtures, and ensuring all floors and surfaces are pristine.
