Frequently Asked Questions

Do you feature motorized roller shades?

Yes, we highlight a variety of advanced motorized options for your convenience. Motorized roller shades offer effortless light control at the touch of a button, ideal for hard-to-reach windows or simply modernizing your home.

How long do plantation shutters last?

High-quality plantation shutters are renowned for their durability. With proper care and standard maintenance, premium custom shutters are built to last a lifetime, making them a fantastic long-term investment for your property.

Can I get custom drapes for oversized windows?

Absolutely. Custom window treatments are specifically tailored to fit the exact dimensions of your home, including oversized, arched, or unconventionally shaped windows. Custom drapes provide seamless coverage regardless of window scale.

What is the difference between blinds and shades?

Blinds typically feature hard slats (made of wood, faux wood, or metal) that can be tilted to adjust light. Shades are constructed from continuous pieces of soft fabric that roll up or fold down, offering a softer aesthetic and varying degrees of light filtration.

Do window treatments help with energy efficiency?

Yes. Proper window treatments like cellular shades and plantation shutters provide excellent insulation against heat and cold. By blocking intense sunlight in the summer and reducing drafts in the winter, they help maintain a comfortable indoor climate.
