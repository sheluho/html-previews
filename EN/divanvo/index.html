Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our cabinetry and design services.

What styles of kitchen cabinets do you offer?
We manufacture a wide range of custom cabinets, from traditional raised panel designs to highly popular shaker cabinets and flat-panel modern styles. Our kitchen showroom features multiple displays to help you visualize these options.
Do you provide complete kitchen design services?
Yes. We offer comprehensive kitchen design. Our specialists work with you to plan your layout, select materials, and ensure that your new cabinetry fits perfectly into your home’s aesthetic and functional needs.
Can I visit the kitchen showroom without an appointment?
While walk-ins are welcome to browse our displays, we highly recommend scheduling an appointment if you wish to speak directly with a designer or discuss a specific custom cabinets project.
Are your cabinets manufactured locally?
Yes, as a dedicated manufacturer, we oversee the production of all our custom cabinets. This allows us to maintain strict quality control, offer highly specialized sizes, and ensure superior craftsmanship for every order.
How long does the manufacturing process take?
Timelines vary based on the complexity of your kitchen design and the specific finishes chosen. On average, custom cabinets take several weeks to manufacture once the final design is approved. We will provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.
