Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between blinds and shades?

Blinds are typically made of hard materials like wood, faux wood, or aluminum and have individual louvers (slats) that can be tilted to adjust light. Shades are made from continuous rolls of fabric or woven materials that pull up or down, offering a softer look and varying degrees of light filtering.

Are plantation shutters energy efficient?

Yes, plantation shutters are excellent insulators. Because they are custom-fitted tightly to your window frame, they create a barrier that helps prevent heat from entering during the summer and escaping during the winter, potentially lowering your energy bills.

How do I choose the right window treatments for my home?

The best choice depends on your specific needs for privacy, light control, and interior style. For instance, roller shades are great for a modern, minimalist look with UV protection, while custom drapes add warmth and texture. A professional consultation helps assess your space and functional requirements.

Can custom drapes be paired with other window coverings?

Absolutely. Layering is a popular design technique. Pairing custom drapes with roller shades or wood blinds provides maximum flexibility. You can keep the shades drawn for privacy while leaving the drapes open to frame the window beautifully.
