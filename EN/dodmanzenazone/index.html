Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries regarding our door installation services.

How long does a typical door installation take?

A standard interior door installation usually takes a few hours, while a complete front door replacement may take a full day to ensure the frame is perfectly sealed, insulated, and structurally sound.

What are the benefits of fiberglass doors over traditional wood?

Fiberglass doors offer superior resistance to environmental factors. Unlike wood, they will not warp, swell, or rot due to moisture. They also provide excellent insulation and require significantly less maintenance while mimicking the texture of natural wood.

Do you handle front door replacement for older historic homes?

Yes, our recommended installation partners are highly experienced in retrofitting modern entry doors into older frames, ensuring that the historical aesthetic is preserved while upgrading the home's security and energy efficiency.

Can I customize the hardware on my new interior doors?

Absolutely. You can select from a wide variety of handle sets, hinges, and lock mechanisms to perfectly match your home's interior design theme when finalizing your order with the service provider.

Are your entry doors energy efficient?

Yes, energy efficiency is a core focus. The entry doors we promote feature advanced weatherstripping, insulated cores, and thermal breaks to drastically reduce drafts, helping you maintain a comfortable indoor temperature and lower energy bills.
