Frequently Asked Questions

What are the main benefits of spray foam insulation? ▼

Spray foam provides an exceptional airtight seal that traditional materials often miss. It boasts a high R-value, significantly reduces moisture intrusion, helps block outdoor noise, and creates a highly energy-efficient envelope for your home.

How do I know if my home needs new attic insulation? ▼

Common signs include noticeable drafts, uneven temperatures between rooms, unusually high heating or cooling bills, and issues with ice dams on your roof during winter. A professional inspection can determine your exact needs.

Is vinyl siding a durable option for house siding? ▼

Yes, modern vinyl siding is highly durable, cost-effective, and engineered to withstand strong winds and impacts. It requires very little maintenance, never needs painting, and is resistant to rot and pests.

What is the difference between vinyl and fiber cement siding? ▼

While vinyl is lighter and generally more affordable, fiber cement siding is significantly thicker and heavier. Fiber cement offers superior fire resistance, can mimic wood textures more closely, and typically has a longer lifespan under extreme conditions.

How long does a typical home insulation project take? ▼

Most residential attic insulation or spray foam projects can be completed within 1 to 3 days. The exact timeframe depends on the size of your home, the accessibility of the spaces, and the specific type of insulation being installed.
