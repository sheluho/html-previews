Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our event catering services.

Do you offer both plated dinner and buffet catering options?

Yes, we specialize in both styles of service. Whether you prefer the elegance and formal structure of a plated dinner or the vibrant variety and flow of buffet catering, our culinary team will design a menu and service plan that perfectly fits the tone of your event.

How far in advance should we book our wedding catering?

For wedding catering, we recommend securing your date at least 12 to 18 months in advance, especially for peak seasons (spring and fall). This allows ample time for menu design, tastings, and coordinating logistics with your venue.

Can you accommodate specific dietary restrictions and allergies?

Absolutely. We take dietary needs very seriously. Our chefs are highly experienced in creating exceptional gluten-free, vegan, vegetarian, and allergy-friendly dishes that are just as flavorful and visually stunning as our standard menu items.

What is included in your corporate catering packages?

Our corporate catering packages are comprehensive. They typically include customized menu design, professional waitstaff and bartenders, high-quality serving equipment, set up, and breakdown. We tailor each proposal based on the specific needs of your corporate event.

Do you provide private tastings prior to booking event catering?

We offer exclusive private tasting sessions for clients who have contracted our event catering services. This tasting allows you to sample your proposed menu, finalize flavor profiles, and discuss presentation details directly with our culinary team.
