Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a standard house cleaning?

A standard house cleaning covers the essential upkeep of your home. This typically includes dusting surfaces, wiping down kitchen counters and appliance exteriors, cleaning bathrooms (toilets, showers, sinks), vacuuming carpets, and mopping hard floors.

Do you offer recurring cleaning schedules?

Yes, the providers we promote offer flexible recurring cleaning schedules. You can arrange for weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly maid service to ensure your home remains consistently pristine without any hassle.

How does deep cleaning differ from regular service?

Deep cleaning is a much more intensive process. It includes everything in a standard clean, plus detailed work like hand-wiping baseboards, cleaning inside window sills, scrubbing grout, and reaching areas that aren't typically addressed during routine maintenance.

Is move-out cleaning suitable for landlords and tenants?

Absolutely. Move-out cleaning is specifically designed to return an empty property to a highly presentable state. It is ideal for tenants hoping to secure their deposit return, as well as landlords preparing a unit for new occupants.

Are the cleaning products used safe?

The service providers highlighted through Dorsi Vault are committed to using high-quality, professional-grade cleaning products. Many emphasize eco-friendly and health-conscious solutions that are tough on dirt but safe for your family and pets.
