Frequently Asked Questions

Insights into the bespoke furniture process.

What exactly is bespoke furniture?

Bespoke furniture is entirely custom-made to your exact specifications. Unlike mass-produced items, every dimension, material, finish, and detail is selected by you and crafted by a skilled custom furniture maker to fit your space perfectly.

How long does it take to create a custom sofa?

The timeline for a custom sofa generally ranges from 8 to 12 weeks, depending on the complexity of the design and the availability of your chosen fabrics. This ensures the frame is built robustly and the upholstery is applied flawlessly.

Can I choose the specific wood for my custom dining table?

Absolutely. A hallmark of handmade furniture is the ability to select your preferred timber. Whether you desire the rich tones of walnut, the durability of white oak, or the unique grain of cherry, the choice is yours.

Is there a physical showroom I can visit?

Yes, the provider operates a dedicated custom furniture maker and showroom space in Rhode Island. Visiting allows you to experience the comfort of the seating, view wood finish samples, and appreciate the craftsmanship of the handmade furniture in person.

Why invest in handmade furniture over store-bought?

Handmade furniture offers unparalleled longevity, precise customization, and unique character. Instead of adapting your home to fit mass-market dimensions, bespoke furniture is crafted to enhance your specific lifestyle and aesthetic, making it an heirloom investment.
