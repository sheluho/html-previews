Frequently Asked Questions

How often does my lawn need mowing and maintenance?

Routine lawn mowing depends heavily on the season, grass type, and local climate. Generally, weekly mowing during peak growing seasons keeps grass healthy and prevents weed overgrowth. Our experts can assess your specific yard to recommend an optimal schedule.

What is included in your landscape installation services?

Landscape installation covers everything from planting new trees and shrubs to laying fresh sod, installing mulch beds, and executing full garden design makeovers. The service is tailored entirely to your aesthetic goals and property layout.

When is the best time to schedule tree service?

While emergency tree service can happen year-round, preventive pruning and trimming are usually best done during the dormant winter months. This minimizes stress on the tree and prepares it for vigorous spring growth.

Do you offer customized garden design plans?

Yes, our partners specialize in custom garden design. They work closely with you to select the right plants, hardscapes, and layouts that thrive in your specific soil and sunlight conditions, creating a cohesive and beautiful outdoor space.

Is professional lawn care safe for my children and pets?

Safety is a top priority. The lawn care and fertilization products used by our partners are applied strictly according to industry guidelines. You will be provided with specific instructions, such as keeping pets off the grass until treatments have fully dried.
