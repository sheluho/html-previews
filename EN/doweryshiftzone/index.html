Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer custom menus for wedding catering?

Absolutely. We believe your event should reflect your personal taste. We work closely with our clients to design fully customized menus that highlight local, seasonal ingredients and honor your specific culinary vision for your big day.

What is the difference between your buffet catering and plated dinner options?

A plated dinner provides a traditional, formal experience where guests are served multi-course meals directly at their seats. Buffet catering offers a more relaxed, interactive atmosphere where guests can choose from an abundant spread of diverse dishes. Both are executed with the highest level of service and presentation.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions for event catering?

Yes, accommodating dietary needs is a priority for us. Whether your guests require gluten-free, vegan, vegetarian, or allergen-conscious options, our culinary team will ensure that everyone at your event enjoys a safe and delicious meal without compromising on flavor.

How far in advance should we book corporate catering?

For large-scale corporate events or annual galas, we recommend booking 3 to 6 months in advance to secure your date and allow ample time for menu development. For smaller meetings or more casual gatherings, please reach out as soon as possible, and we will do our best to accommodate your timeline.

Do you provide staffing and rentals for the event?

Yes, we offer comprehensive event staffing, including professional servers, bartenders, and culinary personnel. We can also coordinate the necessary rentals—such as linens, glassware, and flatware—to ensure a seamless and fully supported catering experience from setup to breakdown.
