Frequently Asked Questions

What types of custom cabinets do you offer?

We manufacture a wide array of custom kitchen cabinets, ranging from traditional raised-panel designs to highly popular, transitional shaker cabinets, and ultra-modern flat-panel styles. Every piece is built to your specific room dimensions.

Can I visit your kitchen showroom without an appointment?

While walk-ins to our kitchen showroom are always welcome, we highly recommend scheduling an appointment. This ensures one of our kitchen design experts is available to give you undivided attention and guide you through our product selections.

How does the kitchen design process work?

The process begins with an initial consultation to discuss your vision, budget, and functional needs. We then measure your space, present 3D renderings of the proposed kitchen design, and refine the layout and material choices with you before manufacturing begins.

What makes shaker cabinets so popular?

Shaker cabinets are celebrated for their clean lines, unpretentious design, and timeless appeal. Their classic five-piece door with a recessed center panel blends seamlessly into both traditional and contemporary kitchen designs, making them incredibly versatile.

Do you handle the installation of the cabinets?

As a promotional partner for premier cabinetry manufacturers, we connect you directly with the experts who handle both the creation and professional installation of your custom cabinets, ensuring a perfect fit and finish.
