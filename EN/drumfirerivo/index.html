Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book a moving company?
We recommend booking your moving company at least four to six weeks in advance, especially during the busy summer months. This ensures we can secure your preferred date and allocate the appropriate crew size for your home.
Do your local movers provide boxes and supplies?
Yes, our local movers can supply high-quality moving boxes, specialized wardrobe cartons, bubble wrap, and packing tape. You can choose to have these delivered prior to moving day if you prefer to pack yourself, or we can bring them on the day of the move if we are handling the packing.
What is included in your professional packing services?
Our packing services include all necessary materials and the labor required to safely box your belongings. We carefully wrap fragile items, categorize boxes by room, and provide a detailed inventory. We offer both full-home packing and partial packing for specific, difficult rooms like kitchens or libraries.
Are your long distance movers licensed for interstate travel?
Absolutely. The long distance movers we coordinate with maintain the proper federal operating authority required for interstate commerce. Compliance with national transportation regulations ensures your goods are handled safely and legally across state borders.
How do you protect large furniture during the move?
Our moving teams utilize heavy-duty, quilted moving blankets to wrap all large furniture pieces before they are loaded onto the truck. Additionally, we use shrink wrap to secure doors and drawers, and specialized padding for delicate corners to prevent scuffs and scratches.
