Frequently Asked Questions

What defines a premium residential moving company?

A premium residential moving company prioritizes the safety of your belongings, clear communication, and punctuality. They offer comprehensive solutions, from standard transport to full-scale packing services, ensuring a seamless experience.

Are packing services included in the move?

Packing services are typically offered as an optional add-on to customize your moving package. You can choose partial packing for fragile items or a full-service option where the movers handle everything in your home.

What is the difference between local movers and long distance movers?

Local movers generally handle relocations within the same city or state, often charging by the hour. Long distance movers manage interstate or cross-country relocations, where pricing is typically based on the weight of the shipment and the total distance traveled.

How far in advance should I book my move?

It is recommended to book your moving company at least 4 to 6 weeks in advance, especially during the peak summer moving season. Early booking ensures you secure your preferred dates and gives you ample time to coordinate.

How are my items protected during transit?

Professional movers use specialized equipment, moving blankets, and secure strapping to protect your belongings in the truck. For maximum security, utilizing professional packing services guarantees that items are boxed and padded correctly.
