Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about our premium event catering services.

What types of events do you cater?

We specialize in a wide range of premium events. Our primary focus is on exquisite wedding catering and professional corporate catering, but we also provide exceptional service for social galas, private milestone parties, and large-scale charity events.

Do you offer both buffet catering and plated dinners?

Yes, we offer highly customizable dining formats. You can choose from an elegant plated dinner for a formal atmosphere, dynamic buffet catering for varied flavor profiles, or even interactive food stations depending on the flow of your specific event.

Can you accommodate specific dietary restrictions?

Absolutely. We believe every guest should enjoy an extraordinary meal. Our culinary teams are experienced in designing menus that accommodate vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, and other specific dietary requirements without compromising on taste or presentation.

How far in advance should we book for wedding catering?

Because premium dates fill up quickly, we recommend securing your wedding catering services 9 to 12 months in advance. This allows ample time for menu tastings, detailed planning, and ensuring every culinary element aligns with your vision.

Do you handle event rentals and staffing?

Yes, the catering partners we showcase provide comprehensive, full-service solutions. This typically includes coordinating necessary rentals like tables, premium linens, and glassware, as well as providing a highly trained staff of servers, bartenders, and culinary professionals.
