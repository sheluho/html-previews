Frequently Asked Questions

What types of entry doors do you specialize in?

We focus extensively on premium fiberglass doors and solid wood variants. Fiberglass doors are particularly popular for front door replacement because they offer incredible durability, excellent insulation against weather extremes, and require minimal maintenance while beautifully mimicking the grain of natural wood.

How long does a typical front door replacement take?

Our specialized door installation teams are highly efficient. A standard front door replacement usually takes just a few hours from removing the old frame to fitting, sealing, and testing the new entry door. We ensure your home is secure by the end of the day.

Can you replace all the interior doors in my house at once?

Absolutely. Whole-home interior doors replacement is one of our most popular services. We utilize advanced measuring techniques to custom-cut every door before arrival, allowing us to install multiple interior doors cleanly and efficiently, often within just one or two days.

Do you also install custom closet doors?

Yes, as a comprehensive entry and interior doors installation company, we provide tailored closet door solutions. Whether you need sleek sliding doors, space-saving bi-folds, or mirrored panels, we ensure they are perfectly aligned and glide effortlessly.

Do I need to prepare my home before the door installation?

We kindly ask that you simply clear the immediate areas around the doorways being serviced and remove any fragile items nearby. Our professional team will handle all the heavy lifting, prep work, and final clean-up, leaving you with beautifully finished entry and interior doors.
