Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I choose fiberglass doors for my front entry?

Fiberglass doors are an excellent choice for front door replacement because they offer the aesthetic appeal of natural wood without the maintenance. They are highly resistant to warping, rotting, and cracking, making them ideal for areas with fluctuating temperatures and harsh weather conditions. Additionally, they provide superior insulation.

How long does a standard door installation take?

A standard door installation typically takes between 2 to 4 hours per door, depending on the complexity of the frame and the condition of the existing structure. Complete entry door systems with sidelights or custom sizing may require a full day. Professional installers ensure the job is done efficiently without compromising on the precise fit required for security and weatherproofing.

Should I replace all my interior doors at once?

While replacing all interior doors at once provides immediate visual consistency throughout your home, it is not strictly necessary. Many homeowners choose to phase their upgrades, starting with the main living areas or primary bedrooms. The key is to select a style and finish that can be easily matched if you decide to complete the rest of the home later.

Can you install doors in non-standard frame sizes?

Yes, professional installation teams are equipped to handle custom framing and non-standard sizes. Whether you are dealing with an older home that has settled or a unique architectural opening, experts can either modify the rough opening to fit a standard door or source a custom-built door to fit the existing space perfectly.
