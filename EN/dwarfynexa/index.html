Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our premium wash services.

What exactly is included in a premium car wash?
A premium car wash includes a thorough exterior foam bath, high-pressure rinse, wheel and tire deep cleaning, and a meticulous interior vacuum and wipe-down, leaving your vehicle looking completely refreshed.
How long does the hand car wash take?
Our signature hand car wash typically takes between 45 to 60 minutes, depending on the size and condition of the vehicle. We take our time to ensure every detail is handled with precision.
Can I upgrade to an auto wash membership later?
Absolutely. You can upgrade to an auto wash membership at any time. Members enjoy the convenience of daily washes, priority lane access, and exclusive discounts on detailing services.
Is the express wash safe for newer paint jobs?
Yes, our express wash utilizes state-of-the-art, soft-touch materials and pH-balanced soaps that are completely safe and gentle on all types of modern automotive paint and clear coats.
Do you offer interior detailing alongside the exterior wash?
Yes. While our express wash focuses on the exterior, our premium car wash and hand car wash packages include comprehensive interior vacuuming, dashboard conditioning, and window cleaning.
