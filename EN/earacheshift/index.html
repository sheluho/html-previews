Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between your recommended method and traditional steam cleaning?

Traditional steam cleaning often uses harsh soaps and chemicals that leave behind a sticky residue. This residue actually attracts dirt, causing your carpets to soil faster. The methods we promote use advanced, chemical-free technology (like alkaline water) to flush out dirt, resulting in a true zero-residue clean that stays fresh longer.

Is the upholstery cleaning process safe for my delicate furniture?

Yes. Upholstery cleaning requires a delicate touch to avoid shrinking or damaging the fabric. The professionals utilize specialized tools and non-toxic solutions to lift away body oils, dust, and allergens from your sofas and chairs without compromising the material integrity.

Can professional carpet cleaning remove tough pet odors?

Absolutely. Standard carpet cleaning only cleans the surface, but specialized pet treatments are designed to reach the backing of the carpet where urine and odors get trapped. The targeted treatments neutralize the odor at its source rather than just masking it with perfumes.

How does the rug cleaning process work?

Area rugs, especially Oriental or woven rugs, require distinct care compared to wall-to-wall carpeting. The rug cleaning process involves carefully dusting the rug to remove dry particulate soil, followed by a gentle, residue-free washing process that protects the dyes and fringe, leaving the rug vibrant and thoroughly sanitized.

Are the stain removal products safe for children and pets?

Yes, safety is a top priority. The stain removal and overall cleaning solutions recommended on our platform are non-toxic, eco-friendly, and free from harsh chemicals. Once the carpets and upholstery are dry, they are completely safe for babies to crawl on and pets to play on.
