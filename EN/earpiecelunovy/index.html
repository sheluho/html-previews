Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about booking and preparing for your photography sessions.

How far in advance should we book our wedding photographer?

It is highly recommended to secure your date at least 9 to 12 months in advance. Popular dates during the peak wedding season fill up quickly. Reaching out early ensures that Studio Veil can accommodate your preferred schedule and begin the planning process.

Do you offer destination wedding photography?

Yes, destination wedding photography is a core offering. Whether you are traveling across the country or internationally, customized travel packages can be arranged to ensure you receive the same exceptional quality and artistic vision anywhere in the world.

Are engagement photos included in the packages?

Engagement photos are available as a standalone service or integrated into comprehensive wedding packages. These sessions are highly encouraged, as they allow you to become comfortable with your photographer's style before the actual wedding day.

When is the best time for bridal portraits?

Bridal portraits are typically scheduled 1 to 2 months prior to the wedding day. This timeline provides an excellent opportunity for a trial run of your hair and makeup, ensuring everything looks flawless on camera without the time constraints of the wedding day itself.

How long does it take to receive our final gallery?

Careful editing and curation take time to ensure the highest quality. Generally, a sneak peek is provided shortly after the event, while the complete, meticulously edited gallery of your wedding photography is delivered within 6 to 8 weeks following the celebration.
