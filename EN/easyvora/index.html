Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our entry and interior doors services.

What materials are best for front door replacement?

For front door replacement, we highly recommend fiberglass doors due to their exceptional durability, energy efficiency, and resistance to denting, rotting, and rusting. Wood and steel are also excellent options depending on your aesthetic preferences and security needs.

How long does the door installation process typically take?

A standard door installation usually takes a few hours per door. However, if the frame requires extensive repair or custom fitting, the process may take slightly longer. We always strive to minimize disruption to your home.

Can you replace both entry doors and interior doors at the same time?

Absolutely. We frequently handle full-house projects that encompass both entry doors and interior doors. Coordinating these upgrades together ensures a cohesive design throughout your home and can often be completed more efficiently.

Are fiberglass doors energy efficient?

Yes, fiberglass doors are among the most energy-efficient options available. They typically feature a solid polyurethane foam core that provides superior insulation, helping to keep your home comfortable year-round and potentially lowering your energy bills.

Do you offer custom sizing for older homes with non-standard frames?

Yes. Older homes often feature unique architectural details and non-standard openings. Our door installation experts are highly skilled in custom-fitting entry doors and interior doors to ensure a perfect, secure, and aesthetically pleasing result regardless of the frame size.
