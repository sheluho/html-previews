Frequently Asked Questions

What does a turnkey renovation include?

A turnkey renovation means we handle everything from the initial structural planning and permitting through the entire construction phase and final finishing touches. You simply turn the key and walk into your newly finished, beautifully appointed space.

Do you specialize in whole home remodeling?

Yes, our primary focus is on large-scale, whole home remodeling projects. We excel in environments where we can completely reimagine the flow, function, and aesthetic of your property to create a cohesive living experience.

Can you manage complex home additions?

Absolutely. Adding square footage requires careful planning and deep structural expertise. As an experienced general contractor, we ensure your home addition seamlessly integrates with your existing architecture, both visually and structurally.

How long does a typical home renovation take?

Timelines vary significantly based on the scope of work. A whole home remodel typically requires several months of active construction, preceded by thorough planning, material sourcing, and permitting phases to ensure a smooth build.

What makes Ecartaro different from other contractors?

We bring an editorial lifestyle approach to construction. We prioritize not just structural integrity and code compliance, but the visual harmony, material quality, and refined details that elevate a house into a beautifully curated home.
