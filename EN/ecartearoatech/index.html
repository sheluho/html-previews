Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our custom closet processes.

What is the process for designing custom closets?

The process begins with an in-home measurement and consultation. We assess your space, inventory your storage needs, and create a 3D digital model of your new custom closets. Once the design is approved, the components are precision-cut and scheduled for installation.

How long does a walk-in closet installation take?

Most standard walk-in closet installations are completed within one to two days. However, the timeline can vary depending on the complexity of the design, the size of the room, and any specialized lighting or finishes required.

Can you build a freestanding wardrobe?

Yes. If your home lacks traditional closets, we can design and install a stunning freestanding wardrobe. These units offer the same structural integrity and organizational benefits as built-in closets, customized perfectly to your room's aesthetic.

What materials are used for a closet organizer?

Our partners use high-quality, durable materials including furniture-grade melamine, solid wood veneers, and heavy-duty metal hardware. Every closet organizer is built to withstand daily use while maintaining a premium appearance.

Do you optimize small reach-in closet spaces?

Absolutely. Reach-in closet optimization is one of our most requested services. By removing standard single rods and installing tiered hanging systems and custom shelving, we can often double the usable storage in a tight space.
