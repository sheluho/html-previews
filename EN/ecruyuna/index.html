Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries regarding our kitchen design insights.

What styles of kitchen cabinets do you showcase?

We feature a diverse array of profiles, ranging from traditional and classic shaker cabinets to ultra-modern, flat-panel custom cabinets. Our goal is to present options that fit any architectural style or personal aesthetic.

Can I visit a kitchen showroom to see the designs?

Yes, while we act as a promotional partner, we encourage you to visit the official Kountry Kraft kitchen showroom or authorized local dealers. Seeing the custom cabinets in person is the best way to appreciate the fine craftsmanship and finishes.

How long does a custom kitchen design project take?

Timelines for custom cabinetry vary depending on the complexity of the kitchen design, the chosen materials, and current manufacturing schedules. Typically, custom orders take several weeks from final design approval to delivery.

What materials are used in your custom cabinets?

The cabinets featured are constructed using premium quality hardwoods, high-grade veneers, and durable core materials. Finishes include a wide variety of custom stains, paints, and protective topcoats designed for longevity.

Do you offer hardware and internal drawer organization?

Absolutely. A truly functional kitchen relies on smart interiors. The custom cabinets we highlight can be equipped with specialized hardware, built-in spice racks, custom utensil dividers, and advanced drawer organization systems.
