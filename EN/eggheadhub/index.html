Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our custom furniture process.

How long does custom furniture take to build?
The timeline for bespoke furniture varies depending on the complexity of the piece and material availability. On average, a custom dining table or custom sofa takes between 8 to 12 weeks from final design approval to delivery.
Can I choose the wood species for my custom dining table?
Absolutely. We offer a wide selection of premium, sustainably sourced hardwoods including Walnut, White Oak, Cherry, and Maple. During your showroom visit, you can view large sample boards to choose the perfect grain and tone.
What makes bespoke furniture different from store-bought?
Handmade furniture is crafted specifically for your space using traditional joinery methods rather than mass-produced hardware. This ensures superior structural integrity, completely personalized dimensions, and a level of detail that factory-made pieces simply cannot match.
Do you offer custom sofas in specific performance fabrics?
Yes. Our custom sofa program includes hundreds of fabric options, including kid-friendly and pet-friendly performance fabrics that resist stains and wear. We invite you to the showroom to feel the textiles in person.
How do I start the design process for handmade furniture?
Getting started is easy. Simply fill out the contact form below to schedule a consultation. We will discuss your vision, intended use, and space dimensions, and then provide an initial concept sketch and estimate.
