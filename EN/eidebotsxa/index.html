Installation Inquiries.

Common questions about our door replacement process.

How long does a front door replacement typically take?

In most standard cases, a front door replacement can be completed in a single day. This includes removing the old frame, preparing the rough opening, installing the new pre-hung unit, sealing, and adding exterior trim. Custom sizes or structural modifications may require additional time.

Are fiberglass doors better than traditional wood?

For most climates, fiberglass doors offer superior durability. They resist warping, rotting, and denting better than wood, while requiring significantly less maintenance. High-quality fiberglass can also mimic the rich grain of natural wood perfectly, providing the aesthetic appeal without the upkeep.

Do you handle interior doors alongside exterior entries?

Yes, we are a comprehensive entry and interior doors installation company. We can replace a single damaged bedroom door, upgrade all the interior doors in your home to a modern style, or install specialized options like pocket doors and bi-folds for closets.

Will a new entry door improve my home's energy efficiency?

Absolutely. Older doors often have compromised weatherstripping or lack proper core insulation. Modern entry doors feature polyurethane foam cores and advanced weatherstripping systems that drastically reduce drafts and heat transfer, helping to stabilize your indoor climate.

What is included in your door installation process?

Our comprehensive service includes a detailed initial measurement, removal and disposal of your old door, preparation of the opening, professional installation ensuring the unit is plumb and level, application of superior sealants, and installation of your chosen hardware and trim.
