Frequently Asked Questions

What types of events do you provide catering for?

We offer comprehensive event catering for a wide variety of occasions. Our core focus includes wedding catering, corporate catering for galas, board meetings, and private social gatherings.

Do you offer both plated dinner and buffet catering options?

Yes, absolutely. We tailor the dining experience to your preference. Whether you desire a formal, multi-course plated dinner or an interactive, visually stunning buffet catering setup, our team executes both styles flawlessly.

Can you accommodate specific dietary restrictions?

Yes. We understand that guests may have diverse dietary needs. Our culinary team is highly experienced in preparing gluten-free, vegan, vegetarian, and allergen-friendly options without compromising on taste or presentation.

How far in advance should we book our wedding catering?

For wedding catering, we recommend reaching out at least 6 to 9 months in advance. Prime dates during the busy season fill up quickly, and securing your date early ensures ample time for menu tastings and detailed planning.

Are service staff included in your event catering packages?

Yes, professional service staff, including servers, bartenders, and on-site coordinators, can be fully integrated into your custom event catering package to ensure a smooth and seamless experience for you and your guests.
