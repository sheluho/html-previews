Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about HVAC systems and our promotional services.

How often should I schedule HVAC maintenance?

Industry experts recommend having your heating and cooling systems serviced at least twice a year. Schedule an air conditioner tune-up in the spring and a furnace inspection in the fall to ensure optimal performance and prevent unexpected breakdowns.

What are the signs that I need a new air conditioner?

Common signs include age (units over 10-15 years), frequent and costly repairs, rising energy bills, uneven cooling, and loud grinding or squealing noises. If your current unit struggles to keep up, a professional AC installation might be more cost-effective than ongoing repairs.

Can a heat pump replace my traditional furnace?

Yes. A heat pump acts as a highly efficient two-in-one system, providing cooling in the summer and transferring heat indoors during the winter. Depending on your local climate, it can completely replace a traditional furnace while significantly lowering your carbon footprint.

What is involved in a professional AC installation?

A proper AC installation begins with a thorough assessment of your home's square footage and ductwork to determine the correct unit size. Technicians will then safely remove the old equipment, install the new condenser and evaporator coils, check refrigerant levels, and calibrate the thermostat for maximum efficiency.

Why is my heating and cooling system making strange noises?

Unusual sounds like banging, whistling, or rattling are warning signs. They can indicate loose parts, motor issues, or ductwork blockages. It is crucial to have a qualified HVAC technician inspect the system promptly to prevent more severe damage to your furnace or air conditioner.
