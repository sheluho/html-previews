Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a deep cleaning service?

A deep cleaning involves a meticulous, top-to-bottom approach that goes beyond standard maintenance. It typically includes detailed dusting of baseboards, ceiling fans, blinds, and vents, deep scrubbing of bathroom grout and tiles, interior window cleaning, and extensive sanitization of kitchen appliances and cabinets.

Do I need to be home while the maids are cleaning?

No, you do not need to be home. Most clients provide a spare key or an entry code so the professionals can perform the house cleaning while they are at work or running errands. The service providers are fully vetted, trusted professionals.

How often should I schedule a recurring cleaning?

The ideal frequency depends on your household's size and lifestyle. Bi-weekly (every two weeks) is the most popular choice for maintaining a consistently clean home. However, busy families with pets often prefer weekly maid service, while others opt for a monthly refresh.

What is a move-out cleaning, and why is it important?

A move-out cleaning is an intensive service performed in an empty home to prepare it for the next occupants. It is highly detailed and often required by landlords to ensure the return of a security deposit, or appreciated by buyers taking possession of a new property.

Are cleaning supplies and equipment provided?

Yes, the professional house cleaning teams bring their own commercial-grade equipment and high-quality cleaning supplies. If you have specific preferences or require specialized products for certain surfaces, you can arrange this with your service provider.
