Frequently Asked Questions

How long will our cinematic wedding video be?

The length of your final wedding film depends on the package you select. We typically deliver a high-energy highlight reel (3-5 minutes) for easy sharing, alongside a longer documentary-style edit (15-30+ minutes) that includes full speeches, vows, and extended dances.

Do you travel for destination weddings?

Yes, we love to travel! As a dedicated wedding videographer team, we are available for destination weddings and elopements worldwide. Travel fees are calculated based on location and are discussed upfront during your consultation.

What is your approach to event videography?

For corporate and private events, our goal is to capture the atmosphere and key moments without interrupting the flow of the gathering. We use discreet, state-of-the-art equipment to ensure high-quality audio and visuals while remaining a fly on the wall.

How long does it take to receive our wedding film?

Cinematic editing is a detailed and creative process. You can generally expect to receive your highlight film within 4 to 6 weeks after your event, with the full documentary edit following shortly after. We prioritize quality and storytelling in our post-production process.

Do we get to choose the music for our video?

Because we legally license all music used in our films for online sharing, we curate the soundtrack from high-quality licensing platforms. However, we always welcome your input on the genre, tempo, and overall vibe you want your film to convey.
