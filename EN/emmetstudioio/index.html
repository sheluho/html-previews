Frequently Asked Questions

What is your wedding photography style?

Our style is a blend of fine-art editorial and authentic photojournalism. We aim to capture candid, raw emotions alongside beautifully composed, timeless portraits.

Do you offer engagement photos before the wedding?

Yes, we highly recommend an engagement session. It allows us to get to know each other, helping you feel more comfortable in front of the lens before your wedding day.

Can we book you for destination wedding photography?

Absolutely. We are available for destination weddings worldwide. We will work closely with you to manage travel logistics and ensure seamless coverage wherever your love takes you.

When should we schedule our bridal portraits?

Bridal portraits are typically scheduled 1 to 2 months before the wedding day. This gives you a perfect opportunity to do a trial run with your hair, makeup, and dress.

How long does it take to receive our final gallery?

We pour immense care into the editing process. You can expect to receive a curated preview gallery within a few days, and your full, beautifully edited collection within 6 to 8 weeks following the event.
