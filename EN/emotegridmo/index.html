Frequently Asked Questions

What is ceramic coating and why do I need it?

Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer that chemically bonds with your vehicle's factory paint, creating a layer of protection. It prevents dirt, grime, and stain marks from adhering to the clear coat, making it much easier to clean while providing a long-lasting, glossy finish.

How often should I get full auto detailing?

For optimal maintenance, we recommend a comprehensive auto detailing service at least two to three times a year. However, if your vehicle experiences heavy daily use, frequent exposure to harsh weather, or you often transport pets, more frequent interior detailing may be beneficial.

Does paint protection fix existing scratches?

Paint protection itself is designed to shield the paint from future damage. However, before any protective film or coating is applied, we perform paint correction—a process of machine polishing that removes or significantly reduces existing swirls and light scratches, ensuring a flawless surface beneath the protection.

What is included in interior detailing?

Our interior detailing process is highly meticulous. It involves deep vacuuming, steam cleaning carpets and upholstery, conditioning leather surfaces, wiping down and dressing all plastics and vinyl, cleaning interior glass, and neutralizing lingering odors to restore a pristine cabin environment.

How long does the car detailing process take?

The duration depends entirely on the services selected and the initial condition of the vehicle. A standard auto detailing package might take 4 to 6 hours, while advanced paint correction paired with a ceramic coating application can take a full day or more to ensure curing times are respected.
