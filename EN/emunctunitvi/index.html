Frequently Asked Questions

What types of kitchen cabinets are most popular?

Currently, shaker cabinets are highly sought after due to their minimalist design and versatility. However, flat-panel and custom cabinets featuring integrated hardware are also very popular for modern aesthetics.

Why is professional kitchen design important?

Professional kitchen design ensures that your space is optimized for the "working triangle" (sink, stove, refrigerator). It prevents costly layout mistakes and ensures that custom cabinets are measured and installed with precision.

What should I look for in a kitchen showroom?

A quality kitchen showroom should offer a diverse range of physical samples, allowing you to feel the build quality, inspect the hinges, and observe how different finishes react to lighting before making a commitment.

Are custom cabinets worth the investment?

Yes. Unlike stock options, custom cabinets maximize every inch of your available space. They are typically built with superior materials and craftsmanship, resulting in a longer lifespan and higher resale value for your home.

How do I start my renovation journey?

Begin by gathering inspiration and defining your budget. Next, consult with a design expert to discuss your vision. Finally, review material options and finalize your layout before ordering your cabinetry.
