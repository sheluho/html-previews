Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the moving process.

How far in advance should I book my moving company?

It is highly recommended to book your move at least 4 to 6 weeks in advance, especially during the busy summer months. Early booking ensures you secure your preferred date and gives you ample time to prepare.

Do you offer partial packing services?

Yes, flexible packing services are available. You can opt for full-service packing where professionals handle everything, or partial packing where they only pack specific fragile items like kitchenware or artwork while you pack the rest.

Are my belongings protected during the move?

Professional movers provide basic liability coverage as required by law. For added peace of mind, Full Value Protection is typically available for purchase, which covers the repair or replacement of items damaged in transit.

What items cannot be loaded onto the moving truck?

For safety and regulatory reasons, movers cannot transport hazardous materials (paint, chemicals, propane), perishable foods, or living plants. It is also advised to keep important documents, jewelry, and medications with you.

How is the cost of my move calculated?

For local movers, costs are generally calculated based on an hourly rate and the number of crew members needed. For long distance movers, the estimate is usually based on the total weight of your belongings and the distance traveled.
