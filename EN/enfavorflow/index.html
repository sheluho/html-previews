Frequently Asked Questions

What makes energy efficient windows better than standard ones?

Energy efficient windows utilize multiple panes of glass, specialized low-emissivity (Low-E) coatings, and insulating argon gas fills between the panes. This construction significantly reduces heat transfer, keeping your home warmer in winter and cooler in summer while lowering your energy bills.

Are vinyl windows a durable choice?

Yes, modern vinyl windows are exceptionally durable. They resist moisture, do not rot or corrode, and never need to be painted. They provide excellent thermal insulation and are designed to withstand harsh weather conditions over decades of use.

What are double hung windows?

A double hung window features two operable sashes (the framed glass panels) that slide vertically up and down in the frame. This allows you to open both the top and bottom for superior ventilation. Additionally, they often tilt inward, making it easy to clean the exterior glass from safely inside your home.

How long does the window installation process take?

For a standard-sized home, professional window installation typically takes 1 to 2 days. The exact timeframe depends on the number of replacement windows being installed and any structural challenges, but our crews prioritize efficiency and thorough clean-up.

Will replacement windows really lower my energy bills?

Absolutely. By sealing drafts and minimizing the heat transfer through the glass and frames, energy efficient windows ease the workload on your HVAC system. Many homeowners see a noticeable reduction in their monthly heating and cooling costs immediately after installation.
