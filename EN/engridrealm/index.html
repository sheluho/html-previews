Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers about our featured auto detailing and ceramic coating services.

What is ceramic coating and why do I need it? ▼

Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer applied by hand to the exterior of a vehicle. It chemically bonds with the factory paint, creating a layer of protection that is significantly harder and more durable than traditional wax. It defends against UV damage, chemical stains, and makes washing your car much easier due to its hydrophobic properties.

How long does a professional ceramic coating last? ▼

Depending on the specific grade of the coating applied and how well the vehicle is maintained, a professional ceramic coating can last anywhere from 2 to 5 years, or even longer. Regular maintenance washes are essential to preserve its protective and hydrophobic qualities.

What does full interior detailing include? ▼

Full interior detailing goes far beyond a standard vacuum. It typically includes deep extraction of carpets and upholstery, steam cleaning to remove stubborn stains and bacteria, specialized leather cleaning and conditioning, and meticulous dusting and dressing of all hard surfaces, dashboard, and door panels.

Is paint correction necessary before ceramic coating? ▼

Yes, paint correction is highly recommended and often required. Ceramic coating is semi-permanent and will seal in whatever state the paint is in. To ensure a flawless, mirror-like finish, the paint must be polished to remove swirl marks, light scratches, and oxidation before the coating is applied.

How often should I get my car detailed? ▼

For optimal upkeep, it is recommended to have a thorough exterior and interior detailing at least two to three times a year. However, if your vehicle is subjected to harsh weather conditions, frequent travel, or you simply prefer a pristine ride, quarterly detailing is ideal.
