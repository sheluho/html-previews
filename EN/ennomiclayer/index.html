Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers regarding your insulation and siding projects.

What is the difference between spray foam and traditional home insulation?

Traditional fiberglass insulation sits between studs to slow heat transfer, but it can leave small air gaps. Spray foam insulation expands upon application, filling every crack and crevice to create a complete air barrier. This makes spray foam highly effective at stopping drafts and significantly improving energy efficiency in both walls and attics.

How do I know if I need new attic insulation?

Signs that you may need new attic insulation include fluctuating indoor temperatures, unexpectedly high heating or cooling bills, drafts in the upper rooms, or visible moisture and mold in the attic space. Upgrading your attic insulation is one of the most cost-effective ways to stabilize your home's climate.

Which is better: vinyl siding or fiber cement siding?

Both are excellent choices depending on your priorities. Vinyl siding is cost-effective, very low maintenance, and comes in a wide variety of colors. Fiber cement siding is typically more expensive but offers superior durability, fire resistance, and a thicker, more authentic wood-grain appearance. Our contractors can help you weigh the pros and cons for your specific house siding project.

Can I install new house siding over old siding?

While it is sometimes possible to install new vinyl siding over existing wood siding if it is in good condition, it is generally recommended to remove the old material. Removing old siding allows the contractor to inspect the underlying structure for rot or damage, install a fresh moisture barrier, and add exterior home insulation boards if desired.

How long does a typical siding installation take?

The timeline for a house siding replacement depends on the size of your home and the materials chosen. On average, a complete tear-off and installation of new vinyl siding or fiber cement siding takes about one to two weeks. Weather conditions and the need for structural repairs can also impact the schedule.
