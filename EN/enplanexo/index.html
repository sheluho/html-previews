Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in full-service wedding planning? ▼

Full-service wedding planning covers everything from budget creation and venue scouting to design concept, vendor booking, and day-of management. We are with you every step of the way to ensure a cohesive and stress-free experience.

Do I really need a day of coordinator? ▼

Yes! A day of coordinator is essential for managing the timeline, directing vendors, and handling any unexpected hiccups on the actual wedding day. This allows you, your family, and your bridal party to simply relax and enjoy the celebration.

Can you help plan a destination wedding? ▼

Absolutely. We specialize in destination wedding planning, managing the unique challenges of distant venues. From securing room blocks to sourcing local talent, we ensure your destination event is beautifully orchestrated.

When should we hire a wedding planner? ▼

We recommend hiring a wedding planner as soon as you get engaged, ideally 12 to 18 months before your desired date. This gives us ample time to secure top-tier venues and vendors that align with your vision.

How does your wedding coordinator work with our vendors? ▼

Our wedding coordinator serves as the main point of contact for all your vendors leading up to and on the day of the event. We distribute timelines, confirm arrival times, and ensure everyone works together seamlessly.
