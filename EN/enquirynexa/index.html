Frequently Asked Questions

Details on the process of securing your wedding videographer.

Do your partners travel for destination weddings?

Yes, the professionals we promote frequently travel internationally for destination events. Event videography requires flexibility, and bespoke travel packages can be arranged to ensure your multi-day celebration is captured wherever it takes place.

How long does it take to receive our wedding film?

Crafting a cinematic wedding video is a meticulous, artistic process. While timelines vary by provider, most couples receive their final feature films and highlight reels within 12 to 16 weeks following the event date.

Can we choose the music for our video?

To legally share your wedding film online, the music must be properly licensed. Your wedding videographer will typically curate a custom soundtrack from high-quality licensing platforms, selecting pieces that perfectly match the pacing and emotion of your specific footage.

Is drone footage always included?

Drone footage is highly dependent on the location, weather conditions, and local aviation regulations. While it is a popular addition to many wedding videography packages, it cannot be guaranteed if the venue is in restricted airspace or if conditions are unsafe on the day.

How do we start the booking process through EnquiryNexa?

Simply fill out the inquiry form at the bottom of this page with your date and venue details. As a promotional partner, we will forward your request to the premier wedding videography team at Wedding.Film to check availability and schedule an initial consultation.
