Frequently Asked Questions

What is ceramic coating and why do I need it? ▼

Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer applied to the exterior of your vehicle that chemically bonds with the factory paint. It creates a semi-permanent layer of protection that repels water, dirt, and grime, while preventing UV damage and oxidation. It significantly enhances gloss and makes routine maintenance much easier.

How long does a full auto detailing service take? ▼

The duration depends heavily on the condition of the vehicle and the specific services requested. A standard interior detailing might take 2 to 4 hours, whereas a full exterior paint correction followed by a ceramic coating application can take anywhere from 1 to 3 days to ensure the coating cures properly.

Can paint correction remove all scratches? ▼

Paint correction effectively removes surface-level defects, swirl marks, light oxidation, and minor scratches that reside within the clear coat layer. However, if a scratch has penetrated through the clear coat into the base color or primer, it cannot be safely leveled out and will require touch-up paint or respraying.

What is included in your interior detailing package? ▼

Our comprehensive interior detailing includes a thorough vacuuming, steam cleaning of carpets and upholstery, deep cleaning and conditioning of all leather surfaces, detailing of the dashboard, center console, and door panels, as well as streak-free interior glass cleaning to leave the cabin feeling brand new.

How often should I have my car professionally detailed? ▼

For a daily driven vehicle, we recommend a thorough car detailing service every 4 to 6 months to maintain the interior hygiene and exterior clear coat health. If you have applied a ceramic coating, routine safe-washes are sufficient, with a professional maintenance detail recommended annually.
