Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical bathroom renovation take?

The timeline for a bathroom renovation depends on the scope of the project. A simple bathtub replacement might take just a few days, whereas a full bathroom remodel involving layout changes and extensive tile installation can take several weeks.

What are the benefits of a walk-in shower?

A walk-in shower offers enhanced accessibility, making it safer for individuals of all ages. Furthermore, it creates an illusion of a larger space, is generally easier to clean than a traditional tub, and adds a sleek, modern aesthetic to your bathroom remodel.

Do you handle the tile installation as well?

Yes, the full-service bathroom remodeling company we promote handles comprehensive tile installation for floors, shower walls, and backsplashes, ensuring a watertight seal and a beautiful finish.

Can I just get a bathtub replacement instead of a full remodel?

Absolutely. If the rest of your bathroom is in good condition, a targeted bathtub replacement is a cost-effective way to refresh the space without committing to a full bathroom remodel.
