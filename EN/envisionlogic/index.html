Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about the professional house cleaning services we promote.

What is included in a standard house cleaning?

A standard house cleaning generally includes dusting all surfaces, vacuuming carpets, mopping hard floors, emptying trash, and thoroughly cleaning bathrooms and the kitchen. The professional maid service providers we promote tailor this checklist to suit your home's specific layout and needs.

How does deep cleaning differ from recurring cleaning?

While recurring cleaning focuses on maintaining a baseline of cleanliness through regular visits, deep cleaning is a more intensive, time-consuming process. It targets hard-to-reach areas, baseboards, interior windows, inside appliances, and deep grout lines, making it highly recommended for initial visits or seasonal spring cleaning.

Do I need to be home during the maid service?

No, it is not strictly necessary for you to be home. Many clients prefer to provide a key, garage code, or lockbox access so the cleaning professionals can work while the house is empty. The choice is entirely up to your comfort level and schedule.

What exactly is a move-out cleaning?

Move-out cleaning is a specialized service designed for empty homes. It includes all aspects of a deep clean, plus detailing inside all cabinets, drawers, refrigerators, and ovens. It ensures the property is pristine and ready for the next resident or final landlord inspection.

Do the cleaners bring their own supplies and equipment?

Yes, the professional house cleaning teams generally arrive fully equipped with premium cleaning supplies, vacuums, and mops. If you have specific preferences or require the use of specialized eco-friendly products you own, you can typically arrange this with the service provider beforehand.
