Frequently Asked Questions

How does your seasonal tire storage service work?

When you schedule a seasonal tire change, you can opt to leave your off-season tires with the service provider. They are cataloged, cleaned, and stored in a secure, climate-controlled facility to protect them from environmental degradation until you need them next season.

How often should I schedule a tire rotation?

Industry standards generally recommend a tire rotation every 5,000 to 8,000 miles, or roughly every time you have your oil changed. Regular rotation ensures even tread wear across all four tires, significantly extending their usable life and maintaining optimal traction.

What is included in a professional wheel alignment?

A complete wheel alignment involves adjusting the angles of your vehicle's wheels—specifically the camber, caster, and toe—so they are perfectly parallel to each other and perpendicular to the ground, according to the manufacturer's specifications. This prevents the vehicle from pulling to one side and stops premature tire wear.

Do you provide complete tire replacement services?

Yes, the providers we promote offer comprehensive tire replacement. This includes helping you select the appropriate tires for your vehicle and driving habits, removing the old tires, mounting the new ones, and performing precision computer balancing to ensure a smooth, vibration-free ride.

Are my tires insured while in storage?

Tire storage facilities associated with the service providers typically maintain comprehensive insurance coverage against fire, theft, and facility damage. We recommend discussing specific coverage details and terms directly with the service center when you drop off your tires for storage.
