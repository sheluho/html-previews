Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in standard lawn care services?

Standard lawn care typically includes scheduled lawn mowing, edging, and debris blowing. Extended maintenance plans often incorporate fertilization, weed control, and seasonal aeration to keep the turf healthy and resilient throughout the year.

Can you assist with custom garden design?

Yes, the professionals in our network specialize in comprehensive garden design. They work with you to select appropriate plants, optimize layout for sunlight and drainage, and create a cohesive aesthetic that complements your home's architecture.

How often should lawn mowing be scheduled?

For optimal health and appearance, lawn mowing should generally be performed weekly during the peak growing season. The exact frequency can vary based on your specific grass type, weather conditions, and the application of fertilizers.

What does a landscape installation project entail?

Landscape installation involves turning a design into reality. This can include soil preparation, planting trees and shrubs, laying sod or seed, and installing hardscape elements like borders or mulch beds. It is a full-service approach to upgrading your property.

Why is professional tree service important?

Professional tree service ensures the long-term health and structural safety of your trees. Experts can identify diseases early, perform proper pruning to encourage growth, and safely remove dead or hazardous branches that could pose a risk to your home.
