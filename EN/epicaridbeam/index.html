Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about planning your custom storage project.

How long does a custom closet installation typically take?

Most installations are completed within one to two days, depending on the scale and complexity of the design. A large walk-in closet may take slightly longer than a standard reach-in closet.

Can you design a wardrobe for a very small reach-in closet?

Absolutely. Small spaces benefit the most from a professional closet organizer. By utilizing double-hang sections and tailored shelving, we can significantly increase the usable space of any reach-in closet.

Are the custom closets and wardrobe systems adjustable?

Yes, many of the components in a modern closet organizer are fully adjustable. Shelves and hanging rods can often be moved up or down to accommodate changes in your wardrobe over time.

Do I need to empty my existing closet before the design consultation?

It is not necessary to empty your closet for the initial consultation. In fact, seeing your current items helps determine exactly what kind of storage solutions and space allocations you require.

What is the difference between a built-in wardrobe and a walk-in closet?

A walk-in closet is a dedicated room or large alcove you can physically step into, typically offering storage on multiple walls. A built-in wardrobe is usually constructed against a wall in a bedroom, featuring doors that open to reveal the storage space within, functioning much like an integrated piece of furniture.
