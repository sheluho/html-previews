Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical door installation take?

A standard front door replacement usually takes about half a day, ensuring your home is secure by evening. Interior doors installation can vary depending on the number of doors, but we work efficiently to minimize disruption to your daily life.

What are the benefits of fiberglass doors?

Fiberglass doors are highly resistant to weathering, meaning they won't warp, rot, or expand like traditional wood. They offer excellent insulation, improving your home's energy efficiency, and can be customized to mimic the elegant look of real wood grain.

Do you handle custom sizes for entry doors?

Yes, we provide custom sizing and configurations for unique entryways. Our team will take precise measurements to ensure your new entry doors fit perfectly, providing optimal security and insulation.

Are interior doors included in your services?

Absolutely. As a comprehensive entry and interior doors installation company, we offer a wide selection of interior doors, including solid core, hollow core, French doors, and sliding options to suit your home's aesthetic and functional needs.

How do I prepare for my front door replacement?

We ask that you clear the area immediately surrounding the entryway, both inside and outside, to give our installation team ample space to work safely. We handle the removal and disposal of your old door as part of our comprehensive service.
