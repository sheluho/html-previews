Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is a ceramic coating?

Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer applied by hand to the exterior of a vehicle. It chemically bonds with the factory clear coat, creating a semi-permanent layer of protection that repels water, dirt, and UV rays, while enhancing the gloss of your paint.

How long does the paint protection last?

Depending on the grade of the coating chosen and how well the vehicle is maintained, professional ceramic coatings can last anywhere from 2 to 5 years, or even longer. Regular maintenance washes help ensure its longevity.

What is included in an interior detailing service?

Our comprehensive interior detailing includes a thorough vacuuming, deep cleaning of hard plastics and vinyl, steam cleaning or extraction of carpets and upholstery, leather cleaning and conditioning, and streak-free interior glass cleaning.

Do I need paint correction before a coating?

Yes, highly recommended. Paint correction involves machine polishing to remove swirl marks, scratches, and oxidation. Since ceramic coating locks in the condition of the paint underneath it, you want the surface to be as flawless as possible before application.

How often should I get full auto detailing?

For standard daily drivers without a long-term coating, a full auto detailing (interior and exterior) is generally recommended 2 to 3 times a year to maintain the vehicle's condition, protect surfaces, and preserve its resale value.
