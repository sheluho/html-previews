Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries regarding our featured landscaping and lawn care services.

What is typically included in professional lawn care?

Professional lawn care generally encompasses regular lawn mowing, edging, fertilization, and weed management. These combined efforts are essential for maintaining the health, density, and vibrant color of your turf throughout the changing seasons.

How often should landscape maintenance occur?

The frequency depends heavily on the season and the specific growth rate of your grass and plants. During peak growing seasons, weekly or bi-weekly lawn mowing and trimming are recommended to keep the landscape looking pristine and structured.

Do you feature custom garden design?

Yes, our platform highlights providers who specialize in custom garden design. This process involves assessing your soil, sunlight exposure, and aesthetic preferences to create a sustainable and visually appealing planting strategy for your property.

What does a full landscape installation entail?

Landscape installation is the physical implementation of a design plan. It can involve soil grading, planting trees and shrubs, laying new sod, installing irrigation systems, and placing structural elements to completely overhaul an outdoor environment.

Why is dedicated tree service important?

Proper tree service is critical for both safety and aesthetics. Routine trimming removes dead or hazardous branches, prevents disease spread, and encourages healthy, balanced growth, ensuring the largest features of your landscape remain an asset rather than a liability.
