Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical turnaround time for a wedding film?

Most professional wedding videographer teams deliver the final edited films within 8 to 12 weeks after your event. This allows ample time for meticulous storytelling, audio mixing, and custom color grading to ensure perfection.

Do your partners offer drone coverage?

Yes. Aerial footage adds a breathtaking, cinematic scale to any wedding video. Drone coverage is typically included in premium packages, subject to weather conditions and local venue flight restrictions.

How unobtrusive is the event videography process?

The best wedding videography is captured candidly. Experienced videographers use long lenses and natural lighting whenever possible to blend into the background, ensuring you and your guests feel completely at ease while authentic moments are documented.

Can we choose the music for our cinematic wedding video?

Due to copyright laws, popular radio songs cannot be legally used in public or online wedding films. However, our featured creators source high-quality, fully licensed cinematic scores tailored to the specific vibe and emotional tone of your day.

What is the difference between a highlight reel and a documentary edit?

A highlight reel is a highly polished, 4-8 minute artistic short film summarizing the best moments of the day set to music. A documentary edit is a longer, chronological video that typically includes the full ceremony and major reception formalities in real-time.
