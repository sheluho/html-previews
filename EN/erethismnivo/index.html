Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about our replacement windows and installation process.

What makes energy efficient windows worth the investment?

Energy efficient windows use advanced glazing, low-E coatings, and gas fills to drastically reduce heat transfer. This means your HVAC system works less to maintain a comfortable temperature, lowering your monthly utility bills and providing long-term financial savings.

Are vinyl windows better than wood or aluminum?

Vinyl windows are highly popular because they offer exceptional thermal insulation, are remarkably durable, and require virtually zero maintenance. Unlike wood, they won't rot or require repainting, and unlike aluminum, they don't easily transfer heat.

How long does a typical window installation take?

Most standard replacement windows projects can be completed in just one to two days. Our professional installation teams work efficiently to minimize disruption to your daily life while ensuring a perfect, weather-tight seal.

What are the benefits of double hung windows?

Double hung windows feature two operable sashes that slide vertically. This allows for superior ventilation control (opening top and bottom simultaneously) and easy cleaning from inside your home, as the sashes tilt inward.

Do you handle custom sizing for replacement windows?

Absolutely. Every home is unique, and our replacement windows are custom-measured and manufactured to fit your exact existing openings. This guarantees the best possible insulation, security, and aesthetic finish.
