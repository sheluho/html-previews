Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about professional fabric care.

How often should I schedule professional carpet cleaning?

For most residential homes, professional carpet cleaning is recommended every 12 to 18 months. However, households with pets, children, or high foot traffic may benefit from professional steam cleaning every 6 to 9 months to effectively manage dirt buildup and allergens.

Is your upholstery cleaning safe for delicate fabrics?

Yes. Professional cleaners assess the specific fabric type of your furniture before beginning any work. They utilize specialized cleaning solutions and appropriate techniques designed to safely lift stains and dirt without causing shrinkage, color bleeding, or damage to delicate upholstery.

What methods are used for tough stain removal?

Stubborn spots require targeted treatments. Cleaners typically use industry-grade, specialized spotters tailored to the chemical makeup of the stain—whether it's wine, coffee, or pet accidents. This is often followed by hot water extraction (steam cleaning) to thoroughly rinse the area and remove any residue.

How long does it take for carpets to dry after steam cleaning?

Drying times vary based on humidity, airflow, and the specific carpet type, but typically range from 4 to 8 hours. Utilizing ceiling fans or air conditioning can significantly expedite the drying process after a professional steam cleaning session.

Do you handle oriental and area rug cleaning?

Absolutely. Rug cleaning requires distinct methodologies compared to wall-to-wall carpets. Professionals evaluate the rug's dyes and construction to determine the safest cleaning method, ensuring that fringes and fibers are thoroughly cleaned while protecting the rug's structural integrity.
