Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our cabinetry solutions.

What is the advantage of buying from a custom cabinets manufacturer?

Purchasing directly from the manufacturer means you avoid middleman retail markups. It allows for strict quality control, faster production times, and the ability to dictate exact specifications for your custom cabinets rather than relying on pre-fabricated, standard sizes.

Do I need an appointment to visit the kitchen showroom?

While walk-ins are always welcome, we highly recommend scheduling an appointment. This ensures that a dedicated specialist from our kitchen design team is available to give you a personalized tour, review your blueprints, and discuss your specific project needs without interruption.

Are shaker cabinets still in style?

Absolutely. Shaker cabinets remain one of the most popular and versatile choices in modern kitchen design. Their minimalist, clean-lined profile allows them to seamlessly blend into both traditional farmhouse aesthetics and sleek, contemporary spaces, ensuring a timeless investment for your home.

Do you offer full kitchen design services?

Yes. Our comprehensive kitchen design process goes beyond just supplying cabinets. Our experienced designers will work with you to optimize your layout, select appropriate finishes, and create 3D renderings, ensuring your new custom cabinets enhance both the beauty and functionality of your space.

How long does it take to manufacture custom cabinets?

Lead times vary depending on the complexity of your kitchen design, chosen materials, and current manufacturing capacity. During your consultation at the kitchen showroom, our team will provide a detailed timeline for your specific project, from initial design approval to final fabrication.
