Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the wedding photography experience.

Do you offer engagement photos as part of your packages?

Yes, engagement sessions are highly recommended and often included in comprehensive packages. It allows the wedding photographer to understand your dynamic as a couple and helps you feel more comfortable before the main event.

What is the typical turnaround time for wedding galleries?

Most providers aim to deliver a fully edited, high-resolution digital gallery within 6 to 8 weeks after the wedding date. Sneak peeks are often provided within the first week so you have beautiful images to share immediately.

Do you travel for destination wedding photography?

Absolutely. The providers we recommend are experienced in traveling internationally and domestically. Travel fees are typically calculated based on flights and minimal accommodation needs, keeping the process straightforward for you.

How do you handle bridal portraits on the day?

Bridal portraits are typically captured during the getting-ready phase and immediately following the first look or ceremony. The focus is on natural light and gentle direction to create elegant, timeless portraits without taking you away from your guests for too long.

How do we secure a wedding date?

To reserve your date, you simply fill out the inquiry form below. Estocada Zen will connect you with the provider who will review availability. A signed contract and a retainer fee with the actual service provider are required to officially lock in the date.
