Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries regarding HVAC installations and maintenance.

How often should I service my HVAC system?

Industry standards recommend professional maintenance at least twice a year. Typically, you should service your air conditioner in the spring and your furnace in the early fall to ensure peak performance.

What are the signs I need a new air conditioner?

Key indicators include your AC unit being over 10-15 years old, frequent breakdown requirements, rising energy bills despite normal usage, and inconsistent cooling throughout your home.

Do you cover both heating and cooling installations?

Yes, the professionals we promote handle comprehensive HVAC services. This includes complete furnace installations for heating and advanced air conditioner setups for cooling.

What is the main benefit of installing a heat pump?

A heat pump is highly energy-efficient because it transfers heat rather than generating it. It acts as both a heater in the winter and an air conditioner in the summer, providing year-round climate control from one unit.

How long does a typical AC installation take?

Most standard air conditioner installations can be completed within a single day. Complex installations or full HVAC system overhauls involving ductwork may require an additional day or two.
