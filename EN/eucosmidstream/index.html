Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a full bathroom remodel typically take?

The timeline varies significantly based on the project's scope. Generally, a full bathroom remodel takes between 2 to 4 weeks. This timeframe accounts for demolition, necessary plumbing changes, custom tile installation, and final fixture fittings.

Do you handle the plumbing and electrical work?

Yes. As an affiliate for a full-service bathroom remodeling company, the professionals we connect you with manage all necessary plumbing, electrical, and structural modifications using licensed and experienced tradespeople.

Can I convert my standard tub into a walk-in shower?

Absolutely. Tub-to-shower conversions are among the most highly requested services. It is an excellent way to create a more accessible, spacious, and modern bathing experience in your home.

What types of materials are best for bathroom renovations?

For environments exposed to high moisture, non-porous and water-resistant materials are crucial. We highly recommend porcelain for expert tile installation, and solid surfaces like quartz or granite for vanity countertops to ensure superior longevity.

Do I need to purchase my own fixtures and tiles?

You have the option to supply your own materials, but working with a full-service provider means you gain access to a comprehensive selection of premium materials and fixtures. Sourcing and delivery are handled entirely by the contractor to streamline your renovation.
