Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about upgrading your home's windows.

Why should I choose energy efficient windows?
Energy efficient windows utilize advanced glass coatings and specialized gas fills to prevent heat transfer. This means your home stays warmer in the winter and cooler in the summer, significantly reducing the workload on your HVAC system and lowering your monthly utility bills.
What are the benefits of double hung windows?
Double hung windows feature two operable sashes that slide vertically. This allows for excellent ventilation control—you can open the top, the bottom, or both. Additionally, modern double hung designs tilt inward, making it incredibly easy to clean the exterior glass from safely inside your home.
How long does professional window installation typically take?
While timelines vary based on the size of your home and the scope of the project, a dedicated replacement windows installation company can often complete an average-sized home in just one to two days. The focus is always on minimizing disruption while ensuring a flawless, weather-tight seal.
Are custom vinyl windows a durable choice?
Yes, absolutely. Modern vinyl windows are engineered to be incredibly resilient. They resist fading, peeling, and warping under extreme weather conditions. Unlike wood, they never need to be scraped, sanded, or repainted, making them a superb low-maintenance investment for any property.
How do I know it's time for replacement windows?
Key indicators include feeling drafts near the glass, condensation building up between glass panes (indicating a broken seal), difficulty opening or closing the sashes, and noticeable spikes in your heating or cooling bills. If you notice these signs, it's time to consult with an expert.
