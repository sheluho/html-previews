Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about professional tire management.

Why should I use professional tire storage?

Professional tire storage keeps your tires in a climate-controlled environment, preventing the rubber from degrading due to extreme temperature fluctuations. It also frees up substantial space in your garage and includes insurance coverage while stored.

How often do I need a tire rotation?

As a general rule, a tire rotation is recommended every 5,000 to 8,000 miles. Regular rotation promotes even tread wear, extends the life of your tires, and maintains optimal vehicle handling.

What is included in a tire installation service?

Tire installation includes removing the old tires, mounting the new set onto your wheels, balancing the wheels to prevent vibration, and installing new valve stems if necessary. It ensures a safe and secure fit for your vehicle.

When should I get a wheel alignment?

You should consider a wheel alignment whenever you get a tire replacement, if your vehicle pulls to one side while driving, if you notice uneven tread wear, or after hitting a large pothole or curb.

Are stored tires inspected before I pick them up?

Yes, before your seasonal changeover, technicians inspect your stored tires for adequate tread depth, sidewall cracking, and overall safety. This ensures you know the condition of your tires before they are mounted back on your vehicle.
