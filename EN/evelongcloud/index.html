Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your upcoming bathroom remodel.

How long does a standard bathroom renovation take?

The timeline for a full-service bathroom remodeling project varies based on the scope of work. A straightforward bathtub replacement might take a few days, while a complete gut and remodel with custom tile installation can take 2 to 4 weeks. Your contractor will provide a detailed schedule during the consultation.

Can you convert my old tub into a walk-in shower?

Absolutely. Tub-to-shower conversions are one of the most requested services. A modern walk-in shower not only improves the safety and accessibility of your bathroom but also gives the space a larger, more contemporary feel.

Do I need to purchase my own fixtures for the bathroom remodel?

You do not have to source your own materials. The professionals we connect you with offer full-service solutions, meaning they can supply premium fixtures, cabinetry, and tiles. However, if you have specific items already purchased, they can typically integrate them into the design.

What is involved in the bathtub replacement process?

A bathtub replacement involves carefully removing the existing tub, inspecting and updating the underlying plumbing if necessary, preparing the subfloor and walls, and installing the new tub unit. This is often followed by fresh tile installation or wall surrounds to ensure a watertight seal.

How do you ensure the tile installation is waterproof and durable?

Expert contractors utilize industry-standard waterproofing membranes and high-quality thin-set mortars behind the tiles. By combining proper substrate preparation with professional grouting and sealing techniques, the tile installation will remain beautiful, durable, and highly resistant to moisture for years to come.
