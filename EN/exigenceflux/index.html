Frequently Asked Questions

What types of window treatments do you promote? ▼

We specialize in promoting a comprehensive range of premium window coverings, including custom blinds, roller shades, elegant custom drapes, and architectural plantation shutters.

Are plantation shutters energy efficient? ▼

Yes, plantation shutters act as an excellent insulating barrier against the window glass. They help regulate indoor temperatures by blocking intense heat in the summer and reducing drafts during cooler months.

Can roller shades be customized for different light levels? ▼

Absolutely. Roller shades can be tailored with various fabric opacities, ranging from sheer, light-filtering materials for living areas to complete blackout fabrics ideal for bedrooms or media rooms.
