Frequently Asked Questions

What types of window treatments do you showcase?

We highlight a comprehensive selection including custom blinds, roller shades, cellular shades, custom drapes, and premium plantation shutters suitable for various residential and commercial applications.

Are plantation shutters energy efficient?

Yes, plantation shutters are highly regarded for their energy efficiency. They add a solid layer of insulation to your windows, helping to keep interiors cooler in the summer and warmer in the winter.

Can I combine shades and custom drapes?

Absolutely. Layering window treatments, such as pairing functional roller shades with decorative custom drapes, is a popular design choice that offers maximum control over light and privacy while adding depth to the room's decor.

How do I know which custom blinds are right for my space?

Choosing the right blinds depends on your functional needs (like moisture resistance for bathrooms or blackout capabilities for bedrooms) and your aesthetic preferences. Real wood offers warmth, while faux wood provides excellent durability in humid environments.

Do you provide measuring and installation details?

As an independent promotional operator, we introduce you to the styles and benefits of these products. Specific measuring, custom fitting, and professional installation services are coordinated directly through the primary provider.
