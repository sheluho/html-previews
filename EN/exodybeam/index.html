Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I schedule lawn mowing?

For optimal grass health, we recommend weekly lawn mowing during the peak growing seasons of spring and early summer. During slower growth periods, bi-weekly schedules may be sufficient. Consistent lawn care prevents weed overgrowth and keeps your turf dense.

Do you offer custom garden design?

Yes, our landscaping experts collaborate with you to create a personalized garden design. We consider your climate, soil conditions, and visual preferences to select plants and hardscaping elements that thrive year-round.

What is included in landscape installation?

Landscape installation encompasses the physical construction of your garden design. This includes soil preparation, planting of shrubs and flowers, laying fresh sod, installing mulch or decorative rock, and setting up defined garden beds.

Do you provide professional tree service?

Absolutely. Our tree service includes structural pruning, canopy shaping, and general maintenance for small to medium-sized trees and hedges. Proper trimming ensures the structural integrity of the plant and keeps your property safe.

When is the best time for new landscaping projects?

Spring and early fall are generally the best times for major landscape installation, as milder temperatures reduce stress on new plants. However, routine lawn care and maintenance can be initiated at any point during the active growing season.
