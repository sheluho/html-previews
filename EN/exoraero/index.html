Frequently Asked Questions

What does a full-service bathroom remodeling company do?

A full-service firm handles every element of your project from start to finish. This includes design consulting, demolition, plumbing, electrical, custom tile installation, cabinetry, and final fixture placement. You only communicate with one dedicated team.

How long does a custom walk-in shower installation take?

Depending on the complexity of the tile work and custom glass requirements, a walk-in shower conversion typically takes between one to two weeks. The exact timeline is provided during your initial consultation.

Can you replace my existing bathtub with a modern freestanding one?

Yes, bathtub replacement is one of our most requested services. We can remove built-in alcove tubs and reconfigure the plumbing and flooring to accommodate a beautiful freestanding bathtub.

Do I need to hire my own plumber or electrician?

No. As a turnkey bathroom renovation provider, all necessary specialized trades are coordinated and managed under our single point of contact. We ensure all work meets local building codes.

What kind of materials do you use for tile installation?

We utilize high-quality porcelain, ceramic, natural stone, and glass tiles sourced from trusted manufacturers. Proper waterproofing membranes are always installed prior to any tile application to guarantee longevity.
