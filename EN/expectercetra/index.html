Frequently Asked Questions

What are the benefits of upgrading my home insulation?

Upgrading your home insulation significantly improves energy efficiency, meaning your HVAC system works less to heat or cool your home. This leads to lower utility bills, a more comfortable living environment without drafts, and better noise reduction from outside.

How long does vinyl siding typically last?

High-quality vinyl siding is extremely durable and designed to withstand severe weather. With proper installation and minimal routine cleaning, it can last for decades without peeling, rotting, or requiring repainting.

Is spray foam better than traditional fiberglass?

Spray foam is often considered superior because it not only provides a high R-value (thermal resistance) but also acts as an air barrier. It expands to fill tiny cracks and gaps where traditional fiberglass batts might leave spaces, creating a tighter, more efficient home envelope.

Do you promote fiber cement siding installations?

Yes, we connect homeowners with contractors skilled in installing fiber cement siding. It is an excellent, premium option that offers the look of natural wood but with superior resistance to fire, rot, and wood-boring insects.

How do I know if I need new attic insulation?

Common signs include unusually high heating or cooling bills, rooms that are difficult to keep at a comfortable temperature, noticeable drafts, or ice dams forming on your roof during winter. A professional assessment can determine your current insulation levels.
