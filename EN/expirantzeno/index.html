Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical bathroom renovation take?

The timeline for a bathroom remodel varies depending on the scope of work. A straightforward bathtub replacement might take a few days, whereas a full-service bathroom remodeling project involving structural changes and extensive tile installation can take several weeks. Proper planning ensures minimal disruption to your home.

Can I convert my old tub into a walk-in shower?

Absolutely. Tub-to-shower conversions are one of the most popular requests. Removing an unused or outdated bathtub to install a modern walk-in shower greatly improves accessibility and gives the room a more spacious, luxurious feel.

What is included in a full bathroom remodel?

A full-service remodel typically includes demolition of the old fixtures, plumbing updates, electrical work, new drywall or backer board, custom tile installation, new flooring, vanity and sink installation, and the fitting of your new walk-in shower or bathtub. It covers everything from design to the final coat of paint.

Do you feature custom tile installation?

Yes, custom tile installation is a cornerstone of a premium bathroom renovation. The professionals we promote are highly skilled in laying intricate floor patterns, creating custom shower niches, and installing beautiful wall surrounds that handle moisture perfectly.

Is bathtub replacement a complicated process?

When handled by a professional full-service bathroom remodeling company, bathtub replacement is highly streamlined. The experts manage the careful removal of the old unit, address any underlying plumbing or moisture issues, and seamlessly install the new tub with proper sealing and finishing.
