Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries regarding our promoted photography services.

How far in advance should we book our wedding photographer?

It is highly recommended to secure your wedding photographer 9 to 12 months prior to your event. Popular dates during peak wedding seasons book up quickly, so reaching out early ensures you reserve your preferred timeline.

Are engagement photos included in the collections?

Many of the comprehensive packages offered by Studio Finch include an engagement session. Engagement photos are a fantastic way to build a rapport with the camera and create beautiful imagery for save-the-dates or wedding websites.

Do you offer destination wedding photography?

Yes, destination wedding photography is a core specialty. Studio Finch is equipped to travel worldwide to document your celebration. Custom travel quotes are provided based on the location and multi-day coverage requirements of your event.

How is time allocated for bridal portraits on the day?

A carefully structured timeline is developed prior to the event to ensure ample time for bridal portraits. Typically, 45 to 60 minutes are dedicated specifically to capturing stunning portraits of the couple, utilizing the best natural light available.

How many final images will we receive?

The total number of delivered images depends on the hours of coverage and the flow of the day. Generally, couples can expect a curated gallery of high-resolution, fully edited images that beautifully tell the complete story of their wedding photography experience.
