Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about replacement windows and the installation process.

What are the main benefits of replacement windows?

Replacement windows provide numerous benefits, including significant energy efficiency improvements, enhanced home security, better noise reduction, and an immediate boost to your property's curb appeal and overall market value.

Why should I choose vinyl windows over wood?

Vinyl windows are incredibly popular because they are virtually maintenance-free. Unlike wood, they won't rot, warp, or require repainting. They are also highly cost-effective and provide excellent thermal insulation against harsh weather.

How long does window installation typically take?

For a standard home, professional window installation usually takes between 1 to 3 days. The exact timeframe depends on the number of windows being replaced, the type of windows, and any specific structural requirements of your home.

What makes double hung windows so popular?

Double hung windows feature two operable sashes that move up and down independently. This design allows for excellent, customizable ventilation and makes it extremely easy to clean the exterior glass safely from inside your home.

Are energy efficient windows worth the initial investment?

Absolutely. Energy efficient windows reduce the strain on your HVAC system by keeping your home cooler in the summer and warmer in the winter. Over time, the savings on your monthly heating and cooling bills help offset the initial installation cost.
