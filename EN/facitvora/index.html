Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I schedule lawn mowing?

For optimal health and appearance, we recommend scheduling lawn mowing weekly during the peak growing seasons (spring and early summer). During slower growth periods, bi-weekly service is typically sufficient to keep your lawn looking pristine.

Do you provide complete garden design services?

Yes. Our promotional partners offer comprehensive garden design and landscape installation. The process begins with a consultation to understand your vision, followed by selecting the right plants, materials, and hardscaping to suit your property's specific soil and sunlight conditions.

What does your tree service include?

The tree service options range from routine pruning and shaping to full tree removal and stump grinding. Proper tree maintenance is crucial for the safety of your property and the long-term health of the trees themselves.

Do you serve both residential and commercial properties?

Absolutely. The landscaping professionals we showcase are fully equipped to handle projects of all sizes, from private home gardens to large commercial business parks requiring extensive lawn care and groundskeeping.

Is routine lawn care necessary during the winter?

While grass growth slows down significantly, winter is an important time for other property maintenance tasks. Services such as winterizing irrigation systems, dormant pruning, clearing debris, and snow or ice management are vital to protect your landscape until spring.
