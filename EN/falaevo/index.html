Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a custom closet installation take?

The timeline varies depending on the complexity and scale of the project. A standard reach-in closet can often be installed in a single day, whereas a comprehensive walk-in closet or complex wardrobe system may take a few days. The design and manufacturing process prior to installation typically takes several weeks.

Can you design a closet organizer for a very small space?

Absolutely. Small spaces actually benefit the most from custom closets. A thoughtfully designed closet organizer can effectively double your storage capacity in a tight reach-in closet by utilizing vertical space and specialized compartments.

What materials are used for your custom closets?

The systems featured use a variety of premium materials, ranging from durable melamine to high-end wood veneers and solid woods. Hardware includes robust, smooth-gliding tracks and elegant handles to ensure your wardrobe looks sophisticated and functions flawlessly for years.

Do I need to empty my closet before the consultation?

For the initial consultation, it is actually helpful to leave your items in the closet. This allows the designer to evaluate your current storage challenges and see exactly what needs to be accommodated—from long dresses to extensive shoe collections—in your new custom wardrobe.

Are wardrobe designs customizable to match my home's interior?

Yes, complete customization is the core offering. You can choose from a wide array of finishes, colors, door styles, and hardware to ensure your new walk-in closet or built-in wardrobe seamlessly integrates with your home's existing architectural style.
