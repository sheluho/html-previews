Frequently Asked Questions

How does your tire storage service work?

We coordinate the pickup, secure transportation, and warehousing of your off-season tires in a climate-controlled facility. When the new season approaches, we arrange for their return to your preferred service center.

Do you handle the actual tire installation?

We act as your logistical partner. We ensure your tires are delivered directly to authorized local shops who perform the professional tire installation, saving you the hassle of transporting them yourself.

How often should I schedule a tire rotation?

Industry standards generally recommend a tire rotation every 5,000 to 8,000 miles. The seasonal swap is the perfect, convenient time to have this maintenance performed.

What happens if I need a tire replacement?

If inspections during the storage period reveal low tread depth or damage, you will be notified so you can plan for a safe tire replacement before the next driving season begins.

Does seasonal swapping affect my wheel alignment?

Changing tires itself doesn't alter alignment, but the seasonal changeover is an ideal time to have your wheel alignment checked to ensure even tread wear and optimal handling for the months ahead.
