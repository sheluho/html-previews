Frequently Asked Questions

Are packing services included in the standard moving quote?

Typically, standard quotes cover loading, transportation, and unloading. Full-service packing services are usually offered as an add-on option. We highly recommend inquiring directly with the moving company to receive a customized estimate that includes packing.

How far in advance should I book local movers?

For local relocations, it is generally advised to secure your local movers at least four to six weeks in advance. During peak moving seasons, such as summer and end-of-month dates, booking even earlier helps guarantee availability.

What distinguishes long distance movers from local ones?

Long distance movers specialize in interstate logistics, requiring specific DOT licensing, heavier transport vehicles, and detailed route planning. Local movers generally handle intrastate moves within a shorter radius and charge by the hour rather than by weight and distance.

Will my belongings be insured during the move?

Reputable residential moving company providers offer basic liability coverage as required by law. However, for maximum peace of mind, full-value protection plans are strongly recommended and can be discussed during your consultation.

Do I need to empty my dressers before moving day?

This depends on the weight and sturdiness of the furniture. Lightweight clothing can often remain in solid dressers, but fragile items, books, or heavy items must be removed. Your movers will provide specific guidelines before moving day.
