Frequently Asked Questions

Details on our wedding videography process.

Why should we invest in a professional wedding videographer?

While photography captures still moments, a cinematic wedding video captures movement, vows, laughter, and the atmosphere in a way that truly brings the memories back to life. It is an immersive heirloom.

What is typically included in your cinematic wedding video packages?

Our standard collections include comprehensive day-of coverage, multiple camera angles, professional audio capture of vows and toasts, a meticulously edited highlight wedding film, and a documentary edit of the main formalities.

Do you offer event videography for rehearsal dinners?

Yes. We highly recommend adding event videography coverage for welcome parties and rehearsal dinners. These events often contain some of the most heartfelt speeches and intimate moments of the weekend.

How would you describe your filming style?

Our approach is unobtrusive and editorial. We act as quiet observers, allowing the day to unfold naturally while capturing it with an artistic, cinematic lens. We avoid heavily staged moments in favor of authentic emotion.

Do you travel for destination weddings?

Absolutely. We frequently travel worldwide to provide wedding videography for exclusive destination celebrations. Custom travel collections are available upon inquiry.
