Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our house cleaning and maid service options.

What does a standard house cleaning include?

A standard service typically covers dusting all surfaces, vacuuming and mopping floors, sanitizing bathroom fixtures, cleaning kitchen counters, wiping down appliance exteriors, and emptying trash bins. It is designed to maintain a high level of cleanliness on a regular basis.

What is the difference between regular and deep cleaning?

While regular cleaning focuses on visible surfaces and standard maintenance, a deep cleaning is much more intensive. It involves scrubbing baseboards, cleaning inside window sills, reaching behind large appliances, and addressing built-up grime in hard-to-reach areas.

How does move-out cleaning work?

Move-out cleaning is designed for empty properties. It includes a comprehensive deep clean of the entire space, ensuring inside cabinets, drawers, ovens, and refrigerators are wiped down and sanitized, leaving the home completely ready for the next occupants.

Do I need to be home during the maid service?

No, you do not need to be home. Many clients provide entry instructions (such as a lockbox or spare key) so the professionals can perform the house cleaning while you are at work or running errands.

Can I set up a recurring cleaning schedule?

Absolutely. Setting up a recurring cleaning schedule is one of the most popular options. You can choose weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly visits to ensure your home remains consistently pristine year-round.
