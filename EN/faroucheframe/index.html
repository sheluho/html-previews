Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the premium hand car wash? +

Our premium hand car wash involves a thorough exterior foam bath, detailed rim and tire cleaning, and gentle microfiber drying. We rely entirely on manual techniques to ensure your vehicle's paint is treated delicately, preventing the micro-scratches commonly associated with automated brush systems.

How does the auto wash membership work? +

The auto wash membership provides you with priority scheduling and consistent access to our premium car wash services. Depending on the tier selected, members can enjoy routine exterior washes, interior touch-ups, and exclusive pricing on deep-detailing services, ensuring your vehicle remains in pristine condition all month long.

Do you offer a quick express wash? +

Yes. We understand that time is often limited. Our express wash provides a rapid exterior refresh using the same high-quality, pH-balanced soaps and safe drying techniques, allowing you to maintain your vehicle’s shine even on a tight schedule.

How long does interior detailing usually take? +

The duration of interior detailing depends on the current condition of your vehicle and the specific package chosen. Generally, a comprehensive interior detail takes between two to four hours, as our team meticulously vacuums, conditions upholstery, and cleans all glass and dashboard surfaces.

Is a hand car wash truly better for my paint? +

Absolutely. Automated tunnels use abrasive brushes that can trap dirt from previous vehicles, dragging it across your clear coat. A meticulous hand car wash utilizes clean, ultra-soft materials and careful human oversight, providing a superior clean while safeguarding your vehicle's delicate finish.
