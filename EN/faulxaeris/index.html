Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical lead time for custom furniture?

Lead times vary significantly based on the complexity of the design, the current queue of the workshop, and the availability of specific hardwoods. Generally, bespoke furniture commissions take anywhere from 8 to 16 weeks from the finalization of design drawings to delivery.

Can I choose the specific wood for my custom dining table?

Absolutely. Material selection is a core part of the bespoke furniture process. You will be able to discuss options like domestic Walnut, White Oak, Cherry, or Ash. In some cases, you may even be able to review photos of the specific timber slabs before milling begins.

Do you offer upholstery services for a custom sofa?

Yes, the makers we promote often collaborate with master upholsterers. When commissioning a custom sofa or upholstered dining chairs, you can select from a wide range of premium performance fabrics, top-grain leathers, or provide your own material (COM) to perfectly match your interior design scheme.

How do I start the design process for handmade furniture?

The process begins with an inquiry. Submit your ideas, dimensions, and inspiration photos through our contact form. We will forward your details to the custom furniture maker, who will then reach out to schedule an initial consultation to discuss feasibility, budget, and design direction.

Can I visit the showroom to see examples of the work?

Showroom visits are strictly by appointment to ensure each client receives undivided attention. During a visit, you can inspect the quality of the joinery, feel the wood finishes, and discuss your custom furniture project in detail with the artisans.
