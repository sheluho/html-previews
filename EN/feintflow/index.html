Frequently Asked Questions

What makes bespoke furniture superior to store-bought options?

Bespoke furniture is crafted specifically for your space, aesthetic, and functional needs. Unlike mass-produced items, handmade furniture utilizes superior materials, traditional joinery techniques, and offers unparalleled personalization, ensuring the piece lasts for generations.

How long does it take to create a custom dining table?

The timeline varies based on the complexity of the design and material availability. Typically, a custom dining table takes between 8 to 12 weeks from the initial showroom consultation to final delivery, ensuring every detail is perfect.

Can I choose specific fabrics for my custom sofa?

Absolutely. When visiting a custom furniture maker and showroom, you will have access to hundreds of premium fabric and leather swatches. You can select the perfect texture, color, and durability level for your custom sofa.

How do I start the design process?

The process begins with an initial consultation, either virtually or in the showroom. You will discuss your vision, dimensions, and material preferences. From there, designers create sketches or 3D renderings for your approval before the build begins.

Is delivery provided for large custom pieces?

Yes, professional white-glove delivery is typically arranged for all substantial handmade furniture pieces. Delivery teams ensure your new items are safely transported, carefully brought into your home, and fully assembled in your desired room.
