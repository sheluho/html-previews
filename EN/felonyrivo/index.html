Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about planning your menu with our catering partners.

Do you offer custom wedding catering menus?

Absolutely. Our catering partners specialize in fully customized wedding catering. They work closely with couples to design a menu that reflects their personal tastes, dietary requirements, and the overall theme of the celebration.

What is the difference between a plated dinner and buffet catering?

A plated dinner involves guests being served individually portioned meals at their tables, creating a formal and elegant atmosphere. Buffet catering allows guests to serve themselves from a diverse selection of dishes, which is often more interactive and allows for a wider variety of menu options.

Can you accommodate corporate catering for large groups?

Yes, corporate catering is a core service. The providers we promote are fully equipped to handle large-scale business events, conferences, and office parties, ensuring prompt delivery, seamless setup, and professional service.

How far in advance should we book event catering?

For major events like weddings and large corporate functions, it is highly recommended to secure your event catering at least 6 to 9 months in advance. For smaller, private gatherings, a few weeks' notice is typically sufficient, though availability may vary.

Are tastings available before making a decision?

Yes, tasting sessions are highly encouraged, especially for wedding catering. This allows you to sample the dishes, review the presentation, and make any necessary adjustments to your menu before finalizing your contract with the catering provider.
