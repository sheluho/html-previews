We stitch together the highlights of your ceremony and reception into a cohesive, emotionally resonant story that you and your family will cherish for generations.

When you book with Fenaglerzo, you receive the full attention of a dedicated, highly trained wedding videographer. We pride ourselves on blending into the background while still capturing every crucial angle and intimate exchange.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in your standard wedding videography packages? ▼

Our standard packages typically include full-day coverage by a professional wedding videographer, multi-camera setups for the ceremony and speeches, professional audio capture, and a professionally edited cinematic wedding video highlighting the best moments of your day.

Do we get a dedicated wedding videographer for the entire day? ▼

Yes, depending on the package you choose, you will have at least one dedicated wedding videographer present from the early preparation stages until the reception celebrations, ensuring a complete narrative of your wedding film.

How long does it take to receive our cinematic wedding video? ▼

Because we pride ourselves on meticulous digital editing and color grading, the turnaround time for a fully polished cinematic wedding video is typically 6 to 8 weeks. We ensure every frame meets our high standards before delivery.

Do you provide event videography for corporate or private parties? ▼

Absolutely. Our event videography services cover a broad spectrum of gatherings, including corporate events, milestone birthdays, anniversaries, and engagement parties. We apply the same cinematic approach to all our event coverage.

What style of wedding film do you produce? ▼

We specialize in a cinematic, storytelling-driven style. This means your wedding film won't just be a static recording; it will be a beautifully crafted narrative blending natural audio, carefully selected music, and highly artistic visuals.
