Frequently Asked Questions

Details regarding the bespoke ordering process.

What is the process for ordering custom furniture?

The process begins with a consultation at the showroom or via a design request form. You will discuss your vision, dimensions, and material preferences. Following this, a design proposal is created. Once approved, the master craftsmen begin the build process.

How long does it take to build a custom dining table?

Lead times vary depending on the complexity of the design and current workshop capacity. Typically, a custom dining table requires between 8 to 12 weeks from the finalization of the design to completion.

Can I choose the fabric for a custom sofa?

Absolutely. The showroom offers a wide selection of premium, durable fabrics and leathers. You can also provide your own material (COM - Customer's Own Material) if you have a specific textile in mind for your bespoke seating.

Do you arrange delivery for bespoke furniture?

Yes, professional white-glove delivery services are coordinated for all handmade furniture pieces. This ensures that your items are transported safely and installed correctly in your home by experienced handlers.

What types of wood are used for the handmade furniture?

A variety of premium hardwoods are utilized, including White Oak, Walnut, Maple, and Cherry. The choice of wood depends on your aesthetic preference and the functional requirements of the piece being crafted.
