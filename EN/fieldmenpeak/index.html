Common Questions

Insights into our custom closet and organization solutions.

What is the process for designing custom closets?

The process typically begins with an in-home consultation to measure your space and discuss your storage needs. Following that, 3D design plans are created to help you visualize the final walk-in closet or wardrobe before manufacturing and professional installation take place.

Can a closet organizer really improve a small reach-in closet?

Absolutely. A standard single rod leaves a lot of wasted vertical space. A custom closet organizer utilizes double hanging areas, adjustable shelving, and specialized drawers to often double the functional storage capacity of a standard reach-in closet.

What materials are used for your custom wardrobes?

The featured providers utilize a range of high-quality materials, from durable thermally fused laminates (TFL) to premium wood veneers. Hardware includes heavy-duty, full-extension glides and soft-close hinges to ensure your wardrobe operates smoothly for years.

How long does installation take for a full walk-in closet?

While manufacturing times vary, the actual installation of a custom walk-in closet is usually completed within one to two days by experienced professionals, minimizing disruption to your home environment.

Do you feature solutions for spaces other than bedrooms?

Yes. The principles of custom closets apply beautifully to other areas of the home. You can explore tailored storage designs for kitchen pantries, garage storage systems, laundry rooms, and custom home offices.
