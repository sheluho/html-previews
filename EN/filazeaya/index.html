Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a standard lawn care program?

A standard program typically includes scheduled fertilization, targeted weed control, disease management, and surface insect treatments. Regular lawn mowing is also highly recommended to keep your grass healthy, dense, and green throughout the growing season.

Do you promote custom garden design services?

Yes, we promote professional services that tailor garden design to your specific property, soil type, and aesthetic preferences. This includes selecting the right native plants, designing functional layouts, and enhancing overall curb appeal.

What does landscape installation entail?

Landscape installation covers everything from planting new shrubs, flowers, and trees, to complete yard renovations including hardscaping elements. It is the physical execution of your garden design plan.

Is tree service part of the landscaping packages?

Absolutely. Proper tree service, including structural pruning, health checks, and safe removal, is a vital component of comprehensive landscaping. Our partners ensure your trees are safe and structurally sound.

How often should I schedule lawn mowing?

For optimal health and appearance, weekly or bi-weekly lawn mowing is highly recommended, depending heavily on the season, rainfall, and grass type. Consistent cutting encourages lateral growth and a denser turf.
