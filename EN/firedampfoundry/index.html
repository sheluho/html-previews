Frequently Asked Questions

What are the main benefits of energy efficient windows?

Energy efficient windows are designed to prevent heated or cooled air from escaping your home. They typically feature multiple glass panes, specialized gas fills, and Low-E coatings. This means lower utility bills, reduced strain on your HVAC system, and a more comfortable, draft-free indoor environment year-round.

Why should I choose vinyl windows over wood or aluminum?

Vinyl windows are incredibly popular because they offer a superior balance of durability, insulation, and low maintenance. Unlike wood, they won't rot, warp, or require repainting. Unlike aluminum, they do not conduct heat and cold easily, making them much more energy-efficient for residential applications.

How long does a standard window installation take?

The timeline depends on the number of replacement windows being installed and the structural condition of the existing frames. However, a typical whole-home window installation can often be completed in just one to two days by an experienced crew, minimizing disruption to your daily life.

Are double hung windows difficult to clean?

Not at all. In fact, double hung windows are celebrated for their ease of maintenance. Modern designs feature sashes that tilt inward, allowing you to clean both the interior and exterior glass safely and conveniently from inside your home without needing ladders.

Do replacement windows increase property value?

Yes. Installing high-quality replacement windows is widely considered one of the best home improvement investments. They boost curb appeal, improve security, enhance energy efficiency, and are highly attractive features to prospective buyers if you ever decide to sell your property.
