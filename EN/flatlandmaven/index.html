Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical lead time for a custom dining table?

Typically, our custom dining tables take between 8 to 12 weeks from finalized design approval to delivery. This timeline ensures we select the finest dry timber, complete precision joinery, and apply multi-coat durable finishes.

Can I choose the wood species for my bespoke furniture?

Yes, absolutely. We work with premium domestic hardwoods including Black Walnut, White Oak, Cherry, and Hard Maple. During the consultation, we will guide you through the structural and visual characteristics of each wood type to find the perfect match.

Do you offer fabric options for a custom sofa?

Yes, we provide an extensive curation of contract-grade performance fabrics, Belgian linens, wools, and full-grain Italian leathers. We choose materials that resist stains and wear, matching the robust construction of our handmade frames.

How do showroom consultations work?

Our showroom visits are arranged by appointment. This allows our design consultant to provide undivided attention, show you timber samples, discuss dimensions, review architectural sketches, and help you visualize your custom pieces in person.

What makes handmade furniture superior to commercial options?

Commercial furniture utilizes veneers, particleboard, and mechanical fasteners that wear down over time. Our handmade furniture utilizes solid timber, traditional mortise-and-tenon joinery, and specialized finishes that bond with the wood fibers, allowing the piece to age beautifully and survive generations of daily use.
