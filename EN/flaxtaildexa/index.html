Common Questions

Project Clarifications

What is included in your turnkey kitchen renovation service?

Our turnkey kitchen service covers everything: initial design consulting, material selection (cabinets, countertops, hardware), demolition, plumbing, electrical work, structural adjustments, and final installations. You won't need to hire external contractors.

How long does a standard kitchen remodeling project take?

Most complete renovations take between 4 to 8 weeks depending on the complexity, structural changes, and cabinet customization. We provide a detailed project timeline before physical work begins.

Can we customize the cabinet configurations entirely?

Yes. Our custom kitchen cabinets are fabricated to order. We customize everything from dimensions and material choices to internal shelving systems, integrated lighting, and soft-close mechanisms.

What materials do you offer for countertops?

We source premium materials including quartz, natural marble, granite, quartzite, and solid surfaces. Our specialists help you select the material that best matches your lifestyle and aesthetic goals.

Do you work in the Margate, FL area?

Yes, we are proudly based in and service the Margate, Florida community and surrounding coastal areas, bringing professional-grade local craftsmanship to regional homeowners.
