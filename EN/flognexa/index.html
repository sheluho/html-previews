Frequently Asked Questions

What services are included in your standard lawn care plans?

Our standard lawn care packages can be customized to include routine lawn mowing, professional trimming, edging, custom fertilization programs, weed abatement, aeration, and pest treatments. We adapt each schedule according to your grass type and soil conditions.

Can you help with full garden design and landscape installation?

Yes, our partners specialize in comprehensive outdoor transformations. From conceptual garden design planning to complete landscape installation including shrubs, sod, flower beds, hardscapes, and protective mulch layers, we manage the entire project.

How often should my lawn be mowed?

Generally, a weekly lawn mowing schedule is highly recommended during peak growing seasons to preserve root health and prevent weed propagation. We adjust our cutting heights dynamically depending on current temperature and rainfall levels.

Do you provide professional tree service?

Absolutely. Our network includes trained arborists who provide comprehensive tree service, including safety-first pruning, hazardous branch removal, tree health diagnostics, and stump clearing to ensure your property remains safe and beautiful.

How do I get a quote for my landscaping project?

Simply fill out the consultation form below or call us directly at (913) 782-8479. Our team will schedule an initial site walkthrough with our partner specialists to inspect your property and provide a detailed, obligation-free quote.
