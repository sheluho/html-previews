Common Inquiries

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of event catering services do you offer?

We provide comprehensive services including exquisite wedding catering, corporate catering, buffet catering, and customized plated dinner setups. We manage everything from menu creation to execution.

Can you accommodate specific dietary restrictions?

Absolutely. We design bespoke menus that cater to various dietary requirements, including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and allergen-sensitive options, ensuring every guest enjoys an exceptional meal.

How far in advance should we book wedding catering?

We highly recommend booking our wedding catering services at least 6 to 12 months in advance to secure your preferred date and ensure ample time for personalized menu design.

Do you offer tastings before the event?

Yes, private tastings are available for booked events so you can refine your plated dinner or buffet catering selections with our chef before your big day.

Is staffing included in your services?

Yes, we provide fully trained, professional service staff, bartenders, and culinary teams to manage set up, food service, and clean up seamlessly.
