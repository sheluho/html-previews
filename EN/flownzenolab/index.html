Frequently Asked Questions

What defines your approach as a custom furniture maker?

Our approach is rooted in uncompromising artisan techniques combined with modern design sensibilities. As a premier custom furniture maker, we ensure every piece of bespoke furniture is meticulously engineered and handcrafted to match the exact specifications, aesthetic goals, and functional needs of your space.

Can I choose the wood species for my custom dining table?

Absolutely. We source a wide variety of premium and exotic hardwoods, including walnut, oak, maple, and cherry. Whether you prefer a sleek modern finish or a dramatic live edge custom dining table, the choice of material is entirely up to you.

What is the process for ordering a custom sofa?

Ordering a custom sofa begins with a design consultation to understand your space, style, and comfort preferences. You will select from various frame styles, cushion densities, and a vast library of luxury upholstery options before the piece is constructed by skilled artisans.

How long does it take to produce handmade furniture?

Because every piece of handmade furniture is built to order, lead times typically range from 8 to 14 weeks depending on the complexity of the design, the availability of specialized materials, and current workshop schedules.

Do you work with interior designers and architects?

Yes, our custom furniture showroom frequently collaborates with trade professionals. We provide detailed shop drawings, material samples, and dedicated project management to ensure the seamless execution of bespoke furniture packages for residential and commercial projects.
