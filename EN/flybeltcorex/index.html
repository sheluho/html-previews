Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our tire storage, installation, and rotation services.

Why is professional tire storage important?

Tires are highly susceptible to dry rot and cracking when kept in extreme temperatures or damp basements. Our climate-controlled storage keeps rubber at the optimal temperature and humidity level, maximizing tire life.

How often should I schedule a tire rotation?

We recommend a tire rotation every 5,000 to 7,500 miles, or during your seasonal tire installation. Regular rotation ensures even tread wear and improves overall fuel economy.

What are the signs that I need a wheel alignment?

If your vehicle pulls to one side, your steering wheel is crooked when driving straight, or you notice rapid and uneven tire wear, it's time to book a professional wheel alignment.

Can I buy new tires and have them installed directly?

Yes, we provide full tire replacement options. We can help you choose the best tires for your vehicle, manage the tire installation, and coordinate the storage of your old ones if they are still usable.

How do I retrieve my tires from storage when the season changes?

Simply contact us 48 hours before you want them installed. We will prepare your tires, ensure they are inspected, and have them ready for your scheduled seasonal tire swap appointment.
