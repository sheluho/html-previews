Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of wood do you use for your custom furniture?

We build exclusively with sustainably sourced local hardwoods including Black Walnut, Oregon White Oak, Pacific Madrone, and Hard Maple. Every piece is selected for its strength, grain pattern, and structural stability.

Can I customize the dimensions of a custom sofa frame?

Absolutely. Our custom sofas can be tailored to the exact length, depth, and height required to integrate beautifully with your architectural layout and design plans.

How does the bespoke design process work?

We begin with a digital or in-person showroom consultation to discuss your vision, dimensions, and wood preferences. After finalizing technical sketches and slab selection, our partner artisans handcraft your furniture, sending updates along the way.

Where is your showroom located?

Our partner showroom and active design workshop are located in beautiful Anacortes, Washington. Showroom visits and timber selection appointments must be scheduled in advance to ensure a dedicated designer is available.

What is the typical lead time for handmade furniture?

Lead times typically range from 8 to 12 weeks depending on the design complexity, wood sourcing requirements, and current workshop queue. We prioritize quality over speed to ensure perfect execution.
