Common Questions

What to know before you move

How far in advance should I book my moving company?

We recommend booking your residential move at least 4 to 6 weeks in advance. During peak moving seasons (such as summer months and the end of the month), availability fills up quickly. Booking early ensures you secure your preferred dates and gives you ample time to coordinate packing services.

Do you offer packing services for highly fragile items?

Yes. The local movers and long distance movers we partner with use specialized materials, such as custom crating and heavy-duty bubble wrap, to protect fragile items like artwork, antiques, and electronics. Inform us of any highly valuable items during your quote estimation.

What is the difference between local movers and long distance movers?

Local moves are generally those happening within the same state or under a specific mileage radius (usually under 50-100 miles) and are billed by the hour. Long distance moves involve crossing state lines or covering vast distances, and are regulated federally, typically billed by the weight of your items and the distance traveled.

Are my belongings insured during the move?

Basic valuation coverage is included standard with all moving services. However, for complete peace of mind, we highly recommend discussing full-value protection options with the moving company handling your relocation to ensure comprehensive coverage of all your household goods.

How long does a typical residential move take?

It depends entirely on the size of your home and the distance of the move. A local move for a 2-bedroom apartment might take 4-6 hours, while packing and moving a 4-bedroom house could span two days. Long distance moves require additional transit time based on the route.
