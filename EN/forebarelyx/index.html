Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a windshield replacement take?

A standard windshield replacement typically takes about 1 to 1.5 hours to complete. However, the vehicle must sit for an additional "Safe Drive-Away Time" (usually 1 hour) to allow the high-grade urethane adhesive to cure properly before it is safe to drive.

Can you repair a chip instead of replacing the entire windshield?

Yes, in many cases! If the rock chip is smaller than a quarter and not directly in the driver's line of sight, a quick windshield repair or chip repair is highly recommended. This restores structural strength and prevents the damage from spreading further.

Do you offer mobile auto glass services at my location?

Absolutely. Our network provides 100% mobile service, meaning certified technicians will bring all the necessary tools and replacement glass directly to your home, office, or any convenient location of your choice.

Will my insurance cover windshield repair or replacement?

Most comprehensive insurance policies cover auto glass services. Many insurance companies will completely waive your deductible for a windshield repair, making the service free to you. We recommend checking with your provider during the booking process.

What kind of warranty do you provide on car window replacement?

Every professional car window replacement and structural installation booked through our platform comes with a comprehensive warranty covering air leaks, water leaks, and manufacturer defects for as long as you own or lease the vehicle.
