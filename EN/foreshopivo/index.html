Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I schedule professional carpet cleaning? +

For typical households, scheduling carpet cleaning every 12 to 18 months is recommended to maintain fiber integrity and hygiene. Homes with pets, children, or high foot traffic may benefit from professional steam cleaning every 6 to 9 months to manage dirt and allergens effectively.

Is steam cleaning safe for all rug types? +

While steam cleaning is highly effective for synthetic carpets, delicate natural fibers like wool or silk require specialized rug cleaning methods. The professionals we promote assess each rug individually to determine the safest and most effective cleaning temperature and moisture level.

Can you guarantee complete stain removal? +

Advanced stain removal techniques can eliminate the vast majority of spots. However, complete removal depends on the nature of the spill, the fabric type, and how long the stain has set. While perfection cannot be guaranteed in every case, professional treatments offer the best chance of restoration without damaging the material.

How long does upholstery cleaning take to dry? +

Drying times for upholstery cleaning typically range from 4 to 8 hours, depending on the fabric type, the cleaning method used, and the ventilation in the room. Technicians use specialized extraction tools to leave fabrics as dry as possible, ensuring you can return to using your furniture quickly.

Do I need to move my furniture before you arrive? +

It is recommended to clear small items, breakables, and light furniture from the areas to be cleaned. For heavy items like large beds or entertainment centers, technicians generally clean around them. Specific instructions will be provided when you schedule your appointment.
