Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about preparing for your time in front of the lens.

What is your approach to wedding photography?

Our approach is deeply rooted in photojournalism. We aim to capture authentic, unscripted moments rather than heavily posed scenarios. A dedicated wedding photographer will blend into the background to capture the genuine emotions and spontaneous interactions of your special day.

Do you offer engagement photos before the big day?

Absolutely. We highly recommend booking an engagement photos session. It serves as a wonderful icebreaker, allowing us to understand your dynamic as a couple while providing you with beautiful images to use for your wedding website and stationery.

Can we book you for destination wedding photography?

Yes! We love traveling to capture unique love stories. Our destination wedding photography packages are custom-tailored to cover travel, accommodations, and multi-day events if needed, ensuring your exotic backdrop is perfectly immortalized.

How do you handle bridal portraits during the event?

We typically schedule a brief window right after you finish getting ready or during the golden hour for dedicated bridal portraits. This ensures we have the best lighting and a calm environment to capture elegant, solitary moments before the festivities truly begin.

How long does it take to receive our final gallery?

We take immense pride in our post-production process. You can expect a sneak peek within 48 hours of your event. Your complete, high-resolution wedding photography gallery will typically be delivered within 6 to 8 weeks, beautifully edited and ready for print.
