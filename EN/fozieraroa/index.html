Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical front door replacement take?

In most cases, a standard front door replacement can be completed in a single day. Our professional door installation team ensures the old unit is removed carefully, the new frame is perfectly plumbed and sealed, and the site is completely cleaned up before we leave.

Why should I choose fiberglass doors for my entryway?

Fiberglass doors are highly recommended for entry doors because they offer the aesthetic appeal of real wood without the maintenance. They are highly resistant to dents, scratches, warping, and rotting, making them incredibly durable and energy-efficient for varying climates.

Can you install new interior doors into my existing frames?

Yes, if your existing door frames are square and in good condition, we can perform a "slab" installation. Our team will precisely measure, trim, and hinge new interior doors to fit perfectly into your current jambs, which is often a quicker and less intrusive process.

What does your door installation service include?

Our comprehensive door installation service includes a pre-installation measurement, removal and disposal of your old door, installation of the new door unit (including frame, weatherstripping, and threshold for entry doors), installation of hardware, and a final inspection to ensure smooth operation and a weather-tight seal.

Do you provide hardware for entry and interior doors?

Yes, we offer a wide selection of premium handles, locks, and hinges to complement both your new entry doors and interior doors. During your consultation, we can help you select hardware that matches your home's style while providing the security and functionality you need.
