Frequently Asked Questions

What types of events do you cater?

We provide comprehensive wedding and event catering service for a wide range of occasions. This includes wedding receptions, rehearsal dinners, corporate events, milestone birthdays, and grand galas.

Do you offer both buffet and plated dinner options?

Absolutely. We are highly flexible and can design your menu around a lavish buffet catering setup or an elegant, multi-course plated dinner, depending entirely on the atmosphere you wish to create.

Can you accommodate specific dietary restrictions?

Yes, our culinary team is experienced in crafting exquisite dishes that meet various dietary needs, including gluten-free, vegan, vegetarian, and allergy-sensitive requirements, ensuring all your guests dine safely and joyfully.

How far in advance should we book for a wedding?

For wedding catering, we recommend securing your date at least 6 to 12 months in advance. Prime dates during peak wedding seasons book up quickly, and early booking ensures we have ample time to perfect your custom menu.

Do you provide staff and setup services?

Yes, we provide full-service event catering. Our professional team handles the delivery, setup, serving, and breakdown, allowing you to relax and fully immerse yourself in the celebration alongside your guests.
