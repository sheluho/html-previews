Frequently Asked Questions

What does a turnkey kitchen remodeling project entail?

A turnkey kitchen renovation means the provider handles everything from initial design and demolition to the final installation of custom cabinets, countertops, lighting, and plumbing. You work with one cohesive team rather than juggling multiple independent contractors.

Can I just get new countertops or cabinets without a full remodel?

Yes. While full-service kitchen renovation is a specialty, targeted upgrades like installing a new island, replacing outdated surfaces with premium countertops, or fitting custom kitchen cabinetry are also fully supported.

How long does a custom kitchen remodel take?

Timelines vary depending on the scope of the project and the materials selected. A standard full remodel typically ranges from 4 to 8 weeks once construction begins, but exact schedules are provided by the contracting team during your consultation.

Do you provide 3D designs before construction?

Yes, detailed design planning is a crucial part of the process. You will review layout options and visual renderings of your new custom kitchen to ensure the cabinets, countertops, and appliances fit your exact vision before any physical work starts.

Who will be working in my home?

Your project will be executed by vetted, professional installers and craftsmen coordinated by R.M. Kitchens. This ensures high-quality workmanship, strict adherence to safety standards, and a respectful presence in your home throughout the renovation.
