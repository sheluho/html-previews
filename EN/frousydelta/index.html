Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a standard house cleaning?

A standard service generally includes dusting all surfaces, vacuuming carpets, sweeping and mopping hard floors, emptying trash, and thoroughly sanitizing bathrooms and kitchens. The specific 49-point checklist ensures consistent quality in every room.

Do I need to provide the cleaning supplies for the maid service?

No, professional house cleaning teams arrive fully equipped with high-quality, professional-grade supplies and vacuums. However, if you have specific products you strongly prefer them to use on certain surfaces, you can leave them out with instructions.

How do I schedule a recurring cleaning appointment?

Scheduling is simple. Once you request an estimate, a representative will discuss your home's needs and set up a convenient day and time. You can choose from weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly visits depending on your household's activity level.

Is a deep cleaning necessary for my first visit?

It is highly recommended. An initial deep cleaning sets a high standard of cleanliness, addressing accumulated dust and grime. Once the baseline is established, routine recurring cleaning visits are much more efficient and effective at maintaining that pristine state.

Are the house cleaning professionals insured and bonded?

Yes, all recommended maid service providers are fully insured and bonded. This provides you with complete peace of mind, knowing that your property is protected while the professionals work inside your home.
