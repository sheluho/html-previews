Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between custom cabinets and stock cabinets?

Stock cabinets are mass-produced in standard sizes, which can leave awkward gaps in your kitchen layout. As a manufacturer of custom cabinets, we build each piece specifically for your space, ensuring a seamless fit, superior build quality, and complete freedom in style, color, and finish.

Do I need to have a kitchen design ready before visiting?

Not at all. Our team specializes in comprehensive kitchen design. When you visit our kitchen showroom, our experts will work with you from scratch to map out the best layout, optimize storage, and select the perfect materials to bring your vision to life.

Why are shaker cabinets so popular?

Shaker cabinets are celebrated for their minimalist, clean-lined design characterized by a five-piece door with a recessed center panel. This timeless aesthetic easily adapts to both rustic farmhouse and ultra-modern kitchen designs, making them an incredibly versatile and enduring choice.

How long does it take to manufacture custom cabinetry?

Because every project is built from the ground up, the manufacturing timeline typically ranges from 6 to 10 weeks after the final kitchen design is approved. We prioritize meticulous craftsmanship and quality control over rushed production to ensure your cabinets last a lifetime.

Can I see samples of finishes and hardware at your showroom?

Absolutely. Our kitchen showroom is fully stocked with a wide variety of wood species, stain samples, paint finishes, and premium hardware. We highly encourage an in-person visit so you can feel the quality of our craftsmanship firsthand.
