Common Questions

What are the main benefits of energy efficient windows?

They significantly reduce thermal transfer between the interior and exterior of your home. This lowers your reliance on heating and cooling systems, reducing energy bills while keeping your living spaces comfortable regardless of the season.

Why should I choose vinyl windows?

Vinyl windows are incredibly durable, require virtually no maintenance compared to wood, will not peel or rot, and offer excellent insulation properties at a highly competitive price point for modern replacement windows.

How long does window installation typically take?

Most residential window installation projects are completed within one to two days. The exact timeframe depends on the number of replacement windows required and the complexity of the structural fittings.

Are double hung windows difficult to clean?

Not at all. Modern double hung windows are engineered for convenience. They feature top and bottom sashes that tilt inward, allowing you to easily clean both the inside and outside glass surfaces safely from inside your home.

Will replacement windows increase my home's value?

Yes, upgrading to new, high-quality windows is widely considered one of the best return-on-investment home improvements available. They instantly enhance curb appeal, modernize the home's look, and drastically improve energy performance credentials.
