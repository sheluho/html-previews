Frequently Asked Questions

What types of window treatments are highlighted here?
We feature a comprehensive range of premium options, including custom blinds, light-filtering and blackout roller shades, classic plantation shutters, and elegant custom drapes. Every product category is designed to suit distinct architectural styles and functional needs.
How do I choose between blinds and shades?
Blinds typically feature slats (wood or faux-wood) that can be tilted to precisely control light direction and privacy. Shades are made from a continuous roll of fabric, offering a softer look and varying degrees of light filtration depending on the material chosen.
Are the plantation shutters custom-fitted?
Absolutely. All plantation shutters introduced through our platform are meticulously measured and custom-crafted to fit your exact window dimensions, ensuring a seamless, built-in appearance that enhances your home's structural beauty.
Do the providers offer motorized options?
Yes, many of the roller shades and custom blinds available can be upgraded with modern motorization systems. This allows you to adjust your window treatments with the touch of a button or integrate them into your home automation system.
How do I get started with upgrading my windows?
You can begin by filling out the contact form below. We will connect you with experienced professionals who will schedule a consultation to discuss your vision, review fabric and material samples, and take precise measurements of your spaces.
