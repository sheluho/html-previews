Frequently Asked Questions

What types of window treatments do you offer?

We feature a comprehensive selection including custom blinds, sleek roller shades, elegant plantation shutters, and luxurious custom drapes. Each option is tailored to fit your specific window dimensions and interior design preferences.

How do I choose between blinds and roller shades?

Blinds with adjustable slats are ideal if you want precise control over the angle of incoming light. Roller shades, on the other hand, provide a cleaner, more streamlined look and are excellent for complete room darkening or UV protection, depending on the fabric opacity chosen.

Are plantation shutters energy efficient?

Yes, plantation shutters are among the most energy-efficient window treatments available. When closed, they provide a strong barrier against heat transfer, keeping your home cooler in the summer and warmer in the winter, which can help lower utility bills.

Can custom drapes be paired with other window treatments?

Absolutely. Layering custom drapes over blinds or roller shades is a popular design choice. It provides maximum flexibility for light control and privacy while adding significant aesthetic depth and texture to the room.

How do I get started with measuring and selection?

Getting started is simple. You can reach out to schedule an initial consultation. Measurements must be exact to ensure a perfect fit, which is why professional assessment is recommended for your custom blinds, shades, or shutters to guarantee flawless results.
