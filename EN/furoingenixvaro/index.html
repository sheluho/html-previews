Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a ceramic coating last?

Depending on the specific package you select and how well you maintain your vehicle, a professional ceramic coating can last anywhere from 2 to 5+ years. It provides long-term paint protection that outlasts traditional waxes and sealants by a massive margin.

What is included in a full car detailing?

A comprehensive auto detailing includes an extensive exterior wash, chemical decontamination, wheel and tire deep clean, plus an interior detailing process that involves vacuuming, steam cleaning, stain removal, and conditioning of all surfaces.

Does my new car need paint correction?

Surprisingly, yes. New vehicles often arrive from the dealership with factory sanding marks, swirl marks from improper lot washing, or transit damage. Paint correction ensures the clear coat is flawless before we lock it in with paint protection.

Can interior detailing remove heavy smoke odors?

Yes, our deep interior detailing packages utilize specialized enzyme cleaners, steam extraction, and ozone treatments to tackle and neutralize heavy odors at their source, rather than just masking them.

How should I wash my car after ceramic coating?

We recommend a two-bucket hand wash method using pH-neutral soaps designed for coated vehicles. Avoid automatic car washes with harsh brushes or aggressive chemicals, as they can degrade the hydrophobic properties of the coating over time.
