Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I schedule professional carpet cleaning? ▼

For most households, we recommend professional carpet cleaning every 12 to 18 months. However, if you have pets, children, or high traffic areas, scheduling a deep steam cleaning every 6 to 9 months can help maintain optimal indoor air quality and prolong the life of your carpets.

How long does it take for carpets and upholstery to dry? ▼

Drying times can vary based on humidity, airflow, and the specific fabric type. Typically, carpets take about 6 to 10 hours to dry completely after a steam cleaning. Upholstery cleaning usually dries within 4 to 6 hours. Proper ventilation can significantly speed up the process.

Can you remove all types of stains? ▼

While our advanced stain removal techniques are highly effective against a wide variety of tough spots—including pet stains, wine, and coffee—no company can guarantee the complete removal of every single stain. The success depends heavily on the fabric type, what caused the stain, and how long it has been sitting.

Is your rug cleaning process safe for delicate oriental rugs? ▼

Yes. We carefully inspect every rug before beginning the cleaning process. For delicate or oriental area rug cleaning, specialized, gentle methods are used to preserve the natural fibers, prevent color bleeding, and ensure the structural integrity of the piece is maintained.

Do I need to vacuum before the technicians arrive? ▼

A light vacuuming of high-traffic areas is helpful to remove surface debris, but it is not strictly required. The professional cleaning equipment used is incredibly powerful and will extract deep-seated dirt during the steam cleaning process.
