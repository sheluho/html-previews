Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in full-service wedding planning?

Full-service planning covers every aspect of your event. From creating the initial design concept and managing the budget to booking top-tier vendors and overseeing the entire wedding weekend, a dedicated wedding planner handles it all so you can remain stress-free.

Do I really need a day of coordinator?

Absolutely. A day of coordinator is essential to execute the plans you've worked so hard to create. They act as the point of contact for all vendors, manage the timeline, and handle any unexpected issues, allowing you, your family, and your friends to fully enjoy the celebration as guests.

Can you help with a destination wedding?

Yes! Planning a destination wedding requires specialized logistical knowledge. We coordinate travel blocks, manage remote vendors, design multi-day itineraries for your guests, and ensure local legal requirements for marriage are met flawlessly.

When should we hire a wedding coordinator?

For full-service planning, it's best to hire us right after your engagement (12-18 months in advance). If you are looking for a day of coordinator, we recommend booking at least 6-8 months prior to secure your date on our calendar.

How do you handle vendor communication?

We act as the primary liaison between you and your vendor team. We schedule meetings, review contracts, communicate design expectations, and provide final timelines to all vendors to ensure everyone is aligned for a seamless event.
