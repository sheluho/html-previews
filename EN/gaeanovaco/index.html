Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to create custom furniture?

The timeline typically ranges from 8 to 12 weeks, depending entirely on the complexity of the bespoke furniture piece, the availability of selected materials, and the current schedule of the artisan workshop.

Can I choose the fabric for my custom sofa?

Absolutely. There is an extensive library of premium fabrics, durable synthetics, and fine leathers available. You can review swatches during your consultation to ensure the material perfectly suits your lifestyle.

Do you deliver handmade furniture to residential addresses?

Yes, specialized white-glove delivery services are coordinated to ensure your handmade furniture arrives safely. The pieces are carefully transported, assembled, and placed precisely where you want them in your home.

What wood options are available for a custom dining table?

You can choose from a variety of premium, sustainably sourced hardwoods. Popular selections for a custom dining table include rich walnut, classic oak, elegant cherry, and bright maple, each offering a distinct grain pattern and character.

How do I start the bespoke furniture design process?

The process begins with a simple conversation. Fill out our contact form to request an initial consultation. We will discuss your spatial requirements, aesthetic preferences, and begin conceptualizing your unique piece.
