Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a turnkey kitchen renovation?

A turnkey kitchen renovation covers the entire scope of your project from start to finish. This includes the initial design consultation, demolition, structural changes, plumbing, electrical work, flooring, and the installation of custom cabinets and countertops. You simply "turn the key" to a finished space.

How long does kitchen remodeling typically take?

The timeline depends heavily on the complexity and scope of the project. A full-service kitchen renovation generally takes between 4 to 8 weeks once materials have arrived. Planning and custom fabrication times occur prior to the physical construction phase.

Can I choose my own custom cabinets and countertops?

Absolutely. Customization is a key feature of our promoted services. You will work closely with design experts to select the materials, finishes, and hardware that match your personal style and functional requirements for your new custom kitchen.

Do I need to hire separate plumbers and electricians?

No, you do not. A core benefit of a full-service kitchen renovation is that all necessary trades are managed for you. Plumbers, electricians, and carpenters are coordinated seamlessly to keep your project on schedule without you having to act as the general contractor.

How do I start planning my custom kitchen?

The best way to begin is by requesting an initial design consultation. Simply fill out the contact form on this page to get connected with renovation professionals who can evaluate your space and discuss your vision for the ultimate kitchen remodeling project.
