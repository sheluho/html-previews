Frequently Asked Questions

What does your interior detailing include? ▼

Our comprehensive interior detailing service involves thorough vacuuming, deep stain extraction from carpets and upholstery, leather conditioning, and rigorous cleaning of all hard surfaces. We ensure every crevice is addressed to restore your cabin to pristine condition.

How long does a ceramic coating application last? ▼

Depending on the specific package and environmental factors, our professional ceramic coatings provide durable paint protection that can last several years. Proper maintenance and regular washing are essential to maximizing the longevity of the coating.

Do you perform exterior car detailing as well? ▼

Yes, our exterior car detailing is an exhaustive process. It includes safe hand washing, chemical decontamination, clay bar treatments to remove embedded particles, and multi-stage machine polishing to correct minor imperfections and enhance gloss.

What is the advantage of paint protection over traditional wax? ▼

Traditional waxes sit on top of the clear coat and wear off within weeks. Advanced paint protection, such as our ceramic coating, bonds chemically with the surface, offering superior resistance against UV damage, chemical stains, and minor abrasions while maintaining a brilliant shine for years.

How frequently should I schedule an auto detailing appointment? ▼

For optimal maintenance, we recommend a complete auto detailing session every 4 to 6 months. Regular care prevents the buildup of harmful contaminants and ensures your vehicle’s interior and exterior remain in excellent condition year-round.
