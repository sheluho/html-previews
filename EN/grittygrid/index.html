Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the premium car wash?
A premium car wash goes beyond the basics. It typically includes an advanced clear coat protectant, undercarriage flush, wheel brightening, and a high-gloss tire shine. Depending on the specific package chosen, it may also feature a hand dry finish to prevent water spots.
Do I need to make an appointment for a hand car wash?
No appointment is necessary for our standard hand car wash or express wash services. We operate on a first-come, first-served basis, and our efficient team works diligently to get your vehicle shining and back on the road as quickly as possible.
How does the auto wash membership work?
The auto wash membership is a monthly subscription plan. For one flat fee, you can wash your registered vehicle as often as you like during that month. The membership is linked to your license plate or an RFID tag for fast, seamless entry into the wash tunnel.
Is the express wash safe for modern car paint?
Absolutely. The express wash utilizes state-of-the-art soft-touch materials and high-lubricity foams designed specifically to protect clear coats. The equipment is constantly monitored and cleaned to ensure a scratch-free, high-quality wash every time.
Do you offer interior cleaning services?
Yes, interior cleaning is a major part of our detailing packages. While the express wash focuses on the exterior, our premium car wash and hand wash packages offer comprehensive interior vacuuming, window cleaning, and surface wiping to ensure the inside looks as good as the outside.
