Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about upgrading your home's windows.

How do I know it's time for replacement windows?

Signs that you need replacement windows include feeling drafts even when windows are closed, noticeable condensation between glass panes, difficulty opening or closing the sash, and unusually high energy bills. Upgrading can resolve these issues immediately.

What makes energy efficient windows different?

Energy efficient windows utilize multiple panes of glass, specialized gas fills (like Argon), and Low-E (low-emissivity) coatings. These elements work together to reflect heat back to its source, keeping your home cooler in the summer and warmer in the winter.

Are vinyl windows durable?

Yes, premium vinyl windows are incredibly durable. They resist moisture, do not require painting or staining, and will not rot, peel, or warp over time. They offer an excellent balance of longevity, low maintenance, and cost-effectiveness.

What are the benefits of double hung windows?

Double hung windows feature two operable sashes (top and bottom) that slide vertically. This design provides excellent ventilation control and allows both sashes to tilt inward, making it safe and easy to clean the exterior glass from inside your home.

How long does professional window installation take?

The timeline varies based on the number of windows and the specific structural requirements of your home. However, most standard window installation projects can be completed in one to two days by an experienced, professional crew.
