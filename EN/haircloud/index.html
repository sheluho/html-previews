Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a front door replacement typically take?

In most cases, standard front door replacement can be completed in a single day. The door installation experts will remove the old frame, install the new unit, ensure it is properly insulated and sealed, and attach the hardware efficiently.

What are the benefits of choosing fiberglass doors for entryways?

Fiberglass doors are highly recommended for entry doors because they offer the aesthetic appeal of real wood without the susceptibility to warping, rotting, or expanding due to moisture. They are highly energy-efficient and provide excellent security.

Can you install new interior doors in existing frames?

Yes, if your existing door frames are square and in good condition, professionals can often install "slab" interior doors. However, if the frame is damaged or out of square, a pre-hung door installation is usually recommended for the best results.

What is included in the door installation process?

A comprehensive door installation includes the removal and disposal of the old door, preparation of the opening, precise fitting of the new door, installation of weatherstripping and insulation, attachment of hardware, and a final operational check.

Are custom sizes available for older homes?

Absolutely. Many entry and interior doors installation company providers offer custom sizing to accommodate the unique dimensions often found in older or historically significant homes, ensuring a perfect fit without compromising the home's character.
