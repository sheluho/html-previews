Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a standard house cleaning?

A standard house cleaning typically covers essential upkeep tasks. This includes dusting surfaces, wiping down countertops, vacuuming carpets, mopping hard floors, and thoroughly cleaning bathrooms and kitchens to maintain a fresh environment.

How often should I schedule a recurring cleaning?

The ideal frequency depends on your lifestyle and household size. Many clients prefer bi-weekly recurring cleaning to keep their homes consistently tidy, while larger or busier families often benefit from a weekly maid service.

What makes a deep cleaning different?

Deep cleaning is a much more intensive process compared to standard upkeep. It involves detailed attention to neglected areas, such as scrubbing baseboards, cleaning inside appliances, dusting ceiling fans, and removing deep-seated grime throughout the home.

Do I need to be home during the maid service?

No, it is entirely up to your preference. Most clients choose to provide access instructions ahead of time so the professionals can perform the house cleaning while they are at work or running errands.

Is move-out cleaning necessary if I'm renting?

Yes, landlords usually require the property to be returned in its original condition. A professional move-out cleaning ensures all areas are thoroughly sanitized and spotless, which greatly improves the chances of receiving your full security deposit back.
