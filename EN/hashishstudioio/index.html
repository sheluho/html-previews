Frequently Asked Questions

What does your basic lawn care program include?

Our core program focuses on the foundational health of your grass. It typically includes regular lawn mowing, scheduled fertilization treatments, and proactive weed control. We assess the specific soil conditions of your yard to determine the exact nutrients needed for optimal growth.

Do you provide custom garden design?

Yes, garden design is a significant part of what we do. Before any landscape installation begins, our team works with you to select plants, materials, and layouts that complement your home while thriving in your specific climate and light conditions.

How often should I schedule lawn mowing?

The frequency of lawn mowing depends heavily on the season and the type of grass you have. During peak growing seasons in spring and early summer, weekly mowing is generally recommended to maintain an even height and promote lateral growth. In slower seasons, bi-weekly schedules may be sufficient.

Is tree service included in general landscaping?

Basic shrub trimming is often part of standard maintenance, but specialized tree service—such as canopy reduction, deep root feeding, or disease management—is usually handled as a separate service to ensure it receives the expert attention required for large plant health.

What makes your landscaping and lawn care company different?

Our difference lies in our comprehensive approach. We don't just cut grass; we manage the entire ecosystem of your yard. By combining expert garden design with consistent, science-based lawn care, we ensure your outdoor space remains healthy, vibrant, and structurally sound year after year.
