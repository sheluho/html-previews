Frequently Asked Questions

What is a turnkey kitchen remodeling service?

A turnkey kitchen remodeling service means that a single company manages every aspect of your renovation project from start to finish. This includes the initial layout design, demolition, electrical and plumbing work, sourcing materials, building custom cabinets, installing countertops, and final finishing touches. You simply "turn the key" and walk into your finished, ready-to-use custom kitchen.

How long does it take to build and install custom cabinets?

The timeline for custom cabinets varies depending on the complexity of the design, the materials chosen, and the current fabrication schedule. Generally, fabrication takes between 4 to 8 weeks after final measurements are approved. Installation typically takes an additional 3 to 5 days, ensuring every piece is perfectly aligned and secured in your kitchen space.

Can I choose my own materials for countertops?

Absolutely. The partners we promote offer a vast selection of premium countertops, ranging from highly durable engineered quartz and classic granite to luxurious marble and modern porcelain. You will have the opportunity to view samples and select the exact slabs that will be fabricated and installed in your home.

Do you provide 3D renderings of the custom kitchen design?

Yes, professional 3D renderings are a crucial part of the intelligent approach to a kitchen remodeling project. Before any physical work begins, designers will create realistic digital models of your new space. This allows you to visualize how the custom cabinets, countertops, and lighting will look together, ensuring you are completely satisfied with the design before proceeding.

Are the services suitable for both small and large kitchen layouts?

Yes. Whether you have a compact urban galley or a sprawling open-concept culinary space, custom kitchen solutions are tailored specifically to your footprint. Custom cabinets allow for maximum space utilization in small areas, while large layouts benefit from grand islands, extensive countertops, and integrated appliance designs.
