Frequently Asked Questions

Insights into the bathroom renovation process.

How long does a typical bathroom renovation take?

Timelines vary based on the scope of the project. A straightforward bathtub replacement or shower conversion might be completed in just a few days, while a full-service bathroom remodel involving extensive plumbing changes and custom tile installation can take several weeks.

What are the benefits of a walk-in shower?

A walk-in shower offers enhanced accessibility and safety by eliminating high thresholds. Aesthetically, it creates a sleek, modern look that can make smaller bathrooms feel significantly more spacious and luxurious.

Can you install new tile over existing flooring?

While it is sometimes possible, professional contractors generally recommend removing old flooring to inspect the subfloor for water damage and ensure a level surface. Proper tile installation requires a solid, prepared foundation to prevent cracking and shifting over time.

Do I need to purchase my own fixtures and materials?

As a full-service bathroom remodeling company partner, the professionals we feature typically handle the procurement of all necessary materials, fixtures, and finishes. During the design phase, you will select your preferred styles, and the team will manage the ordering and delivery.

Is a bathroom remodel a good investment?

Yes, updating bathrooms is consistently ranked as one of the top home improvement projects for return on investment. Beyond increasing property value, a well-executed renovation significantly improves your daily quality of life and home comfort.
