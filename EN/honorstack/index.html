Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer both buffet catering and plated dinner options?

Yes, we specialize in both formats. We can customize a lavish buffet catering setup for a more interactive dining experience or provide an elegant, multi-course plated dinner for formal events and wedding receptions.

How far in advance should we book wedding catering?

For wedding catering, we highly recommend booking at least 6 to 9 months in advance. Prime dates during the spring and fall seasons fill up quickly, and early booking allows ample time for custom menu design and tastings.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions for corporate catering?

Absolutely. We understand the importance of catering to all guests. We offer comprehensive options for vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other specific dietary needs across all our event catering menus.

Does your event catering include waitstaff and bartenders?

Yes, full-service event catering includes access to our highly trained professional waitstaff, bartenders, and culinary team to ensure your event runs smoothly from setup to cleanup.

Do you offer menu tastings before the event?

Yes, we strongly encourage scheduling a private tasting session once you have engaged our services. This allows you to experience the quality of our food firsthand and make final adjustments to your custom menu.
