Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions regarding auto detailing and ceramic coating.

How long does a ceramic coating last?

A professionally applied ceramic coating can last anywhere from 2 to 5 years, depending on the specific product grade, your driving habits, and how well the vehicle is maintained. Regular, gentle washing extends the life of the paint protection significantly.

What is included in a complete auto detailing service?

Complete auto detailing typically involves a thorough exterior hand wash, clay bar treatment, polishing, and sealing. It also includes comprehensive interior detailing, which covers vacuuming, steam cleaning carpets, treating leather, and wiping down all interior panels.

Why is paint protection important for my car?

Paint protection defends your vehicle's exterior from environmental hazards like UV rays, bird droppings, tree sap, and road salt. Without a protective layer like a ceramic coating or quality sealant, these elements can quickly degrade and oxidize your clear coat, leading to permanent damage.

Do I still need to wash my car after a ceramic coating?

Yes. While ceramic coating makes your car highly hydrophobic and easier to clean because dirt doesn't bond as easily to the surface, regular washing is still required to remove surface dirt and road grime. Avoid automated brush washes to prevent scratching the coating.

Is interior detailing necessary if my car looks clean?

Yes, interior detailing is crucial for maintaining the health of your vehicle's cabin. Over time, dust, allergens, and bacteria accumulate deep within fabrics and ventilation systems, even if the surface appears clean. Professional detailing sanitizes these areas and conditions materials to prevent cracking and fading.
