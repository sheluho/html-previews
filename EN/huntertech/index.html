Frequently Asked Questions

What does a turnkey renovation include?

A turnkey renovation is a comprehensive service where the general contractor handles everything from start to finish. This includes architectural design, securing permits, material procurement, construction, and final finishing. You simply "turn the key" and walk into your beautifully finished space.

How long does a whole home remodeling project typically take?

Timelines vary greatly based on the scope of work, property size, and material availability. A standard whole home remodeling project generally spans between 4 to 8 months. Detailed timelines are provided by the contractor during the initial planning phase.

Can I live in my house during a home addition project?

It depends on the scale of the addition and how it impacts your daily living areas. For many home addition projects, homeowners can remain in the house while work is isolated to the exterior or specific zones. Your general contractor will advise on safety and comfort before work begins.

Do I need to hire an architect separately?

Not usually. Many top-tier general contractors promoted through our network operate on a design-build model, meaning they have in-house architects and designers. This ensures seamless communication between the design team and the construction crew.

Who handles the building permits?

The professional general contractor you choose will manage all necessary building permits and inspections required by your local municipality. This is a standard part of any reputable turnkey renovation service.
