Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common inquiries about our recommended cleaning processes.

How often should I schedule professional carpet cleaning?

For most households, professional steam cleaning is recommended every 12 to 18 months. Homes with indoor pets, children, or high foot traffic may benefit from professional carpet cleaning every 6 to 9 months to maintain optimal hygiene and appearance.

Can you guarantee complete stain removal?

While advanced stain removal techniques are highly effective on a wide variety of spots—including wine, coffee, and pet accidents—some substances may permanently alter the fabric dye. Technicians assess each spot individually to provide the best possible outcome without damaging the fibers.

Is upholstery cleaning safe for delicate fabrics?

Absolutely. Professional upholstery cleaning begins with a thorough fabric inspection. Depending on the material (such as silk, wool, or synthetic blends), the correct cleaning method and solutions are chosen to safely lift dirt while protecting the fabric's integrity and color.

Do you clean area rugs in the home or take them off-site?

Most synthetic and standard area rug cleaning can be performed effectively right in your home. However, valuable oriental, Persian, or heavily soiled wool rugs may require specialized off-site care to ensure they are cleaned and dried in a controlled environment.

How long does it take for carpets to dry after steam cleaning?

Drying times vary based on humidity, airflow, and the specific carpet type, but generally, carpets are dry to the touch within 4 to 6 hours after a professional steam cleaning session. Keeping windows open or running fans can help speed up the process.
