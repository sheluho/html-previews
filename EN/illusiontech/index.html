Frequently Asked Questions

Do you handle complete front door replacement?

Yes, we specialize in connecting you with experts for complete front door replacement. This process ensures a perfect fit, addresses any underlying frame issues, and significantly upgrades the security and insulation of your home's main entrance.

What are the benefits of fiberglass doors?

Fiberglass doors are highly recommended because they offer the elegant appearance of natural wood without the high maintenance. They are incredibly durable, resist denting and scratching, will not rot or warp due to moisture, and provide excellent thermal insulation.

Can you replace all my interior doors at once?

Absolutely. Comprehensive interior doors installation is a common service requested by homeowners looking to unify their interior design. Upgrading all doors simultaneously ensures consistent styling, better soundproofing between rooms, and smoother operation throughout the house.

How long does a typical door installation take?

A standard single entry door installation usually takes a few hours to complete, ensuring the frame is properly sealed and hardware is meticulously aligned. Larger projects involving multiple interior doors will be scheduled appropriately based on the total scope of work.

Do I need to prepare the area before the team arrives?

We simply ask that you clear the immediate area around the doors being replaced. Removing small furniture, rugs, and fragile items near the entryway allows the installation team safe, unobstructed access to complete the work efficiently and protect your belongings.
