Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is a ceramic coating?

A ceramic coating is a liquid polymer applied by hand to the exterior of a vehicle. It chemically bonds with the factory paint, creating a semi-permanent layer of protection. This layer is much harder than traditional wax, offering superior resistance to UV rays, chemical stains, and minor scratches while providing intense gloss.

How long does a full interior detailing take?

Depending on the size and initial condition of the vehicle, a comprehensive interior detailing usually takes between 2 to 5 hours. We ensure that every surface, crevice, and fabric is thoroughly cleaned, extracted, and conditioned for an immaculate finish.

Does paint protection fix existing scratches?

No, paint protection products like ceramic coatings or sealants are designed to prevent new damage, not fix existing ones. However, we highly recommend our paint correction service prior to coating application, which will safely remove swirl marks and light scratches to ensure the paint looks flawless before it is sealed.

How often should I get full auto detailing?

For a daily driven vehicle, we recommend a full auto detailing (both interior and exterior) every 4 to 6 months. Regular maintenance detailing not only keeps your car looking great but also preserves the integrity of the materials, potentially increasing the resale value of your vehicle.

Is ceramic coating really better than traditional wax?

Yes, significantly. While a high-quality carnauba wax might last 2 to 3 months and offers minimal resistance to harsh environmental factors, a professional-grade ceramic coating can last for years. It creates a much harder, more durable barrier against contaminants and makes routine washing incredibly easy due to its hydrophobic properties.
