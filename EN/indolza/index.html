Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions regarding our door installation services.

What are the benefits of choosing fiberglass doors for an entry?

Fiberglass doors are highly durable, resistant to warping and rotting, and offer excellent energy efficiency. They can also mimic the beautiful look of natural wood without the extensive maintenance required, making them ideal for a lasting front door replacement.

Do you handle both entry doors and interior doors?

Yes, we provide comprehensive solutions for your entire home. Whether you are looking for a singular secure entryway upgrade or replacing all the interior doors in your hallway and bedrooms, our partners are equipped to handle projects of all sizes.

How long does a typical front door replacement take?

In most cases, a standard front door replacement can be completed in a single day. The professional door installation team will remove the old frame, install the new unit, ensure it is perfectly plumb and sealed, and clean up the work area promptly.

Can you install new doors in existing, older frames?

Absolutely. Custom fitting is a core part of the door installation service. If your home has non-standard or older frames, the doors are measured and tailored to ensure a precise, secure fit without necessarily tearing out the surrounding walls.

What styles of interior doors are available?

You can choose from a vast array of styles including modern flush doors, traditional paneled designs, solid core doors for better soundproofing, and custom closet doors. The goal is to match your specific interior design aesthetic seamlessly.
