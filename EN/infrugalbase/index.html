Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about planning your next move.

What defines a professional residential moving company?

A professional residential moving company provides a comprehensive suite of services dedicated to household relocations. This typically includes trained personnel, proper moving equipment, specialized trucks, and fully insured operations to safely transport everything from everyday furniture to precious family heirlooms.

Do I need to hire packing services?

While optional, professional packing services are highly recommended for those with tight schedules or large households. Expert packers use industry-standard materials to wrap fragile items securely, significantly reducing the risk of damage during transit and saving you days of exhausting preparation.

What is the difference between local movers and long distance movers?

Local movers typically operate within the same city or state, usually pricing their services by the hour. Long distance movers handle interstate or cross-country relocations, pricing based on the weight of the items and the distance traveled. Long distance moves also require specific federal licensing and logistical planning.

How far in advance should I book my move?

It is best to book your moving company at least 4 to 6 weeks in advance, especially if you plan to move during the peak summer season or at the end of the month. Early booking ensures you secure the best dates and provides ample time to arrange any necessary packing services.

Are my belongings protected during the move?

Yes, reputable movers offer basic liability coverage by default. However, for complete peace of mind, full value protection plans are typically available for purchase. Discuss valuation options with your provider prior to moving day to ensure your items are adequately protected.
