Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in your standard lawn care program?

Our comprehensive lawn care program typically includes scheduled fertilization, pre-emergent and post-emergent weed control, surface insect management, and regular lawn mowing to ensure your grass remains robust and healthy throughout the growing season.

Do you offer custom garden design and landscape installation?

Yes. We provide full-service garden design and landscape installation. From the initial conceptual drawing to soil preparation, planting, and mulching, we handle the entire transformation of your outdoor living area.

How often should I schedule lawn mowing services?

For optimal turf health, we generally recommend weekly lawn mowing during the peak growing season, transitioning to bi-weekly as growth slows in the late summer and fall. We customize the schedule based on your specific grass type and local climate conditions.

What does your tree service entail?

Our tree service includes health assessments, structural and maintenance pruning, disease and pest management, and deep root fertilization. We focus on preserving the vitality of mature trees and safely managing overgrown shrubs.

Are your landscaping practices safe for pets and children?

Absolutely. We prioritize safety by adhering strictly to approved application guidelines for all lawn care treatments. Our technicians always provide clear instructions on how long to keep pets and children off the lawn following any specific application.
