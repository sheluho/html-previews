Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our premium car care recommendations.

What makes a hand car wash superior?

A hand car wash is significantly gentler on your vehicle's paintwork. Automated washes use harsh, rotating brushes that can leave micro-scratches and swirl marks in your clear coat. Hand washing ensures careful, targeted cleaning using soft microfiber materials that protect your finish.

What is included in a premium car wash?

A premium car wash typically includes a meticulous exterior hand wash, deep wheel and rim cleaning, tire dressing, a spot-free rinse, and hand drying. It may also feature basic interior vacuuming and window cleaning to leave your entire vehicle refreshed.

How does the auto wash membership work?

An auto wash membership offers incredible convenience and value for regular customers. By paying a fixed monthly fee, members can enjoy routine washes to keep their vehicle looking its best all month long, along with priority scheduling for deeper detailing services.

Is an express wash thorough enough?

Absolutely. An express wash is specifically designed to safely and quickly remove surface dirt and road grime. It is the perfect maintenance wash for those who are short on time but still demand a clean, brilliant exterior without risking their paint.

Do I need to book an appointment beforehand?

While drive-ups are generally welcome for standard and express wash services, booking an appointment is highly recommended for full interior and exterior detailing. This ensures dedicated time is reserved exclusively for your vehicle's specialized care.
