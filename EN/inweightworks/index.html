Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about our window treatment categories.

What types of window treatments do you feature?

We showcase a comprehensive range of premium options including custom wood and faux-wood blinds, fabric roller shades, cellular shades, elegant custom drapes, and architectural plantation shutters to suit diverse interior styles.

Are plantation shutters suitable for every room?

Yes, plantation shutters are incredibly versatile. While traditional wood shutters are perfect for living areas and bedrooms, specialized composite or vinyl shutters are highly recommended for high-moisture environments like bathrooms and kitchens.

How do custom drapes differ from standard curtains?

Custom drapes are tailored to the exact dimensions of your windows, ensuring a flawless drape and superior light control. They offer extensive customization in fabric choice, lining types (such as blackout or thermal), and pleat styles that off-the-shelf options cannot match.

What is the advantage of roller shades?

Roller shades provide a clean, modern aesthetic with a low profile. They are highly efficient at managing glare and UV rays while taking up minimal space when fully raised, making them ideal for maintaining unobstructed views.

How do I determine the right style for my home?

Selecting the right window treatments depends on your primary needs—whether it's maximizing privacy, darkening a room, or adding a decorative touch. Reviewing our collections and requesting a professional consultation is the best way to match products to your specific lifestyle requirements.
