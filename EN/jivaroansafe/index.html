Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in your wedding videography packages?

Our standard packages include full-day coverage by a professional wedding videographer, a cinematic highlight reel set to licensed music, and a comprehensive documentary-style edit of the ceremony and formal speeches.

How would you describe your wedding film style?

We focus on creating a cinematic wedding video that is deeply emotional, timeless, and story-driven. Rather than just compiling a sequence of events, we craft a narrative that reflects the unique connection between you and your partner.

Do you travel for event videography?

Yes, our team is available for destination weddings and international event videography. We love capturing love stories in new locations. Custom travel quotes are provided during the consultation phase.

How long does it take to receive our wedding video?

We dedicate significant time and attention to the editing process to ensure a flawless final product. Typically, cinematic highlight films are delivered within 8 to 10 weeks following your event date.

Can we choose the music for our cinematic wedding video?

Due to copyright laws, we cannot use mainstream radio music. Instead, we carefully select and license high-quality, cinematic compositions from specialized platforms that perfectly match the mood, pacing, and emotion of your footage.
