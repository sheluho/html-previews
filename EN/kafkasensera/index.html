Inquiries

What is the difference between a wedding planner and a day of coordinator?

A full-service wedding planner is involved from the very beginning, handling budget, vendor selection, and design. A day of coordinator steps in closer to the date (usually a month prior) to manage the logistics and timeline of the actual wedding day, assuming you have already planned the event.

Do you offer destination wedding services?

Yes, we specialize in destination wedding planning. We manage the complexities of international vendor relations, travel logistics for the couple, and event execution in remote or exotic locales to ensure a seamless experience.

How far in advance should we book a wedding coordinator?

For full-service planning, we recommend booking 12 to 18 months in advance to secure preferred venues and vendors. For day of coordination, booking 6 to 8 months prior to your date is ideal to ensure availability.

Are you present for the wedding rehearsal?

Absolutely. Whether you engage us for comprehensive planning or coordination, directing the wedding rehearsal is a standard part of our service to ensure the wedding party is comfortable and prepared for the ceremony.

Can you work within a specific budget?

Part of our role as a professional wedding planning company is to help you maximize your budget. We provide realistic cost projections and allocate resources strategically to prioritize the elements most important to your vision.
