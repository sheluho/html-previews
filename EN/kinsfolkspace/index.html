Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I schedule professional carpet cleaning?

For most households, professional carpet cleaning is recommended every 12 to 18 months. However, if you have indoor pets, children, or a high-traffic environment, you may benefit from scheduling a deep steam cleaning every 6 to 9 months to maintain optimal hygiene and appearance.

Is steam cleaning safe for all types of upholstery?

While steam cleaning is highly effective for many fabrics, it is not universally suitable for all materials. Professional technicians will typically inspect your furniture beforehand to determine the safest upholstery cleaning method, ensuring delicate fabrics like silk or vintage weaves are treated correctly without risk of shrinkage or color bleeding.

Can professional services remove old pet stains and odors?

Yes, targeted stain removal treatments are specifically designed to address stubborn pet accidents. Technicians utilize advanced enzymatic cleaners that break down the organic compounds causing the odor, rather than simply masking the smell, resulting in a fresher and vastly improved living environment.

How long does it take for carpets to dry after a deep clean?

Drying times generally range from 4 to 8 hours depending on humidity, temperature, and the specific carpet cleaning method used. Proper ventilation and air circulation in the room can significantly accelerate the drying process.

Do you provide care for specialized and area rugs?

Absolutely. Rug cleaning requires a different approach than standard wall-to-wall carpeting. The featured providers use gentle yet thorough washing techniques that safeguard the dyes and fibers of your delicate area rugs, ensuring they are returned to you in pristine condition.
