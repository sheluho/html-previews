Common Questions

What does a turnkey kitchen renovation include? +

A turnkey kitchen project means we handle everything from start to finish. This includes the initial design consultation, demolition, structural work, plumbing, electrical, installation of custom cabinets, countertops, and final finishing touches. You simply turn the key and enjoy your new space.

Can I choose my own materials for custom cabinets? +

Absolutely. As part of our custom kitchen design process, we work closely with you to select the exact wood species, finishes, hardware, and internal storage configurations that meet your specific aesthetic and functional requirements.

What types of countertops do you offer? +

We source and install a wide variety of premium countertops, including high-grade quartz, natural granite, marble, and durable solid surfaces. Our experts help you choose the material that best fits your lifestyle and design preferences.

How long does a complete kitchen remodeling project take? +

The timeline varies based on the scope of work and the materials chosen. A standard full-service kitchen renovation typically takes between 4 to 8 weeks once construction begins. We provide a detailed schedule during the planning phase to ensure expectations are aligned.

Do you manage the permits required for the renovation? +

Yes. As a full-service kitchen renovation company, managing all necessary local building permits and inspections is a standard part of our turnkey process, ensuring your project complies with all regulations.
