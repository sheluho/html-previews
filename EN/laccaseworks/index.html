Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about our promoted custom storage solutions.

What is the process for designing custom closets?

The process begins with an initial consultation to measure your space and understand your storage needs. From there, the service provider creates a 3D design of your new walk-in closet or wardrobe. Once the design and finishes are approved, the materials are custom-manufactured and professionally installed in your home.

Can a reach-in closet really be optimized for more space?

Absolutely. A standard reach-in closet usually features a single rod and shelf, which wastes a tremendous amount of vertical space. By installing a custom closet organizer with double hanging rods, adjustable shelving, and tailored drawers, the usable capacity can be drastically increased.

What materials are used for a custom wardrobe?

The promoted partners use a variety of high-quality materials ranging from premium thermally fused laminates (TFL) to natural wood veneers and solid wood accents. Hardware choices include soft-close hinges, full-extension drawer glides, and modern or classic handles to match your home's decor.

Do you only promote bedroom closets?

No, custom casework and organization extends far beyond the bedroom. The featured solutions include custom pantries, mudrooms, home office cabinetry, garage storage systems, and laundry room organizers. Any space that requires organization can benefit from a tailored approach.

How long does installation take?

Installation timelines vary based on the size and complexity of the project. A standard reach-in closet or simple closet organizer can often be installed in a single day. Larger projects, such as an expansive custom walk-in closet or a complex built-in wardrobe, may take a few days to ensure precise fit and finish.
