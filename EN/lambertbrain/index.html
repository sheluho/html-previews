Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I schedule lawn mowing?

For optimal health and appearance, we generally recommend weekly lawn mowing during the peak growing seasons of spring and early summer. During slower growth periods, bi-weekly service may be sufficient to maintain a neat appearance without stressing the turf.

What is included in your landscape installation process?

Our landscape installation involves a comprehensive approach. It begins with an initial consultation and custom garden design, followed by site preparation, soil grading, plant selection, and the physical installation of softscapes (plants, sod) and hardscapes (pathways, retaining walls).

Do you provide year-round lawn care?

Yes, effective lawn care is a year-round commitment. We offer seasonal treatments including pre-emergent weed control in the spring, fertilization in the summer, aeration and overseeding in the fall, and winterization treatments to protect your landscape during colder months.

When is the best time for tree service and pruning?

While emergency tree service can be performed anytime, structural pruning is generally best done during the dormant season (late winter to early spring). This minimizes stress on the tree, reduces the risk of disease transmission, and stimulates robust spring growth.

Are your weed control products safe for pets?

We prioritize safety in all our landscaping practices. Our service partners use EPA-registered products applied according to strict guidelines. We typically recommend keeping pets off treated areas until the application has completely dried, after which it is safe for normal family use.
