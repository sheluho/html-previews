Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about scheduling and preparing for your move.

How far in advance should I book movers?

It is generally recommended to book your residential moving company at least 4 to 6 weeks in advance, especially during peak moving seasons (summer months and weekends). Early booking ensures better availability and allows ample time for planning.

Do you offer packing services?

Yes, the service providers we promote offer comprehensive packing services. You can opt for full-service packing where professionals handle everything, or partial packing for specific, fragile items like dishes, artwork, and electronics.

What is the difference between local movers and long distance movers?

Local movers typically handle relocations within the same city or a short radius and usually charge by the hour. Long distance movers handle interstate or cross-country moves, and their pricing is generally based on the total weight of your belongings and the distance traveled.

How can I prepare for moving day?

To prepare, start by decluttering items you no longer need. If you are packing yourself, label boxes clearly by room and contents. Ensure pathways are clear for the movers, and keep essential items (like documents, medications, and basic toiletries) with you rather than loading them onto the truck.

Are my belongings protected during the move?

The moving companies we connect you with provide basic valuation coverage as standard. For added peace of mind, you can often discuss full-value protection options directly with the provider to ensure your items are adequately covered against unforeseen circumstances.
