Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between a full-service wedding planner and a day of coordinator?

A full-service wedding planner assists you from the moment you are engaged, handling budget tracking, venue scouting, vendor negotiation, and overall design. A day of coordinator typically steps in 4-6 weeks before the event to take over the plans you've already made, manage the timeline, and oversee the logistical execution on the actual wedding day.

Do you handle destination wedding planning?

Yes, we specialize in destination wedding coordination. Planning from afar requires deep logistical knowledge and strong vendor communication. Our team manages travel logistics, local sourcing, and timeline management for multi-day events across various locations.

When should we hire a wedding coordinator?

If you are looking for full-service wedding planning, we recommend reaching out 12 to 18 months before your desired date. For day of coordinator services, booking 6 to 9 months in advance ensures availability, though our active work begins closer to the wedding date.

Can you customize your wedding planning packages?

Absolutely. While we offer structured packages for comprehensive planning and coordination, we understand every celebration is unique. We are happy to tailor our services to fit the specific scale, location, and requirements of your event.

Will you be there for the rehearsal?

Yes. Whether you book us as your full-service wedding planner or your day of coordinator, orchestrating the wedding rehearsal is a standard part of our process to ensure your wedding party knows exactly what to expect on the big day.
