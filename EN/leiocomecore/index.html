Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the carpet take to dry after steam cleaning? ▼

Depending on the ambient humidity, temperature, and airflow within your home, carpets typically require between 4 to 8 hours to dry completely. We utilize advanced, high-powered extraction equipment to remove as much moisture as possible during the final phase of the cleaning process, minimizing your wait time.

Are the cleaning products safe for children and pets? ▼

Yes, safety is a primary consideration in our service protocols. The solutions we use for carpet cleaning and upholstery care are thoroughly tested and formulated to be gentle on your home environment once the application and extraction processes are fully completed. We recommend keeping pets off damp surfaces until they are entirely dry.

Can you guarantee complete stain removal? ▼

While we employ professional-grade stain removal techniques that successfully eliminate the vast majority of spots, certain substances may permanently alter the dye structure of the fibers. Factors such as the age of the stain and previous treatments can affect the outcome. We assess each blemish individually and provide realistic expectations before proceeding.

Do you clean delicate or antique area rugs? ▼

Absolutely. Rug cleaning requires a specialized approach depending on the weave, material, and dye stability. We evaluate your area rugs prior to service to determine the safest and most effective method, ensuring dirt is lifted without compromising the integrity or vibrancy of the delicate fibers.

How often should I have my carpets professionally cleaned? ▼

Industry standards recommend professional carpet cleaning every 12 to 18 months for a typical household. However, if your home experiences high foot traffic, or if you have indoor pets or allergy concerns, scheduling a deep cleaning every 6 to 9 months is highly advisable to maintain optimal indoor air quality and fiber resilience.
