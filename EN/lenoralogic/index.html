Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about custom window coverings.

What types of window treatments do you highlight?
We feature a comprehensive range of premium options including custom blinds, various styles of shades, elegant custom drapes, timeless plantation shutters, and modern roller shades designed to fit any architectural style.
How do I choose the right custom blinds for my home?
Choosing the right custom blinds involves considering your needs for light control, privacy, and interior decor. Wood blinds offer a classic, warm look, while faux wood provides excellent durability for high-humidity areas like bathrooms and kitchens.
Are plantation shutters energy efficient?
Yes, plantation shutters are among the most energy-efficient window treatments available. When closed, they provide excellent insulation against both heat and cold, helping to regulate your home's temperature and potentially lowering energy costs.
Do roller shades come with motorization options?
Absolutely. Modern roller shades often feature advanced motorization options, allowing you to control your window treatments with a remote, a smartphone app, or even integrate them into your home automation system for ultimate convenience.
What is the typical process for ordering custom drapes?
The process generally begins with a design consultation to discuss your vision and select fabrics. This is followed by precise professional measurements of your windows. Finally, the custom drapes are crafted to exact specifications and professionally installed for a flawless finish.
