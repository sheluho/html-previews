Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the premium hand car wash?

The premium hand car wash includes a comprehensive exterior hand wash, wheel and tire cleaning, interior vacuuming, window wiping, and a final protective shine application to keep your vehicle looking its best.

How does the auto wash membership work?

Our auto wash membership provides you with unlimited washes for a flat monthly fee. You can simply drive up, have your vehicle recognized, and enjoy a premium car wash as often as you like without any hassle.

Is the express wash safe for my car's paint?

Yes. The express wash uses advanced, soft-touch materials and high-quality pH-balanced cleaning agents specifically designed to lift dirt safely without scratching or dulling your vehicle's clear coat.

Do I need an appointment for a wash?

Appointments are not necessary for our standard express wash or if you hold an auto wash membership. However, scheduling in advance for a premium hand car wash or detailing service is recommended to ensure minimal wait times.

What makes Levecore different from other services?

We focus exclusively on connecting drivers with elite, high-performance car wash options. From precision cleaning technology to dedicated hand care, we ensure every vehicle receives a meticulous, flawless finish.
