Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about door installation and replacement.

What are the benefits of fiberglass doors over traditional wood?

Fiberglass doors offer incredible durability and require significantly less maintenance than natural wood. They resist warping, rotting, and expanding in humid climates. Modern manufacturing allows fiberglass entry doors to mimic the rich grain of authentic wood perfectly, giving you high-end aesthetics with superior longevity and energy efficiency.

How long does a typical front door replacement take?

A standard front door replacement usually takes a single day to complete. This includes removing the old door, preparing the frame, installing the new entry door, weatherproofing, and final cleanup. Complex installations involving sidelights or structural frame repairs may take slightly longer, but the goal is always to secure your home the same day.

Do you install all types of interior doors?

Yes, professional installation services cover a wide variety of interior doors. This includes traditional hinged doors, sliding barn doors, pocket doors, French doors, and bi-fold closet doors. Whether you need solid core doors for bedroom privacy or glass-paneled doors for a home office, expert installers can handle the framing and fitting.

Why shouldn't I just install the door myself?

Proper door installation is a highly precise task. If a door is not perfectly plumb and level, it will bind, fail to latch securely, or allow drafts and moisture to enter your home. Professionals ensure the frame is square, the hinges are properly mortised, and the weatherstripping creates a tight seal, protecting your investment and your home's energy efficiency.

Can new entry doors improve my home's energy efficiency?

Absolutely. Upgrading to modern entry doors, especially insulated fiberglass doors, can significantly reduce heat transfer. Combined with professional door installation that guarantees airtight weatherstripping and proper caulking around the frame, a new front door can eliminate drafts, lower your heating and cooling bills, and create a more comfortable indoor environment.
