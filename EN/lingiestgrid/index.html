Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about our wedding planning process.

What is included in your full-service wedding planning package?
We handle everything from budget creation and vendor selection to design conceptualization and timeline management. A dedicated wedding planner will be with you every step of the way to ensure your vision is fully realized.
Do I really need a day of coordinator if my venue provides a manager?
Yes. A venue manager focuses exclusively on the building, catering, and venue staff. Our day of coordinator focuses entirely on you, your personal timeline, and your specific external vendors, ensuring your personal wedding planning vision is executed.
How much experience do you have with a destination wedding?
Our team has extensive experience organizing destination weddings across various global locations. We expertly navigate local regulations, secure reliable international vendors, and manage complex travel logistics for a stress-free experience.
When should we hire a wedding coordinator?
We recommend securing your wedding coordinator at least 9 to 12 months in advance, especially if you require full-service wedding planning. This ensures availability and provides ample time for meticulous preparation.
Can you help us stay within our specific wedding budget?
Absolutely. A core part of our wedding planning service is strict budget management. We provide realistic cost estimates, negotiate with vendors on your behalf, and track every expense to keep your celebration financially comfortable.
