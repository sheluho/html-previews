Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to design and install custom closets?
The timeline for custom closets typically ranges from a few weeks to a couple of months, depending on the complexity of the design and materials chosen. Following your initial consultation, a precise timeline will be provided by the installation team.
Can a closet organizer be fitted into a small reach-in closet?
Absolutely. In fact, a reach-in closet benefits the most from a professionally designed closet organizer. By utilizing adjustable shelving, double-hang rods, and specialized compartments, we can significantly increase the usable storage space in tight quarters.
What materials are used for your built-in wardrobes?
The providers we showcase use a variety of high-quality, durable materials including premium melamine, thermally fused laminates, and real wood veneers. You can choose from numerous finishes and hardware options to ensure your new wardrobe matches your home's aesthetic perfectly.
Do I need to empty my walk-in closet before the consultation?
No, you do not need to empty your walk-in closet for the initial consultation. In fact, seeing your current items helps the designers understand exactly what kind of storage solutions (shoe racks, long hanging sections, drawers) you will need.
Are the closet systems adjustable if my needs change?
Yes, most of our custom closets feature adjustable shelving and track systems. This means that as your wardrobe changes over the years, your closet organizer can be reconfigured easily to accommodate different types of clothing or accessories.
