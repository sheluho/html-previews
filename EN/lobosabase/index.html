Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in your wedding videography packages?

Our packages typically include full-day coverage by a lead wedding videographer, multiple camera angles during the ceremony, professional audio recording, and a beautifully edited cinematic wedding video highlight reel. Extended packages also include documentary edits of the full ceremony and speeches.

How long does it take to receive our wedding film?

We dedicate significant time to the editing process to ensure a flawless final product. You can generally expect to receive your final wedding film within 8 to 12 weeks following your event date, depending on the season and the complexity of your event videography package.

Do you offer event videography for rehearsal dinners?

Yes, we highly recommend adding rehearsal dinner coverage. Documenting the speeches and intimate moments the night before adds incredible depth and storytelling elements to your final cinematic wedding video.

How does a wedding videographer work alongside a photographer?

We pride ourselves on being team players. We communicate actively with your photographer throughout the day to ensure we both get the shots we need without getting in each other's way. Our goal is a seamless, cooperative environment that prioritizes your experience.

Can we choose the music for our cinematic wedding video?

For highlight films, we license high-quality, professional music from specialized platforms to ensure your video can be shared online without copyright issues. We take your musical tastes and the vibe of your day into account when selecting the perfect emotional soundtrack for your film.
