Frequently Asked Questions

What makes this carpet cleaning method different?

Unlike traditional methods that rely heavily on soaps and detergents, the process we highlight uses electrolyzed water technology. This means your carpet cleaning leaves zero sticky residue, allowing carpets to stay cleaner for a significantly longer period and drying much faster.

Is the upholstery cleaning safe for delicate fabrics?

Yes. Professional upholstery cleaning begins with a thorough assessment of your furniture's fabric type. The cleaning solutions and techniques are then adjusted to safely lift dirt and stains without compromising the color, texture, or structural integrity of delicate materials.

How long does rug cleaning take to dry?

Because the advanced extraction methods use less water than conventional soaking techniques, dry times for rug cleaning are drastically reduced. Most area rugs and carpets are dry to the touch and ready for normal traffic within a few hours.

Can you handle severe stain removal and pet odors?

Absolutely. Targeted stain removal is a core specialty. Biological issues like pet urine require more than just surface cleaning; specialized treatments are utilized to neutralize odors at the source beneath the carpet fibers, rather than just masking them.

Does the steam cleaning alternative use harsh chemicals?

No. The primary advantage of the services promoted by Lophura Network is the commitment to a chemical-free clean. Instead of harsh detergents common in traditional steam cleaning, the process relies on altered water that acts like a powerful solvent but is entirely safe for families and pets.
