Frequently Asked Questions

Details about our process and what to expect.

What defines a cinematic wedding video? +

A cinematic wedding video goes beyond traditional recording. It utilizes creative camera angles, professional audio capture, and sophisticated storytelling editing techniques to create a film that feels like a beautifully directed movie about your relationship, rather than just a home video of the day.

How many wedding videographers will be at our event? +

For most standard event videography packages, we provide two dedicated videographers. This allows us to capture multiple angles simultaneously—such as both partners' reactions during the ceremony—and ensures comprehensive coverage of your venue and guests.

Do you provide the raw unedited footage? +

Yes, raw footage is often available as an add-on to our main packages. While our primary focus is delivering a polished, cinematic wedding film, we understand that some couples wish to preserve every single clip recorded throughout the day for their personal archives.

How long does it take to receive our final wedding film? +

The editing process for a high-quality cinematic wedding video is intricate and requires meticulous attention to detail. Typically, you can expect to receive your completed highlight film and documentary edits within 10 to 14 weeks following your event date.

Can we choose the music for our video? +

We license high-quality, professional music specifically curated to match the mood and pacing of your footage. While we welcome your input on the general vibe and genre, our editors select the final tracks to ensure the cinematic rhythm and emotional impact of the film are perfectly synchronized.
