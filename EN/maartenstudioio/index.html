Frequently Asked Questions

What is a ceramic coating and why do I need it?

Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer applied to your vehicle's exterior that chemically bonds with the factory paint. It creates a durable layer of protection against environmental damage, UV rays, and chemical stains, while making your car significantly easier to clean.

How long does a standard auto detailing session take?

The duration depends on the specific service package. A basic exterior wash and interior detailing typically takes 2 to 4 hours. Advanced paint correction and ceramic coating applications can take anywhere from a full day to multiple days to ensure proper curing.

Can paint correction remove deep scratches?

Paint correction is highly effective at removing surface-level imperfections like swirl marks, light oxidation, and shallow clear-coat scratches. However, if a scratch has penetrated through the clear coat into the base color or primer, it may require professional touch-up painting rather than just polishing.

What does interior detailing include?

Comprehensive interior detailing involves thorough vacuuming, steam cleaning of carpets and upholstery, deep cleaning of all hard surfaces, window cleaning, and specialized conditioning for leather to prevent cracking and fading.

How often should I have my car detailed?

For optimal maintenance, we recommend a full detail every 4 to 6 months. Regular maintenance washes in between will help preserve the condition of your paint protection and keep your interior feeling fresh.
