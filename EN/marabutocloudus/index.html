Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build custom furniture?
Because every piece of bespoke furniture is crafted from scratch, timelines typically range from 8 to 12 weeks. This allows our artisans the necessary time to source premium materials, prepare the wood, and execute the fine joinery required for lasting quality.
Can I choose the specific wood for my custom dining table?
Absolutely. A core feature of our custom furniture maker and showroom experience is material selection. We offer a wide variety of domestic and imported hardwoods, including walnut, oak, maple, and specialty reclaimed slabs.
What makes your bespoke furniture different from mass-produced items?
Mass-produced furniture is built for averages, using veneers and cost-cutting construction methods. Our handmade furniture is built for you. We utilize solid wood construction, traditional joinery techniques, and hand-applied finishes to ensure your piece is structurally superior and visually unique.
Do you offer custom sofa configurations for difficult spaces?
Yes. Whether you have an unusually shaped living room or require specific seating depths, a custom sofa can be tailored to the exact inch. We help you select the ideal frame shape, cushion density, and performance fabric to match your lifestyle.
Can I visit the showroom to see the handmade furniture in person?
Yes, visiting our custom furniture maker and showroom is highly encouraged. It allows you to experience the tactile quality of our finishes, test the comfort of our seating, and consult directly with our design team to start planning your bespoke furniture project.
