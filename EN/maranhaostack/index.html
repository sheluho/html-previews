Frequently Asked Questions

What types of window treatments do you showcase?
We feature a comprehensive range of custom solutions, including real wood and faux wood plantation shutters, custom blinds, roman shades, roller shades, and beautifully tailored custom drapes.
Are the blinds and shades custom-measured for my windows?
Yes, all window treatments introduced through our platform are designed to be custom-fitted. Professional measurement ensures that your new shades or shutters fit your exact window dimensions perfectly.
What is the difference between blinds and shades?
Blinds are typically made of "hard" materials like wood, faux wood, or metal and feature slats that can be tilted to control light. Shades are made of "soft" materials or fabrics (like roller shades or roman shades) and are drawn up or down to cover the window.
Do plantation shutters provide good insulation?
Absolutely. Plantation shutters are excellent for insulation. When closed, they provide a solid barrier against the window, helping to keep your home cooler in the summer and warmer in the winter, potentially reducing energy costs.
