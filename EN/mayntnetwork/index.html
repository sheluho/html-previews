Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common queries about HVAC installation, maintenance, and repair.

How often should my HVAC system be serviced?

It is generally recommended to have your heating and cooling system inspected and serviced at least once a year. Ideally, your air conditioner should be checked in the spring, and your furnace should be inspected in the fall before the heavy usage seasons begin.

What are the signs I need a new air conditioner?

If your AC unit is over 10-15 years old, requires frequent repairs, struggles to maintain a consistent temperature, or causes sudden spikes in your energy bills, it might be time to consider a full AC installation and upgrade to a more efficient model.

Are heat pumps better than traditional furnaces?

Heat pumps are highly energy-efficient because they transfer heat rather than generating it. While traditional furnaces are excellent for extreme cold, a heat pump serves as an all-in-one heating and cooling solution that can significantly reduce utility costs in moderate climates.

How can I improve my home's indoor air quality?

Regularly changing your HVAC filters is the most crucial step. Additionally, scheduling routine duct cleaning, ensuring your heating and cooling systems are well-maintained, and possibly integrating a whole-home air purifier can vastly improve indoor air quality.

Do you actually perform the AC installation or repair?

No, Maynt Network is an independent promotional operator. We connect you with verified, professional HVAC contractors like Chapman Engineering, who perform the actual assessments, installations, and repairs on your property.
