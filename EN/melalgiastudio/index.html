Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in your wedding catering service?

Our wedding catering packages typically include menu planning, food preparation, delivery, and setup. Depending on your chosen package, it may also encompass professional waitstaff, elegant tableware, and post-event cleanup to ensure a stress-free day.

Do you offer both buffet catering and plated dinner options?

Yes, we specialize in both styles. Buffet catering is perfect for offering your guests a wide variety of choices in a more relaxed atmosphere, while our plated dinner service provides a formal, highly orchestrated dining experience for sit-down events.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions for event catering?

Absolutely. We understand the importance of catering to all guests. Our culinary team can seamlessly incorporate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and allergen-conscious options into any event catering menu without compromising on flavor or presentation.

How far in advance should we book corporate catering?

For large-scale corporate catering events or multi-day conferences, we recommend reaching out at least 4 to 6 weeks in advance. For smaller meetings or executive lunches, a 1 to 2-week notice is typically sufficient to secure your preferred date and menu selections.

Do you provide staffing and equipment for the event?

Yes, our comprehensive services can include highly trained servers, bartenders, and on-site chefs. We can also coordinate the rental of essential equipment such as tables, linens, glassware, and warming chafers to ensure your event runs flawlessly from start to finish.
