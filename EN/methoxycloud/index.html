Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries regarding our moving network.

Do you provide packing services before moving day?
Yes, professional packing services are a core offering among the moving companies we promote. Experienced crews can pack your entire home, partial areas, or just fragile items using high-quality materials to ensure safe transit.
What is the difference between local movers and long distance movers?
Local movers typically handle relocations within the same city or state, usually completed within a single day. Long distance movers specialize in cross-state or cross-country moves, requiring more complex logistics, DOT compliance, and multi-day transit schedules.
How far in advance should I schedule a moving company?
It is generally recommended to schedule your moving company at least 4 to 6 weeks in advance, especially during the peak summer moving season. Early booking ensures you secure your preferred dates and allows ample time for inventory planning.
Are there items movers are legally not allowed to transport?
Yes. For safety and regulatory reasons, movers cannot transport hazardous materials, flammable liquids, explosives, or perishable foods. You will receive a complete list of restricted items during your initial consultation.
How is the cost of my move calculated?
Local moves are typically billed at an hourly rate based on the crew size needed. Long distance moves are generally calculated based on the total weight of your belongings and the mileage between origin and destination, along with any added packing services.
