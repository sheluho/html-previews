Frequently Asked Questions

What makes your custom furniture unique?

Every piece of bespoke furniture showcased through Metronym is built entirely from scratch by master craftsmen. Unlike mass-produced items, handmade furniture relies on traditional joinery techniques, premium hardwood selections, and meticulous hand-finishing to ensure durability and distinct character.

How long does a custom dining table take to build?

Because true craftsmanship takes time, a custom dining table typically requires between 8 to 12 weeks from the final design approval to completion. This timeline allows for proper wood acclimation, precision milling, detailed assembly, and the multi-step curing process required for a durable finish.

Can I request specific wood species for my bespoke furniture?

Absolutely. The artisans source a wide variety of premium domestic and exotic hardwoods, including Walnut, White Oak, Maple, and Cherry. During your consultation, you can discuss the specific visual characteristics and durability requirements for your custom furniture to select the perfect material.

Do you offer design consultations for custom sofas?

Yes, design consultations are a critical part of the bespoke seating process. You will work closely with the design team to determine the ideal dimensions, frame geometry, cushion fill, and upholstery fabric to ensure your custom sofa perfectly matches your interior space and comfort preferences.

How is the handmade furniture delivered?

All bespoke pieces are carefully blanket-wrapped and transported via specialized white-glove delivery services. This ensures that your custom dining table, sofa, or cabinetry arrives in pristine condition and is properly installed in the exact room of your choosing.
