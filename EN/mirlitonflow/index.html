Frequently Asked Questions

How often does my property require professional lawn mowing?

The ideal frequency for lawn mowing depends heavily on the season, weather conditions, and grass type. Generally, weekly mowing during peak growing seasons ensures the turf remains healthy and robust without causing stress to the root system.

What is included in a typical landscape installation?

Landscape installation encompasses everything from initial ground preparation and soil grading to the planting of shrubs, flowers, and turf. It may also involve installing hardscaping elements, mulch, and irrigation systems to complete the garden design.

When is the best time to schedule tree service?

While emergency tree service can be performed year-round, routine pruning and maintenance are typically best done during the late winter or early spring dormant season. This minimizes sap loss and protects the tree from pests and diseases.

Can garden design improve my property value?

Absolutely. Strategic garden design and well-maintained landscaping are proven to significantly boost curb appeal and overall property value. A cohesive outdoor aesthetic creates a strong first impression for visitors and potential buyers alike.

How long does it take to see results from professional lawn care?

Results vary based on the initial condition of the yard and the specific treatments applied. Weed control may show visible wilting within days, while comprehensive fertilization and seeding programs typically reveal a thicker, greener lawn over the course of several weeks to a few months.
