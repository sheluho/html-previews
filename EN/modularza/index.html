Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries regarding our catering promotional services.

What types of events are covered by the catering service?
The provider specializes in a wide array of gatherings, primarily focusing on comprehensive wedding catering, professional corporate catering, and customized special event catering for private parties and milestone celebrations.
Do you offer both buffet catering and plated dinner options?
Yes, clients have the flexibility to choose between various service styles. Whether you prefer the elegance of a formal plated dinner served course-by-course or the abundant variety of premium buffet catering, the menus can be tailored to your preference.
Can dietary restrictions and allergies be accommodated?
Absolutely. The culinary teams are highly experienced in designing custom menus that safely and deliciously accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other specific dietary requirements without compromising on taste or presentation.
How far in advance should I book wedding catering?
For wedding catering, it is highly recommended to secure your date at least 6 to 12 months in advance, especially if your event falls during peak wedding season. Early booking ensures availability and allows ample time for menu tastings and customization.
Does corporate catering include service staff and equipment?
Yes, full-service corporate catering options include professional, uniformed staff to manage setup, service, and cleanup. Necessary catering equipment, serving dishes, and presentation materials are also provided to ensure a seamless experience for your business event.
