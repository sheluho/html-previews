Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our premium car wash service.

Why is a hand car wash better than an automated wash?

Automated washes use harsh brushes and recycled water that can leave micro-scratches and swirl marks on your clear coat over time. A premium hand car wash uses clean, soft microfiber materials and targeted pressure to safely remove dirt without damaging the paint.

What does the auto wash membership include?

Our auto wash membership offers you the convenience of unlimited premium hand washes for a single monthly fee. It covers the complete exterior hand wash, wheel cleaning, and tire dressing, allowing you to maintain a spotless vehicle year-round.

How long does an express wash take?

An express wash is designed to be efficient without sacrificing quality. Generally, it takes between 20 to 30 minutes. This includes a thorough exterior hand wash, light interior vacuuming, and a quick wipe-down of all hard interior surfaces.

Do you use pH-balanced cleaning products?

Yes. We strictly use premium, pH-balanced soaps and conditioners. These specialized products effectively dissolve dirt and road grime without stripping existing wax or ceramic coatings from your vehicle's exterior.

Is it necessary to book an appointment?

While walk-ins are welcome for standard and express washes, we highly recommend booking an appointment online to guarantee your preferred time slot and minimize any waiting periods. Auto wash membership holders enjoy expedited priority scheduling.
