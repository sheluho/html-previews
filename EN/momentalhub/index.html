Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer both buffet catering and plated dinner options?

Yes, we excel in both styles. A plated dinner offers a traditional, elegant dining experience with precise portioning and elevated presentation. Our buffet catering provides a more relaxed, interactive atmosphere, allowing guests to sample a wider variety of seasonal dishes. We can help you decide which style best suits your venue and event flow.

How far in advance should we book our wedding catering?

For wedding catering, we recommend booking 9 to 12 months in advance, especially for dates during the peak summer and early fall seasons. This allows ample time for personalized menu design, tasting sessions, and seamless coordination with your chosen venue and other vendors.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions for event catering?

Absolutely. We pride ourselves on creating inclusive menus. Whether your guests require gluten-free, vegan, vegetarian, or nut-free options, our culinary team will craft delicious alternatives that rival the main courses in flavor and presentation.

What is included in your corporate catering packages?

Our corporate catering packages are fully customizable. They typically include menu planning, food preparation, professional delivery, and setup. Depending on your needs, we can also provide full-service staffing, elegant serving ware, and beverage stations to ensure your corporate event runs flawlessly.

Do you provide service staff and rentals?

Yes, we offer comprehensive event catering solutions. We can supply highly trained servers, bartenders, and kitchen staff to manage your event. Additionally, we can assist in coordinating necessary rentals such as linens, glassware, flatware, and plates through our trusted local partners.
