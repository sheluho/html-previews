Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about starting your kitchen renovation.

How long does a full kitchen remodeling project take?

Timelines vary based on the scope of work. A full turnkey kitchen remodel typically takes anywhere from 4 to 8 weeks after materials arrive. Smaller updates like installing new countertops or refacing cabinets can often be completed in less than a week.

Do you offer fully custom kitchen designs?

Yes. The contractors highlighted on our site specialize in creating a custom kitchen tailored entirely to your layout, functional requirements, and personal aesthetic preferences.

What types of countertops are most durable?

Quartz and granite are among the most durable materials for countertops. Quartz is engineered to be non-porous and highly resistant to stains and scratches, making it a popular choice for busy kitchens.

Can I keep my existing cabinets?

Absolutely. If your cabinet boxes are in good structural condition, cabinet refacing or repainting is a cost-effective way to update your kitchen's look without the expense of completely new custom cabinets.

What does a "turnkey kitchen" mean?

A turnkey kitchen project means the service provider handles every single aspect of the renovation. From initial 3D design and pulling necessary permits, to plumbing, electrical work, drywall, installing cabinets, and the final cleanup. You simply turn the key and start cooking.
