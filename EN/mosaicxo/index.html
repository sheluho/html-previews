Frequently Asked Questions

Insights into our detailing and protection processes.

What exactly is a ceramic coating?

Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer applied to your vehicle's exterior. It chemically bonds with the factory paint, creating a semi-permanent layer of protection. This layer repels water, dirt, and UV rays, making the car much easier to clean while enhancing its natural gloss.

How often should I get full auto detailing?

For a daily driven vehicle, we recommend a full auto detailing service at least two to three times a year. This regular maintenance removes deep-seated contaminants and ensures your interior materials and exterior paint remain in optimal condition.

Does interior detailing remove all stains and odors?

Our comprehensive interior detailing utilizes steam cleaning and specialized extraction methods that successfully remove the vast majority of stubborn stains and lingering odors. While some set-in chemical stains may be permanent, our process vastly improves the look and smell of any cabin.

What is included in your paint protection service?

Our paint protection service begins with a thorough wash and chemical decontamination, followed by paint correction to remove swirl marks and light scratches. Finally, we apply a high-grade sealant or coating to lock in the finish and shield it from environmental hazards.

Do I need to leave my car at your facility?

Yes, services like multi-stage paint correction and ceramic coating require a controlled environment to ensure the products bond correctly to the surface. Depending on the package chosen, we may need your vehicle for a full day or more to guarantee flawless results.
