Common Inquiries

What types of kitchen cabinets do you offer?

We specialize in a wide range of styles, including highly sought-after shaker cabinets and fully custom cabinets tailored to your specific dimensions, material choices, and aesthetic preferences.

Can I visit a kitchen showroom before making a decision?

Absolutely. Experiencing the materials and finishes in person is a crucial part of the kitchen design process. Our showroom features numerous displays to inspire your project and help you visualize the final result.

How long does it take to manufacture custom cabinets?

Production times vary based on the complexity of your kitchen design and the specific materials selected. During your initial consultation, we will provide a detailed and realistic timeline for your specific project.

Do you offer design assistance?

Yes, our team is dedicated to helping you achieve the perfect layout and look. We guide you through selecting the right kitchen cabinets and hardware to maximize both storage capacity and visual style.

Are shaker cabinets a good choice for a modern home?

Shaker cabinets are incredibly versatile. Their clean lines and simple profiles make them an excellent foundation for both traditional and modern kitchen designs, adapting beautifully depending on the hardware and finishes you choose.
