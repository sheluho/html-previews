Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our promoted house cleaning services.

What is included in a standard house cleaning?

A standard house cleaning typically includes dusting all surfaces, vacuuming carpets and rugs, sweeping and mopping hard floors, wiping down kitchen counters and appliance exteriors, and a full sanitization of bathrooms including toilets, sinks, and tubs. It's designed to maintain a high level of cleanliness on a regular basis.

Do the professionals bring their own cleaning supplies?

Yes, the maid service teams come fully equipped with their own vacuums, mops, and professional-grade, eco-friendly cleaning products. You do not need to provide any supplies unless you have a specific product you prefer them to use on delicate surfaces.

What is the difference between standard and deep cleaning?

Deep cleaning goes further than standard maintenance. It involves detailed work such as hand-wiping baseboards, cleaning the interior of windows, scrubbing grout lines, and cleaning inside appliances like the oven and refrigerator. Deep cleaning is recommended for first-time visits or an annual spring refresh.

How do I set up a recurring cleaning schedule?

Setting up a recurring cleaning is simple. You can fill out the contact form below to request an estimate. Once connected with the service provider, you can choose a frequency that fits your lifestyle—whether that is weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly—ensuring your home stays continually fresh.

Is the move-out cleaning guaranteed to satisfy my landlord?

A move-out cleaning is highly intensive, designed to tackle empty spaces, inside cabinets, and heavy buildup to prepare the home for new occupants. While the professionals execute a rigorous checklist designed to meet standard property management expectations, specific guarantee terms are handled directly by the service provider you book with.
