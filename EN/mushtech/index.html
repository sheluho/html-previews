Frequently Asked Questions

Common queries regarding HVAC, heating and cooling, and maintenance.

How often should I schedule HVAC maintenance?

It is highly recommended to have your heating and cooling system serviced at least twice a year. Schedule a check-up for your air conditioner in the spring and an inspection for your furnace in the fall to ensure optimal performance year-round.

What are the benefits of a heat pump over a traditional furnace?

A heat pump operates by transferring heat rather than generating it, making it extremely energy efficient. In moderate climates, it provides both heating and cooling from a single unit, which can significantly lower utility costs compared to running a separate AC and furnace.

How do I know if it's time for a new AC installation?

If your air conditioner is over 10-15 years old, requires frequent repairs, or struggles to keep your home cool despite running constantly, it might be time for a replacement. A new AC installation will provide better efficiency, reliability, and improved indoor air quality.

Do you service all brands of heating and air conditioning units?

Yes, the professional technicians we connect you with are highly trained to perform diagnostics, maintenance, and repairs on all major brands and models of HVAC equipment.

What should I check before calling for an emergency furnace repair?

Before placing a service call, verify that your thermostat is set to "Heat" and the temperature is set higher than the current room temperature. Check your electrical panel for a tripped breaker, and ensure your furnace filter isn't clogged, as a dirty filter can cause the system to shut down safely.
