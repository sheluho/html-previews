Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common inquiries about our cabinetry and design process.

What types of kitchen cabinets do you offer?

We provide a wide spectrum of options including fully custom cabinets, semi-custom solutions, and high-quality ready-to-assemble units. Our inventory covers various styles, featuring prominently our highly sought-after shaker cabinets.

Can I visit your kitchen showroom?

Yes, visiting our kitchen showroom is highly recommended. It allows you to experience the texture, finish, and build quality of our materials firsthand, which is crucial for making informed design decisions.

Do you provide full kitchen design services?

Absolutely. Our comprehensive kitchen design services cover everything from the initial layout and 3D modeling to the final selection of hardware and finishes, ensuring your new space is cohesive and functional.

How long does it take to get custom cabinets manufactured?

The timeline varies based on the complexity of the design and current manufacturing schedules. Typically, fully custom cabinets can take several weeks from final sign-off to delivery. We will provide a specific timeline during your consultation.

Are shaker cabinets a good choice for modern homes?

Yes, shaker cabinets are incredibly versatile. Their clean lines and lack of ornate detailing make them a perfect fit for contemporary, modern, and transitional home styles, while still offering robust durability.
