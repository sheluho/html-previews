Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical walk-in shower installation take?
A standard walk-in shower installation or bathtub replacement generally takes between 1 to 3 days. However, full bathroom remodels that involve extensive tile installation or plumbing relocation may require additional time. Your contractor will provide a precise timeline during the initial consultation.
Do you offer custom tile installation for the shower walls?
Yes, our affiliated professionals provide extensive tile options. From classic subway tiles to intricate mosaic accents and large format porcelain, expert tile installation is a core part of the bathroom renovation process to ensure a waterproof and beautiful finish.
Can I convert my existing bathtub into a walk-in shower?
Absolutely. Bathtub replacement and conversion is a specialty. The contractors safely remove your old tub, adjust the plumbing as needed, and install a custom walk-in shower base in the exact footprint of your previous bathtub.
Are your bathroom remodeling services focused on accessibility?
Yes, accessibility is a primary focus for many clients. We can connect you with experts who install low-threshold bases, strategically placed ADA-compliant grab bars, built-in benches, and non-slip flooring to ensure a safer bathing experience.
What is the process for starting a bathroom renovation?
It begins with submitting an inquiry through our contact form. You will be connected with a local bathroom remodeling specialist who will provide a free, no-obligation consultation to discuss your design preferences, budget, and structural needs before providing a comprehensive quote.
