Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in your standard lawn care program?

Our standard program includes seasonal fertilization, broadleaf weed control, and regular lawn mowing to ensure optimal growth. We tailor the specific applications based on your local soil conditions and grass type to maximize results.

Do you offer custom garden design and landscape installation?

Yes, we provide full-scale garden design and landscape installation services. From selecting the right native plants to laying new sod and installing hardscapes, our team manages the entire transformation from concept to completion.

How often should I schedule tree service?

We recommend an annual inspection for mature trees to check for disease and structural integrity. Routine pruning should generally be done every 1 to 3 years depending on the species to maintain health and safety.

Are your landscaping treatments safe for pets and children?

Yes, safety is a priority. We utilize approved, targeted applications for weed control and fertilization. We will provide specific instructions on when it is safe to resume normal activities on your lawn following any treatment.

When is the best time to start a lawn care regimen?

Early spring is the ideal time to begin a comprehensive lawn care program, as it prepares your grass for the growing season. However, our team can implement corrective landscaping strategies at any point in the year to improve your yard's health.
