Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about booking and working with a professional wedding photographer.

How far in advance should we book our wedding photographer?

It is highly recommended to secure your wedding photography services 9 to 12 months in advance. Popular dates during peak wedding seasons book up very quickly. Early booking ensures availability and allows ample time to plan your engagement photos.

Do you offer engagement photos as part of your packages?

Yes, many of the collections offered by JPP Studios include an engagement session. These sessions are a fantastic way for couples to become comfortable in front of the camera and to build a rapport with their photographer before the wedding day.

What is the approach to destination wedding photography?

Destination wedding photography requires careful logistical planning. The provider will work closely with you to arrange travel details and schedule timelines that take advantage of the unique lighting and scenery of your chosen location, ensuring a seamless experience abroad.

Can we schedule a session for bridal portraits before the wedding?

Absolutely. Bridal portraits are a wonderful tradition. Scheduling a session a few weeks before the wedding allows you a trial run of your hair and makeup, and provides dedicated time to capture stunning, editorial-style portraits without the rush of the wedding day schedule.

How and when will we receive our final images?

Following the event, the provider meticulously edits the collection. Final, high-resolution images are typically delivered via a secure online gallery within 6 to 8 weeks, allowing you to easily view, download, and share your memories with family and friends.
