Frequently Asked Questions

What is the process for designing custom closets?
The process typically begins with a comprehensive in-home or virtual consultation to measure your space and understand your storage needs. From there, designers create a 3D rendering of your proposed closet organizer. Once the design is approved, the custom components are manufactured and scheduled for professional installation.
Can you build a walk-in closet in a small room?
Absolutely. Even compact spaces can be transformed into highly efficient walk-in closets. By utilizing corner shelving, multi-level hanging rods, and custom-depth drawers, the design maximizes every available cubic inch to create a surprisingly spacious storage area.
What materials are used for your wardrobe systems?
The wardrobe systems featured through our partners are crafted from premium, commercial-grade materials. This includes thermally fused laminates, solid wood veneers, and heavy-duty metal hardware to ensure your custom closets stand the test of time and daily use.
How long does a closet organizer installation take?
Most reach-in closet and standard walk-in closet installations are completed within a single day. Larger, more complex custom wardrobes or multi-room projects may require additional days. The installation teams prioritize cleanliness and respect for your home throughout the process.
Do you design reach-in closets for kids' rooms?
Yes. Reach-in closets for children's rooms are highly popular. The best designs feature adjustable shelving and hanging rods that can be repositioned as your child grows, ensuring the closet organizer remains perfectly suited to their needs from toddlerhood through their teenage years.
