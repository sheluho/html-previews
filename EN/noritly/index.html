Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose between blinds and shades?

Custom blinds offer precise control over light direction by tilting the slats, making them great for privacy while still letting light in. Shades, such as roller shades, consist of a continuous piece of fabric that provides a clean look and excellent light-filtering or room-darkening capabilities. The choice depends on your functional needs and aesthetic preferences.

Are your plantation shutters custom made to fit?

Yes, the plantation shutters provided by the professionals we promote are completely custom-measured and built to the exact specifications of your windows. This ensures a flawless fit, maximum energy efficiency, and a seamless integrated look within your home's architecture.

Can I get motorized options for my window treatments?

Absolutely. Many modern window solutions, particularly roller shades and certain custom blinds, can be upgraded with motorization. This allows you to control your natural light with a remote, a smartphone app, or even integrate them into your smart home system for ultimate convenience.

What types of custom drapes are available?

The providers we connect you with offer a vast selection of fabrics, patterns, and opacities for custom drapes. Whether you need sheer drapes to soften the sunlight in a living room or heavy, lined blackout drapes for a bedroom, there are tailored options to meet your exact design vision.

How long does the design and installation process take?

Timelines vary based on the complexity of the order and the specific products chosen. Custom creations like plantation shutters generally take a bit longer to manufacture than standard roller shades. Once you submit a request, the service provider will give you an accurate timeline from the initial consultation and measurement to the final professional installation.
