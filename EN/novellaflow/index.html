Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to craft bespoke furniture?

Timelines vary depending on the complexity of the design and the availability of specific materials. Generally, a bespoke piece like a custom dining table or a custom sofa takes between 8 to 14 weeks from final design approval to delivery.

Can I select the exact wood slab for my custom dining table?

Yes. For live-edge and solid wood tables, clients are often involved in the slab selection process. The artisans source extraordinary, sustainably harvested woods, allowing you to choose the grain, color, and character that best suits your space.

Are custom sofas tailored to specific comfort preferences?

Absolutely. A true custom sofa is built from the frame up to accommodate your desired seat depth, back height, and foam density. You can specify whether you prefer a firm, supportive sit or a relaxed, sink-in feel, alongside your choice of premium upholstery.

Do you provide complete room designs or just individual pieces?

The makers we promote specialize in both. While they are renowned for statement pieces like a handmade furniture dining table, they frequently collaborate with architects and designers to create cohesive casegoods, built-ins, and seating arrangements for entire residential or commercial projects.

Where is the handmade furniture crafted?

The bespoke furniture featured through our promotional network is typically handcrafted in localized, dedicated workshops, such as Sentient Furniture's facilities in Brooklyn, New York. This ensures meticulous quality control and supports highly skilled domestic artisanship.
