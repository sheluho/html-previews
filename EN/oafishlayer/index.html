Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to create a piece of custom furniture?

The timeline for bespoke furniture varies depending on the complexity of the design, the materials selected, and the artisan's current schedule. Generally, you can expect a timeframe of 8 to 14 weeks from final design approval to delivery.

Can I provide my own fabric for a custom sofa?

Yes, many of the custom furniture makers we feature offer COM (Customer's Own Material) options for upholstery projects. You will need to consult regarding the required yardage and ensure the fabric is suitable for the intended use.

What types of wood are available for a custom dining table?

Artisans typically work with a variety of premium domestic and exotic hardwoods, including Walnut, White Oak, Cherry, Maple, and Ash. The choice of wood significantly influences both the aesthetic and durability of your handmade furniture.

Do you have handmade furniture ready to purchase immediately?

While our primary focus is on commissioned bespoke furniture, some associated showrooms may occasionally have floor models or limited-run pieces available for immediate acquisition. However, the core offering is made-to-order.

How do I begin the design process?

The process begins with an initial consultation to discuss your vision, space requirements, and functional needs. From there, preliminary sketches or renderings are created, materials are selected, and a formal proposal is presented before craftsmanship begins.
