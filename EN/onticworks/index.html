Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about our wedding photography process.

How far in advance should we book our wedding photographer?

We recommend securing your date at least 9 to 12 months in advance, especially for peak wedding seasons. Our schedule fills up quickly, and early booking ensures we are available to dedicate our full attention to your special day.

Do you offer destination wedding photography?

Yes! We love traveling to capture love stories worldwide. Our destination wedding photography packages are custom-built to cover travel, accommodation, and multi-day events like welcome dinners and farewell brunches.

Are engagement photos included in your packages?

Most of our comprehensive wedding photography collections include a complimentary engagement session. We highly recommend doing one, as it helps you get comfortable in front of the camera and allows us to understand your dynamic as a couple before the wedding.

Can we schedule standalone bridal portraits?

Absolutely. We offer dedicated sessions for bridal portraits either before or after your wedding day. This gives you a relaxed environment to capture stunning, editorial-style images without the time constraints of the actual wedding day timeline.

When will we receive our final photo gallery?

We typically deliver a sneak peek gallery within a few days of the wedding. The complete, fully edited digital gallery of your wedding photography will be delivered within 6 to 8 weeks, depending on the season and the scope of the event.
