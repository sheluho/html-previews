Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about preparing for your wedding photography experience.

Do you offer destination wedding photography?

Yes, our featured wedding photographer travels worldwide to capture your celebration wherever it may be. We can help arrange customized travel packages depending on your location.

When should we book our engagement photos?

We recommend scheduling your engagement photos 6 to 8 months prior to your wedding date. This timeline provides ample opportunity to use the images for your save-the-date cards and wedding website.

Are bridal portraits included in the packages?

Depending on the specific collection you choose, dedicated bridal portraits can be seamlessly integrated into your photography timeline, either on the wedding day or during a separate session.

How long does it take to receive our wedding gallery?

Most couples receive their fully edited, high-resolution wedding photography gallery within 6 to 8 weeks after their event. A smaller "sneak peek" gallery is often provided within the first week.

Can we customize our wedding photography coverage?

Absolutely. Every love story is entirely unique, and custom packages can be tailored to perfectly fit your specific timeline, aesthetic preferences, and coverage needs.
