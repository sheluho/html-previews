Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I have my HVAC system serviced?

It is highly recommended to have your heating and cooling system serviced at least twice a year. Schedule an air conditioner tune-up in the spring and a furnace inspection in the fall to ensure optimal performance.

What are the signs I need a new air conditioner?

If your AC unit is over 10-15 years old, requires frequent repairs, struggles to maintain comfortable temperatures, or causes unexpected spikes in your energy bills, it might be time for a professional AC installation.

Can a heat pump replace my traditional furnace and AC?

Yes. A heat pump acts as a two-in-one system, providing both heating and cooling. In moderate climates, it is an incredibly energy-efficient alternative to running a separate furnace and air conditioner.

How do I improve my home's indoor air quality?

Start by regularly changing your HVAC filters every 1 to 3 months. Additionally, professionals can install air purifiers, humidifiers, or UV lamps directly into your existing heating and cooling system to drastically reduce airborne pollutants.

Do the promoted contractors offer emergency repairs?

Many of the HVAC partners we connect you with offer emergency heating and cooling services. When your furnace quits on a freezing night or your AC fails in a heatwave, prompt assistance is available.
