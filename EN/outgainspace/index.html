Frequently Asked Questions

How would you describe your wedding videography style?

Our style is highly cinematic, documentary-driven, and editorial. We avoid overly staged or cheesy setups. Instead, we act as quiet observers, capturing the natural flow of your day to create a sophisticated and emotionally resonant wedding film.

Do you provide raw footage of the event?

While our primary focus is delivering a polished, perfectly edited cinematic wedding video, we do offer options to purchase a hard drive containing unedited, raw footage from the day for archival purposes. This can be added to any collection.

How long does it take to receive our wedding film?

The editing process for a premium cinematic film requires meticulous attention to detail, color grading, and audio mixing. Typically, our couples receive their final highlight films and documentary edits within 12 to 16 weeks following the event date.

Do you travel for destination weddings?

Absolutely. As an experienced event videography company, we frequently travel for destination celebrations. Custom travel quotes are provided based on the location, covering flights, accommodation, and necessary local transport.

How do you handle music selection for the video?

Music is the heartbeat of a great film. We license high-quality, cinematic tracks from premium libraries to ensure your video can be shared legally online. We select music that perfectly matches the pacing and emotional tone of your specific wedding day.
