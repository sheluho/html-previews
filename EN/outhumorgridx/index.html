Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about exterior improvements and energy upgrades.

What are the distinct benefits of spray foam over traditional home insulation?
Spray foam creates an airtight seal that traditional fiberglass batts simply cannot achieve. It expands into gaps and cracks, significantly reducing drafts and moisture penetration. This superior seal leads to much higher energy efficiency, lower heating and cooling bills, and better structural support for your walls.
How long does a vinyl siding installation typically take?
For an average-sized home, professional siding contractor teams can usually complete a full vinyl siding installation in roughly one to two weeks, depending on weather conditions and the complexity of the home's architecture. The process involves removing old materials, preparing the exterior wall, adding a weather barrier, and placing the new siding panels.
Does fiber cement siding require a lot of maintenance?
No, fiber cement siding is highly regarded for its low-maintenance properties. It resists rot, termites, and warping. Unlike natural wood, it does not require frequent scraping or painting, though giving it a gentle wash once a year will help preserve its premium appearance and keep the factory finish looking sharp.
How do I know if my house needs new attic insulation?
Key signs that your attic insulation needs upgrading include unexpectedly high energy bills, inconsistent temperatures between rooms, noticeable drafts, and the rapid melting of snow on your roof during winter (which can cause ice dams). If you look into your attic and the insulation level is below or just level with the floor joists, it's time to add more.
Are your house siding options resistant to severe weather?
Yes. Modern house siding materials, specifically premium vinyl siding and rigid fiber cement siding, are engineered to withstand high winds, heavy rain, and extreme temperature fluctuations. They form a resilient outer grid that protects your home's structural framing from moisture damage year-round.
