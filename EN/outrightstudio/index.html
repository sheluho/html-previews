Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical bathroom remodel take?

The timeline varies depending on the scope of the project. A standard bathtub replacement or walk-in shower conversion can often be completed in just a few days, whereas a full bathroom renovation involving structural changes and custom tile installation may take a few weeks.

Do you handle the tile installation and plumbing updates?

Yes. Outright Studio coordinates all aspects of the design, and our trusted installation partners handle the technical work, including professional tile installation, plumbing adjustments, and fixture upgrades necessary for your complete bathroom remodel.

Can I convert my existing tub into a walk-in shower?

Absolutely. Tub-to-shower conversions are one of our most popular services. We carefully remove the old tub and install a beautiful, accessible walk-in shower customized to fit the exact footprint of your previous bathtub.

What is included in a full bathtub replacement?

A standard bathtub replacement includes the removal and disposal of your old unit, preparation of the underlying area, installation of the new tub, and updating the immediate wall surrounds or fixtures as specified in your design plan.

How do I start planning my bathroom renovation with Outright Studio?

Simply fill out the contact form on this page. We will review your initial details and reach out to schedule a consultation. During this consultation, we'll discuss your vision, evaluate your current space, and map out the next steps for your bathroom remodel.
