Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our paint protection and car detailing services.

What exactly is a ceramic coating?

A ceramic coating is a liquid polymer applied to the exterior surfaces of a vehicle. It chemically bonds with the factory paint, creating a semi-permanent layer of protection that repels water, resists chemical stains, and provides a brilliant, long-lasting shine.

How long does paint protection last?

The longevity of paint protection varies depending on the specific package chosen and how the vehicle is maintained. High-quality ceramic coatings typically last anywhere from two to five years, drastically outperforming traditional waxes and sealants.

What is included in full auto detailing?

Full auto detailing typically encompasses both a meticulous exterior wash and decontamination, alongside deep interior detailing. This includes vacuuming, upholstery cleaning, leather conditioning, window cleaning, and dressing all interior plastics.

Do I need paint correction before a ceramic coating?

Yes, it is highly recommended. Because a ceramic coating seals the paint beneath a hard, transparent layer, any existing swirls, scratches, or imperfections will be locked in. Paint correction ensures the surface is flawless before the protective barrier is applied.

How do I maintain my car after interior detailing?

Maintaining your interior detailing involves regular vacuuming to remove loose debris and promptly wiping down surfaces with a gentle, non-abrasive cleaner. Using sunshades can also help protect conditioned leather and plastics from UV degradation.
