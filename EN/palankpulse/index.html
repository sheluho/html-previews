Frequently Asked Questions

Insights on selecting the perfect window treatments.

What are the main benefits of installing plantation shutters?

Plantation shutters offer excellent light control, superior insulation against heat and cold, and a timeless aesthetic that can actually increase the value of your home. They are highly durable and easy to maintain compared to fabric options.

How do I choose between traditional blinds and roller shades?

Blinds feature slatted louvers that can be tilted to direct light while maintaining privacy, making them highly adjustable. Roller shades consist of a single piece of fabric that rolls up, offering a cleaner, more minimalist profile and options ranging from sheer to complete blackout.

Can custom drapes and shades be motorized?

Yes, modern window treatments, including custom drapes, roller shades, and even certain types of blinds, can be equipped with motorization. This allows you to control natural light with the push of a button or integrate them into smart home systems for automated scheduling.

What are the best window treatments for energy efficiency?

Cellular shades (honeycomb shades) are generally considered the most energy-efficient due to their trapped air pockets that insulate windows. Plantation shutters and heavy custom drapes with thermal linings also provide significant insulation benefits.

Why is professional measurement important for custom window treatments?

Windows are rarely perfectly square. Professional measurement ensures that your custom blinds, shades, or shutters fit precisely within the frame, eliminating light gaps, ensuring smooth operation, and providing a flawless, built-in appearance.
