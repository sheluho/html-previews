Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our event catering services.

Do you offer full-service wedding catering?

Yes, our partners specialize in comprehensive wedding catering. This includes menu design, food preparation, staffing, and day-of execution to ensure your celebration goes off without a hitch.

What is the difference between buffet catering and a plated dinner?

Buffet catering allows guests to serve themselves from a variety of stations, which is great for a more relaxed, social atmosphere. A plated dinner is a more formal, sit-down experience where courses are served directly to guests at their tables.

Can you handle corporate catering for large-scale events?

Absolutely. Corporate catering is a core expertise. Whether you are hosting a 50-person seminar or a 500-guest gala, our affiliated culinary teams have the logistics and experience to manage large crowds efficiently.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes. Any event catering menu can be customized to accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and allergy-specific requirements so that all your guests enjoy a safe and delicious meal.

How far in advance should I book my event catering?

For wedding catering, we recommend reaching out 9 to 12 months in advance. For corporate and social events, 3 to 6 months is typically sufficient, though we encourage booking as early as possible to secure your preferred date.
