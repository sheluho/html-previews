Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in your tire storage service?
Our promoted tire storage includes secure, climate-controlled warehousing for your off-season tires. We handle the heavy lifting, ensuring your tires are kept in optimal conditions to prevent dry rot and degradation.
How often should I schedule a tire rotation?
Generally, a tire rotation is recommended every 5,000 to 8,000 miles, or in alignment with your vehicle manufacturer's guidelines. Regular rotation ensures even tread wear, extending the overall lifespan of your tires.
Do you offer complete tire replacement?
Yes, the providers we promote offer comprehensive tire replacement services. This includes helping you select the right tires for your vehicle, followed by professional mounting, balancing, and tire installation.
Why is wheel alignment important after new tire installation?
Proper wheel alignment ensures your new tires meet the road at the correct angle. This prevents premature, uneven wear and ensures your vehicle handles safely and predictably, protecting your investment in new tires.
Can I drop off my tires for storage anytime?
Drop-offs for tire storage are typically coordinated with your seasonal tire installation appointment. It is recommended to schedule ahead to ensure space and seamless service when transitioning between summer and winter sets.
