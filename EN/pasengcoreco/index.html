Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our landscaping and lawn care services.

How often should I schedule lawn mowing?

For optimal health and appearance, we recommend weekly lawn mowing during peak growing seasons (spring and early summer). During slower growth periods, bi-weekly service is usually sufficient to maintain a pristine look without over-stressing the grass.

Do you offer custom garden design consultations?

Yes, our partners specialize in custom garden design. The process begins with a comprehensive assessment of your outdoor space, sunlight exposure, and soil quality, followed by a collaborative design phase to ensure the final landscape aligns with your vision.

What does landscape installation typically involve?

Landscape installation covers everything from initial site preparation and soil grading to the strategic planting of trees, shrubs, and flowers. It may also include laying sod, installing mulch or decorative stone, and setting up necessary irrigation to support new growth.

Is professional tree service necessary for my property?

Absolutely. Regular tree service, including careful pruning and health inspections, is vital for preventing disease, encouraging strong structural growth, and mitigating safety hazards caused by dead or overhanging branches near your home.

When is the best time of year to start landscaping projects?

Spring and early fall are generally the best times for major landscape installation and planting. The moderate temperatures and natural moisture during these seasons give new plants, trees, and grass the best opportunity to establish strong root systems before extreme weather hits.
