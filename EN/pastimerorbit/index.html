Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our recommended tire care services.

How does the seasonal tire storage work?

When you swap your summer or winter tires, the partnered facility cleans, tags, and securely stores your off-season set in a climate-controlled warehouse until you are ready for your next tire installation.

How often should I schedule a tire rotation?

Generally, a tire rotation is recommended every 5,000 to 8,000 miles. Rotating your tires regularly ensures even tread wear and extends the overall life of the set.

Do you provide full tire replacement services?

Yes, our recommended partners carry a wide range of premium brands for complete tire replacement. They can handle mounting, balancing, and proper disposal of your old tires.

Is a wheel alignment necessary when buying new tires?

It is highly recommended. A proper wheel alignment protects your investment by ensuring the new tires do not wear out prematurely due to suspension misalignment.
