Frequently Asked Questions

Are packing services included in the move? ▼

Packing services are available as an add-on to your residential move. The moving company offers comprehensive packing and unpacking options to save you time and protect your fragile items.

What is the difference between local movers and long distance movers? ▼

Local movers typically handle relocations within the same city or a short radius, usually billed by the hour. Long distance movers manage interstate or cross-country moves, which require specialized logistics and are usually billed by weight and distance.

How far in advance should I book my moving company? ▼

We recommend booking your residential moving company at least 4 to 6 weeks in advance, especially during the busy summer months. This ensures you secure your preferred date and gives you ample time to prepare.

Do the movers dismantle and reassemble furniture? ▼

Yes, most standard residential moves include basic dismantling of large furniture (like beds) and reassembling them at your new destination. Please verify specific oversized items with the provider beforehand.

Is my property insured during the transition? ▼

The third-party moving company provides basic liability coverage as required by law. Full-value protection or additional insurance can usually be purchased directly from the provider for total peace of mind.
