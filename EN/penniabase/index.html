Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about residential moving company services.

What exactly is included in professional packing services?
Comprehensive packing services typically include all necessary materials like boxes, tape, and bubble wrap. The moving company's team will carefully wrap fragile items, box up your belongings room by room, and label everything clearly to make unpacking at your new residential property as easy as possible.
How far in advance should I book local movers?
For local movers, it is highly recommended to book at least 2 to 4 weeks in advance. During peak moving seasons, such as summer months or weekends, securing your moving company even earlier ensures you get your preferred date and time.
Do long distance movers provide tracking for my belongings?
Yes, most premium long distance movers offer tracking systems or dedicated dispatch coordinators. This allows you to stay updated on the location of your moving truck and the estimated time of arrival at your new destination.
What makes a great residential moving company?
A great residential moving company is characterized by transparent pricing, professional and trained staff, proper licensing, and excellent communication. They prioritize the safety of your items and offer flexible solutions like specialized packing services to meet your unique needs.
Are moving supplies included in the standard quote?
Basic moving equipment like dollies and blankets to protect furniture are typically included. However, consumable packing supplies (boxes, tape, paper) are usually billed separately unless you have specifically requested comprehensive packing services in your quote.
