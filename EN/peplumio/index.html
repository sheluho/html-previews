Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about booking your wedding photographer.

How far in advance should we book a wedding photographer?
It is generally recommended to secure your wedding photographer 9 to 12 months in advance, especially if you are planning a celebration during peak wedding season. Popular dates book quickly, so reaching out early ensures you have the best chance of securing your preferred artist for your special day.
Are engagement photos included in standard packages?
Many comprehensive collections do include engagement sessions, as they are a fantastic way for you to get comfortable in front of the camera and build a rapport with your photographer before the wedding. You can discuss specific package inclusions directly with the studio during your initial consultation.
Can we schedule a separate session for bridal portraits?
Absolutely. Dedicated bridal portraits are highly encouraged. Scheduling a session before the wedding day allows for a relaxed environment where you can capture elegant, timeless portraits without the time constraints or rush of the actual wedding day schedule.
Do you accommodate destination wedding photography?
Yes, destination wedding photography is a specialized service offered by the studio. Whether you are hosting an intimate elopement abroad or a large international celebration, experienced photographers are available to travel and document your event with the same high standard of artistry.
How long does it take to receive the final image gallery?
The editing process is meticulous to ensure every image meets professional standards. Typically, couples can expect to receive their fully edited, high-resolution digital gallery within 6 to 8 weeks following the wedding date. A small preview gallery is often provided within the first few days.
