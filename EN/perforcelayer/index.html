Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer both buffet catering and plated dinner options?
Yes, the original provider specializes in both formats. Whether you prefer the elegance of a formal plated dinner or the variety and social atmosphere of buffet catering, menus can be completely customized to fit your vision.
How far in advance should I book my wedding catering?
For wedding catering, it is highly recommended to secure your date at least 6 to 12 months in advance. Peak wedding seasons book up quickly, and early scheduling ensures ample time for menu tastings and refinements.
Can you accommodate dietary restrictions for event catering?
Absolutely. Accommodating dietary needs—including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and allergy-specific requests—is a top priority in all event catering services to ensure every guest enjoys a safe and delicious meal.
What is included in corporate catering packages?
Corporate catering packages typically include the food preparation, delivery, professional setup, and essential serving equipment. Additional services such as staffing, beverages, and cleanup can be arranged based on the scale of your event.
Do you handle large-scale events?
Yes. The catering teams are fully equipped to handle events of all sizes, from intimate private dinners to large corporate galas and expansive outdoor weddings, providing consistent quality and service regardless of the guest count.
