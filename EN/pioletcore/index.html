Frequently Asked Questions

What is the process for ordering custom cabinets?

The process begins with a comprehensive kitchen design consultation. We discuss your layout, functional needs, and aesthetic preferences. From there, precise measurements are taken, and our partners manufacture your custom cabinets to those exact specifications before final delivery.

Do you have a kitchen showroom I can visit?

Yes, as a promotional partner for premium manufacturers like Kountry Kraft, we encourage clients to visit physical kitchen showrooms to experience the quality, feel the wood textures, and see various shaker cabinets and modern styles in person.

What are shaker cabinets?

Shaker cabinets are a highly popular style of cabinetry characterized by a five-piece door with a recessed center panel. Their clean, simple lines make them incredibly versatile, allowing them to fit perfectly into both traditional and contemporary kitchen designs.

How long does kitchen design and manufacturing take?

Because true custom cabinets are built from scratch specifically for your home, timelines can vary. Typically, the kitchen design phase takes a few weeks, followed by 8 to 12 weeks for manufacturing, depending on the complexity of the order and current material availability.

Do you offer hardware with your kitchen cabinets?

Absolutely. Selecting the right knobs, pulls, and hinges is a crucial part of kitchen design. Our showroom consultations cover a wide array of premium hardware options that complement both modern slabs and classic shaker cabinets.
