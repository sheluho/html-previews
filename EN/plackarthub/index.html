Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our custom furniture process.

What is the typical lead time for custom furniture?

Because every piece of our bespoke furniture is meticulously crafted to order, standard lead times generally range from 8 to 12 weeks. This allows us to source the finest materials and dedicate the required hours to ensure your custom sofa or table meets our rigorous quality standards.

Can I choose the specific wood for my custom dining table?

Absolutely. We offer a wide variety of premium and reclaimed woods, including walnut, white oak, maple, and rare historical timbers. During your consultation at our custom furniture maker showroom, we will guide you through our material samples to find the perfect grain and finish for your space.

How do I start the design process for a custom sofa?

The process begins with a conversation. You can reach out via our contact form or visit our showroom. We'll discuss your dimensions, comfort preferences, and fabric desires. From there, our team will provide sketches and fabric swatches before finalizing the build of your handmade furniture.

Do you design bespoke furniture for commercial spaces?

Yes, we frequently collaborate with businesses, architects, and interior designers to create stunning commercial environments. Whether you need a massive boardroom table or customized reception seating, our custom furniture maker capabilities scale to meet commercial demands.

Is all of your handmade furniture crafted locally?

Yes, we take immense pride in our local craftsmanship. Every piece of bespoke furniture is built by our dedicated team of artisans in our own workshop. This allows us to maintain strict quality control and ensure every detail aligns perfectly with your vision.
