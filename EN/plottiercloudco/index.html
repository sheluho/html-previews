Frequently Asked Questions

Answers about your upcoming bathroom renovation.

How long does a walk-in shower installation take?

Most walk-in shower installations are highly efficient and can typically be completed in as little as one to two days. This minimizes disruption to your home while still ensuring a high-quality, watertight finish.

Do you offer custom tile installation?

Yes, custom tile installation is a core part of a comprehensive bathroom remodel. Expert installers handle a variety of premium materials to create beautiful, durable wall surrounds and slip-resistant flooring.

Is a bathtub replacement a messy process?

Professional installation teams prioritize cleanliness and respect for your home. Drop cloths are used, and debris is promptly removed, making the bathtub replacement process as clean and unobtrusive as possible.

What is included in a full bathroom remodel?

A full bathroom remodel can include everything from a new walk-in shower or bathtub replacement to updated vanities, modern fixtures, upgraded lighting, and comprehensive tile installation for both walls and floors.

Are your bathroom renovation materials waterproof and mold-resistant?

Absolutely. The materials utilized for wet areas are specifically engineered to be non-porous, highly waterproof, and naturally resistant to mold and mildew, ensuring a safe and easily maintainable environment.
