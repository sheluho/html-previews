Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should we book a wedding photographer?

It is highly recommended to secure your wedding photographer 9 to 12 months in advance, especially if you are planning a celebration during the peak wedding season. Early booking guarantees availability and allows ample time to plan engagement photos.

Do you provide standalone engagement photos?

Yes, engagement photos can be booked as a standalone session. However, many couples choose to bundle them with comprehensive wedding photography packages to build a rapport with the photographer before the actual wedding day.

What exactly happens during bridal portraits?

Bridal portraits are typically scheduled a month or two before the wedding. It is a dedicated session where the bride dresses in her full wedding attire, allowing for stunning, stress-free photographs in a controlled environment without the time constraints of the wedding day.

Do you travel for destination wedding photography?

Absolutely. Destination wedding photography is a major focus. Travel logistics and accommodations are coordinated well in advance to ensure the photographer is on-site and prepared to document your international celebration seamlessly.

How long does it take to receive our final wedding gallery?

Post-production is a meticulous process to ensure every image meets high editorial standards. Generally, you can expect to receive your fully edited, high-resolution digital gallery within 6 to 8 weeks following your wedding date.
