Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my wedding catering?

For peak wedding season, we recommend securing your wedding catering service at least 9 to 12 months in advance. This ensures availability and provides ample time to curate and perfect your customized menu through tasting sessions.

Do you offer options for dietary restrictions?

Absolutely. The event catering professionals we recommend are highly skilled at accommodating various dietary needs, including vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, and allergy-specific requirements. These can be seamlessly integrated into both buffet catering and plated dinner menus.

What is the difference between buffet catering and a plated dinner?

A plated dinner is a formal service where guests remain seated and are served predetermined courses by waitstaff, ideal for elegant events. Buffet catering allows guests to serve themselves from a variety of stations, encouraging mingling and offering a wider array of choices in a more relaxed setting.

Can you handle large-scale corporate catering events?

Yes, our partners are fully equipped for extensive corporate catering needs. Whether it's a multi-day conference requiring breakfast, lunch, and snack breaks, or a large annual gala, the logistical expertise is in place to deliver hot, fresh food efficiently to hundreds of guests.

Are staff and equipment included in the catering service?

Most full-service event catering packages include the necessary culinary staff, servers, and basic serving equipment. Specific rentals like fine china, specialized linens, or custom decor for a plated dinner may require additional coordination, which your designated caterer will discuss during planning.
