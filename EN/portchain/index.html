Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a standard windshield replacement take?

A professional windshield replacement typically takes between one to two hours to complete. Following the installation, there is a mandatory safe drive-away time of about one hour to allow the urethane adhesive to cure securely before the vehicle is driven.

Can you repair my windshield, or does it require a full replacement?

It depends on the size and location of the damage. Generally, if the chip or crack is smaller than a quarter and is not directly in the driver's line of sight, a windshield repair is possible. Larger cracks or edge damage usually necessitate a full replacement for safety reasons.

Do you handle side and rear car window replacement?

Yes, we provide comprehensive auto glass services. We can perform a car window replacement for driver and passenger side doors, quarter glass, and rear windshields on virtually all makes and models.

Is chip repair a permanent fix?

Yes, a professional chip repair restores the structural integrity of the glass and prevents the damage from spreading further. While a slight blemish may remain visible where the impact occurred, the glass will be strong and secure.

What should I do immediately after my glass is damaged?

If you notice a chip or crack, keep the area clean and dry. Avoid running the defroster on high heat or blasting the air conditioning, as sudden temperature changes can cause the crack to spread rapidly. Contact us as soon as possible to evaluate whether a repair or replacement is needed.
