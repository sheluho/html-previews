Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our custom closet solutions.

What is the process for getting custom closets designed?

The process begins with an initial consultation to measure your space and discuss your storage needs. Following that, a 3D design is created to visualize the new custom closets. Once approved, the materials are manufactured and professional installation is scheduled.

Can you design a walk-in closet for an unusually shaped room?

Absolutely. Custom walk-in closet systems are specifically designed to accommodate difficult angles, sloped ceilings, and irregular floor plans, maximizing every available inch of your space.

Are your closet organizer systems adjustable?

Yes, many of the closet organizer systems feature adjustable shelving and hanging rods. This flexibility allows you to reconfigure your wardrobe layout as your seasonal clothing or storage needs change over time.

Do you provide solutions for small reach-in closets?

Yes, reach-in closet transformations are a specialty. By utilizing double-hang sections, built-in drawers, and specialized shelving, a standard reach-in closet can hold significantly more than a traditional single-rod setup.

How long does it take to install a new wardrobe system?

Most standard custom closets and wardrobe installations are completed within a single day. Larger, highly detailed walk-in closets or multi-room projects may require additional time, which is always communicated upfront.
