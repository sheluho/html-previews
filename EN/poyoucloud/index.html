Frequently Asked Questions

What types of kitchen cabinets do you showcase?
We feature a wide range of styles through our manufacturing partners, from highly sought-after shaker cabinets to ultra-modern, flat-panel designs. All options are premium custom cabinets built to last.
Can I visit a kitchen showroom before deciding?
Absolutely. We highly recommend visiting a dedicated kitchen showroom to view the craftsmanship, test the hardware, and see the finishes in person before finalizing your kitchen design.
Are shaker cabinets a good choice for modern homes?
Yes, shaker cabinets are incredibly versatile. Their clean lines and timeless profile allow them to blend seamlessly into both traditional and highly contemporary kitchen design layouts.
Do you offer custom cabinets for unusual room shapes?
Yes. The primary advantage of custom cabinets is their ability to be manufactured to the exact specifications of your space, no matter how unique or challenging the room's dimensions may be.
How does the kitchen design process work?
The kitchen design process begins with a consultation to understand your needs. From there, experts draft precise layouts, help you select materials in the kitchen showroom, and manage the technical specifications for manufacturing.
