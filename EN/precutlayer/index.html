Frequently Asked Questions

Why should we hire a full-service wedding planner?

A full-service wedding planner saves you hundreds of hours by managing every logistical and design element. We leverage industry relationships to secure the best vendors, keep your budget on track, and ensure a cohesive design, allowing you to enjoy your engagement without the stress of project management.

What exactly does a day of coordinator do?

A day of coordinator (often starting 4-6 weeks out) takes over the plans you've already made. We review vendor contracts, create a minute-by-minute timeline, run your rehearsal, and manage all on-site logistics on the wedding day so you don't have to answer questions or solve problems while in your wedding attire.

Do you handle destination wedding planning?

Yes, we specialize in destination wedding planning. We manage the complex logistics of planning from afar, including scouting international or out-of-state venues, coordinating guest travel and accommodations, and vetting local vendors to ensure high standards are met wherever you choose to celebrate.

How far in advance should we book a wedding coordinator?

For full-service planning, we recommend booking 12 to 18 months in advance to ensure the best selection of venues and vendors. If you are hiring us strictly as a day of coordinator, reaching out 6 to 9 months prior to your date is ideal to secure our availability.

Can you help us stay within our wedding budget?

Absolutely. Budget management is a core component of our full-service wedding planning. We help you establish realistic expectations, allocate funds strategically according to your priorities, and track expenditures throughout the entire process to prevent costly surprises.
