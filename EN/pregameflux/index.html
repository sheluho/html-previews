Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take for carpets to dry after steam cleaning?

Drying times can vary depending on humidity, airflow, and the type of carpet. However, professional steam cleaning typically utilizes powerful extraction equipment that removes the majority of the moisture. In most residential settings, carpets will be dry to the touch and ready for normal foot traffic within 4 to 6 hours.

Are the cleaning solutions used safe for pets and children?

Yes, the providers we promote prioritize the use of environmentally friendly and non-toxic cleaning agents. These solutions are formulated to be tough on dirt and stains while remaining completely safe for households with children and pets once the cleaning process is complete and the area has dried.

Can professional cleaning remove old or set-in stains?

While no guarantee can be made for every single mark, advanced stain removal techniques are highly effective against a wide variety of stubborn blemishes, including wine, coffee, ink, and pet accidents. Technicians assess the fabric and the nature of the stain to apply targeted treatments that yield the best possible results without damaging the material.

How often should I schedule professional upholstery cleaning?

For standard household usage, it is generally recommended to have your upholstery professionally cleaned every 12 to 24 months. However, if you have a busy home with indoor pets, young children, or allergy sufferers, scheduling a deep cleaning every 6 to 12 months can help maintain optimal indoor air quality and fabric longevity.

Do you handle delicate area rug cleaning?

Absolutely. Rug cleaning requires a specialized approach, especially for oriental, Persian, or antique rugs made from sensitive natural fibers like wool or silk. The professionals utilize gentle, pH-balanced solutions and controlled drying processes to ensure the structural integrity and color vibrancy of your valuable textiles are preserved.
