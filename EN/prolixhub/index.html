Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our door installation services.

What is the best material for entry doors?

For most climates, fiberglass doors are considered the superior choice. They offer the aesthetic appeal of traditional wood but are highly resistant to warping, rotting, and denting. They also provide excellent insulation, making them highly energy-efficient.

How long does a typical door installation take?

A standard front door replacement usually takes about half a day to complete. This includes removing the old frame, preparing the opening, installing the new unit, ensuring it is perfectly plumb and weather-sealed, and cleaning up the workspace.

Can I replace interior doors without changing the frame?

Yes, if your existing door frames are square and in good structural condition, we can perform a "slab replacement." This involves custom-fitting new interior doors to your existing frames, which is often a more streamlined and cost-effective approach.

Are new entry doors energy efficient?

Absolutely. Modern entry doors, especially premium fiberglass models, feature advanced insulating cores and high-quality weatherstripping. Proper door installation eliminates drafts and drastically reduces heat transfer, which can lower your monthly energy bills.

Do you offer custom sizing for front door replacement?

Yes, we provide tailored solutions for homes with non-standard entryways. During the initial measurement phase, the exact dimensions are recorded to ensure your new custom entry door fits flawlessly into your existing architecture.
