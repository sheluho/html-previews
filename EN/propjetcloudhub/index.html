Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our premium vehicle care promotions.

What is included in a premium car wash?

A premium car wash typically includes a thorough exterior wash, wheel cleaning, tire dressing, undercarriage flush, and an application of protective wax or sealant to maintain your vehicle's shine and protect its paint.

How does the auto wash membership work?

An auto wash membership offers you unlimited washes for a flat monthly fee. It provides excellent value for drivers who want to keep their vehicle looking pristine year-round without paying per visit.

Is a hand car wash better than an automated one?

Yes, a hand car wash provides meticulous, personalized attention to your vehicle. It is generally gentler on the paint and allows specialists to target stubborn grime that automated brushes might miss.

How long does an express wash take?

Our express wash is designed for speed and efficiency, typically taking only 5 to 10 minutes. It is perfect for maintaining your vehicle's appearance when you are on a tight schedule.
