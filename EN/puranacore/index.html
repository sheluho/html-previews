Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about our detailing and protection services.

What exactly is a ceramic coating?

A ceramic coating is a liquid polymer applied to your vehicle's exterior. It chemically bonds with the factory paint, creating a semi-permanent layer of protection and a highly hydrophobic surface that makes cleaning much easier.

How long does paint protection last?

Depending on the specific grade of ceramic coating applied and how well the vehicle is maintained, it can provide durable paint protection lasting anywhere from two to five years or even longer.

What does interior detailing include?

Comprehensive interior detailing involves a deep clean of the entire cabin. This typically includes thorough vacuuming, steam cleaning of carpets and upholstery, leather conditioning, and careful wiping and protection of all hard surfaces.

How often should I get car detailing done?

For optimal maintenance and to preserve the value of your vehicle, thorough auto detailing is generally recommended two to three times a year. This should be supplemented by regular, careful hand washes.

Is ceramic coating better than traditional wax?

Yes, for long-term care. While traditional carnauba wax provides a warm shine, it only lasts a few months and offers minimal resistance to chemicals. Ceramic coating offers significantly longer-lasting and much more durable paint protection against environmental hazards.
