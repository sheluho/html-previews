Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our bathroom renovation process.

How long does a walk-in shower installation typically take?

Many of our standard walk-in shower installations and bathtub replacements can be completed in as little as one to two days. A full bathroom remodel involving extensive tile installation and structural changes will take longer, but we always provide a clear, upfront timeline before starting.

Can you replace my existing bathtub with a modern shower?

Absolutely. Bathtub replacement is one of our most popular services. We expertly remove your old, bulky tub and install a sleek, easily accessible walk-in shower in the exact same footprint, minimizing disruption to the rest of your bathroom.

Do you handle the complete bathroom remodel or just the shower area?

We are a full-service bathroom remodeling company. While we specialize in walk-in showers, our team manages comprehensive renovations. This includes vanities, flooring, lighting, plumbing updates, and custom tile installation across the entire space.

What kind of options do I have for tile installation?

We offer a vast selection of premium materials for your tile installation, including ceramic, porcelain, natural stone, and glass mosaics. Whether you want a minimalist modern look or a classic, textured feel, our design consultants will help you choose the perfect combination for your walls and floors.

Are your walk-in showers safe for seniors and those with limited mobility?

Yes, accessibility is a core focus of our designs. Our walk-in showers can be outfitted with ultra-low or zero-threshold entry points, ADA-compliant grab bars, slip-resistant flooring, and comfortable built-in seating, ensuring a safe and relaxing bathing experience for everyone.
