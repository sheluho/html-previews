Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book a moving company?

It is generally recommended to book your residential moving company at least 4 to 6 weeks in advance. During peak moving seasons, such as summer, providing even more notice ensures you secure your preferred date and time.

Do you provide packing services and materials?

Yes, the movers we promote offer comprehensive packing services. This includes supplying all necessary boxes, bubble wrap, packing paper, and tape to ensure your belongings are securely prepped for transit.

What is the difference between local movers and long distance movers?

Local movers typically handle relocations within the same city or state, usually completed in a single day and billed hourly. Long distance movers manage interstate relocations, which require more complex logistics, federal compliance, and are generally priced based on the weight of your items and the distance traveled.

Are my belongings insured during the move?

Professional moving companies offer basic liability coverage by default. For added peace of mind, full value protection options are also available to ensure your items are comprehensively covered throughout the moving process.

Can you move large or specialty items like pianos?

Absolutely. Experienced movers have the specialized equipment and training required to safely transport oversized, heavy, or fragile items, including pianos, antiques, and large safes.
