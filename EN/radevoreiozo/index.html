Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the custom furniture process take?

The timeline for bespoke furniture varies based on complexity, material availability, and the current queue. Typically, a custom piece requires 8 to 12 weeks from the finalization of the design to completion in the workshop.

Can I request specific dimensions for a custom dining table?

Absolutely. As a dedicated custom furniture maker and showroom, every piece is scaled precisely to your environment. You can specify exact length, width, and height requirements during the consultation phase.

Do you design and build custom sofas?

Yes, we coordinate the creation of custom sofas. This involves constructing robust, handcrafted hardwood frames combined with premium upholstery selections, ensuring your seating is as durable as it is beautiful.

What materials do you use for your handmade furniture?

The primary medium is domestically sourced, premium hardwood such as walnut, white oak, cherry, and maple. We prioritize sustainably harvested timber that offers superior strength and exceptional natural grain character.

Is the custom furniture maker and showroom open to the public?

Consultations at the showroom are typically held by appointment to ensure each client receives undivided attention. You can initiate an inquiry through our platform to schedule a dedicated session with the craftsmen.
