Frequently Asked Questions

How long does door installation typically take?

Most interior doors and front door replacement projects are efficiently completed within one to two days. Our goal is to transform your space quickly while minimizing any disruption to your daily routine.

Do you recommend installing fiberglass doors?

Yes, we highly recommend fiberglass doors for entryways. They offer incredible durability, resist warping and rotting, require very low maintenance, and provide outstanding energy efficiency for your home.

Will I need to paint the doors myself after installation?

Not at all. Our entry and interior doors often come factory-finished in the premium color or stain of your choice, ensuring a flawless look so no painting is required once the door installation is complete.

Can you replace custom-sized doors?

Absolutely. We utilize precise 3D measurement technology to ensure your new entry doors and interior doors are custom-cut to fit perfectly into your home's existing, unique frames.

How do I prepare for my door installation?

Simply clear any furniture or personal items from the area around the doorways. Our professional installation team handles the rest, from the safe removal of your old doors to a thorough cleanup after the new doors are set.
