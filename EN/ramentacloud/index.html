Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about door installation and replacement.

What are the main benefits of fiberglass doors?

Fiberglass doors are highly favored for their exceptional durability and minimal maintenance requirements. Unlike wood, they resist warping, rotting, and expanding due to moisture. They also offer excellent insulation properties, making them highly energy-efficient for front door replacement projects.

How long does a typical front door replacement take?

A standard front door replacement usually takes about half a day to complete, assuming there is no extensive structural damage to the surrounding frame. Professional installers ensure the old unit is removed cleanly and the new door is fitted, sealed, and aligned perfectly on the same day to maintain your home's security.

Do the services cover interior doors as well as exterior?

Yes, professional door installation covers both exterior and interior doors. Whether you are updating a single bedroom door or replacing all the interior doors in your home for a cohesive look, skilled technicians can handle the entire process, including hardware installation and trim finishing.

How should I prepare my home for door installation?

To ensure a smooth installation process, please clear the area around the doorway, both inside and outside. Remove any fragile items, furniture, or rugs near the work zone. This provides the installation team with the necessary space to work safely and efficiently.

Will new entry doors improve my home's energy efficiency?

Absolutely. Modern entry doors, particularly high-quality fiberglass doors, are manufactured with advanced insulating cores. Combined with professional installation that ensures proper weatherstripping and a tight seal, a new entry door can significantly reduce drafts and help lower your heating and cooling costs.
