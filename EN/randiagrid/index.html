Frequently Asked Questions

What are the benefits of energy efficient windows?

Energy efficient windows reduce heat transfer, keeping your home cooler in the summer and warmer in the winter. This significantly lowers your reliance on HVAC systems, thereby decreasing your monthly energy bills. Furthermore, they protect interior furnishings from harmful UV rays.

Why choose double hung windows over other styles?

Double hung windows are incredibly versatile. Both the top and bottom sashes can be opened to improve air circulation. Additionally, the sashes tilt inward, making it extremely easy to clean both the inside and outside of the glass from safely within your home.

How long does professional window installation usually take?

The exact timeline depends on the number of replacement windows being installed. However, an experienced window installation crew can typically replace standard household windows within one to two days. Their expertise minimizes disruption to your daily routine.

Are vinyl windows durable?

Yes, vinyl windows are renowned for their durability and low maintenance. Unlike wood, vinyl frames will not rot, warp, peel, or require repainting. They naturally resist moisture and stand up remarkably well to extreme weather conditions over decades.

How do I know if I need replacement windows?

Key signs include feeling drafts near the window, noticeable condensation or fogging between glass panes, difficulty opening or closing the sashes, rotting frames, or unexpectedly high energy bills. If you experience these issues, it is time to consider replacement windows.
