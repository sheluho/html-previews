Project Inquiries

What does "turnkey kitchen remodeling" actually mean?

A turnkey kitchen remodeling project means we handle the renovation from start to finish. You do not need to hire separate contractors for demolition, cabinetry, or countertop installation. We manage the entire lifecycle of the project so that when we hand over the keys, the kitchen is fully functional and ready for use.

Are your custom cabinets built to order?

Yes, every set of custom cabinets we integrate is built precisely for your home's unique dimensions. We do not use filler panels to make standard boxes fit. The cabinetry is designed to maximize your specific storage needs, matching your chosen aesthetic perfectly.

What materials are available for the countertops?

We offer a wide selection of premium countertops, including high-durability quartz, natural granite, and luxury marble. During the design phase of your custom kitchen, we will help you select a material that balances your aesthetic desires with your practical, everyday cooking needs.

How long does a typical custom kitchen renovation take?

Timelines vary based on the complexity of the design and material availability. However, because we provide a turnkey kitchen remodeling service, our processes are highly streamlined. Most custom installations take between 4 to 8 weeks from the day demolition begins to final walkthrough.

Can I replace just the cabinet doors instead of a full remodel?

Yes. If the structural boxes of your current setup are in excellent condition, we can provide custom cabinet doors and updated hardware to refresh the space without a complete tear-out. This is a great way to elevate the look of your kitchen efficiently.
