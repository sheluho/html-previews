Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a ceramic coating last?

A professional-grade ceramic coating can last anywhere from 2 to 5 years, depending on the specific product applied and how well the vehicle is maintained. Regular, gentle hand washing and avoiding harsh automated car washes will maximize the longevity of the coating.

What is included in a full interior detailing?

Our comprehensive interior detailing includes thorough vacuuming, steam cleaning or hot water extraction for carpets and upholstery, leather cleaning and conditioning, and detailed wiping of the dashboard, console, and door panels to remove dust, grime, and stains.

Do you offer paint correction for severe scratches?

Yes, we provide multi-stage paint correction. This process removes a thin layer of clear coat to level out deep scratches, swirl marks, and oxidation. However, scratches that have penetrated completely through the clear coat to the base paint may require touch-up painting rather than just polishing.

How often should I get my car detailed?

For optimal maintenance, we recommend a full auto detailing service every 4 to 6 months. Regular maintenance washes in between these major details will keep the paint protection intact and the interior fresh.

Is ceramic coating better than traditional wax?

Absolutely. Traditional waxes typically wear off within a few months and offer limited resistance to chemicals and UV rays. Ceramic coating forms a semi-permanent bond with the clear coat, offering superior durability, higher gloss, and extreme hydrophobic properties that wax simply cannot match.
