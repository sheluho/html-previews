Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a standard house cleaning?

A standard professional house cleaning includes dusting surfaces, vacuuming carpets, mopping hard floors, wiping down kitchen counters and appliances, and thoroughly cleaning bathrooms (sinks, toilets, showers, and mirrors). It is designed to maintain a high level of cleanliness in your home on a regular basis.

Do I need to be home during the maid service?

No, you do not need to be home. Many clients prefer to provide a spare key, garage code, or lockbox access so the cleaning professionals can work while the house is empty. Rest assured, all personnel are thoroughly vetted and trained to respect your property.

What is the difference between regular and deep cleaning?

While regular cleaning covers the essential maintenance tasks, a deep cleaning is much more intensive. It targets hard-to-reach areas, built-up grime, baseboards, interior windows, blinds, and inside large appliances if requested. Deep cleaning is highly recommended for initial visits or seasonal refreshes.

How does the recurring cleaning schedule work?

You can set up a recurring cleaning schedule that best fits your lifestyle—usually weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly. Once your schedule is established, the cleaning team will arrive on your designated day and time slot to ensure your home remains consistently pristine.

What does a move-out cleaning entail?

Move-out cleaning is a comprehensive service designed for empty homes. It includes everything in a deep clean, plus thorough cleaning inside cabinets, drawers, closets, and major appliances like the refrigerator and oven. The goal is to leave the space completely spotless for the next occupants or a final landlord inspection.
