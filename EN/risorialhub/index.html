Common Questions

Insights into our wedding videography process.

How would you describe your style of wedding videography?

Our approach is heavily focused on creating a cinematic wedding video. This means we prioritize storytelling, high-quality audio capture of vows and speeches, and beautiful, intentional framing. We blend documentary-style observation with subtle direction during portrait sessions to ensure your film feels both authentic and highly polished.

Do you travel for event videography outside of Los Angeles?

Yes, while we are primarily based in Southern California, we frequently travel for destination weddings and special events. Please inquire with your specific location details so we can provide a customized quote that includes travel arrangements for our team.

How long does it take to receive our final wedding film?

Crafting a meticulous cinematic wedding video takes time and careful attention to detail. Our standard turnaround time is typically between 8 to 12 weeks following your event date. We ensure that coloring, audio mixing, and narrative editing are perfected before delivering your final heirloom.

Will you work well with our photographer?

Absolutely. Collaboration is key to successful event videography. We pride ourselves on being unobtrusive and working seamlessly alongside your chosen photography team. We communicate throughout the day to ensure we both capture the shots we need without getting in each other's way.

Do you capture audio as well as video?

Yes, professional audio capture is a cornerstone of our work as a premium wedding videographer. We use discrete lavalier microphones during the ceremony and tap into the DJ's soundboard during the reception to ensure your vows, speeches, and ambient sounds are crystal clear in your final film.
