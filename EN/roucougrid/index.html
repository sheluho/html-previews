Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about our detailing and protection services.

What exactly is a ceramic coating?

A ceramic coating is a liquid polymer applied to the exterior of a vehicle. It chemically bonds with the factory paint, creating a semi-permanent layer of protection. This layer is highly hydrophobic, making it easier to clean, and provides strong resistance against UV rays, chemical stains, and minor abrasions.

How long does a full auto detailing session take?

The duration depends heavily on the condition of the vehicle and the specific services requested. A standard interior detailing might take a few hours, while an intensive paint correction followed by a ceramic coating application can take one to two full days to ensure optimal curing and perfection.

Does paint protection fix existing scratches?

Paint protection products, like coatings or films, are designed to prevent future damage, not fix existing ones. However, our process includes paint correction prior to application. During the correction phase, we use specialized polishing techniques to safely remove swirl marks and light scratches before sealing the paint.

What is included in your interior detailing service?

Our comprehensive interior detailing includes deep vacuuming of all carpets and upholstery, steam cleaning to sanitize surfaces, meticulous cleaning of the dashboard, console, and door panels, as well as specialized conditioning treatments for leather and plastics to prevent cracking and fading.

How often should I have my car professionally detailed?

For optimal maintenance and to preserve your vehicle's value, we recommend a thorough car detailing every 4 to 6 months. Vehicles with ceramic coatings require less frequent intensive detailing but still benefit from regular professional maintenance washes to keep the coating performing at its peak.
