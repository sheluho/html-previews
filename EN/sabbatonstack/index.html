Common Questions

Details about our custom closet design and installation process.

How long does a custom closets installation take?

Most installations are completed within one to two days, depending on the scale of the project. A large master suite will take longer than a single reach-in space. The exact timeline is established during the final design approval phase.

Can you design a closet organizer for odd-shaped rooms?

Absolutely. Sloped ceilings, awkward alcoves, and non-standard room dimensions are where custom solutions excel. A tailored closet organizer is designed specifically to maximize storage in these challenging areas where store-bought systems fail.

What materials do you use for a custom wardrobe?

We utilize a range of high-quality, durable materials including premium wood veneers, thermally fused laminates, and solid wood components. During the consultation, you can select finishes and hardware that perfectly match your home's aesthetic.

Do I need to empty my walk-in closet before installation?

Yes, the space must be completely clear of all clothing, personal items, and existing wire shelving or rods before the installation team arrives. This ensures a clean workspace and allows for precise measurements and mounting.

How do I start the process for a new reach-in closet?

The first step is to schedule an initial design consultation. We will evaluate your current space, discuss your storage needs, take precise measurements, and begin drafting a 3D model of your new customized storage layout.
