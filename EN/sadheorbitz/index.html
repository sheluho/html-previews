Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common inquiries about our photography process.

What is included in your wedding photography packages?

Our standard packages include full-day coverage by a lead wedding photographer, professionally edited high-resolution images, an online viewing gallery, and printing rights. Custom packages can also include albums and second shooters.

Do you offer engagement photos before the wedding?

Yes, we highly recommend booking an engagement photos session. It allows us to build rapport, helps you feel more natural in front of the camera, and provides you with beautiful images for your invitations.

Are you available for destination wedding photography?

Absolutely. We specialize in destination wedding photography and are available to travel worldwide. We handle our own travel logistics to ensure a seamless experience for you.

How long does it take to receive our bridal portraits and wedding gallery?

We take immense pride in our editorial editing process. You can expect to receive your fully edited digital gallery, including all bridal portraits and candid moments, within 6 to 8 weeks following your wedding day.

How do we book you as our wedding photographer?

Simply fill out the inquiry form in the contact section below to check our availability. We will then schedule a consultation to discuss your vision, customize your package, and secure your date with a retainer.
