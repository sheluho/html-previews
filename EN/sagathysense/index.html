Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about seasonal tire management.

How does the tire storage process work?
Once your seasonal swap is complete, your off-season set is cleaned, tagged, and transported to a secure, climate-controlled facility. This specialized tire storage environment prevents rubber degradation caused by extreme temperature fluctuations and UV exposure.
Do you handle the actual tire installation?
Yes. As a promotional partner, we connect you directly with professionals who perform the tire installation. They will remove your current set, install your seasonal set, and ensure everything is torqued to the correct specifications.
Is tire rotation included in the service?
A basic tire rotation is naturally part of the seasonal swapping process, as tires are typically remounted in different positions to ensure even tread wear. This helps maximize the lifespan of your investment.
What if I need a wheel alignment or new tires?
During the changeover, technicians inspect your treads. If they notice uneven wear indicating the need for a wheel alignment, or if the tread depth is too low and requires a tire replacement, they will notify you so you can address it promptly.
Can I access my tires while they are in storage?
The tires are kept in a secure bulk facility to ensure safety and climate control. While you cannot access the facility directly, you simply schedule your next seasonal swap, and your tires will be retrieved and ready for your appointment.
