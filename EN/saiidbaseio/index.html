Frequently Asked Questions

Insights into modern door installation and upgrades.

What types of entry doors are highly recommended?

We highly recommend fiberglass doors for their incredible durability, low maintenance, and excellent weather resistance. However, premium wood and steel doors remain excellent choices depending on your specific architectural style and security requirements.

How long does a front door replacement typically take?

A standard front door replacement is typically completed within a single day by experienced door installation teams. This ensures your home remains secure overnight and causes minimal disruption to your daily routine.

Are custom sizes available for interior doors?

Yes. Professional door installation companies can source and install custom-sized interior doors to perfectly fit any unique architectural dimensions, historic frames, or specialized design requirements within your home.

Are fiberglass doors energy efficient?

Absolutely. Modern fiberglass doors are engineered with excellent insulating cores. They provide superior thermal performance, helping to keep your home's climate controlled and potentially reducing your monthly energy bills.

What is included in the standard door installation process?

The professional door installation process generally includes the careful removal and disposal of your old door, precise fitting and leveling of the new frame, comprehensive weather sealing, installation of hardware, and a final quality inspection to ensure flawless operation.
