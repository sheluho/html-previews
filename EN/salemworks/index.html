Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our house cleaning services.

What is included in a standard house cleaning?

Standard house cleaning generally includes dusting all surfaces, vacuuming carpets, mopping hard floors, and thoroughly sanitizing bathrooms and kitchens. It is designed to maintain a high level of cleanliness on a regular basis.

How long does a deep cleaning usually take?

The duration of a deep cleaning depends entirely on the size and current condition of your home. Typically, it takes a professional team anywhere from 4 to 8 hours to ensure every nook and cranny is addressed perfectly.

Do I need to be home during the maid service?

No, you do not need to be home. Many clients provide a spare key, garage code, or lockbox access so our professional teams can perform the house cleaning while they are at work or running errands.

What is the difference between standard and move-out cleaning?

A move-out cleaning is far more intensive. It includes everything in a standard clean plus detailed cleaning inside all cabinets, drawers, refrigerators, ovens, and thorough scrubbing of baseboards and window sills to prepare the home for its next resident.

How do I schedule recurring cleaning appointments?

Scheduling is simple. Use the contact form below to request a quote, and a representative will help you establish a customized weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly recurring cleaning schedule that fits your life perfectly.
