Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about planning your move.

How far in advance should I book my moving company?

We recommend booking your move at least 4 to 6 weeks in advance, especially during the busy summer months. This ensures we can secure your preferred dates and adequately prepare our local movers or long distance movers for your specific requirements.

Do you offer full-service packing services?

Yes, we provide comprehensive packing services. Our trained professionals use high-quality materials to safely pack your entire home, fragile items, or just specific rooms you need help with, saving you significant time and effort.

What is the difference between local movers and long distance movers?

Local moves are generally defined as relocations within the same state and under a certain mileage radius (often 50-100 miles). Long distance moves involve crossing state lines or traveling vast distances within a large state, requiring different logistical planning, federal compliance, and pricing structures.

How are moving costs calculated?

For local moves, costs are typically based on an hourly rate determined by the number of movers and trucks required. Long distance moves are usually calculated based on the total weight of your belongings and the distance traveled. Additional services like packing will be factored into your custom quote.

Are there items your movers cannot transport?

Yes, for safety and legal reasons, our moving company cannot transport hazardous materials, flammable items, explosives, corrosive chemicals, live plants, or perishable food. We recommend transporting important personal documents, jewelry, and medications yourself.
