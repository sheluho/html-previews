Frequently Asked Questions

What is a turnkey renovation?

A turnkey renovation means the entire process is handled for you. A dedicated general contractor manages the design, permitting, construction, and finishing touches, so you don't have to juggle multiple vendors.

Do you handle home additions as well as interior remodels?

Yes, the providers we promote specialize in both comprehensive interior home remodeling and structural home additions designed to seamlessly expand your living space.

How long does a whole home remodeling project typically take?

Timelines vary significantly based on the scope of work. A complete home renovation typically takes several months. Your general contractor will provide a detailed schedule before construction begins.

Will I need to hire my own architect?

No, a true design-build approach means all architectural and design services are coordinated for your project under one umbrella, simplifying communication.

Who manages all the necessary building permits?

Your general contractor handles all local building permits and coordinates the required inspections to ensure your home renovation complies with all municipal codes.
