Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer both buffet catering and plated dinners?
Yes, the culinary partners we feature are highly versatile. They offer expansive buffet catering setups ideal for large corporate gatherings, as well as elegant plated dinner services that are perfect for formal wedding catering and upscale social galas.
Can dietary restrictions be accommodated for wedding catering?
Absolutely. Modern event catering requires flexibility. Chefs are accustomed to designing specific menus or alternative plates to accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other common dietary requirements without compromising on taste or presentation.
How far in advance should we book corporate catering?
For large-scale corporate catering events or multi-day conferences, it is recommended to secure your date several months in advance. However, some providers can accommodate smaller business luncheons with a few weeks' notice, depending on seasonal availability.
Is event catering available for outdoor venues?
Yes, professional catering teams are equipped to handle logistical challenges, including outdoor venues with limited kitchen facilities. They can set up mobile prep stations to ensure a flawless plated dinner or buffet service regardless of the location.
Do you provide staff for the events?
Comprehensive event catering generally includes trained waitstaff, bartenders, and on-site culinary management. The exact staffing levels will be tailored to your guest count and chosen service style, ensuring every guest receives prompt and polite attention.
