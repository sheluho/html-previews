Frequently Asked Questions

Insights into our remodeling process and how we operate.

What is included in a whole home remodeling project?

A whole home remodeling project encompasses comprehensive updates to your property. This can include structural changes, altering floor plans, completely updating kitchens and bathrooms, upgrading electrical and plumbing systems, and renewing interior finishes. We handle it all from concept to final inspection.

Why should I hire a general contractor instead of managing it myself?

Managing a major home renovation requires coordinating multiple specialized trades, navigating municipal building codes, and securing permits. As your general contractor, we take on all liability, scheduling, and quality control, ensuring the project stays on schedule and meets stringent building standards.

How does a turnkey renovation process work?

Our turnkey renovation approach provides an end-to-end solution. We collaborate with you during the design phase, handle all material procurement, manage the construction site, and oversee final detailing. You simply turn the key and walk into a fully finished, beautifully crafted home without the daily stress of project management.

Can you help design and build a home addition?

Absolutely. Whether you are looking to add a second story, expand your kitchen footprint, or build an in-law suite, we specialize in home additions. We ensure the new structure integrates seamlessly with your existing home's architecture, matching rooflines, siding, and interior flow perfectly.

Do you handle all the necessary construction permits?

Yes. Securing the proper permits is critical for the safety and legality of your home renovation. Our team handles the entire permitting process with the city or county, ensuring all architectural plans comply with local zoning laws and building codes before we ever break ground.
