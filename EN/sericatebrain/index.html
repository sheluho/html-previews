Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I choose energy efficient windows?

Energy efficient windows utilize advanced materials like Low-E glass coatings and insulating gas fills to significantly reduce heat transfer. This keeps your home warmer in the winter and cooler in the summer, leading to reduced utility bills and a more comfortable living space.

What are the main benefits of vinyl windows?

Vinyl windows are incredibly popular because they offer a fantastic balance of durability, energy efficiency, and cost-effectiveness. They do not require painting, resist moisture and decay, and provide excellent thermal insulation compared to older aluminum or wood frames.

How does the window installation process work?

Professional window installation begins with precise measurements of your existing frames. Once your custom replacement windows are manufactured, installation experts remove the old units, prepare the openings, securely fit the new windows, and thoroughly seal and insulate the gaps to ensure maximum performance.

Are double hung windows difficult to maintain?

Not at all. In fact, double hung windows are celebrated for their ease of maintenance. Because both the top and bottom sashes can tilt inward, you can safely and easily clean both the inside and outside of the glass from inside your home.

Will replacement windows really lower my heating bills?

Yes, upgrading from single-pane or older, drafty double-pane frames to modern energy efficient windows can dramatically reduce energy loss. By creating a tighter seal and providing better insulation, your HVAC system won't have to work as hard, which directly translates to lower heating and cooling costs.
