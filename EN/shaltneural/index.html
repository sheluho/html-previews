Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly does a full-service wedding planner do?

A full-service wedding planner manages the entire scope of your event. This includes budget creation, venue scouting, vendor negotiation, design conceptualization, and comprehensive timeline management. We act as your project manager from day one until the final guest departs.

Do I need a day of coordinator if my venue has a manager?

Yes. A venue manager is responsible for the venue's facilities and staff, ensuring the building operates correctly. A day of coordinator works exclusively for you, managing your personal timeline, directing external vendors (like photographers and florists), and handling personal details so you can relax.

How far in advance should we start wedding planning?

We recommend beginning the wedding planning process 12 to 18 months prior to your desired date. This allows ample time to secure high-demand venues and premier vendors without feeling rushed.

Can your team assist with a destination wedding?

Absolutely. Our destination wedding services are built to manage complex travel logistics, multi-day itineraries, and international vendor communications, ensuring your faraway celebration is as seamless as a local one.

Will a wedding coordinator work with vendors I've already hired?

Yes. When you hire us as your wedding coordinator, we take over the communication and timeline coordination with the vendors you have already selected, ensuring everyone is aligned and prepared for the big day.
