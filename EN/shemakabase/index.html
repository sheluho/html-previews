Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about preparing for your move.

How far in advance should I book my moving company?

We recommend booking your movers at least 4 to 6 weeks in advance, especially during the busy summer months. Early booking ensures you secure your preferred dates and allows ample time to organize packing services if needed.

Do your long distance movers handle interstate relocations?

Yes, our network of long distance movers specializes in interstate relocations. They are fully equipped to manage the complex logistics, transportation routing, and regulatory requirements involved in moving your belongings across state lines.

What is included in your packing services?

Our packing services can be customized to your needs. A full-service pack includes providing all necessary boxes, tape, and protective materials, followed by the professional wrapping and boxing of your entire household. We also offer partial packing for specific rooms or fragile items.

Are the local movers equipped to handle heavy furniture?

Absolutely. The local movers we connect you with are trained professionals who come equipped with dollies, moving blankets, straps, and the physical expertise required to safely disassemble, move, and reassemble large, heavy furniture pieces.

How is the cost of my residential moving company service calculated?

Costs vary depending on the type of move. Local moves are typically billed at an hourly rate based on the number of movers and trucks required. Long distance moves are generally calculated based on the total weight of your inventory and the distance traveled.
