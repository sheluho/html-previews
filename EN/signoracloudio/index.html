Frequently Asked Questions

How long does window installation typically take?

Most standard window installation projects can be completed in a single day, depending on the number of windows being replaced. The professional crews ensure minimal disruption to your daily routine while working efficiently to secure your new windows.

What are the main benefits of vinyl windows?

Vinyl windows are highly popular because they are incredibly durable, energy efficient, and require very little maintenance. Unlike wood, they won't rot, peel, or require repainting, making them a smart, long-term investment for any home.

Do you offer classic double hung windows?

Yes, double hung windows are among the most requested styles. They feature two operable sashes that move vertically, allowing for excellent ventilation. Many modern designs also tilt inward, which makes cleaning the exterior glass from inside your home very easy.

How do energy efficient windows save me money?

Energy efficient windows are constructed with specialized glass coatings and insulated frames that reduce heat transfer. This means your home stays warmer in the winter and cooler in the summer, reducing the workload on your HVAC system and lowering your monthly energy bills.

What is the process for getting replacement windows?

The process begins with a free, no-obligation estimate where an expert will measure your current frames and discuss your styling preferences. Once you choose your custom replacement windows, they are manufactured to fit exactly, followed by scheduling a convenient date for your professional window installation.
