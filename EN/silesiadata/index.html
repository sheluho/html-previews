Frequently Asked Questions

What types of events do you cater?

The catering partners we highlight specialize in a wide variety of gatherings, including full-scale wedding catering, large corporate catering events, private parties, and intimate family celebrations.

Do you offer both buffet catering and plated dinners?

Yes, you can choose the style that best fits your event. Options range from casual, interactive buffet catering stations to elegant, multi-course plated dinner service.

How far in advance should we book wedding catering?

For major events like weddings, it is highly recommended to secure your event catering services 6 to 12 months in advance to ensure availability on your preferred date.

Can the caterers accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely. The culinary teams are experienced in crafting menus that accommodate vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, and other specific dietary needs without compromising on flavor or presentation.

Who handles the service on the day of the event?

Professional, fully trained catering staff will be on-site to handle all aspects of food service, from setup and serving to breakdown and cleanup, ensuring your event runs smoothly.
