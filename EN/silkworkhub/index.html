Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about upgrading your home's exterior.

What are the primary benefits of replacement windows?
Upgrading your home with new replacement windows provides multiple benefits. They significantly improve energy efficiency, reducing heating and cooling costs. They also enhance your home's curb appeal, increase property value, reduce exterior noise transmission, and offer better security through modern locking mechanisms.
Are vinyl windows a good choice for my home?
Yes, vinyl windows are an exceptional choice. They are highly durable, resistant to rot and corrosion, and require virtually no maintenance compared to traditional wood frames. Furthermore, modern vinyl frames are engineered with internal chambers that provide excellent thermal insulation, making them a key component of energy efficient windows.
Why should I choose double hung windows?
Double hung windows are incredibly versatile. Because both the top and bottom sashes are operable, you can customize airflow—opening the top to let warm air escape and the bottom to let cool air in. Additionally, most modern double hung designs feature tilt-in sashes, making it safe and easy to clean the exterior glass from inside your home.
How long does the window installation process typically take?
The duration of professional window installation depends on the number of units being replaced and the complexity of the job. However, a standard whole-house replacement typically takes 1 to 3 days. Professional crews work efficiently to minimize disruption to your daily routine while ensuring a weather-tight fit.
Do energy efficient windows really save money?
Absolutely. By minimizing thermal transfer, energy efficient windows reduce the workload on your HVAC system. While savings vary based on climate and the condition of your old frames, homeowners often see a noticeable decrease in their monthly utility bills, allowing the investment to pay for itself over time.
