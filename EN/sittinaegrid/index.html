Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about professional carpet and upholstery maintenance.

How often should I schedule professional carpet cleaning?

For most households, it is recommended to have carpets professionally steam cleaned every 12 to 18 months. Homes with pets, young children, or heavy foot traffic may benefit from cleaning every 6 to 9 months to maintain optimal hygiene and appearance.

Is steam cleaning safe for all types of upholstery?

While steam cleaning is highly effective for many synthetic and durable natural fabrics, extremely delicate materials may require dry cleaning methods. Professional technicians assess the fabric type before selecting the appropriate cleaning method to prevent shrinkage or damage.

How long does it take for carpets to dry after service?

Drying times typically range from 4 to 8 hours depending on humidity, temperature, and the specific cleaning method used. Proper ventilation and air circulation can significantly speed up the drying process.

Can all stains be completely removed?

While advanced stain removal techniques are highly successful, some substances can permanently alter or bleach the carpet dyes. Prompt treatment is always the best defense against permanent staining.

Do you offer specialized rug cleaning for delicate area rugs?

Yes, area rugs, especially Oriental or Persian rugs, require specialized attention to preserve their dyes and fibers. Tailored cleaning solutions are utilized to gently but thoroughly clean delicate rugs without causing color bleeding.
