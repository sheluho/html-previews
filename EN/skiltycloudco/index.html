Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a turnkey kitchen project?

A turnkey kitchen renovation includes every phase of the project handled by professionals. This covers initial 3D design, demolition, structural changes, plumbing and electrical work, installation of custom cabinets, countertops, flooring, and final painting. You simply "turn the key" and walk into a finished space.

How long does standard kitchen remodeling take?

Timelines vary based on the scope of the project. A cosmetic update might take 3-4 weeks, while a full custom kitchen remodel involving structural changes and custom cabinetry can take 8-12 weeks. An accurate timeline is provided during your initial consultation.

Can I choose my own materials for cabinets and countertops?

Absolutely. The design process is highly collaborative. You will have access to a wide variety of premium materials, finishes, and hardware. Our partners will guide you through selecting the best countertops and cabinets to fit your budget and aesthetic goals.

Do I need to hire separate contractors for plumbing and electrical?

No. One of the main benefits of full-service kitchen remodeling is that all specialty trades are coordinated for you. The project manager schedules licensed plumbers, electricians, and carpenters to ensure the workflow is seamless and up to code.

How do I start the design process for my custom kitchen?

Getting started is easy. Simply fill out the contact form on this page. A representative will reach out to discuss your ideas, schedule an in-home measurement, and begin drafting the layout for your new space.
