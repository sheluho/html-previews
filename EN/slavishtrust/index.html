Frequently Asked Questions

What does a turnkey renovation actually include?

A turnkey renovation means that as your general contractor, we handle absolutely everything. From the initial design and acquiring necessary permits to demolition, construction, plumbing, electrical, and final finishing touches. You simply turn the key and walk into your fully completed, newly renovated home.

How long does whole home remodeling usually take?

The timeline for whole home remodeling varies significantly based on the scope of the project, the size of the home, and material availability. Typically, comprehensive projects can take anywhere from 3 to 8 months. We provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation so you know exactly what to expect.

Do I need to hire an architect for a home addition?

In most cases, yes, a home addition requires architectural plans for structural integrity and local permitting. However, as your dedicated general contractor, we coordinate directly with experienced architects and engineers to ensure the design aligns perfectly with construction realities and your budget.

Can I live in my house during a major home renovation?

It depends on the extent of the work. If we are performing a complete whole home remodeling project that affects critical areas like all bathrooms and the primary kitchen simultaneously, we strongly recommend relocating temporarily for your safety and comfort. For phased projects or isolated home additions, staying in the home may be possible.

Are you fully licensed and insured as a general contractor?

Yes, we exclusively partner with fully licensed, bonded, and insured professionals for every project. Masterpiece Construction TX maintains all necessary credentials to legally and safely perform extensive home renovation and construction work, giving you complete peace of mind.
