Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our window services.

What are the primary benefits of energy efficient windows? +

Energy efficient windows significantly reduce thermal transfer, meaning your heating and cooling systems don't have to work as hard to maintain your desired indoor temperature. This leads to noticeably lower utility bills, increased indoor comfort, and a reduced carbon footprint for your home.

How long does a typical window installation take? +

For most standard homes, our professional window installation teams can complete the job in 1 to 2 days. The exact timeframe depends on the number of replacement windows being installed and any structural adjustments required, but we always prioritize speed without sacrificing quality.

Why should I choose vinyl windows over wood or aluminum? +

Vinyl windows offer the best combination of durability, energy efficiency, and low maintenance. Unlike wood, they won't rot, warp, or require regular painting. Unlike aluminum, vinyl acts as an excellent insulator, preventing heat from escaping during the winter or entering during the summer.

Do you offer double hung windows? +

Yes, double hung windows are among our most popular options. Both the top and bottom sashes can slide vertically and tilt inward, making them incredibly easy to clean from inside the home while providing excellent ventilation control.

What is the process for getting replacement windows? +

The process begins with a free, no-obligation consultation where we assess your current windows and discuss your goals. We then take precise measurements, help you select your ideal window styles, and order the custom units. Finally, our certified crew arrives for a seamless and clean window installation.
