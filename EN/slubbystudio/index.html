Frequently Asked Questions

What materials are best for entry doors?

Fiberglass doors are highly recommended for entry doors due to their exceptional durability, energy efficiency, and resistance to warping or rotting. They offer the stunning visual aesthetics of natural wood grain without the heavy maintenance requirements.

Do you help coordinate front door replacement services?

Yes, front door replacement is a core specialty highlighted on our platform. We connect you with professionals who help you select the perfect style, manage the complete removal of your old frame, and execute a secure installation of your new door.

How long does a typical door installation take?

A standard interior door installation can often be completed in a few hours. However, a full front door replacement—which includes structural framing, sealing, and weatherproofing—typically requires a full day to ensure optimal security and insulation.

Can replacing my interior doors reduce noise?

Absolutely. Upgrading from hollow-core to solid-core interior doors significantly improves sound dampening between rooms. This creates a quieter, more peaceful home environment, especially beneficial for home offices and bedrooms.

Do I need to prepare anything before the installation team arrives?

Installers generally ask that you clear the immediate area around the doorway. Removing any furniture, rugs, wall hangings, or fragile items gives the technicians ample space to work safely, efficiently, and cleanly during the door installation process.
