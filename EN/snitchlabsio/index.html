Frequently Asked Questions

Information regarding the wedding photography services we promote.

What is included in a standard wedding photography package?

While specific packages vary by the provider, a standard offering typically includes full-day coverage by a primary wedding photographer, a curated online gallery of high-resolution images, and a consultation to discuss your timeline and vision.

Do you cover destination wedding photography?

Yes, the professionals we promote are well-equipped to handle destination wedding photography. They will travel to your chosen location to ensure your international or out-of-state celebration is documented with the same level of detail and artistry.

Should we schedule engagement photos before the wedding?

Scheduling engagement photos is highly recommended. It provides a wonderful opportunity to build rapport with your wedding photographer, understand their shooting style, and receive beautiful portraits to use for save-the-dates or wedding websites.

When is the best time for bridal portraits?

Bridal portraits are typically captured either during a dedicated session a few weeks before the wedding or during a scheduled block of time on the actual wedding day. A dedicated session allows for more creative freedom and a relaxed environment.
