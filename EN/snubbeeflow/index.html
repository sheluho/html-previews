Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our premium car wash services.

What is included in a premium car wash?

Our premium car wash includes a thorough hand wash of the exterior, wheel and tire cleaning, a gentle chamois dry, interior vacuuming, dashboard wipe-down, and streak-free interior and exterior window cleaning.

How long does a hand car wash take?

A standard hand car wash typically takes between 30 to 45 minutes, depending on the size and condition of your vehicle. Express wash services are designed to get you back on the road in 15 to 20 minutes.

Is an express wash safe for my vehicle's paint?

Absolutely. Unlike automated machines that can leave micro-scratches, all our washes are performed by hand using soft, non-abrasive microfiber materials and pH-neutral soaps to protect your clear coat.

How does the auto wash membership work?

The auto wash membership is a monthly subscription that allows you to bring your vehicle in for unlimited exterior washes. It is linked to your vehicle's license plate and offers the best value for regular customers.

Do I need to book an appointment in advance?

Walk-ins are always welcome for our standard and express washes. However, if you are looking for extensive detailing or paint correction services, we recommend booking an appointment to ensure prompt service.
