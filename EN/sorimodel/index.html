Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our custom furniture process.

How long does it take to build custom furniture?

Because every piece is built to order, timelines vary depending on complexity and material availability. Typically, a custom dining table or bespoke storage unit takes between 8 to 12 weeks from final design approval to delivery.

Can I visit the showroom to see samples?

Absolutely. Our custom furniture maker and showroom facility is designed to let you experience our craftsmanship firsthand. You can view wood finishes, feel upholstery fabrics, and see completed handmade furniture pieces on display.

Do you help with the design of a custom sofa?

Yes, our design consultants work closely with you to finalize dimensions, seat depth, cushion firmness, and fabric selection. We ensure your custom sofa not only looks stunning but provides optimal ergonomic comfort for your specific needs.

What types of wood do you use?

We source premium, sustainably harvested hardwoods including Walnut, White Oak, Maple, and Cherry. The choice of material is entirely up to you and will be discussed during your initial bespoke furniture consultation.

How do I start the commissioning process?

Simply fill out the consultation form at the bottom of this page. One of our design specialists will reach out to discuss your ideas, space requirements, and budget to begin crafting your personalized proposal.
