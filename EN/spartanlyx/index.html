Frequently Asked Questions

Details on our bespoke process.

How long does it take to build a custom dining table?

Lead times vary depending on the complexity of the design and the availability of specific materials. Typically, a custom dining table requires 8 to 12 weeks from final design approval to completion. We prioritize quality and precision over speed in all our handmade furniture.

Can I provide my own fabric for a custom sofa?

Yes, we gladly accept Customer's Own Material (COM) for custom sofa commissions. However, the fabric must be evaluated by our workshop to ensure it meets our durability and tailoring standards before we begin the upholstery process.

Do I need an appointment to visit the showroom?

While walk-ins are welcome to browse our floor models, we highly recommend scheduling an appointment if you wish to discuss a bespoke furniture project. This ensures a dedicated designer is available to consult with you uninterrupted.

What materials do you use for your bespoke furniture?

We source only premium, sustainably harvested hardwoods such as walnut, white oak, cherry, and maple. Our finishes are commercial-grade yet hand-applied, designed to protect your custom furniture while highlighting the natural beauty of the wood grain.

Do you deliver outside the local area?

Yes, we arrange specialized white-glove delivery services for our handmade furniture nationwide. Shipping costs are calculated based on the dimensions of the piece, such as a large custom dining table, and the final destination.
