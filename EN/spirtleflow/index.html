Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my moving company?

We recommend scheduling your residential moving company at least four to six weeks in advance, especially during peak summer months. This ensures you secure the exact dates you need with expert local movers or long distance movers.

Are packing services included in standard quotes?

Packing services are typically offered as an add-on to provide you with maximum flexibility. You can choose a full-service pack, a partial pack for fragile items, or opt to handle the packing yourself.

What is the difference between local movers and long distance movers?

Local movers typically handle relocations within the same state or a certain mile radius and charge by the hour. Long distance movers manage interstate moves across the country, where pricing is generally based on the total weight of your inventory and the distance traveled.

Can you move specialty or oversized residential items?

Yes. The moving professionals highlighted through Spirtleflow are trained to carefully handle heavy, oversized, or high-value residential items, ensuring they are properly protected and transported safely.

Do I need to empty my dresser drawers before moving?

In many cases, lightweight clothing can remain in sturdy dressers. However, for the safety of your furniture and the movers, it is advised to remove heavy items, breakables, and valuables. Your local movers will provide specific guidance based on your furniture's build.
