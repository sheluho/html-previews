Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take for the carpets to dry?
Drying times vary depending on the specific cleaning method utilized. Low-moisture methods can dry in as little as one hour, allowing you to return to normal activities quickly. Traditional steam cleaning may take slightly longer, typically between 4 to 8 hours, depending on room ventilation and humidity levels.
Can you guarantee complete stain removal?
While professional stain removal treatments are highly effective on most everyday spots (such as food, dirt, and mild pet accidents), no company can guarantee 100% removal of every stain. Factors like the age of the stain, previous chemical treatments, and the type of carpet fiber play a significant role.
Is upholstery cleaning safe for delicate fabrics?
Yes. Professional upholstery cleaning begins with an assessment of your furniture's fabric to determine the safest and most effective cleaning approach. Specialized solutions are used to gently lift dirt and oils without causing shrinkage, color bleeding, or fabric degradation.
Do I need to vacuum before the technicians arrive?
It is generally recommended to do a light vacuuming to remove surface debris and pet hair before the professional carpet cleaning process begins. However, the cleaning technicians will perform a thorough, deep-extraction process that targets the dirt embedded far below the surface.
Are the cleaning solutions safe for pets and children?
The professional providers we promote utilize industry-standard, high-quality cleaning solutions that are formulated to be tough on dirt but safe for your home environment. Once the carpets and upholstery are fully dry, they are completely safe for everyday use by both pets and children.
