Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a standard AC installation? ▼

A standard AC installation typically includes the removal of your old unit, positioning and securing the new air conditioner, connecting the necessary refrigerant lines and electrical wiring, and performing a comprehensive system test to ensure optimal cooling performance.

How often should I schedule HVAC maintenance? ▼

It is highly recommended to service your heating and cooling systems at least twice a year. Schedule an inspection for your air conditioner in the spring and a check-up for your furnace in the fall to ensure peak efficiency before extreme weather hits.

What are the benefits of upgrading to a heat pump? ▼

A heat pump provides both heating and cooling from a single unit, making it an incredibly energy-efficient choice. By transferring heat rather than generating it, heat pumps can significantly lower your monthly utility bills while maintaining a consistent, comfortable indoor climate.

How do I know if my furnace needs replacing? ▼

Signs that you may need a new furnace include frequent breakdowns, uneven heating throughout your home, unusual noises (like banging or rattling), rising energy bills, and if the unit is over 15 years old. A professional HVAC technician can provide a definitive assessment.
