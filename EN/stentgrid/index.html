Frequently Asked Questions

What is your approach to wedding videography? +

Our approach is highly documentary and unobtrusive. As your wedding videographer, we aim to capture genuine interactions rather than staging stiff poses. This allows us to produce a cinematic wedding video that feels authentic, emotional, and true to your unique personalities.

How far in advance should we book a wedding videographer? +

We recommend booking 9 to 12 months in advance, especially if your wedding is during peak season. Securing your date early ensures that our dedicated team is available to craft your perfect wedding film.

Do you offer both wedding and event videography? +

Yes. While we specialize in weddings, our event videography services cover a wide range of celebrations. We frequently film rehearsal dinners, engagement parties, and multi-day cultural events to provide a comprehensive narrative of your entire celebration.

What makes a cinematic wedding video different from regular coverage? +

A cinematic wedding video is distinguished by its storytelling focus, advanced camera techniques, high-end audio capture, and professional color grading. Instead of a simple chronological recording, we edit your footage into a compelling, movie-like experience that highlights the emotional core of your day.

How long does it take to receive our final wedding film? +

Because of the meticulous care we take in editing, color grading, and audio mixing, it typically takes between 8 to 12 weeks to deliver the final wedding film. This ensures that every frame meets our high standards for cinematic quality.
