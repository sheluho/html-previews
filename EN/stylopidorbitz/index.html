Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a front door replacement typically take?
While the exact timeline depends on the complexity of the frame and the specific door selected, most professional front door replacement projects are completed within a single day. The installation team ensures everything is properly sealed, weatherproofed, and cleaned up before they leave.
What are the benefits of fiberglass doors over traditional wood?
Fiberglass doors offer incredible durability and are highly resistant to warping, rotting, and expanding caused by weather changes. They provide excellent insulation for energy efficiency and can beautifully mimic the natural grain of wood without the intensive maintenance requirements.
Do you promote both entry doors and interior doors?
Yes, Stylopid Orbitz highlights comprehensive door solutions. Whether you need a secure, statement-making entry door or a cohesive set of high-quality interior doors for your bedrooms and closets, our partners are equipped to handle the entire scope of the project.
Is professional door installation really necessary?
Absolutely. Even the highest quality door will fail to provide security and insulation if it is not installed correctly. Professional door installation ensures that the frame is perfectly plumb, the hinges are properly weighted, and the weatherstripping creates an airtight seal.
Can I get custom sizing for my interior doors?
Yes, homes often settle over time, making standard door sizes a poor fit for older frames. The professionals we promote specialize in measuring and customizing interior doors to perfectly match your existing archways and frames for a seamless, factory-finish look.
