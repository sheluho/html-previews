Frequently Asked Questions

What does a full-service wedding planner actually do?

We handle everything from budget creation and vendor negotiations to event design and day-of execution. Our goal is to ensure a cohesive, beautifully structured, and stress-free experience from start to finish.

Do you offer day of coordinator services if we plan the rest?

Yes. Our expert wedding coordinator team can step in a few months prior to your date to review your contracts, finalize logistical details, and seamlessly manage the actual wedding day timeline.

Can you help us plan a destination wedding?

Absolutely. We specialize in destination wedding planning, managing the unique travel logistics, international vendor communications, and multi-day guest itineraries to ensure a flawless getaway.

When should we hire a wedding planner?

We recommend booking our full-service wedding planning 12 to 18 months in advance to secure the best venues and vendor teams, though we can accommodate shorter timelines depending on our current availability.

Will we lose control of our wedding vision?

Not at all. Our role is strictly to execute your unique vision. We provide the structural expertise, organizational framework, and curated options, but you retain full control and make all final decisions.
