Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I schedule professional carpet cleaning?
For most households, we recommend professional carpet cleaning at least once every 12 to 18 months. Homes with indoor pets, children, or high foot traffic may benefit from scheduling a cleaning every 6 to 9 months to maintain optimal fabric health and appearance.
Is steam cleaning safe for all types of upholstery?
While steam cleaning is highly effective for many fabrics, it is not universally safe for all materials. Our technicians always inspect your upholstery first to determine the safest and most effective cleaning method, ensuring delicate or vintage fabrics are treated properly.
Can you completely remove old, set-in pet stains?
We utilize advanced stain removal solutions designed specifically to break down the proteins in pet stains. While many severe, old stains can be vastly improved or fully removed, success depends on the age of the stain and the specific carpet fiber.
Do I need to move my furniture before you arrive?
We ask that you remove small items, breakables, and light furniture from the areas being cleaned. Our team can carefully clean around heavier pieces like large beds or entertainment centers to ensure a thorough result without risking damage to your belongings.
How long does it take for carpets to dry after cleaning?
Drying times vary depending on the humidity, airflow, and the specific carpet material, but typically range from 4 to 8 hours. We use specialized extraction equipment during the steam cleaning process to pull out as much moisture as possible to accelerate drying.
