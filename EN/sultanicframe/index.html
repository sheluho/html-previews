Common Questions

What makes custom closets better than store-bought systems?

Custom closets are tailored exactly to the dimensions of your room and the specific needs of your wardrobe. Unlike one-size-fits-all store systems, a custom build utilizes higher quality materials and ensures no space is wasted, providing a seamless, built-in look.

Can a closet organizer fit into my existing reach-in closet?

Yes, a professional closet organizer system is highly adaptable. It can be installed into standard reach-in closets to replace inefficient single-rod setups with a mix of double-hang sections, shelving, and drawers.

What is the process for designing a walk-in closet?

The process typically begins with an in-home consultation to measure the space and inventory your clothing. This is followed by a 3D design phase where you select finishes and accessories, culminating in a professional installation by skilled craftsmen.

Do you recommend freestanding wardrobe options?

Absolutely. For historic homes or rooms lacking built-in alcoves, a custom freestanding wardrobe provides massive storage capacity while acting as a beautiful, functional piece of furniture that enhances the room's decor.

How long does it take to install custom closets?

Installation time varies based on the scope of the project. A standard reach-in closet can often be completed in a few hours, while a large, complex walk-in closet may take one to three days to perfectly assemble and finish.
