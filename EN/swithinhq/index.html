Frequently Asked Questions

What is involved in a bathroom remodel?

A full bathroom remodel can include everything from updating fixtures and lighting to extensive structural changes like custom walk-in showers, bathtub replacements, new vanity installations, and complete floor-to-ceiling tile installation. The scope is entirely customizable to your needs.

How long does a walk-in shower installation take?

Depending on the complexity of the project and whether structural changes or new plumbing are required, a standard walk-in shower conversion typically takes just a few days. We prioritize efficiency without compromising on the quality of the finish.

Can you replace my existing bathtub without a full remodel?

Yes, absolutely. A standalone bathtub replacement is a very common request. We can remove your old tub and replace it with a modern, high-quality alternative that fits within your existing space, minimizing the overall disruption to your home.

Do you provide custom tile installation?

Yes, custom tile installation is a specialty. Whether you are looking for slip-resistant flooring, a beautiful mosaic backsplash, or large-format tiles for your shower walls, we handle all aspects of the tile work to ensure a waterproof and visually stunning result.

How do I get an estimate for my bathroom renovation?

Simply fill out the contact form below with your project details. A representative will reach out to schedule an initial consultation, discuss your vision, and provide a clear, detailed estimate for your specific bathroom renovation needs.
