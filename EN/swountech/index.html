Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from traditional steam cleaning?

Traditional steam cleaning often uses excessive water and harsh chemicals. The methods we promote use hot carbonating extraction, which requires significantly less water. This means your carpets dry in hours instead of days, reducing the risk of mold and mildew growth.

Do you offer upholstery cleaning for delicate fabrics?

Yes! The upholstery cleaning services featured are designed to be tough on dirt but gentle on your furniture. Specialized equipment and non-toxic solutions safely lift away dirt and allergens, extending the life of your sofas, chairs, and loveseats without damaging delicate weaves.

Can you handle tough pet stains and odors?

Absolutely. Specialized stain removal and pet urine odor removal treatments are available. These processes break down the odor-causing crystals directly at the source rather than just masking the smell with perfumes, effectively saving your carpets from permanent damage.

Is the rug cleaning process safe for oriental and area rugs?

Yes, professional rug cleaning is meticulously tailored to the specific fibers and dyes of your area rugs. Expert technicians evaluate the rug to ensure a safe, customized, and thorough clean without color bleeding or structural damage.

How often should I schedule professional carpet cleaning?

For optimal home health, allergen reduction, and carpet longevity, industry experts recommend professional carpet cleaning every 6 to 12 months. Homes with high foot traffic, children, or indoor pets may benefit from more frequent cleanings.
