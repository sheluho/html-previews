Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about our tire care and maintenance services.

How often should I schedule a tire rotation?

We generally recommend getting a tire rotation every 5,000 to 7,000 miles. Regular rotation prevents uneven tread wear, which can extend the lifespan of your tires and maintain safe handling.

Do you offer seasonal tire storage?

Yes, we provide secure, climate-controlled tire storage. This service is perfect for safely keeping your winter or summer tires in prime condition while they are not in use, freeing up valuable space in your garage.

What is included in your tire installation service?

Our comprehensive tire installation includes the removal of your old tires, professional mounting of the new tires, computerized spin balancing, and installing a new valve stem, ensuring everything is perfectly calibrated for the road.

How do I know when it's time for a tire replacement?

Key indicators include tread depth falling below 2/32 of an inch, visible cracks or bulges in the sidewall, or experiencing excessive vibration while driving. If you are unsure, drop by for a professional inspection.

Why is wheel alignment important after getting new tires?

A proper wheel alignment ensures that all four wheels are pointing in the exact right direction. Without alignment, your brand-new tires may suffer from rapid, uneven wear, reducing their lifespan and negatively affecting your vehicle's steering and fuel efficiency.
