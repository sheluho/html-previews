Common Inquiries

Do you offer both plated dinner and buffet catering options?

Yes, the providers we promote offer a full range of service styles. You can choose an elegant plated dinner for a formal atmosphere or opt for versatile buffet catering to provide your guests with a wider variety of choices. The service style can be customized based on your venue, guest count, and personal preference.

How far in advance should we book our wedding catering?

Wedding catering is highly seasonal and dates fill up quickly. It is generally recommended to secure your caterer 9 to 12 months prior to your wedding date. This ensures availability and provides ample time to schedule tastings, finalize menus, and coordinate logistics with your venue.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions for corporate catering?

Absolutely. Professional corporate catering teams are well-equipped to handle a variety of dietary requirements, including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and specific food allergies. When planning your event, simply provide a list of known restrictions, and a safe, delicious menu will be curated for all attendees.

What is included in your full-service event catering?

Full-service event catering typically encompasses much more than just food preparation. It often includes menu consultation, trained waitstaff and bartenders, setup and breakdown of the dining areas, and the provision of essential serving equipment. Specific inclusions vary by package and provider, which will be clearly detailed during your consultation.

Do you provide tastings for prospective couples?

Yes, tasting sessions are a crucial part of the wedding catering planning process. Once you have established a preliminary proposal with the catering team, a private tasting can be scheduled. This allows you to experience the quality and presentation of the dishes firsthand and make any necessary adjustments to your final menu.
