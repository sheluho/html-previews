Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our premium vehicle care solutions.

What is included in the premium car wash? ▼
The premium car wash includes a thorough exterior hand wash, detailed wheel and tire cleaning, a protective wax application, and a meticulous interior vacuum and wipe-down of all hard surfaces.
How does the auto wash membership work? ▼
The auto wash membership allows you to pay a flat monthly fee for unlimited exterior washes. Members also receive priority service lanes and exclusive discounts on advanced detailing services.
Is a hand car wash better than an automated tunnel? ▼
Yes. A hand car wash is significantly gentler on your vehicle's clear coat. Automated brushes can sometimes hold dirt from previous vehicles, causing micro-scratches. Hand washing ensures specialized care for every contour of your vehicle.
How long does an express wash take? ▼
Our express wash is designed for maximum efficiency, typically taking between 15 to 20 minutes from start to finish, ensuring you get back on the road quickly with a brilliant shine.
Do you clean leather and fabric upholstery? ▼
Yes. As part of our upgraded interior detailing packages, we offer specialized conditioning for leather seats and deep-extraction cleaning for fabric upholstery to remove stains and odors.
