Common Inquiries

How long does it take to manufacture custom blinds? ▼

Because every window is measured and fitted individually, the manufacturing process for custom blinds typically ranges from 3 to 6 weeks. High-demand materials or specialty shapes may require additional time to ensure perfect craftsmanship.

Are plantation shutters energy efficient? ▼

Yes. Plantation shutters are widely considered one of the most energy-efficient window treatments available. They provide an excellent barrier against heat transfer, keeping your home cooler in the summer and warmer in the winter, which can help lower utility costs.

Can roller shades block out all sunlight? ▼

Absolutely. Roller shades are available in various fabric opacities, including dedicated blackout materials. Blackout roller shades are ideal for bedrooms and home theaters where complete darkness and maximum privacy are essential.

What is the ideal application for custom drapes? ▼

Custom drapes are perfect for adding softness, framing large windows, and improving room acoustics. They are frequently layered over existing blinds or shades to provide dual-functionality: sheer light filtering during the day and heavy fabric coverage at night.

How do I prepare for a window treatments consultation? ▼

Simply have an idea of your preferred aesthetic and the primary function you need (e.g., light blocking, privacy, or decor). A professional will handle all the precise measurements and bring material samples directly to your space to ensure a perfect match.
