Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I schedule professional carpet cleaning?

For optimal health and appearance, it is generally recommended to have your carpets professionally steam cleaned every 12 to 18 months. Homes with pets, children, or high foot traffic may benefit from professional cleaning every 6 to 9 months to maintain air quality and fabric longevity.

Is steam cleaning safe for all types of upholstery?

While steam cleaning is highly effective for many fabrics, it is not suitable for everything. Professional technicians evaluate your furniture's material beforehand to determine the safest and most effective upholstery cleaning method, whether that involves hot water extraction or a specialized dry cleaning process.

Can you guarantee complete stain removal?

Experienced technicians utilize advanced stain removal techniques to lift even the most stubborn blemishes. However, the success of removal depends on the age of the stain, the type of carpet or fabric, and whether any previous chemical treatments were applied. The professionals aim for the best possible restoration.

Do I need to move my furniture before the rug cleaning starts?

Most professional cleaning teams will assist in moving light furniture such as chairs and small tables. For larger or highly delicate items, it is recommended to clear the area beforehand to ensure the carpet cleaning process is as thorough and efficient as possible.

How long does it take for carpets to dry after a deep clean?

Drying times vary depending on humidity, airflow, and the specific cleaning method used. Generally, carpets cleaned via hot water extraction (steam cleaning) take between 4 to 8 hours to dry completely. Good ventilation can significantly speed up this process.
