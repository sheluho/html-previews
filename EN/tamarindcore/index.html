Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between custom closets and standard modular shelving?

Custom closets are designed and built specifically for the exact dimensions of your space. Unlike off-the-shelf modular systems, custom solutions maximize every inch, account for architectural quirks, and are constructed with premium materials designed to match your home's aesthetic.

How long does it take to install a walk-in closet?

The installation timeline for a walk-in closet typically ranges from one to three days, depending on the complexity and size of the design. The majority of the work is done off-site during the manufacturing phase, ensuring minimal disruption to your home during the actual setup.

Can a reach-in closet really provide that much extra storage?

Absolutely. A professionally designed reach-in closet often doubles the usable space of a standard single-rod closet. By incorporating double hanging sections, adjustable shelving, and specialized drawers, vertical space is fully utilized, making it highly efficient.

What materials are used for the wardrobe construction?

Premium custom wardrobe systems use a variety of high-quality materials, ranging from durable thermally fused laminates to solid wood veneers. The hardware usually includes heavy-duty, soft-close hinges and drawer slides that can withstand daily use for years.

Do you offer a closet organizer system for pantries and garages?

Yes. The principles of a great closet organizer apply perfectly to kitchen pantries, mudrooms, home offices, and garages. The shelving, cabinetry, and organizational accessories are tailored to the specific weight requirements and storage needs of those distinct spaces.
