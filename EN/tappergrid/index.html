Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our landscaping and lawn care services.

How often should I schedule lawn mowing?

For a pristine, healthy yard, we generally recommend weekly or bi-weekly lawn mowing during the peak growing seasons. The exact frequency depends on your grass type, weather conditions, and personal preference. Consistent lawn care prevents overgrowth and keeps weeds at bay.

Do you offer custom garden design?

Yes! Professional garden design is a cornerstone of our landscape installation services. Experts will work with you to select the right plants, materials, and layout that complement your home's architecture and thrive in your local climate.

What is included in landscape installation?

Landscape installation can include everything from planting new trees, shrubs, and flower beds to laying down fresh sod or mulch. It also covers hardscaping elements like borders, retaining walls, and decorative stone work, ensuring a complete transformation of your property.

Can you handle large tree service jobs?

Absolutely. The teams we promote are fully equipped to handle comprehensive tree service tasks, including safe branch pruning, debris removal, and overall property cleanup. Maintaining healthy trees is vital for both the safety and aesthetic appeal of your landscaping.

When is the best time for landscaping upgrades?

While routine lawn care is year-round, major landscape installations and garden designs are often best planned in the early spring or fall. These seasons offer milder temperatures, which help new plants establish strong root systems before the extreme heat of summer or cold of winter sets in.
