Frequently Asked Questions

What does a full-service wedding planner actually do?
A full-service wedding planner manages the entire lifecycle of your event. We handle budget development, venue scouting, vendor negotiation, aesthetic design, and comprehensive timeline creation, ensuring every element aligns with your vision from day one.
When should we hire a day of coordinator?
Ideally, you should secure a day of coordinator a few months before your wedding. This allows us time to review your contracts, familiarize ourselves with your vendors, and build a cohesive execution plan so you aren't managing logistics on your actual wedding day.
Do you specialize in destination wedding planning?
Yes, we have extensive experience managing destination weddings. We coordinate travel logistics, liaise with international vendors across different time zones, and ensure that the standard of design and execution remains exceptional regardless of the location.
How much involvement will we have in the wedding planning process?
Your involvement is entirely up to your preference. Some couples wish to be involved in every vendor meeting, while others prefer to make high-level decisions and leave the detailed execution to their wedding coordinator. We adapt our communication style to suit your needs.
What is the difference between a venue coordinator and a wedding coordinator?
A venue coordinator works for the venue, ensuring their facilities run smoothly and their specific rules are followed. An independent wedding coordinator works directly for you, managing your external vendors, your personal timeline, and advocating for your specific vision throughout the entire event.
