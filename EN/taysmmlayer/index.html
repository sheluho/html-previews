Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our tire and alignment services.

How does your seasonal tire storage work?

It's simple. When you come in for your seasonal tire replacement, we label and securely store your off-season tires in a specialized climate-controlled facility. When the season changes again, just schedule an appointment and we'll have them ready to mount.

When should I schedule a tire rotation?

We generally recommend a professional tire rotation every 5,000 to 8,000 miles. Regular rotation ensures even tread wear, which maximizes the life of your tires and improves overall vehicle handling.

Do I need a wheel alignment with a new tire replacement?

Yes, it is highly recommended. Getting a precision wheel alignment when installing new tires protects your investment by preventing premature and uneven tire wear right from the start.

What types of tires do you service?

The facilities we promote service all major brands of tires for passenger cars, SUVs, and light trucks. Whether you need winter, all-season, or performance tires, professional installation is available.

How long does a standard tire installation take?

A standard tire installation—including mounting and balancing for a complete set of four tires—typically takes under an hour. If you are also scheduling a wheel alignment, expect the total service time to be slightly longer.
