Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in tire storage?

Tire storage includes safe, dry, and climate-monitored environments for your off-season tires. They are fully insured and kept away from harmful UV rays and moisture until you need them installed again.

Do you perform wheel alignment?

While mobile services primarily focus on installation and tire rotation, specialized partners can handle complete wheel alignment at their stationary facilities to ensure your vehicle tracks perfectly straight.

How often should I get a tire rotation?

It is generally recommended to schedule a tire rotation every 5,000 to 8,000 miles. Regular rotation promotes even tread wear, which extends the life of the tires and maintains optimal handling.

When is it time for tire replacement?

You should consider tire replacement when the tread depth falls below 2/32 of an inch, if you notice significant uneven wear, visible cracks in the sidewall, or if the tires are more than six years old.

How does the mobile installation process work?

A certified technician arrives at your home or workplace with your stored or newly purchased tires. They safely lift your vehicle, perform the tire installation and balancing, and take your old set away for storage or recycling.
