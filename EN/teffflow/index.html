Frequently Asked Questions

What are the benefits of upgrading my home insulation?

Upgrading your home insulation provides better temperature regulation, significantly reduces your monthly heating and cooling bills, lessens noise from outside, and improves your property's overall energy efficiency rating.

How do I choose between vinyl siding and fiber cement siding?

Vinyl siding is cost-effective, low-maintenance, and comes in many colors. Fiber cement siding offers a more premium look, exceptional durability, and high fire resistance. The best choice depends on your budget, climate, and aesthetic goals.

Is spray foam insulation better than traditional options?

Spray foam is often considered superior because it expands to fill gaps and cracks, creating an airtight seal that traditional fiberglass batts cannot achieve. This results in maximum thermal resistance and moisture control.

How long does a typical house siding installation take?

Most average-sized home siding projects can be completed in one to two weeks, depending on the weather, the size of your home, and the type of materials being installed.

Can proper attic insulation really lower my utility bills?

Absolutely. Because heat rises, an under-insulated attic is the primary source of heat loss in the winter. Adding high-quality attic insulation traps that air inside, drastically reducing the workload on your HVAC system.
