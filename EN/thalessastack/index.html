Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our cleaning processes.

How is your process different from traditional steam cleaning?

Traditional steam cleaning typically floods carpets with hot water mixed with soaps or detergents. If not extracted perfectly, this leaves a sticky residue that attracts new dirt rapidly. Our method uses a patented, purified fluid that cleans effectively without detergents, meaning zero residue is left behind and your carpet stays cleaner, longer.

How long will it take for my carpets to dry?

Because we don't flood the carpet padding with soapy water, our drying times are significantly faster than industry averages. Most carpets are dry to the touch within a few hours, depending on humidity and airflow in your home.

Can you remove severe pet stains and odors?

Yes. Standard surface cleaning rarely fixes deep pet issues. We offer specialized stain removal and biological treatments that penetrate the carpet fibers and backing to break down odor-causing molecules, rather than just masking them with perfumes.

Is upholstery cleaning safe for all fabric types?

Our technicians are trained to identify different fabric types before beginning any upholstery cleaning. We adjust our moisture levels, pressure, and techniques to ensure delicate natural fibers are cleaned safely, while synthetic blends get the robust extraction they need.

How often should I schedule professional rug cleaning?

For high-traffic areas, we recommend professional rug cleaning every 12 to 18 months. Regular maintenance removes abrasive dirt particles that slowly cut and degrade rug fibers over time, extending the life and vibrancy of your investment.
