Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a turnkey kitchen renovation?
A turnkey kitchen renovation means the entire process is managed for you from start to finish. This typically includes initial design consultations, 3D modeling, demolition, plumbing and electrical updates, installation of custom cabinets and countertops, flooring, and final finishing touches. You simply turn the key and walk into a completed space.
Are the cabinets truly custom-built for my space?
Yes. The providers we promote specialize in custom kitchen cabinets that are measured and crafted to fit the exact dimensions of your room. This allows for maximizing storage in awkward corners, accommodating specific appliance sizes, and achieving a seamless, built-in look that stock cabinetry cannot provide.
What types of countertops do you recommend?
The choice of countertops depends heavily on your lifestyle and aesthetic goals. Quartz is highly popular for its durability and low maintenance, while granite offers unique, natural patterns. Marble provides unparalleled elegance for baking areas but requires more care. During the design phase, experts will help you select the perfect material for your custom kitchen.
How long does a typical kitchen remodeling project take?
Timelines vary significantly based on the scope of the project. A straightforward update replacing just cabinets and countertops might take a few weeks once materials arrive. However, a full-scale renovation involving structural changes, moving plumbing, and custom fabrication can take several months from planning to completion.
Do I need to change my layout during a remodel?
Not necessarily. If your current layout functions well, you can achieve a dramatic transformation simply by upgrading to new custom cabinets, fresh countertops, and modern appliances in the existing footprint. This approach often saves time and reduces costs associated with moving electrical and plumbing lines.
