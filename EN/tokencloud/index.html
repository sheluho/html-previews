Frequently Asked Questions

What types of custom window treatments do you feature?

We feature a comprehensive selection of window coverings, including custom blinds, roller shades, elegant custom drapes, and durable plantation shutters to fit any window size or shape.

Are plantation shutters suitable for high-humidity areas like bathrooms?

Yes. Many of our partners offer composite or specialized plantation shutters that are specifically designed to resist moisture, making them perfect for bathrooms and kitchens.

How do I know which shades or blinds will fit my windows?

Through our partner network, professional measuring services are typically available to ensure that your custom blinds or shades are tailored precisely to the exact dimensions of your windows.

Can I motorize my new roller shades?

Absolutely. Motorization is a highly popular option for roller shades and custom blinds. You can control your window treatments via remote, smartphone app, or integrate them into your smart home system.

Do custom drapes help with home insulation?

Yes, custom drapes with proper lining provide excellent thermal insulation. They can help keep your home cooler in the summer and warmer in the winter, potentially reducing energy costs.
