Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about commissioning bespoke furniture.

How long does it take to build a custom dining table?

The timeline for a custom dining table typically ranges from 8 to 12 weeks. This allows the artisan to source the perfect premium hardwoods, meticulously craft the joints, and apply a flawless, durable finish.

Can I choose the fabric for my custom sofa?

Absolutely. When you visit a recommended custom furniture maker and showroom, you will have access to an extensive library of premium fabrics, leathers, and performance materials to perfectly match your interior design.

What makes bespoke furniture better than store-bought?

Bespoke furniture is tailored precisely to your spatial requirements and design tastes. Unlike mass-produced items, handmade furniture utilizes solid wood construction, traditional joinery, and superior finishes ensuring it lasts for generations rather than just a few years.

Do you provide design consultations?

Yes, the showrooms we feature employ experienced design consultants. They will work closely with you to understand your vision, measure your space, and draft detailed plans before the building process begins.

Where is the handmade furniture built?

We pride ourselves on promoting local artisans and domestic workshops. By choosing a custom furniture maker through our platform, you are directly supporting skilled craftsmen dedicated to the art of woodworking within your region.
