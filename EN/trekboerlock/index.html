Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our premium car wash services and membership options.

What is included in the auto wash membership?
The auto wash membership grants you unlimited access to our express wash services throughout the month. Members also receive priority lane access and exclusive discounts on our premium hand car wash and full detailing packages.
Is the premium hand car wash safe for ceramic coatings?
Absolutely. Our premium hand car wash uses pH-neutral soaps, ultra-soft microfiber mitts, and specialized techniques designed specifically to maintain and protect delicate finishes, including ceramic coatings, wraps, and custom paint jobs.
How fast is the express wash?
The express wash is designed for maximum efficiency, typically taking under 5 minutes from start to finish. It utilizes advanced, safe-touch technology to deliver a brilliant clean and spot-free dry, perfect for busy schedules.
Do I need to book an appointment in advance?
No appointment is necessary for our express wash services; you can simply drive up. However, for our premium hand car wash and extensive detailing packages, we highly recommend scheduling ahead to guarantee a dedicated time slot.
Can I cancel my auto wash membership at any time?
Yes, our auto wash membership is completely flexible. There are no long-term contracts or hidden cancellation fees. You can cancel or pause your membership easily prior to your next billing cycle.
