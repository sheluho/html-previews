Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the premium car wash?

Our premium car wash includes a meticulous hand wash, wheel and tire detailing, undercarriage flush, and the application of a high-grade protective sealant to enhance your vehicle's gloss and safeguard the clear coat.

How does the auto wash membership work?

The membership is a monthly subscription granting you access to an unlimited number of express washes or a set number of hand car wash sessions, depending on the tier you select. It is designed for maximum convenience and consistent vehicle care.

Do you offer interior detailing services?

Yes, interior detailing is a core component of our offerings. We provide deep extraction for carpets, leather conditioning, odor removal, and precise cleaning of all interior panels and glass surfaces.

Is the express wash safe for my car's paint?

Absolutely. Our express wash utilizes touchless technology and extremely soft microfiber materials combined with pH-balanced soaps to ensure dirt is safely lubricated and rinsed away without scratching your vehicle's paint.

How long does a hand car wash take?

A standard premium hand car wash typically takes between 45 minutes to an hour, ensuring every detail is addressed without rushing the process. For extensive detailing, we recommend allocating a half-day.
