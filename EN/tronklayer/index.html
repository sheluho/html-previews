Common Questions

Everything you need to know about preparing for your new space.

How long does a typical custom closet installation take?

Most standard installations for a reach-in closet or a mid-sized walk-in closet are completed within a single day. Larger, more complex projects involving extensive custom wardrobes or multiple rooms may require two to three days. The exact timeline is always discussed during the final design consultation.

Do you offer solutions tailored for very small reach-in closets?

Absolutely. Small spaces often benefit the most from a professional closet organizer. By utilizing adjustable shelving, layered hanging rods, and strategic accessory placement, we can dramatically increase the usable volume of even the most compact hall or bedroom closet.

Can I customize the finishes and hardware of my new wardrobe?

Yes, customization is at the core of the service. You can select from a wide array of wood grains, solid colors, and textured finishes. Additionally, hardware options like drawer pulls, handles, and hanging rods come in various metals and styles to perfectly complement your home's interior design.

What components are typically included in a closet organizer system?

A comprehensive system usually includes a mix of long-hang and short-hang sections, adjustable open shelving for sweaters and shoes, and soft-close drawers for undergarments. We also frequently incorporate specialized accessories like pull-out hampers, tie racks, and velvet-lined jewelry trays based on your needs.

Do I need to clear out my existing space before the installation day?

Yes, the area must be completely empty before the installation team arrives. This ensures a safe working environment and allows the technicians to properly prepare the walls, remove any old wire shelving or baseboards if necessary, and install your new custom closets efficiently.
