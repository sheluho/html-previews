Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to design and install custom closets?

The timeline varies depending on the complexity and scale of the project. A standard reach-in closet organizer can often be designed and installed within a few weeks, while an expansive, highly customized walk-in closet may take longer to perfect. We will provide a clear timeline during your initial design consultation.

Can you maximize the space in a very small reach-in closet?

Absolutely. Small spaces are where intelligent design truly shines. By utilizing vertical height, implementing tiered hanging rods, and adding specialized shallow drawers, we can dramatically increase the functional storage capacity of even the most compact reach-in closets.

Do you offer adjustable wardrobe organizer systems?

Yes, many of our wardrobe configurations feature adjustable shelving and track systems. This allows your closet to evolve as your wardrobe changes over time. You can easily reconfigure shelf heights to accommodate new boots, longer coats, or different storage boxes without needing a total redesign.

What materials are used for your wardrobe installations?

Our promotional partners utilize premium, durable materials designed to withstand daily wear and tear. This typically includes high-grade melamine, solid wood veneers, and robust metal hardware for hanging rods and drawer glides. A variety of finishes are available to seamlessly match your home's interior decor.

How do I prepare my space before the walk-in closet installation?

Prior to installation day, we ask that you completely clear out your existing closet, removing all clothing, boxes, and loose items. If existing wire shelving needs to be removed, this is generally handled prior to the new installation. We will provide a comprehensive preparation checklist well in advance of our arrival.
