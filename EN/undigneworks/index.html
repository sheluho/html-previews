Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a standard house cleaning?

A standard professional house cleaning generally includes dusting, vacuuming, mopping floors, and thoroughly cleaning the bathrooms and kitchen surfaces. It is designed to maintain a consistently high level of cleanliness throughout your home.

How often should I schedule recurring cleaning?

Scheduling for recurring cleaning depends entirely on your lifestyle, household size, and preferences. Most of our clients opt for weekly or bi-weekly maid service to ensure their environment stays continuously fresh without becoming unmanageable.

What makes deep cleaning different from a regular service?

Deep cleaning goes far beyond surface-level tidying. It involves intensive scrubbing of neglected areas, such as baseboards, interior windows, inside appliances, and hard-to-reach corners. It requires more time and effort than a standard visit.

Do I need to be home during the move-out cleaning?

No, you do not need to be present. In fact, many clients prefer to grant access to the property once it is empty so the cleaning crew can efficiently perform the comprehensive move-out cleaning without any obstructions.

Are the cleaning products safe for pets and children?

The professional cleaning providers we connect you with prioritize your health and safety. You can discuss specific product preferences, including green cleaning options, directly with the maid service to ensure a safe environment for your family.
