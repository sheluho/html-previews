Frequently Asked Questions

What does a full-service bathroom remodeling company do?

We handle every aspect of your bathroom renovation from start to finish. This includes layout design, demolition, plumbing updates, bathtub replacement, walk-in shower installation, and final tile installation, ensuring a seamless and cohesive result.

How long does a typical bathroom renovation take?

Timelines vary based on the scope of the project. A straightforward bathtub replacement or walk-in shower conversion might be completed rapidly, while a complete bathroom remodel with intricate custom tile installation will take longer. We provide a clear timeline during the consultation phase.

Can you convert my old tub into a walk-in shower?

Absolutely. Tub-to-shower conversions are one of our most popular services. We safely remove the old unit, prepare the plumbing and waterproofing, and install a spacious, modern walk-in shower tailored to your bathroom's dimensions.

Do you assist with selecting materials for tile installation?

Yes. A successful bathroom remodel relies heavily on the right materials. We guide you through options for flooring, wall tiles, and accents, ensuring the chosen materials are durable, water-resistant, and align with your desired aesthetic.

Is my bathroom layout restricted to the current plumbing?

Not necessarily. While keeping the existing layout is often more cost-effective, a full bathroom renovation allows for reconfiguring plumbing lines to optimize space. We evaluate your home's structure during planning to determine the feasibility of moving fixtures.
