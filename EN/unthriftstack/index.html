Frequently Asked Questions

What types of kitchen cabinets do you feature? ▼

We feature a wide variety of styles, with a strong focus on high-quality custom cabinets and timeless shaker cabinets. Whether you prefer a traditional look, a transitional feel, or a sleek modern aesthetic, our partner's manufacturing capabilities can accommodate your vision seamlessly.

Can I view the cabinets in person before making a decision? ▼

Absolutely. We highly recommend visiting the dedicated kitchen showroom. Seeing the detailed displays in person allows you to appreciate the craftsmanship, test the smooth-glide hardware, and compare different solid wood finishes side-by-side.

Do you offer professional kitchen design services? ▼

Yes, expert kitchen design is a core part of the process. Experienced designers will work closely with you to measure your space, discuss your daily functional needs, and create a comprehensive layout that maximizes both beauty and utility.

Are the cabinets manufactured locally? ▼

Yes, the products we promote are proudly manufactured in the USA by the American Made Cabinet Company. This ensures rigorous quality control, supports local craftsmanship, and often results in more reliable and transparent delivery timelines.

How long does the custom cabinetry process take? ▼

Timelines can vary based on the complexity of your specific kitchen design and current manufacturing schedules. Generally, after the final design is approved and measurements are verified, production takes several weeks. Your designer will provide a detailed timeline during your initial consultation.
