Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical window installation take? ▼

Most standard residential window installation projects can be completed in one to two days, depending on the number of windows and the specific requirements of your home's structure.

Why should I choose vinyl windows? ▼

Vinyl windows are incredibly popular because they offer excellent thermal insulation, require virtually no maintenance, and do not peel, crack, or warp over time like traditional wood frames might.

Will energy efficient windows really lower my utility bills? ▼

Yes. By minimizing heat transfer, energy efficient windows reduce the workload on your HVAC system. Over time, the savings on heating and cooling costs can be substantial, helping offset the initial investment.

Are double hung windows secure? ▼

Absolutely. Modern double hung windows come equipped with robust locking mechanisms and durable frames, providing excellent security for your home alongside their operational convenience.

When is the best time of year to install replacement windows? ▼

While spring and fall are popular due to mild weather, professional installation teams are equipped to replace windows year-round. They use techniques to minimize indoor temperature fluctuations during the process.
