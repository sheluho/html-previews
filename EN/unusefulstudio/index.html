Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a turnkey kitchen renovation?

A turnkey kitchen project includes absolutely everything required to complete your renovation. This covers the initial design phase, material sourcing, demolition, electrical and plumbing work, custom cabinets installation, countertops fitting, and final finishing touches.

Do you design custom kitchen cabinets for unique spaces?

Yes. Our partners specialize in designing and building custom cabinets that fit perfectly into unconventional spaces, ensuring you get maximum storage efficiency without compromising on aesthetics.

How long does a typical kitchen remodeling project take?

Timelines vary depending on the scope of work and material availability. However, a standard full-scale custom kitchen remodeling project usually takes between 4 to 8 weeks from demolition to final walkthrough.

What types of countertops do you offer?

We provide access to a wide range of premium countertops, including high-grade granite, durable quartz, and luxurious marble. The team will help you select the best material suited for your cooking habits and design preferences.

Do I need to hire my own contractors for plumbing and electrical?

No, you do not. Our full-service approach means that all necessary trades, including licensed plumbers and electricians, are coordinated and managed as part of your turnkey kitchen package.
