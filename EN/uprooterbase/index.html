Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about planning your kitchen renovation.

What is included in a turnkey kitchen renovation?

A turnkey kitchen renovation covers the entire project lifecycle from start to finish. This includes initial design consultations, demolition, plumbing and electrical updates, installation of custom kitchen cabinets, premium countertops, and final finishing touches. You deal with a single point of contact rather than juggling multiple contractors.

Can I fully customize my cabinets and countertops?

Absolutely. Customization is the heart of a bespoke kitchen. You can choose from a wide variety of materials, finishes, hardware, and structural layouts for your cabinets. Similarly, countertops can be selected based on your preference for materials like granite, quartz, or marble, ensuring they perfectly match your design vision.

How long does a typical kitchen remodeling project take?

The timeline varies based on the scope of the project. A standard full-service kitchen renovation typically takes anywhere from 4 to 8 weeks once construction begins. Turnkey kitchen projects are optimized for efficiency, minimizing downtime and disruption to your daily life while maintaining high standards of craftsmanship.

Do you offer design assistance for a custom kitchen?

Yes, professional design assistance is a critical component of any premium remodeling service. Expert designers work closely with you to create blueprints and 3D renderings, ensuring the proposed custom kitchen maximizes both space and aesthetic appeal before any physical work begins.

What materials are best for high-traffic kitchen countertops?

For high-traffic kitchens, durability is key. Quartz is highly recommended due to its non-porous nature, making it resistant to stains and scratches without requiring regular sealing. Granite is another excellent, heat-resistant option that adds natural beauty and robust longevity to your cooking space.
