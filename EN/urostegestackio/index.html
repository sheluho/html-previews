Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer tastings for wedding catering?

Yes, we highly recommend scheduling a tasting session for all prospective wedding catering clients. This allows you to experience the quality of the cuisine firsthand and collaborate with the chefs to finalize your perfect menu.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions for a plated dinner?

Absolutely. The culinary teams we partner with are experienced in crafting delicious alternatives for various dietary needs, including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and nut-free options. Just let us know your requirements during the planning phase.

How far in advance should we book your event catering services?

We suggest booking as early as possible to secure your desired date, especially for large-scale wedding catering or major corporate events. Ideally, reaching out 6 to 12 months in advance ensures ample time for planning and menu refinement.

Do you provide staff for corporate catering events?

Yes, comprehensive corporate catering packages typically include professional serving staff, bartenders, and on-site coordinators to ensure your event runs smoothly from setup to breakdown, allowing you to focus on your guests.

What is included in your standard buffet catering package?

Standard buffet catering packages generally include the selected food items, high-quality chafing dishes to maintain temperature, elegant serving utensils, and staff to manage the buffet line and replenish dishes. Linens and tableware can also be arranged based on your specific venue needs.
