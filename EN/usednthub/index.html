Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about the wedding photography services we promote.

What is typically included in wedding photography packages? ▼

Most packages feature comprehensive day-of coverage, a secondary wedding photographer, professional editing, and a private online gallery. Many studios also offer printed albums and high-resolution digital downloads.

Do the studios you feature travel for destination wedding photography? ▼

Yes! We actively promote studios that specialize in destination wedding photography. They are equipped to travel globally to document your beautiful getaway ceremony.

When should we schedule our engagement photos or bridal portraits? ▼

It is highly recommended to schedule your engagement photos 6 to 9 months before the wedding. Bridal portraits are typically done 1 to 2 months prior to the big day to ensure everything is perfect.

How long does it take to receive our final wedding photographer gallery? ▼

Turnaround times vary by the studio you select, but you can generally expect to receive your fully edited wedding photography gallery within 4 to 8 weeks after your ceremony.

Can we provide a shot list for our wedding photography? ▼

Absolutely. The professionals we connect you with encourage couples to share a list of must-have family combinations and specific creative shots to ensure your vision is realized.
