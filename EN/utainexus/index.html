Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the carpet take to dry?

Unlike traditional steam cleaning which can leave carpets wet for 12 to 24 hours, the oxygen-powered, low-moisture system we promote typically allows carpets to dry in just about one hour.

Is the carpet cleaning process safe for pets and children?

Yes. The core cleaning solutions utilized are eco-friendly, non-toxic, and considered safe for use in homes with children and pets once applied correctly by professionals.

Do you offer upholstery cleaning for delicate fabrics?

Absolutely. The upholstery cleaning services featured employ specialized techniques that are tough on embedded dirt but exceptionally gentle on delicate furniture fabrics.

Can you handle tough stain removal?

Yes, advanced spot treatments are available to tackle the most stubborn stains, including pet accidents, wine spills, and heavily trafficked areas, breaking them down at the molecular level.

Why is this better than traditional steam cleaning?

Traditional steam cleaning relies on pumping gallons of water into your carpet, which can compress the pad and lead to mold or mildew if not dried properly. The low-moisture alternative uses 95% less water, preventing these issues entirely.
