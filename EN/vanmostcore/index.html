Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our wedding planning services.

What exactly does a full-service wedding planner do?
A full-service wedding planner handles your event from start to finish. This includes budget creation, venue scouting, vendor contract negotiations, design conceptualization, tracking RSVPs, and overseeing the entire wedding weekend. We serve as your primary point of contact for everything related to your wedding.
Do I need a day of coordinator if my venue provides one?
Yes. A venue coordinator works for the venue to ensure the building, food, and in-house staff are functioning properly. A dedicated day of coordinator works exclusively for you. We manage your timeline, assist the wedding party, set up personal decor, coordinate with external vendors, and ensure your unique vision is executed properly.
Can you help plan a destination wedding?
Absolutely. Our destination wedding services are comprehensive. We assist with finding the perfect remote location, managing travel logistics for guests, securing reliable local vendors, and designing a multi-day itinerary that provides an incredible experience for everyone attending.
When should I hire a wedding coordinator?
If you are seeking full-service wedding planning, it is best to hire us immediately after getting engaged—ideally 12 to 18 months before your desired date. If you are looking for a day of coordinator, we recommend securing our services at least 6 months in advance to ensure availability and a smooth handover process.
Are you familiar with Dallas wedding venues?
Yes, we have extensive experience operating in the Dallas area. We maintain strong relationships with top-tier Dallas venues and trusted local vendors, ensuring your event benefits from our local expertise and established professional network.
