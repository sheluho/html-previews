Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I schedule lawn mowing?

For optimal health and appearance during the active growing season, we recommend weekly lawn mowing. This frequency prevents the grass from becoming overgrown, which can stress the turf and lead to disease when cut too drastically. We adjust the schedule based on seasonal growth patterns and weather conditions.

Do you provide custom garden design?

Yes, our landscape installation process begins with a comprehensive garden design phase. We evaluate your property's soil, sunlight exposure, and existing architecture to create a cohesive plan. Whether you prefer a low-maintenance modern look or a lush, traditional garden, we tailor the design to your specific aesthetic and functional goals.

What does your tree service include?

Our comprehensive tree service covers everything from routine pruning and structural trimming to health assessments and disease management. We also handle the safe removal of dead or hazardous trees and provide stump grinding services to clear your landscape for new growth.

Do you service commercial properties?

Absolutely. We provide dedicated landscaping and lawn care solutions for commercial clients, including retail centers, office parks, and HOA communities. We understand that a well-maintained commercial property is vital for making a strong first impression on clients and residents.

When is the best time for new landscape installation?

While hardscaping can often be installed year-round, the optimal time for planting varies by region. Generally, early spring and early fall are ideal for adding new plants, shrubs, and turf, as the moderate temperatures and natural rainfall help establish root systems before extreme summer heat or winter freezes.
