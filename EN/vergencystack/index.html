Frequently Asked Questions

Do you manufacture the custom cabinets locally?

Yes, our featured cabinetry is proudly crafted by skilled artisans. As a dedicated kitchen cabinets showroom and manufacturer partner, we ensure all woodwork meets rigorous standards of quality and durability before it ever reaches your home.

What should I bring to my kitchen showroom appointment?

We recommend bringing rough measurements of your space, any architectural blueprints if you are building new, and inspiration photos. This allows our kitchen design experts to understand your vision and provide more accurate recommendations.

Are shaker cabinets still a popular choice?

Absolutely. Shaker cabinets remain one of the most requested styles due to their clean lines and timeless appeal. They transition beautifully between traditional, transitional, and contemporary kitchen design themes.

Can you match a specific wood stain or paint color?

Yes, custom color matching is a significant part of our custom cabinets offering. Whether you want a specific vibrant hue or a natural stain that matches your existing hardwood floors, our finishing department can accommodate your request.

How long does the kitchen design and manufacturing process take?

Timelines vary based on the complexity of the project and current production schedules. Typically, after the final kitchen design is approved, manufacturing takes several weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during your showroom consultation.
