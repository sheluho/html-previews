Common Questions

What is included in full-service wedding planning?

Full-service wedding planning is our most comprehensive offering. It includes budget creation, venue scouting, vendor matching, contract negotiation, complete event design, timeline creation, and full wedding coordination on the day of the event. We are by your side for every decision.

When should we hire a day of coordinator?

We recommend booking your day of coordinator at least 4 to 6 months in advance. Although we manage the actual wedding day, we step in several weeks prior to review your contracts, contact your vendors, and finalize a seamless timeline to ensure nothing is overlooked.

Do you travel for a destination wedding?

Absolutely. We have extensive experience managing destination weddings across the globe. We handle the complex logistics of international travel, sourcing local vendors, and planning multi-day events like welcome dinners and farewell brunches for your guests.

How is a wedding planner different from a venue coordinator?

A venue coordinator works for the venue and focuses primarily on food, beverage, and facility operations. As your dedicated wedding planner, we work exclusively for you. We manage your entire vendor team, personal details, timeline, and overall design to ensure your specific vision is realized.

Can you help us stay within our wedding budget?

Yes. Budget management is a key component of our full-service wedding planning. We help you allocate your funds effectively, prioritize elements that matter most to you, and connect you with trusted vendors who align with your financial parameters without compromising on quality or style.
