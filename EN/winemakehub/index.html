Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide tastings for wedding catering?

Yes, we strongly encourage a tasting session for our wedding catering clients. This allows you to experience our culinary style, sample potential menu items, and collaborate directly with our chefs to ensure the final plated dinner or buffet catering selection is exactly as you envisioned for your big day.

Can you accommodate specific dietary restrictions?

Absolutely. Our event catering team is highly experienced in designing menus that accommodate various dietary needs, including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and allergy-specific requirements. Just let us know during the planning phase, and we will ensure all your guests enjoy a safe and delicious meal.

What is the difference between a plated dinner and buffet catering?

A plated dinner is a more formal, structured service where seated guests are served individual courses directly by our waitstaff. Buffet catering offers a more relaxed, interactive experience where guests can choose from a variety of beautifully displayed dishes at self-serve or chef-attended stations. We can advise on which style best suits your event's flow.

Do you handle corporate catering for multi-day events?

Yes, we provide comprehensive corporate catering for single-day meetings, multi-day conferences, and company retreats. We can manage all food and beverage needs, from morning coffee and continental breakfasts to working lunches and elegant evening networking receptions.

How far in advance should I book my event catering?

For wedding catering, we recommend booking 9 to 12 months in advance to secure your preferred date, especially during peak seasons. For corporate catering and private events, 4 to 8 weeks is generally sufficient, though we always try to accommodate shorter notice requests when our schedule permits.
