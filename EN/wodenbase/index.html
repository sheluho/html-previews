Frequently Asked Questions

What styles of kitchen cabinets do you manufacture? ▼

We specialize in a wide range of styles, from traditional raised-panel designs to modern, flat-panel European aesthetics. Our most popular requests are for custom shaker cabinets, known for their versatility and timeless appeal. Because we manufacture custom cabinets, we can match virtually any style or finish you desire.

Can I visit a kitchen showroom to view materials? ▼

Yes. Visiting the kitchen showroom is a critical step in the kitchen design process. You will be able to feel the quality of the wood, test the hardware mechanisms (like soft-close hinges and drawer glides), and view various stain and paint finishes under different lighting conditions.

Do you provide custom kitchen design services? ▼

Absolutely. Professional kitchen design is integral to our offering. The design team will work with your floor plan to optimize layout, improve storage efficiency, and ensure that your new custom cabinets perfectly complement your home's architecture.

Are you a direct manufacturer? ▼

Yes, the partner provider operates as both a kitchen cabinets showroom and manufacturer. This allows for strict quality control over every piece of millwork, faster turnaround times on custom modifications, and direct accountability from the initial design to the final installation.

How long does the custom cabinet process take? ▼

Timelines vary based on the complexity of the kitchen design and current manufacturing schedules. Generally, after the final design is approved and measurements are verified, manufacturing takes several weeks. Your designer will provide a specific timeline during your initial consultation.
