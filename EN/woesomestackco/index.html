Frequently Asked Questions

What is the process for designing custom closets?

Our process begins with an in-home or virtual consultation to measure your space and discuss your storage needs. We then create a 3D digital model of your new walk-in closet or reach-in closet. Once you approve the design, our craftsmen build your system, and our certified team handles the final installation.

Do you offer solutions for small reach-in closets?

Absolutely. We specialize in maximizing tight spaces. A well-designed closet organizer in a reach-in closet can double or even triple your usable storage space through the intelligent use of double-hang rods, adjustable shelving, and specialized drawers.

What materials are used in your closet organizers?

We use only the highest quality, durable materials for our custom closets. This includes heavy-duty thermally fused laminate panels, robust metal hardware, soft-close hinges, and premium wood finishes designed to withstand daily wear and tear for years.

How long does a wardrobe installation take?

Most standard wardrobe and reach-in closet installations are completed within a single day. Larger, more complex walk-in closets may take two days. Our team ensures a clean, efficient process that minimizes disruption to your daily life.

Can I customize the shelving and drawer layout?

Yes, full customization is the core of our service. Your closet organizer system is built entirely around your wardrobe. You decide the ratio of hanging space, the number of drawers, shoe racks, and specialized accessories like jewelry trays or tie racks.
