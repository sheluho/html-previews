Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries regarding auto glass replacement and repair processes.

How long does a typical windshield replacement take?
Typically, a full windshield replacement takes about an hour to complete physically. However, it requires an additional hour minimum for the high-strength urethane adhesive to cure safely before the vehicle is ready to drive.
Can my damaged windshield be repaired instead of replaced?
If the damage is a chip smaller than a quarter and is not located in the driver's direct line of sight or at the very edge of the glass, a professional chip repair is usually possible, effective, and highly recommended.
Do I need to bring my car into a shop for auto glass service?
Not always. Many of the specialized providers we promote offer convenient mobile auto glass services. Their technicians can come directly to your home or workplace to perform the windshield replacement or repair on-site.
Will a windshield repair completely hide the original damage?
While a professional windshield repair fully restores the structural integrity of the glass and significantly improves its visual appearance by clearing out the break, a faint blemish or outline of the original chip may still be slightly visible.
How soon can I wash my car after a car window replacement?
It is generally recommended by experts to wait at least 24 to 48 hours before using a high-pressure commercial car wash. This allows the new seals and urethane around the auto glass to fully cure and set without disturbance.
