Frequently Asked Questions

How does the seasonal tire storage work?

When you come in for your seasonal tire swap, the facility will carefully clean, label, and safely store your off-season tires in a climate-controlled warehouse. When the next season arrives, simply book an appointment, and your tires will be ready for installation.

How often should I schedule a tire rotation?

It is generally recommended to get a tire rotation every 5,000 to 7,500 miles, or at the same time as your routine oil change. This ensures even tread wear and extends the overall life of your tires.

Does wheel alignment come with new tires?

While a standard tire replacement includes mounting and balancing, a wheel alignment is typically a separate service. However, it is highly encouraged to check your alignment when installing new tires to prevent premature and uneven wear on your new investment.

Can I just get a tire replacement without storage?

Absolutely. If you have space at home or are simply purchasing new tires to replace worn-out ones, you can utilize the tire installation service as a standalone option. Tire storage is completely optional.

Are my stored tires insured against damage?

Yes, when you use the tire storage facilities provided by our partners, your tires are kept in a secure, monitored environment. Please consult the provider directly during drop-off for specific details regarding their facility's liability and coverage policies.
