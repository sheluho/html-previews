Frequently Asked Questions

What does a turnkey kitchen remodel include?

A turnkey kitchen remodel covers everything from the initial design and material selection to demolition, plumbing, electrical work, and final installation. It ensures a seamless process without you having to manage multiple contractors or coordinate complex timelines.

Can I choose custom cabinets for my space?

Absolutely. Custom kitchen cabinets are a core component of a high-end renovation. They are designed specifically to fit your unique layout, maximizing storage efficiency while perfectly matching your architectural and aesthetic preferences.

What types of countertops are recommended?

We highlight a variety of premium countertops, including durable quartz, classic granite, and luxurious marble. The best choice depends heavily on your daily cooking habits, maintenance preferences, and your overarching design vision for the room.

How long does a full kitchen remodeling project take?

Timelines vary based on the complexity and scope of the project. However, a comprehensive kitchen remodeling endeavor typically takes between 6 to 12 weeks of active construction once all custom materials are ordered and delivered to the site.

Do I need to have my own design before starting?

Not at all. The full-service approach includes professional design consultations. Experts will work closely with you to envision, draft, and refine your custom kitchen layout before any demolition or construction begins.
