Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to help you plan your next home remodeling project.

What exactly is included in a turnkey renovation?

A turnkey renovation covers the entire scope of your project from start to finish. This includes initial consultations, architectural design, securing necessary permits, sourcing all materials, and executing the full construction phase. As your general contractor, we manage the entire workflow so you don't have to coordinate multiple separate vendors.

How do I prepare for a whole home remodeling project?

Preparation begins with establishing your priorities and budget. Think about how you want your space to function, collect design inspirations, and clear out personal belongings from the areas being renovated. Once engaged, we will provide a detailed timeline and preparation checklist specific to your project's scope.

Do you handle the architectural design as well as construction?

Yes. We utilize a design-build approach, meaning both the architectural design and the construction phases are managed under one roof. This ensures seamless communication, reduces the risk of errors, and often speeds up the overall home renovation timeline.

How long does a typical home addition take?

The timeline for a home addition varies significantly based on complexity, size, and local permitting processes. Generally, design and permitting can take a few months, while the physical construction phase might take anywhere from 3 to 6 months. We provide a tailored schedule during your initial consultation.

Why should I hire a general contractor instead of managing it myself?

Managing a whole home remodeling project involves coordinating plumbers, electricians, carpenters, inspectors, and suppliers. A professional general contractor takes on this immense logistical burden, mitigating risks, ensuring building code compliance, and keeping the project on schedule and budget, ultimately saving you time and stress.
