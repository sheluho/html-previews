Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about preparing for your cinematic wedding video.

How far in advance should we book our wedding videographer?
It is highly recommended to secure your wedding videographer at least 9 to 12 months before your wedding date. Popular dates in the spring and fall tend to book up exceptionally fast. Early booking ensures you get the cinematic wedding video team of your choice and allows ample time for pre-production planning.
What makes a cinematic wedding video different from traditional videography?
A cinematic wedding video goes beyond merely recording the events of the day. It employs high-end equipment, creative angles, and expert storytelling techniques. During post-production, professional editing, color grading, and audio mixing are used to craft a compelling, movie-like narrative that captures the true emotion of your wedding film.
Do you provide raw footage along with the final wedding film?
The primary focus is always on delivering a polished, emotionally resonant cinematic wedding video. However, depending on the specific event videography package you choose, unedited raw footage can often be provided as an add-on or included in higher-tier packages for archival purposes.
How long does it take to receive our final cinematic wedding video?
Crafting a high-quality wedding film requires meticulous attention to detail in the editing room. Typically, you can expect to receive your final, fully edited cinematic wedding video within 8 to 12 weeks following your event date.
Can you accommodate destination event videography?
Absolutely. Whether your celebration is local or requires travel, comprehensive event videography services are available for destination weddings. Travel logistics and accommodations are coordinated in advance to ensure seamless coverage of your multi-day event wherever it takes place.
