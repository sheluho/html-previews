Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our custom systems.

How long does it take to build a custom closet?

Timelines vary based on the complexity of the design and materials chosen. Generally, after the initial consultation and approval of the CAD design, manufacturing and installation can take anywhere from 3 to 6 weeks.

Can you convert a spare bedroom into a walk-in closet?

Absolutely. Transforming an unused room into a master dressing suite is one of our most popular requests. We can design island counters, seating areas, and wall-to-wall cabinetry to create a truly luxurious space.

Do you offer solutions for small reach-in closets?

Yes. A standard reach-in closet often suffers from poor layout. By utilizing double-hang sections, vertical shelving towers, and sliding hardware, our closet organizer systems can dramatically increase the usable storage space of small closets.

What materials do you use for your wardrobes?

We utilize a range of premium materials depending on your aesthetic preferences and budget. This includes high-grade thermally fused laminates, real wood veneers, and custom painted finishes, all paired with heavy-duty metal hardware.

Will the installation process disrupt my home?

The installation teams are trained to be efficient and respectful of your property. Because the components are pre-manufactured to exact specifications, most standard installations are completed in a single day with minimal dust or noise.
