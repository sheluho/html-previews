Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about heating and cooling services.

How often should an HVAC system be serviced?
It is generally recommended to have your heating and cooling systems inspected and maintained by a professional at least once a year. This helps ensure optimal performance, improves energy efficiency, and extends the longevity of your equipment.
What is involved in a new AC installation?
A professional AC installation involves carefully assessing your home's square footage and layout, selecting the appropriately sized unit, safely installing the indoor and outdoor equipment, securing ductwork if necessary, and thoroughly testing the system for maximum efficiency and cooling power.
Can a heat pump replace my traditional furnace?
Yes, in many moderate climates, a modern heat pump can effectively replace a traditional furnace. Heat pumps are highly versatile systems that provide efficient heating during the winter months and reverse their operation to offer excellent cooling during the summer.
Why is my air conditioner making strange noises?
Unusual sounds such as grinding, squealing, or rattling from your air conditioner can indicate underlying issues with the fan motor, compressor, or loose internal components. It is best to turn off the unit and have an experienced HVAC technician diagnose the problem promptly to prevent further damage.
How can I improve my home's heating and cooling efficiency?
You can boost efficiency by scheduling regular maintenance, regularly replacing air filters, sealing drafts around windows and doors, ensuring your home is properly insulated, and installing a smart or programmable thermostat to better manage your energy usage.
