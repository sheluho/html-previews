Frequently Asked Questions

How does the seasonal tire storage work? +

We facilitate connections to secure, climate-controlled facilities where your off-season tires are kept safe from harsh elements. When the weather changes and it is time for a swap, simply schedule an appointment, and your tires will be prepared for immediate installation.

How often should I get a tire rotation? +

As a general rule, a tire rotation is recommended every 5,000 to 8,000 miles, or during every other oil change. Regular rotation ensures even tread wear, which maximizes the life of your tires and improves overall vehicle safety and handling.

Do you provide new tire replacement services? +

Yes, we can connect you with service centers that handle comprehensive tire replacement. Whether your current tires are worn out or you are looking to upgrade, expert technicians will ensure your new tires are mounted, balanced, and installed correctly.

Why is wheel alignment important when getting new tires? +

A proper wheel alignment ensures that your vehicle's suspension is correctly adjusted so that the tires meet the road at the proper angle. Getting an alignment with your tire installation prevents premature and uneven wear on your new investment.

Can I store tires that are mounted on wheels? +

Absolutely. The tire storage facilities accommodate both loose tires and tires already mounted on rims. Storing them mounted makes the seasonal changeover process faster and more efficient when you return for your tire installation.
