Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the custom kitchen design process typically take?

The timeline for kitchen design varies depending on the complexity of your project. Typically, initial consultations and finalized designs take 2-4 weeks. Manufacturing your custom cabinets then takes additional time, ensuring every piece meets our exacting standards before installation.

Can I see physical samples in your kitchen showroom?

Absolutely. Our kitchen showroom is fully equipped with extensive displays. You can view full-scale setups, examine various wood species, test hardware, and see different finishes under proper lighting to make confident decisions for your space.

Do you manufacture the custom cabinets yourselves?

Yes, we are a direct manufacturer. This allows us to maintain strict quality control over every aspect of production, from selecting the raw materials to the final finishing coats, ensuring your custom cabinets are built to last a lifetime.

Are shaker cabinets still a popular choice?

Shaker cabinets remain one of the most requested styles due to their timeless appeal and versatility. Their simple, clean lines allow them to blend seamlessly into traditional, transitional, and even modern home aesthetics.

Do you handle the installation of the cabinetry?

Yes, professional installation is a critical part of our service. Our experienced installation teams ensure that the custom cabinets we manufacture are fitted perfectly into your home, guaranteeing proper alignment and function.
