Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical bathroom renovation take? ▼

The timeline for a bathroom remodel varies based on the scope of work. A simple bathtub replacement might take just a few days, while a full, custom bathroom renovation involving new plumbing and extensive tile installation generally takes between two to four weeks.

Can you convert my existing tub into a walk-in shower? ▼

Yes, tub-to-shower conversions are one of our most popular services. We carefully remove the old tub and install a beautiful, accessible walk-in shower, complete with custom fixtures and secure waterproofing.

Do you handle all aspects of the bathroom remodel? ▼

Absolutely. As a full-service promotional partner for turnkey solutions, we connect you with teams that manage the entire process—from design and demolition to plumbing, electrical, and final tile installation.

What materials are best for bathroom tile installation? ▼

Porcelain and ceramic are excellent choices due to their water resistance and durability. For a more luxurious feel, natural stone is also an option, provided it is properly sealed. We help you choose the best materials for your specific design and budget.

Will a bathroom remodel add value to my home? ▼

Yes, a well-executed bathroom renovation is one of the highest returning investments you can make in your home. Modernizing fixtures, adding a walk-in shower, and improving the overall aesthetic significantly boosts property appeal.
